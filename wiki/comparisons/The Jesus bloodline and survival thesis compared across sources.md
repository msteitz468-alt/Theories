## Overview

Three sources build on the same striking premise — that a **hidden bloodline tied to Jesus and the
ancient Near East secretly steers Western history**, guarded by secret societies — yet they reach
radically different conclusions. The contrast is the point: the *same structural claim* yields a
sacred human dynasty in two sources and a malevolent non-human one in the third. Each position is
attributed and **not adjudicated**.

## Side-by-side comparison

| Source | Core claim | Nature of the bloodline | Status of Jesus | Conclusion |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Holy Blood and the Holy Grail** | Jesus married the Magdalene; their line became the Merovingians | Sacred *human* descent | Historical; survived to found a dynasty | A rightful royal/spiritual heritage guarded by the Priory of Sion |
| **Custodians of Truth** | "Rex Deus" families descend from Jesus's priestly circle | Sacred *human* lineage | Historical; teachings & line preserved | Guardians of suppressed true teachings, seeding nobility & the Templars |
| **Icke (Biggest Secret / 2018)** | Elite royal bloodlines secretly rule | *Reptilian / non-human* hybrid | A "mythological composite," **not historical** | Bloodline = malevolent controllers to be exposed |

## Where they converge

1. **A hidden royal/sacred bloodline secretly steers history**, traceable to the ancient Near East.
2. **Secret societies guard it** — the Priory of Sion (
   [[Claim: Ordre de Sion created and controlled the Knights Templar from behind the scenes]]), the
   Rex Deus orders ([[Rex Deus families deliberately seeded European noble houses and administered Charlemagne's empire]]),
   or Icke's "Babylonian Brotherhood."
3. **The establishment/Church suppressed the truth** — Custodians'
   [[The institutional Church suppressed the true teachings of Jesus and monopolized spiritual knowledge during the Dark Ages]].
4. **European royalty is the vehicle** of the bloodline.

## Where they sharply diverge

- **Nature of the bloodline — the decisive split.** A *sacred human* descent of Jesus (Holy Blood,
  Custodians) vs a *reptilian, non-human* line ([[Claim: Icke asserts the House of Windsor is a reptilian Black Nobility bloodline]]).
- **Was Jesus real?** A literal historical figure whose line survived (Holy Blood, Custodians) vs a
  [[Jesus and Major Religion Figures Are Mythological Composites, Not Historical Persons|mythological composite who never existed]] (Icke).
- **Valence.** A rightful, sacred heritage to be *restored* (Holy Blood, Custodians) vs malevolent
  shapeshifting controllers to be *overthrown* (Icke, e.g.
  [[Claim: Icke asserts JFK's assassination was a Masonic ritual 'Killing of the King']]).
- **Evidence base.** The contested *Dossiers Secrets*/parchments (Holy Blood) vs an oral Rex Deus
  family tradition (Custodians) vs Icke's channeled/synthesized cosmology.

## Notes on source posture

Investigative alternative-history resting on documentary claims whose authenticity is itself disputed
(Holy Blood — see the controversies below), an insider oral-tradition account (Custodians), and a
channeled/synthetic conspiracist cosmology (Icke). Icke essentially *borrows the bloodline structure*
of the Grail thesis and inverts its meaning.
