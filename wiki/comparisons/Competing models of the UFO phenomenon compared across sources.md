## Overview

`topic/ufo` is the wiki's largest cross-source cluster (six sources, 160+ claims), and the
ingested books answer the basic question — *what are UFOs?* — in mutually incompatible ways.
This page lines the models up. Per the wiki's contradiction protocol, each model is attributed
to its source and **not adjudicated**; the value is in seeing exactly where the accounts collide.

## The five models

1. **Nuts-and-bolts nonhuman craft + government cover-up (the disclosure model)** —
   *[[In Plain Sight: An Investigation into UFOs and Impossible Science (Coulthart, 2021)]]* and
   *[[Imminent: Inside the Pentagon's Hunt for UFOs (Elizondo, 2024)]]*. UFOs/UAP are physical
   vehicles of non-human origin, some recovered and reverse-engineered, the knowledge hidden
   inside compartmented government programs.
2. **Interdimensional / control-system model** —
   *[[Passport to Magonia: From Folklore to Flying Saucers (Vallée, 1969)]]* and
   *[[Messengers of Deception (Vallee)]]*. The phenomenon is real but *not* interplanetary craft;
   it is the modern form of the fairy-faith and apparitions, a "control system" acting on human
   belief, possibly exploited by human "Manipulators."
3. **Government–alien pact / EBE model** — *[[Behold a Pale Horse (Cooper, 1991)]]*. Crashed
   discs and live aliens ("EBEs") were recovered, a secret group (Majesty Twelve) struck a deal
   with the aliens, and the aliens genetically engineered humanity.
4. **Ancient-astronaut model** — *[[Chariots of the Gods (von Däniken)]]*. UFOs are the same
   extraterrestrial visitors who came in prehistory, were worshipped as gods, and seeded
   civilization.
5. **Terrestrial-secret-technology / hoax model** —
   *[[Area 51 — An Uncensored History of America's Top Secret Military Base (Jacobsen 2010)]]*.
   At least some "saucer" lore traces to secret human aerospace projects and, in Jacobsen's most
   contested claim, a Soviet (Stalin/Mengele) disinformation hoax behind Roswell.

## Where they actually overlap

- **The secrecy/cover-up is common ground.** Coulthart, Elizondo, *and* Cooper all assert a
  deliberate government program hiding UAP knowledge. See
  [[The Admiral Wilson Memo describes a reverse-engineering program working on craft not made by human hands]],
  [[Eric Davis briefed a Defence agency that the US holds off-world vehicles not made on this earth]], and
  [[Claim: Cooper asserts 16 crashed alien craft and 65 bodies were recovered by the US government 1947–1952]].
- **"Non-human bodies."** Elizondo's [[CIA autopsy of nonhuman body showed smooth brain and reptile-like features]]
  and [[Ciudad Acuna Mexico 1950 crash retrieved one nonhuman body]] echo Cooper's
  [[Claim: Cooper asserts EBE was a live chlorophyll-based alien captive who died June 1952 and inspired the film E.T.]].
- **Civilization-from-the-gods.** Cooper's
  [[Claim: Cooper asserts aliens confirmed to Majesty Twelve that they created the human race through genetic manipulation]]
  parallels von Däniken's [[Extraterrestrials visited Earth in prehistory and were worshipped as gods (von Däniken)]].

## Where they sharply collide

- **ET craft vs. control system.** The disclosure authors take the "nuts and bolts" reality
  Vallée explicitly rejects — see [[Vallée argues the extraterrestrial hypothesis is an insufficient explanation]],
  [[Vallée argues UFOs are real and physical but not extraterrestrial spacecraft]], and
  [[Vallée proposes a control system that shapes belief through engineered apparitions]]. Notably,
  Coulthart's source includes the same 1946 Swedish "ghost rockets" Vallée's "Major Murphy" reads
  as an early human deception (compare [[The 1946 Swedish ghost rockets were judged beyond any known earthly culture]]
  with [[The Martian Conspiracy (Vallée's first stratagem hypothesis)]]).
- **Real aliens vs. engineered hoax.** Cooper's recovered-EBE narrative and Jacobsen's
  Soviet-hoax explanation for Roswell are mutually exclusive accounts of the *same* events
  ([[Roswell disc child bodies Stalin Mengele hoax per EG&G engineer Jacobsen source]]).
- **Disclosure as truth vs. disclosure as psyop.** Where Coulthart/Elizondo treat official
  disclosure as the surfacing of a real secret, Vallée's *Messengers of Deception* warns that the
  disclosure push itself may be a manipulation — see
  [[Occult contactee groups are infiltrated and exploited by intelligence and "Manipulator" networks]].

## Notes on source posture

The cluster spans an investigative journalist (Coulthart), a former Pentagon program head
(Elizondo), a scientist-skeptic of the ET reading (Vallée), a conspiracist insider-claimant
(Cooper), a popular ancient-astronaut author (von Däniken), and a mainstream investigative
historian (Jacobsen). They agree most on *secrecy* and least on *what is being kept secret* —
which is the single most useful thing this comparison surfaces.
