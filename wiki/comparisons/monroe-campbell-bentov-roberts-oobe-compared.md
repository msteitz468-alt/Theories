## Overview

This page compares the theoretical, physiological, and metaphysical models of out-of-body experiences (OBEs) and non-physical reality developed by four prominent figures in the alternative consciousness literature:
1. **[[Robert Monroe (1915–1995)|Robert A. Monroe]]**: A pioneer who provided a detailed phenomenology of out-of-body states and established a training system (Hemi-Sync).
2. **[[Thomas Campbell (physicist)|Thomas Campbell]]**: A physicist and early Monroe Institute researcher who developed a digital, information-based model of consciousness.
3. **[[Itzhak Bentov]]**: A biomedical inventor who formulated a mechanical, vibrational, and toroidal model of consciousness.
4. **[[Jane Roberts (Seth)|Jane Roberts (Seth)]]**: A medium who channeled the "energy personality essence" Seth, presenting a multidimensional, belief-centered model of reality.

## 2. Core Comparative Dimensions

### A. The Nature of Reality & Space-Time
* **Monroe (Phenomenological Layers):** Frames reality as structured layers or "Focus levels" (e.g., Focus 12, 15, 21, 27) and distinct "Locales" (Locale I, II, and III) that represent different distances from physical constraints. Space-time is a specific training environment. See [[Focus Levels Map]] and [[Locales in Non-Physical Reality]].
* **Campbell (Digital Simulation):** Views reality as a virtual simulation. Physical Matter Reality (PMR) is a calculated ruleset running inside the Absolute Unbounded Manifold (AUM)—a digital consciousness network. Time and space are merely parameters of the simulation ruleset. See [[Concept: PMR (Physical Matter Reality)]] and [[Concept: AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold)]].
* **Bentov (Vibrational Torus):** Outlines a physical-metaphysical closed-loop universe shaped like a self-reflecting torus. Matter is not solid but consists of electromagnetic fields vibrating like a pendulum. Space-time is curved, and all points in the torus are connected through a central hollow core of absolute void. See [[Concept: Toroidal Universe Model]] and [[Concept: Null Point]].
* **Roberts/Seth (Mental Projection):** Asserts that physical matter is a direct projection of consciousness, created by thoughts and beliefs. Time is simultaneous ("the spacious present"), meaning past, present, and future coexist. Space-time features invisible "coordinate points" that act as channels for translating thought-energy into physical matter. See [[Physical matter is a projection of consciousness formed by thoughts and emotions]] and [[Space-time coordinate points act as channels and accelerators for translating psychic energy into physical matter]].

### B. The Out-of-Body Experience (OBE) Mechanism
* **Monroe (Separation of the Second Body):** Describes a physical-like "Second Body" (astral body) separating from the physical body, preceded by intense internal vibrations. The Second Body acts as a localized vehicle that moves through walls and travels to different locales.
* **Campbell (Data Stream Shift):** Reframes the "Second Body" as a habit of mind carried over from physical conditioning. In Campbell's model, consciousness is fundamental and does not sit inside a physical body. An OBE is simply an individuated consciousness switching its attention from the PMR virtual reality data stream to a Nonphysical Matter Reality (NPMR) data stream. See [[Claim: OBEs are consciousness accessing NPMR directly, bypassing PMR experience]].
* **Bentov (Phase Shift to absolute space-time):** Explains the OBE through biomedical entrainment. Deep meditation or breathing creates a standing wave resonance of approximately 7 Hz in the body's cardiovascular system, which entrains the brain. At the extreme endpoints of the brain's electromagnetic vibrations (the null point), consciousness halts relative to physical oscillations, achieving infinite velocity and entering the non-local absolute void. See [[Meditation entrains the heart-aorta system and the brain at a 7Hz standing wave resonance]].
* **Roberts/Seth (Focusing the Inner Senses):** Describes the soul as a multidimensional gestalt of simultaneous personalities. During sleep or OBEs, consciousness disengages its focus from the outer physical senses and redirects its attention through the "inner senses" to navigate non-physical dimensions and interact with alternate selves. See [[The soul is a multidimensional gestalt of consciousness existing simultaneously in the spacious present]].

### C. Cosmological Purpose
* **Monroe (Loosh Cultivation & School):** Suggests the Earth system is an educational school for souls to learn empathy, and a system designed to harvest or cultivate "Loosh"—a refined emotional energy of love and empathy that is of vital significance to the non-physical universe. See [[Earth as a Compressed Learning System]] and [[The Gathering]].
* **Campbell (Entropy Reduction):** Asserts that the entire system evolved to lower its entropy. Evolving toward lower entropy is equivalent to growing in love, cooperation, and organization. Incarnating in the PMR ruleset serves as a high-entropy learning lab to test and refine consciousness quality.
* **Bentov (Quality Spectrum & Return to Creator):** Measures the quantity of consciousness by its frequency complexity, and its quality by the ratio of subjective-to-objective time. Bentov views the universe as a playground where consciousness evolves through various kingdoms (mineral, plant, animal, human, spiritual) to access the universal hologram and ultimately merge back into the absolute void of the Creator. See [[Consciousness is defined by subjective time dilation relative to objective clock time]] and [[Universal information is stored holographically and can be retrieved by a quiet mind]].
* **Roberts/Seth (Self-Realization & Co-Creation):** Focuses on self-realization and the understanding of personal creative power. The purpose of incarnation is to learn how to manipulate energy, realizing that "you create your own reality" based on conscious beliefs, and to expand the multidimensional soul through creative expression. See [[The physical environment and bodily health are direct materializations of an individual's conscious beliefs and expectations]] and [[Reincarnational lifetimes exist simultaneously as simultaneous selves in the spacious present, constantly interacting psychologically and in dreams]].

## 3. Key Metaphysical Overlaps

1. **The Primary Nature of Consciousness:**  
   All four models reject materialism, asserting that consciousness exists independently of, and prior to, the physical brain.
2. **Subjective Time Dilation:**  
   All four state that objective time is a construct. Monroe (Focus 15 is "state of no time"), Campbell (time as a ruleset variable), Bentov (consciousness quality measured by subjective time dilation), and Roberts/Seth (the spacious present) all treat time as a localized, non-fundamental dimension.
3. **Reality as an Educational Construct:**  
   Monroe (school for Loosh/empathy), Campbell (evolution simulator), Bentov (evolutionary bands), and Roberts/Seth (co-creation laboratory) all agree that physical existence is designed specifically for the development of consciousness.
