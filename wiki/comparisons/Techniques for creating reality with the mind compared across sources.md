## Overview

Eight of the wiki's consciousness/esoteric sources share one practical claim: that a person can
**deliberately shape external reality through mind, emotion, and intention**. This page compares the
*method and the proposed mechanism* — a different cut from
[[Metaphysical Reality Models Compared]], which compares what reality fundamentally *is*. Each
position is attributed and **not adjudicated**.

## Side-by-side comparison

| Source | What shapes reality | Proposed mechanism | The practice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hicks (Law of Attraction)** | Your dominant vibration/emotion | Like attracts like — "that which is like unto itself is drawn" | Align your feelings; reach for better-feeling thoughts; allow |
| **Zeland (Reality Transurfing)** | Choice among pre-existing variants | You *select* a "life track" in the "alternatives space," not create it | Reduce *importance*, "outer intention," stop fighting the "pendulums" |
| **Dispenza (Becoming Supernatural)** | Clear intention + elevated emotion | Brain + heart **coherence** collapses quantum potentials | Disciplined meditation; rehearse the future emotionally |
| **The Kybalion** | Mind — "THE ALL is Mind" | **Mental Transmutation**: change mental states via Polarity/Rhythm | Hermetic mental alchemy; raise vibration |
| **Carroll (chaos magic)** | The trained **will** | "Magic is causing change in conformity with will," via **gnosis** | Sigils + the altered/"no-mind" gnostic state |
| **Roberts (Seth)** | Your beliefs and expectations | Physical reality **materializes belief**; the "point of power is the present" | Examine and rewrite limiting beliefs in the now |
| **Bentov** | Consciousness + resonance | Holographic, non-local universe entrained by vibration | Meditation induces resonance with the field |

## Where they converge

1. **Consciousness is primary and reality is responsive to it** — from the Kybalion's
   [[THE ALL is Mind; the Universe is a Mental creation of THE ALL]] to Seth's
   [[Physical matter is a projection of consciousness formed by thoughts and emotions]].
2. **Emotion/feeling is the lever, not just thought.** Hicks's
   [[You Get What You Think About, Whether You Want It or Not]], Dispenza's coherence of "clear
   intention + elevated emotion," and Seth's belief-as-feeling all make affect central.
3. **Belief/expectation filters what manifests** — Seth's
   [[The physical environment and bodily health are direct materializations of an individual's conscious beliefs and expectations]].
4. **A special inner state amplifies it** — meditation (Dispenza, Bentov), gnosis
   ([[Magic is causing change in conformity with will; gnosis (the gnostic state) is the necessary condition]]),
   alignment (Hicks), reduced importance (Zeland).
5. **The present moment is where change happens** — Seth's "point of power," Dispenza's "living in the
   future from the present," Zeland's current track.

## Where they sharply diverge

- **The mechanism:** *vibration/resonance* (Hicks, Bentov, Kybalion) vs *quantum-field collapse*
  (Dispenza) vs *selection among existing variants* in the
  [[The Brain Stores Addresses to Information in the Alternatives Space|alternatives space]] (Zeland)
  vs *materialization of belief* (Seth) vs *imposition of will* (Carroll).
- **Create vs select:** Hicks and Seth say you *attract/create* (
  [[You Are the Sole Creator of Your Own Reality]]); Zeland insists you *do not create* but choose a
  pre-existing track.
- **Effort vs allowing:** chaos magic and Dispenza demand active will/discipline; Law of Attraction
  and Transurfing emphasize *letting go*, alignment, and reducing importance/force.
- **Register & ethics:** secular-scientific framing (Dispenza, Bentov) vs channeled metaphysics
  (Hicks = "Abraham," Seth = a channeled entity) vs occult-initiatory (Kybalion, Carroll — the latter
  explicitly embracing a will-to-power the others avoid).

## Notes on source posture

Channeled/dictated (Hicks, Roberts), Hermetic-initiatory (the Kybalion), practical-occult (Carroll),
scientific-experiential with measurement claims (Dispenza, Bentov — see the strict-extraction notes on
their pages), and a systematic model presented as discovered technique (Zeland). None are adjudicated
here; the wiki records the technique each *teaches*.
