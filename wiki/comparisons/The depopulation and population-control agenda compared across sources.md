## Overview

This cluster is unusual: the wiki holds both the **primary documents** (the Club of Rome's own
reports) and the **conspiracist reception** that reads those same documents as a deliberate
depopulation plot. The page lines them up so the hinge is visible — where environmental-limits
*scholarship* ends and *genocide-blueprint* allegation begins. Each position is attributed and
**not adjudicated**.

## Sensitivity note

Several claims here are false or harmful as factual statements (notably Cooper's assertion that AIDS
was engineered for population reduction, and various COVID-origin/treatment claims). The wiki records
*that the source makes the claim*, attributes it, and does not endorse it.

## Where they converge

1. **Population and resources are a central elite preoccupation** — agreed by the scholarship and the
   conspiracist sources alike.
2. **The Club of Rome documents are real and influential** — [[Humanity has overshot the Earth's carrying capacity]]
   and the genuine line [["The common enemy of humanity is Man"]] both come straight from the reports.
3. **Top-down policy is involved** in the "limits/sustainability" agenda.
4. **The conspiracist sources quote the primary documents as their evidence** — making this a case
   where text and conspiratorial reception sit side by side.

## Where they sharply diverge — intent and reading

- **Scholarship vs. plot.** *Limits to Growth* explicitly frames the sustainability transition as one
  that [[A deliberate transition to sustainability is possible — and need not be authoritarian or impoverishing|need not be authoritarian]];
  Coleman reads the very same corpus as
  [[The Global 2000 Report is a blueprint for global genocide]] and
  [[The Club of Rome treats overpopulation as the enemy and population reduction as policy]].
- **Mechanism.** Voluntary throughput-reduction (Limits to Growth — note its claim that "the limits
  are to throughput, not to numbers") vs *engineered scarcity/disease* (Coleman, Cooper) vs *total
  control via Agendas* (Icke) vs *pandemic + Great Reset* (COVID-19).
- **"The common enemy is Man."** In *First Global Revolution* this is rhetoric about needing a unifying
  challenge; Coleman/Cooper/Icke treat it as a confession of anti-human intent. Same words, opposite
  meanings.

## Notes on source posture

Two genuine Club of Rome reports (Meadows et al.; King & Schneider) sit against four conspiracist
works (Coleman, Cooper, Icke, Mercola & Cummins) that read elite depopulation into them. The wiki
records the documents as what they say and the allegations as attributed claims.
