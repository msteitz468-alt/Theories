## Overview

Several sources claim consciousness survives bodily death and map the realms it then enters. This page
compares those **post-mortem cartographies** and their accounts of reincarnation — a narrower focus
than the wiki's OBE-method pages ([[Monroe, Campbell, Bentov, Roberts — OBE & Consciousness Models Compared]]
and [[Monroe vs. Campbell — OBE Frameworks Compared]]), and it adds Oahspe's afterlife cosmology, which
those pages don't cover. Each position is attributed and **not adjudicated**.

## Side-by-side comparison

| Source | Survives death? | Post-mortem geography | Reincarnation? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monroe** | Yes | "Focus levels" 22–28, "rings" / holding zones, Lifeline retrieval | Yes — the "I-There" cluster of all one's lives |
| **Oahspe** | Yes | Graded heavens; the soul resurrects upward through *etherea* toward Jehovih | **No** — progressive resurrection, not rebirth on Earth |
| **Roberts (Seth)** | Yes | A multidimensional "gestalt" in the "spacious present" | Yes — but lives are **simultaneous**, not serial |
| **Campbell (My Big TOE)** | Yes | NPMR (non-physical reality) as data-states within AUM | Yes — serial lives accumulating "quality" |
| **Bentov** | Yes | Holographic levels; consciousness evolves across lives | Yes — rebirth as evolution of consciousness |

## Where they converge

1. **Consciousness survives bodily death** — Monroe's
   [[Claim: Non-physical existence continues after physical death]] is echoed by all five.
2. **The beyond is structured and state-correlated** — Monroe's
   [[Claim: Locales in non-physical realm correlate with mental states]], Oahspe's graded heavens, and
   Campbell's NPMR all tie one's post-mortem location to one's inner condition.
3. **The soul develops progressively** — accumulating "quality" (Campbell's
   [[Claim: Individuated consciousness accumulates quality across multiple PMR lifetimes]]), resurrecting
   through heavens (Oahspe), or fulfilling value (Seth).
4. **The realms are reachable in life** via OBE or meditation (Monroe, Campbell, Bentov).

## Where they sharply diverge

- **The reincarnation model — the key split.** *Serial* rebirth (Monroe's I-There, Campbell, Bentov)
  vs *simultaneous* lives in the spacious present (Seth's
  [[Reincarnational lifetimes exist simultaneously as simultaneous selves in the spacious present, constantly interacting psychologically and in dreams]])
  vs **no rebirth at all** — Oahspe teaches one-way resurrection upward, not return to Earth.
- **The geography.** Numbered Focus levels and an afterlife "Interstate" with a retrieval program
  (Monroe) vs the heavens and *etherea* of [[Etherea Is Made of Ethe, the Solvent of All Corporeal Substance]]
  (Oahspe) vs probable/simultaneous selves (Seth) vs NPMR computational reality (Campbell).
- **Register.** First-person OBE travelogue (Monroe), channeled scripture (Oahspe, Seth),
  physicist's systems model (Campbell), scientist-mystic synthesis (Bentov).
- **Purpose of the journey.** Accumulate love/quality (Monroe, Campbell) vs ascend toward the Creator
  Jehovih (Oahspe) vs value fulfillment and belief-creation (Seth).

## Notes on source posture

Experiential (Monroe, Bentov), channeled/revealed (Oahspe, Roberts), and model-building (Campbell).
The wiki records each cartography as the account that source gives.
