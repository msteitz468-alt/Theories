## Overview

A large family of ingested sources share one core story: **non-human beings came to Earth in deep
antiquity, genetically engineered humanity, and either ruled or still rule us**. But they frame the
beings, their motives, and their link to the modern world in incompatible ways. This page sets the
models side by side. Per the wiki's contradiction protocol, each position is attributed to its
source and **not adjudicated**; the page records the shared skeleton and the points of collision.

## The models

1. **Zecharia Sitchin — the rationalist astronaut model** (*[[The Twelfth Planet (Sitchin)]]*):
   the Anunnaki / Nefilim were "flesh-and-blood astronauts" from a planet (Nibiru) who came to mine
   gold and engineered humans as workers. No reptilians, no ongoing rule — a paleo-history.
2. **David Icke — the interdimensional reptilian model** (*[[The Biggest Secret (Icke, 1999)]]*,
   *[[Children of the Matrix (Icke, 2001)]]*, *[[Everything You Need to Know But Have Never Been Told (Icke, 2018)]]*):
   the Anunnaki are a reptilian/4th-density race; hybrid bloodlines secretly rule today; leaders can
   shape-shift; the system is a control matrix.
3. **William Bramley — the Custodial-ownership model** (*[[The Gods of Eden (Bramley)]]*): humanity
   is a slave race "owned" by an extraterrestrial "Custodial society," manipulated through religion
   and the "Brotherhood of the Snake."
4. **Paul Wallis — the biblical-exegesis model** (*[[Escaping from Eden (Wallis)]]*): a re-reading
   of Genesis in which the *elohim* are non-divine ETs ("the Powerful Ones") who engineered humans;
   the Snake is ambivalent — a "Council member" who gave an upgrade.
5. **Jim Marrs — the secret-society-lineage model** (*[[Rule by Secrecy (Marrs)]]*): the modern
   elite's secret-society lineage traces back to the Sumerian Anunnaki, who may still guide it.
6. **Erich von Däniken — the ancient-astronaut baseline** (*[[Chariots of the Gods (von Däniken)]]*):
   prehistoric ET visitors worshipped as gods who genetically produced modern humans.

## Where the sources converge

The shared skeleton — recorded as their common assertion, not as established fact:

1. **Non-human beings genetically engineered humanity.** Sitchin's
   [[Modern man was genetically engineered by the Nefilim from Homo erectus about 300,000 years ago]]
   and [[Man was created as a 'primitive worker' to relieve the mutinying Anunnaki of gold mining]]
   meet von Däniken's [[The visitors genetically produced modern humans (von Däniken)]], Wallis's
   [[Genesis describes humanity engineered from earth by the Powerful Ones]], and Bramley's reading
   of [[The Garden of Eden story symbolizes the Custodial enslavement of humanity]].
2. **"The gods" of Sumer/Genesis were physical beings, not deities.** Sitchin's
   [[The Nefilim were flesh-and-blood astronauts from the Twelfth Planet, not mythical gods]] and
   Bramley's [[Jehovah was a succession of Custodial management teams, not a Supreme Being]].
3. **A continuous lineage links those beings to present power.** Icke's
   [[Claim: Icke asserts reptilian-Nordic hybrid bloodlines (Nefilim) have ruled Earth continuously from Sumer to the present]]
   and Marrs's [[The secret tradition traces back through the mysteries to Sumer and the Anunnaki (Marrs)]].
4. **Gold/mining or exploitation as the original motive.** Sitchin's
   [[The Nefilim came to Earth about 445,000 years ago to mine gold]] and Bramley's
   [[Humanity is a slave race owned by an extraterrestrial Custodial society]].

## Where they sharply collide

- **Flesh-and-blood vs. interdimensional.** Sitchin's astronauts are *physical* visitors with an
  orbital mechanics argument; Icke's are *4th-density reptilians* who shape-shift — see
  [[Claim: Icke asserts the Anunnaki of Sumerian tablets were a reptilian extraterrestrial race]],
  [[Claim: Icke asserts fourth-dimensional reptilian entities control Earth by possessing Illuminati bloodline bodies]],
  and [[Claim: Icke asserts world leaders can physically shape-shift between human and reptilian form]].
  Sitchin's text contains no reptilians; Icke *builds on* Sitchin but transforms the Anunnaki into them.
- **Ancient history vs. ongoing rule.** Sitchin and von Däniken stop at prehistory; Icke, Bramley,
  and Marrs assert the control is *present-day*.
- **The Snake: villain or liberator.** Bramley's
  [[The Brotherhood of the Snake was founded by Ea then corrupted into a tool of spiritual repression]]
  and Icke's reptilians make the serpent the enemy; Wallis's
  [[The Snake was a Council member who gave humans a forbidden upgrade]] makes it humanity's
  benefactor — a direct inversion of the same motif.
- **Conspiracy intent vs. neutral history.** Sitchin presents a value-neutral paleo-history; Icke,
  Bramley, and Marrs read the same material as an ongoing *enslavement/control* program.

## Notes on source posture

The cluster runs from a self-styled ancient-language scholar (Sitchin) and a popular ancient-
astronaut author (von Däniken), through a UFO/secret-history synthesist (Bramley) and a Christian
re-interpreter of scripture (Wallis), to a conspiracy journalist (Marrs) and the maximalist
reptilian theorist (Icke). The chain of *influence* is visible in the wiki itself: later authors
(Icke, Marrs) explicitly build on Sitchin's Anunnaki while layering reptilian, occult, and
ongoing-control claims he never made — the clearest case in the catalog of one source's framework
being absorbed and radically transformed by others.
