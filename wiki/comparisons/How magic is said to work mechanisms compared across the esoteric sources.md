## Overview

The wiki's four practical-magic sources each offer an explicit account of **why magic works** — and
they propose different mechanisms for the same craft. This page compares those mechanisms and the
underlying model (mind, will, spirit, or sympathy). Each position is attributed and **not
adjudicated**; ritual procedures are catalogued descriptively, not endorsed.

## Side-by-side comparison

| Source | What magic *is* | Proposed mechanism | Model |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Kybalion** | Mental alchemy | The cosmos is Mind, so changing mind changes reality (mental transmutation) | **Idealist/mentalist** |
| **Carroll (chaos magic)** | "Change in conformity with will" | The **gnostic state** (altered/no-mind) impresses will via sigils; belief is a tool | **Psychological/agnostic** |
| **Brand (Success Magick)** | Contacting angelic powers for results | Speak divine/Aethyr names over sigils, then give the power away | **Spirit-model** (but democratized) |
| **Kynes (correspondences)** | Sympathetic association | Items linked by correspondence channel intent; the mind works by association | **Sympathetic/symbolic** |

## Where they converge

1. **Directed intention/will is the engine** — from Carroll's
   [[Magic is causing change in conformity with will; gnosis (the gnostic state) is the necessary condition]]
   to the Kybalion's mental transmutation.
2. **Symbols are the interface** — sigils (Carroll, Brand), divine names (Brand), correspondences
   (Kynes) all mediate between intent and result.
3. **A focused or altered state helps** — gnosis (Carroll), the meditative ritual posture (Brand),
   the contemplative "web"-building of Kynes.
4. **All four are operative, not merely theoretical**, and several rest on the Hermetic
   [[The Principle of Correspondence (Kybalion)|"as above, so below"]] logic.
5. **Detachment from the outcome** recurs — Carroll's
   [[Lust of result short-circuits magic; desire must be encoded and forgotten to work]] mirrors
   Brand's "offering to humanity, seeking nothing for yourself."

## Where they sharply diverge

- **The mechanism.** All-is-Mind *mental transmutation* (Kybalion) vs *will + gnosis* (Carroll) vs
  *angelic agency* (Brand) vs *sympathetic correspondence* (Kynes).
- **Spirit-model vs psychological-model.** Brand posits real external entities (angels, the
  [[The Thirty Aethyrs (Enochian)|Aethyrs]]); Carroll treats spirits as optional and belief as a
  dial to be turned; the Kybalion locates everything within Mind; Kynes grounds it in association and
  sympathy.
- **The role of belief/feeling.** Carroll: belief is a *tool* to be shifted at will. Brand:
  [[Brand teaches the magic works whether or not the practitioner feels anything|it works whether or not you feel anything]].
  The Kybalion: the mental state *is* the lever.
- **Ceremony & accessibility.** Carroll's stripped, personal method vs Brand's
  [[Brand teaches a standardized ritual method of sigil-gazing, divine names, and an offering to humanity|fixed simple ritual]]
  vs Kynes's elaborate "web" of [[Magical correspondences (Kynes)|correspondences]] vs the Kybalion's
  purely internal alchemy.

## Notes on source posture

Hermetic doctrine presented as ancient wisdom (the Kybalion), late-20th-c. practical occultism that is
deliberately results-not-dogma (Carroll), a democratized Enochian how-to (Brand), and a compiled
Pagan/Wiccan reference (Kynes). The wiki records the mechanism each *teaches*.
