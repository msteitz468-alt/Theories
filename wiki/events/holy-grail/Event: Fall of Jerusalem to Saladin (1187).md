**Event Summary**

Saladin's reconquest of Jerusalem in October 1187 ended the Crusader Kingdom's control of the city. In the context of *The Holy Blood and the Holy Grail*, this event is significant because it brought to an end the period during which the Prieuré de Sion and the Knights Templar could openly operate from their Jerusalem base. The loss of Jerusalem accelerated the institutional separation between the Prieuré de Sion and the Knights Templar — a separation the source authors associate with the Cutting of the Elm at Gisors in 1188.
