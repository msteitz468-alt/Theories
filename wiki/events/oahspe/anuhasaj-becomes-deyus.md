## Event Overview

The first self-made false Godhead. In the **Book of Wars Against Jehovih**,
[[Anuhasaj (De'yus, the false Lord God)]] — counseled by **Satan (self)** — rose through centuries of
feigned humility to be crowned **Lord God**, then proclaimed himself the Creator
under the name **De'yus** (Dyaus/Deity). He re-established the false heaven
[[Hored (the first heaven of the earth)]] as "the All Highest Place," diverted to himself the spirits owed to
the true God's kingdom Craoshivi, made **Anubi** "Master of the Scales" his "Son
and Savior of Men," and exalted confederate Lords (Osiris, Te-in, Sudga, Baal).
This is the archetypal origin of [[False Gods and Self-Godhood]] — angels saying "There is
nothing higher than man... I am the highest" (see [[Self Is the Satan That Is the Origin of All False Gods]],
[[God and Lord Are Titles of Exalted Mortals, Not the Creator]]).
