## Event Overview

The submersion of the continent of [[Pan (Whaga, the submerged continent)]] (Whaga), Oahspe's version of the
Deluge. Because the earth's heavens had become overrun with [[Drujas, Fetals, and the Bondage of Hells]] — "the
heavenly kingdoms founded by Gods and Lords have become pest houses for drujas
and fetals" — Jehovih commanded [[Aph, Son of Jehovih]] to "carry away their heaven and
earth." Aph ringed the continent with "pillars of fire" and a net "a thousand
miles" high, stationed etherean [[The Etherean Ships of Fire]] from earth to Chinvat, and "the
vortex of the earth closed in... and the land sank down beneath the water, to
rise no more." Billions of bound spirits were delivered on "birth-blankets"; the
sacred [[The I'hins (the sacred little people)]] were saved in ships and scattered to the four divisions of
the earth.
