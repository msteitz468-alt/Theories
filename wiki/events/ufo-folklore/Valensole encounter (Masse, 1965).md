## Event Overview

On 1 July 1965 at 6:00 a.m. lavender farmer Maurice Masse approached a landed egg-shaped craft and two small beings; one pointed a small tube at him and he became unable to move while remaining fully conscious. The beings left and the craft vanished. Masse suffered weeks of compulsive sleepiness afterward and reported a strong impression that the beings were 'good'.
