## Event Overview

On 19 January 1966 banana grower George Pedley reported a blue-grey craft rising from a swamp; investigators found several circular 'nests' of flattened and uprooted reeds (clockwise and counter-clockwise). Vallée sets these alongside the Ohio ring (1965) and the Charlton crater (1963) as modern instances of the folkloric 'fairy ring'.
