## Event Overview

On the night of 21 August 1955 a Kentucky farm family reported a siege by several small 'little men' with oversized round heads, long taloned arms and large lidless eyes set on the sides of the head, who floated when shot and ran on all fours. Vallée notes the glowing aluminium-like appearance recalls the 'sylphs of 1491'.
