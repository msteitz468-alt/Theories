## Event Overview (from the source)

To supply the labour the mutinying Anunnaki refused to provide, [[Enki (Sitchin)]] and the mother-goddess **Ninhursag** fashioned modern man. Enki, told the gods wished to "form an *adamu*," declared "IT EXISTS!" and instructed that the "image of the gods" be **bound upon an already-existing creature** — *Homo erectus*. After repeated trial-and-error, *Homo sapiens* was produced ~300,000 years ago: a being that is both a product of evolution and a deliberate creation of the gods, made to serve as a "primitive worker."

## Participants and Their Roles

[[Enki (Sitchin)]] — devises the method; Ninhursag ("Mammu," the Mother Goddess) — carries out the gestation/formation "following processes and formulas devised by Enki"; the assembled Nefilim — commission the work.
