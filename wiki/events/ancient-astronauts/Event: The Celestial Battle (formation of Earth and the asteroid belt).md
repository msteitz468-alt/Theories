## Event Overview (from the source)

Per Sitchin's literal reading of the *Enuma Elish* (Ch. 7), the early solar system — the Sun and nine planets — was invaded by a large interloping planet, **Marduk** ([[The Twelfth Planet (Nibiru)]]), arriving from outer space. Passing Neptune, Uranus, Saturn and Jupiter, its course was bent inward and it acquired seven satellites (the "winds"). It met the watery planet **Tiamat**: the two did not collide directly, but Marduk's satellites smashed into Tiamat, splitting her. One half, struck again on a later orbit, was crushed into the **asteroid belt** ("the Hammered Bracelet"); the other half was driven to a new orbit to become **Earth**, gaining its rotation and Tiamat's waters and Moon-host. Marduk's "arrow" — an electrical discharge — "extinguished her life-breath."

## Participants and Their Roles

[[Marduk (Sitchin)]] — the invading planet/god; Tiamat — the destroyed watery planet (also read as a "monster goddess"); Kingu — Tiamat's chief satellite, which Sitchin associates with our Moon's origin story.
