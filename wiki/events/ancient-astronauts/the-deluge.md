## Event Overview (from the source)

A sudden slippage of the Antarctic ice sheet into the southern seas — its onset linked to the nearing Twelfth Planet — sent an immense tidal wave around the globe, ending the last ice age. The Nefilim, who foresaw it, swore an oath (urged by [[Enlil (Sitchin)]]) to **conceal the danger from mankind** so that humans would perish while the gods saved themselves by rising in their craft. [[Enki (Sitchin)]] broke the oath, warning his servant **Ziusudra / Atra-Hasis / Noah** to build the ark. The flood destroyed the gods' Earth installations (the African mines, Nippur, the Sippar spaceport); afterward the surviving Nefilim taught agriculture and animal husbandry to the human remnant.

## Participants and Their Roles

[[Enlil (Sitchin)]] — imposes the oath of secrecy, seeking mankind's demise; [[Enki (Sitchin)]] — warns Ziusudra and enables human survival; the Nefilim — ride out the flood in shuttlecraft and later re-establish civilization.
