## Event Description from Source

Per [[Hidden History (Jeffries)]], on October 25, 2002, a Beechcraft King Air A100 carrying Minnesota Senator Paul Wellstone, his wife Sheila, his daughter Marcia, and five others crashed near Eveleth, Minnesota, while on approach to the Eveleth-Virginia Municipal Airport. All eight aboard were killed. The NTSB ruled the crash was caused by pilot error.

Jeffries alleges the crash was politically motivated murder, citing a prior threat from Vice President Dick Cheney, anomalous early FBI presence at the scene, unusual flight characteristics documented by witnesses, and widespread suspicion among congressional colleagues.
