## Event Overview (drawn from the source)

After the sinking of Atlantis, Thoth led the surviving Atlanteans to "the land of the Children of KHEM" (Egypt), subdued and civilized the "barbarous" inhabitants by his "magic-science," and raised the Great Pyramid over the entrance to the Halls of Amenti — installing a crystal in its apex, hidden chambers, and his records. He ruled for some 16,000 years, then withdrew into Amenti, appointing guardians who became the pyramid priests.

## Participants and Their Roles

- **Thoth** — conqueror, teacher, builder of the pyramid and "blaster" of a path to Amenti.
- **The Pyramid Priests** — descendants of Thoth's appointed guardians; later deified him as the God of Wisdom and carried the tablets abroad.
