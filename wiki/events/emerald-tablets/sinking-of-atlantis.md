## Event Overview (drawn from the source)

Per the tablets, Atlantis — a civilization of ten island-kingdoms led from the island of Undal — was destroyed when proud adepts "opened the gateway that led to below," prompting the Dweller (Horlet) to call on the Seven Lords' powers and "change the Earth's balance," sinking all the islands beneath the sea except Unal. Thoth and the surviving "sons of Atlantis" fled by ship to Egypt (Khem).

## Participants and Their Roles

- **The Dweller (Horlet)** — rose from his trance ("AGWANTI"), commanded the Three messengers, and triggered the cataclysm via the Seven Lords.
- **Thoth** — evacuated his people and the records of Atlantis aboard "the great ship of the Master."
- **The Children of Light** — preserved in Unal as future teachers.
