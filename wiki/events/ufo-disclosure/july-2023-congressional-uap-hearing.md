## Event Overview

On July 26, 2023, the House Oversight Committee held a UAP hearing featuring three witnesses testifying under oath: Cmdr. Dave Fravor (USS Nimitz Tic Tac witness), Lt. Ryan Graves (former Navy pilot and UAP advocate), and David Grusch (former NRO officer and NGA representative to the UAP Task Force). The hearing was historic as the first Congressional UAP session with witnesses testifying under oath about crash retrievals and nonhuman bodies.

Grusch testified that the UAP Task Force was refused access to a crash-retrieval program involving nonhuman origin vehicles and that nonhuman bodies had been recovered. He described a "sophisticated disinformation campaign targeting the US populace." Grusch had previously filed complaints with the Intelligence Community Inspector General before going public as a protected whistleblower.

The hearing followed the May 2022 public UAP hearing (first in 50 years) and preceded the UAP Disclosure Act (signed December 2023).
