## Event Overview

In September 1964, telescopic film directed by Lt Bob Jacobs of a dummy nuclear warhead launched from Vandenberg AFB captured an object circling the warhead at high speed and firing four beams of light, after which the warhead tumbled. Major Florenze Mansmann screened it, ordered Jacobs to say it never happened, and decades later confirmed the account in signed letters, calling the object an extra-terrestrial craft.
