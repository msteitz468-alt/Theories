## Event Overview

On their third visit to observe alleged test flights, Bob Lazar and companions drove down Groom Lake Road toward the base. Area 51 security detained them, required ID, and they answered questions for the Lincoln County Sheriff's Department before release.

## Participants and Their Roles

Lazar had previously brought his wife Tracy, friend Gene Huff, and ufologist John Lear to viewing points with binoculars and video camera on two earlier occasions per the source.

## Key Claims Arising from This Event

Led to Lazar's termination meeting at Indian Springs Air Force Base the following day; guard from perimeter confirmed Lazar among trespassers; Lazar warned of espionage arrest if he returned.
