## Event Overview

About 2:30 am on the North West Cape, Annie Farinaccio and two Australian Federal Protective Service officers reported a low diamond/triangular craft with rows of lights that pursued their vehicle, shot up and down instantaneously, and appeared to land in scrub. Two days later US military police took Farinaccio onto the base where US officers and civilians pressed her to call it a weather balloon; the officers' confiscated photographs were never returned.
