## Event Overview

On 17 November 1986 the crew of Japan Airlines flight 1628 reported two glowing craft and a gigantic 'mothership' ('two times bigger than an aircraft carrier') near Anchorage. The FAA called the radar returns 'clutter'; FAA division chief John Callahan said CIA agents later confiscated the radar and voice data at a Washington meeting and ordered its existence denied, but he kept copies.
