## Event Overview

On 10 April 1997 Steven Greer, Edgar Mitchell and Cmdr Willard Miller briefed Vice Admiral Thomas Wilson, then Deputy Director of the DIA. Greer's group says Wilson admitted that, after investigating codenames in a leaked NRO document, he traced a contractor-run program and was told 'you don't have a need to know'. Wilson, in a letter to Coulthart, confirmed the meeting but denied acknowledging any such program.
