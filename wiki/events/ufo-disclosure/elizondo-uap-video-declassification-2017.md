## Event Overview

Prior to his October 2017 resignation, Elizondo filed DoD Form 1910 requesting declassification of three UAP videos: the FLIR video (Tic Tac, USS Nimitz 2004), the GoFast video (USS Roosevelt 2015), and the GIMBAL video (USS Roosevelt 2015). The DoD Office of Prepublication and Security Review (DoDOPSR) responded by recommending full public release rather than restricted distribution, and stamped the form UNLIMITED DISTRIBUTION. Elizondo received email confirmation of the approval.

The three videos were subsequently published in conjunction with the December 16, 2017 New York Times and Politico stories on AATIP. The DoD later confirmed the videos' authenticity while issuing contradictory statements about their intended distribution scope.
