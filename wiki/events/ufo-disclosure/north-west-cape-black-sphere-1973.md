## Event Overview

On the evening of 25 October 1973 — hours after Kissinger raised US forces to DEFCON-3 during the Yom Kippur War — Australian fire captain Bill Lynn and a US Lt Cdr (Moyer/Meyer) independently filed RAAF 'Unusual Aerial Sighting' reports of a large black sphere hovering near the base that 'accelerated beyond belief' and vanished northward. The original RAAF records later disappeared from the National Archives.
