## Event Overview

On the nights of 20 and 30 December 1978, glowing objects accompanying an Argosy cargo aircraft off Kaikoura were tracked on both Wellington radar and the aircraft's radar, witnessed by crew and reporters and filmed by cameraman David Crockett. The Royal NZ Air Force quickly blamed Venus and squid-boat lights; air-traffic controller John Cordy and reporter Dennis Grant told Coulthart that explanation does not fit and that they were never interviewed.
