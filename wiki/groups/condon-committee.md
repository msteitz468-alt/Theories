US Air Force–funded University of Colorado study of UFOs (1966–68) led by physicist Edward Condon, which concluded further study could not be justified. Coulthart cites Dr Robert Low's leaked memo — that 'the trick would be' to give the public the impression of a 'totally objective study' — and Harry Turner's view that the report was discredited. It nonetheless acknowledged the 1956 Bentwaters–Lakenheath case as a probable genuine UFO.
