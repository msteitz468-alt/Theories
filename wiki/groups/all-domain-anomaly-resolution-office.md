## Identity and Nomenclature

AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office) replaced the UAP Task Force in July 2022. It was formally authorized and codified by the NDAA FY2023 UAP legislation signed by President Biden on December 23, 2022. AARO is responsible for all-domain UAP investigation and reporting, with a mandate broader than the UAPTF's — covering air, sea, space, and any other domain. AOIMSG (Airborne Object Identification and Management Synchronization Group) was announced in November 2021 as an intermediate step before AARO's establishment.

## Primary Textual Appearances

In Chapter 23, Elizondo describes AARO's creation as a major milestone in the disclosure effort — the first time a DoD office was explicitly authorized to investigate UAP across all domains and to report findings to Congress on a statutory basis.
