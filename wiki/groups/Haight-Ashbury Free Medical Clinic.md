## Description from Source

The free clinic founded by Dr. [[David E. Smith]] at 558 Clayton Street in June 1967, an icon of the "summer of love" offering nonjudgmental care to Haight-Ashbury youth. In *Chaos*, O'Neill argues it was also a drug-research center: staff received private and NIMH (CIA-fronted) grants, patients were effectively "research subjects," and it hosted the amphetamine/LSD studies of David Smith and [[Roger Smith (parole officer)]] (the Amphetamine Research Project) — and an office for MKULTRA-linked [[Louis Jolyon West]]. Manson and the Family were frequent visitors during their formative San Francisco year.
