## Identity and Nomenclature (from the Source)

In *Everything You Need to Know But Have Never Been Told* (2018), David Icke identifies a specific network of technology institutions, corporations, and agencies as a unified group serving the Archontic transhumanist agenda. He describes Silicon Valley as "The Devil's Playground" and "a very sinister place controlled by very sinister people." The group is not a formal organisation but a coordinated network linked by funding, personnel movement, and shared agenda.

Named members and their roles:
- **DARPA** (Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency): "the technological development arm of the Archontically-controlled Pentagon"; funded the internet, Siri (via CALO project), gene editing, and brain-computer interface research
- **Google/Alphabet**: Founded with CIA/NSA/DARPA funding; controls global information access; has health division Verily (Project Baseline); DeepMind accessed 1.6 million NHS patient records; Executive Chairman Eric Schmidt is Bilderberg Group and Trilateral Commission member
- **Facebook/Building 8**: Developing direct brain-computer interface (bypassing speech/keyboard); headed by Regina Dugan (ex-DARPA director)
- **Singularity University**: Founded 2008 by Ray Kurzweil and Peter Diamandis at NASA Research Park; funded by Google; promotes human-AI merger
- **Apple**: Siri traced to DARPA's CALO project
- **Amazon**: Bezos holds $600 million CIA cloud contract; pursuing global commerce monopoly

The connecting thread in Icke's account is personnel movement: Regina Dugan (DARPA director → Google executive → Facebook Building 8 head) is cited as the clearest example.

Chapter 16 (The Assimilation) is dedicated to this network. Individual corporations are referenced throughout the book's political sections.
