## Description from Source

The commune of mostly young women (plus men such as Tex Watson) that Charles Manson gathered in San Francisco's Haight-Ashbury in 1967 and later moved to the Spahn Ranch near Los Angeles. In *Chaos*, the Family is presented as a product of its time and milieu rather than the isolated cult of the *Helter Skelter* narrative: its members frequented the [[Haight-Ashbury Free Medical Clinic]], were bound to Manson through daily LSD use and "ego negation," and received unusual legal leniency. Members committed the 1969 Tate–LaBianca murders.
