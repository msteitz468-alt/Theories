## Cooper's Service with Naval Intelligence

Cooper's account of the Office of Naval Intelligence is primarily autobiographical — it is the institution through which he claims to have discovered the material he later published. He describes being called into "the office of the Chief of Staff for Naval Intelligence," where his security clearance was upgraded to "Top Secret, Q, Sensitive Compartmentalized Information with access authorized on a strict need-to-know basis." He was ordered to report to "the officer in charge of the CINCPACFLT Intelligence Briefing Team" at 0400 hours the following morning.

Cooper states he was later given "another upgraded clearance in the crypto category and served as the designated SPECAT operator when I was on watch in the command center."

## Discovery of the Alien Question

Cooper writes: "What I learned during the time I spent with that briefing team is what led me on my 18-year search that has culminated in the writing of this book." He claims that the Top Secret documents he read while with Naval Intelligence included MAJIC/Operation Majority material about alien contacts, the Majesty Twelve group, and the alien treaty with the Eisenhower administration.

He further states: "Top Secret documents that I read while with Naval Intelligence stated that President Eisenhower had commissioned the JASON Society to examine all of the evidence, facts, lies, and deception and find the truth of the alien question."

## Kennedy Assassination Discovery

Cooper states: "On the day that I learned that the Office of Naval Intelligence had participated in the assassination of President John F. Kennedy and that it was the Secret Service agent driving the limo that had shot Kennedy in the head, I went AWOL with no intention of ever returning." He later returned to duty after being persuaded by a friend named Bob Swan.

## Early Encounter with ONI

Cooper also describes an earlier encounter with a commander from the Office of Naval Intelligence who boarded his ship after the crew witnessed a UFO. The commander interviewed Cooper about what he had seen and then extracted a promise of silence.

## Named ONI Agent

Cooper states that Bruce Maccabee is "ONI (Office of Naval Intelligence)" — naming him in a list of UFO researchers he claims were working as government agents. He states these names appeared in the Naval Intelligence documents he read.

## Role of Naval Intelligence Documents as Source

Cooper repeatedly attributes specific claims to "Top Secret documents that I read while in Naval Intelligence" or "documents that I viewed while I was in Naval Intelligence." These attributed documents are the primary evidentiary basis for his claims about Majesty Twelve, the alien treaty, and the Kennedy assassination.

## Primary Textual Appearances

The Office of Naval Intelligence appears in the autobiographical opening chapters of Cooper's book (particularly the account of his service and the events that led him to go AWOL) and throughout Chapter Twelve ("The Secret Government") as the source of the classified documents Cooper claims to have read.
