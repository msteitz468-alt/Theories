# Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn

## Identity and Nomenclature

In the Foreword to *The Mystical Qabalah*, [[Dion Fortune (Violet Mary Firth, 1890–1946)]] refers to the order founded by **MacGregor Mathers** — the Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn — as the organisation of which she was at one time a member, having joined after Mathers's death. She adopts Mathers's transliteration system (from *The Kabbalah Unveiled*) because it is the one generally used by esoteric students.

## Primary Textual Appearances

- Foreword: as the source of the transliteration conventions and of the practical Qabalistic tradition she draws on, mediated through Mathers and Crowley.
