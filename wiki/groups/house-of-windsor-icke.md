**Identity and Nomenclature (from the source)**

In *The Biggest Secret* the House of Windsor is "the highest profile reptilian family on the planet," a **Black Nobility bloodline** of the [[Babylonian Brotherhood (Icke)]] that "operate at the heart of the global manipulation" — though Icke says they are still "gofers," close to but not at the very top of the pyramid. He repeatedly puns "El-lizard-birth" on Elizabeth and "Bowes-Lyon."

**Source-Specific Portrait**

Icke reads the **1953 coronation** as an undisguised Brotherhood/Pagan ritual: regalia kept overnight in the Jerusalem Chamber, the Stone of Destiny, anointing with the "messeh fat" oil of the Egyptian "Royal Court of the Dragon," and a crown set with the 12 stones of "the Breastplate of Aaron." He traces the Windsor bloodline through **William of Orange** (who "signed into existence the Bank of England"), William the Conqueror, the Hanoverians, Saxe-Coburg-Gotha, and the Black Nobility of Venice back to the Phoenicians, Egypt, Sumer and the reptilians. He stresses the European royal houses "are not families at all, they are one family."

He devotes extended attention to **Prince Philip**'s Nazi-connected German relatives (the Hesse princes, the SS officer Prince Christoph), the wartime correspondence recovered from Kronberg Castle, and the boarding-school system (Gordonstoun, Schloss Salem) as Brotherhood "mind control." Via [[Arizona Wilder]] he alleges the Windsors shape-shift into reptiles at rituals (e.g. the 1981 "Awakening of the Bride").
