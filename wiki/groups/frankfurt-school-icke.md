## Identity and Nomenclature (from the Source)

In *Everything You Need to Know But Have Never Been Told* (2018), David Icke describes the Frankfurt School as "The Rothschild-connected Frankfurt School, established by fellow Zionists in the 1920s ... among the most infamous of social engineering operations." It is described as first based in Frankfurt, Germany (the "Rothschild home city"), then located in Switzerland and the United States.

Icke presents the Frankfurt School not as an academic institution but as a deliberate elite social engineering project whose goals were implemented as long-term transformations of Western society.

Goals attributed to the Frankfurt School by Icke:
- Creation of racism offences (and hate crime laws)
- Continual change to create confusion and undermine stability
- Teaching sex to young children
- Huge immigration to destroy national identity
- Promotion of excessive drinking
- Emptying of churches (undermining social cohesion)
- A legal system biased against victims of crime
- Dependency on the state (then removing benefits once dependence is achieved)
- Control and dumbing down of the media
- Encouraging the breakdown of the family

Icke presents all these as having been successfully implemented in Western societies in the decades since the Frankfurt School formulated them.

## Primary Source Appearances

The Frankfurt School is introduced in Chapter 8 (Casting the Spell) as a social engineering vehicle. Its connection to political correctness is developed in Chapter 10 (Advancing the Spell). It is referenced as the institutional origin of the PC movement throughout chapters 10 and 11.
