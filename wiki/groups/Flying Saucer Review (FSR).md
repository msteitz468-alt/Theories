London-based UFO journal (founded 1955) that Vallée repeatedly cites as the most reliable serious outlet for first-hand reports; its 1966 special issue 'The Humanoids' (chaired by editor Charles Bowen) listed over three hundred landing reports with bibliographies and served as a key source for Vallée's catalogue.
