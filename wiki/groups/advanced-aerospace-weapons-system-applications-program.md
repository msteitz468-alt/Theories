## Identity and Nomenclature

AAWSAP (Advanced Aerospace Weapons System Applications Program) was the Defense Intelligence Agency-funded predecessor to AATIP. It was funded at $22 million through the efforts of Senators Harry Reid, Ted Stevens, and Daniel Inouye. Jim Lacatski served as its director. The program contracted with Robert Bigelow's Bigelow Aerospace Advanced Space Studies (BAASS) as its primary research vehicle.

## Primary Textual Appearances

Elizondo was recruited into the program world through AAWSAP in early 2009. AAWSAP commissioned 38 Defense Intelligence Reference Documents (DIRDs) — academic theoretical research papers covering topics in advanced propulsion, zero-point energy, remote viewing, and UAP-related physics — from scientists coordinated by Hal Puthoff. The program also conducted fieldwork at Skinwalker Ranch.
