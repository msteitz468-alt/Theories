## Identity and Nomenclature

EG&G is described as a top secret and Q-cleared contractor at the Nevada Test Site and Area 51 with more power, access, and little oversight than other contractors.

## Primary Textual Appearances

- **Chapter 1**: Edward Teller referred Bob Lazar to EG&G; Lazar interviewed at McCarran Airport EG&G building; Dennis Mariani as supervisor; fleet of unmarked 737s with call sign Janet.

## Source-Specific Portraits

Operates secure hangars at McCarran with armed guards; flies personnel to Groom Lake with window curtains drawn for newcomers.
