# The Society of the Inner Light

## Identity and Nomenclature

In the Foreword to *The Mystical Qabalah*, [[Dion Fortune (Violet Mary Firth, 1890–1946)]] names the **Society of the Inner Light** (which she founded, earlier styled the **Fraternity of the Inner Light**) as the school in which pupils are trained in the Western Esoteric Tradition. The Tree of Life is described as "the system upon which pupils are trained in the Fraternity of the Inner Light."

## Primary Textual Appearances

- Foreword: the Society is presented as offering courses for serious students of the Western Esoteric Tradition, with a London contact address.
- Throughout: Fortune writes as an instructor conveying the practical, initiatory use the fraternity makes of the Qabalah.
