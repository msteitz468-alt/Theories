**Identity and Nomenclature (from source)**

Per Icke's synthesis of Sumerian tablet translations (primarily via Zechariah Sitchin), the Anunnaki are a physically real reptilian extraterrestrial race. Their Sumerian epithet translates as "Those who from Heaven to Earth Came" and "The Righteous Ones of the Blazing Rockets" — indicating space travel. They are also identified with: the Nefilim of the Old Testament ("Sons of God" who interbred with human women); the "Dragon Kings" and "Serpent Kings" of ancient Egypt and Sumer; the "gods" recorded across all ancient traditions under different names.

Icke explicitly distinguishes his use of Sitchin's translations from Sitchin's conclusions: Sitchin "will simply not accept the existence of a reptilian or serpent race, despite the scale of evidence."

**Physical Description (from source)**

The Anunnaki in full reptilian form: tall, humanoid, snake-like eyes and skin. Their hybrid offspring (Illuminati bloodlines) appear human but contain sufficient reptilian DNA to shape-shift when conditions permit — or when blood is withheld. The "fourth-dimensional" form of the Anunnaki is their "home" state; they inhabit third-dimensional human hybrid bodies as a control vehicle.

**Relationship with Other Extraterrestrial Races (from source)**

Three main physical forms are described operating on Earth:
1. "Blue-eyed blonds" / Nordics (from Pleiades, Lyra, etc.)
2. Reptilians of various expressions (including the Anunnaki; from Draco, Orion, Sirius)
3. "Greys" (connected to the classic UFO alien image; linked to reptilians)

The most important interbreeding was between the Anunnaki reptilians and the Nordic race, creating the hybrid "Aryan" or "noble" bloodlines placed in royal power. These are the Illuminati bloodlines.

**Historical Trajectory (per source)**

- Prehistory: The Anunnaki interbreed with human and Nordic populations during the Lemurian and Atlantean periods
- Post-cataclysm (~10,000 BC): They return to rebuild, establishing Sumer (~4000 BC) as the new centre
- Sumer to Rome: The bloodlines rule as pharaohs, kings, and emperors
- Rome to present: The bloodlines shift from overt to covert control; secret society network maintained
- Present: They occupy the positions of presidents, central bankers, media owners

**Source-Specific Portrait**

Icke frames the Anunnaki as fundamentally fourth-dimensional beings using third-dimensional human-reptilian hybrid bodies as a "genetic space suit." Their consumption of human blood is described as functionally necessary to maintain the mammalian form. Their dependence on negative emotional energy (fear, terror) generated by war, suffering, and sacrifice makes human misery their "food supply."
