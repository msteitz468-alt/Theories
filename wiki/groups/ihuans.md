## Identity and Nomenclature

The I'huans are the copper-colored, "large and red" race born of the union of
the [[The I'hins (the sacred little people)]] with the darker [[The Druks]]. They are the warrior race from
whom many of Oahspe's lawgivers sprang — [[Abram (Abraham, Oahspe)]] ("an I'huan, large and
red, like new copper") and [[Moses (Oahspe)]] ("a pure I'huan, copper coloured") are
named as I'huans. They mark a middle grade between the sacred I'hins and the
fallen Druks ([[The Races of Man: Asu, I'hin, Druk, I'huan, and Ghan]]).
