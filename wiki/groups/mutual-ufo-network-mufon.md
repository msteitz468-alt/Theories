**Identity and Nomenclature (from the source)**

MUFON (the Mutual UFO Network) is the volunteer civilian UFO-investigation organization through which [[Chuck Zukowski]] and [[Debbie Ziegelmeyer]] were trained and accredited as **field investigators**. The book describes its apparatus in detail: the MUFON Field Manual, the MUFON Field Investigator card, standardized questionnaire forms, a classification scheme for incident types, and conferences/meetings.

**Source-Specific Portrait**

Mezrich presents MUFON as primarily a **data-collection** body: per Zukowski, "MUFON was all about collecting data; if you wanted to get that data analyzed by real scientists, in real laboratories, you needed to outsource." Debbie's MUFON connections could not get the Roswell artifact laboratory analysis, which is what drove Chuck to go public via the 2008 Roswell press conference. MUFON reports form much of the raw material Zukowski plotted onto his 37th-parallel map.
