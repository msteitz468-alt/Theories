UAP-disclosure advocacy effort founded by [[Steven Greer (disclosure activist)]] (CSETI 1990; Disclosure Project 1992) that gathered witness testimony from military and government insiders, culminating in a 2001 National Press Club event. Coulthart credits its access while doubting some of Greer's more extreme cover-up claims.
