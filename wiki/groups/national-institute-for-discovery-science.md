Privately funded research organisation created by billionaire [[Robert Bigelow (aerospace entrepreneur)]] in 1995 to investigate UAPs, the paranormal and 'the physics of consciousness'. NIDS bought and ran full-time surveillance of [[Skinwalker Ranch]] from 1996 and investigated cattle mutilations across Utah, Nevada and New Mexico. Its scientists included [[Eric Davis (physicist)]], Dr Colm Kelleher and Colonel John Alexander; [[Edgar Mitchell (Apollo 14 astronaut)]] sat on its advisory board. Much of its research remains confidential.
