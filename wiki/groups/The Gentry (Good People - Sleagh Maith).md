The Celtic fairy races as described by Evans-Wentz's informants: a tall, noble, 'military-aristocratic' people, neither human nor spirit, who live in mountains and the sea, travel in companies, take an interest in human affairs, can appear and vanish, and 'take' people body and soul. Vallée argues the Gentry are indistinguishable from the occupants of modern UFOs.
