## Identity and Nomenclature (from the source)

An order of dark adepts described in Tablet 6 — "Banded together as in order, BROTHERS OF DARKNESS… antagonist they to the children of men" — who work secretly, reach into "man's mind-space," intrude through dreams, and bind souls with the "veil of night" using "forbidden knowledge."

## Primary Textual Appearances

- Tablet 6 (The Key of Magic): their methods and the counter-techniques against them (self-examination, the head-to-foot vibration wave, the protective circle and invocation of the Seven Lords).

## Source-Specific Portrait

The text warns they especially target advanced seekers of Light, and that some who "walk in DARK BRIGHTNESS… are not the children of LIGHT." Tablet 15 reframes darkness as "disorder" that can be "transmuted" into Light.
