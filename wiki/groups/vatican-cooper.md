## Identity and Historical Role

Cooper refers to the Vatican as "the 'Open Friendly Secret Society'" that "actually ruled most, if not all, of the known world at one time." He situates it historically within his broader framework of secret societies, noting that early Christians were themselves a secret society because Roman authorities considered them dangerous to imperial rule.

## Illuminati Infiltration

Cooper asserts that "The Vatican has been infiltrated over many years by the Illuminati." He cites several pieces of evidence for this claim:

- In 1738 Pope Clement XII issued a Papal Bull stating that any Catholic who became a Mason would be excommunicated.
- In 1884 Pope Leo XIII issued a proclamation stating that Masonry was one of the secret societies attempting to "revive the manners and customs of the pagans" and "establish Satan's kingdom on Earth."
- Piers Compton, in his book *The Broken Cross*, traced the infiltration and found use of the all-seeing eye in the triangle by leading Catholics and by the Jesuits. The symbol appeared in the seal of the Philadelphia Eucharistic Congress in 1976 and on a special issue of Vatican stamps in 1978.
- Mr. Compton claimed that several hundred leading Catholic priests, bishops, and cardinals are members of secret societies.
- An Italian journal listed more than 70 Vatican officials who were Freemasons, including Pope Paul VI's private secretary, the director general of Vatican radio, the Archbishop of Florence, the prelate of Milan, the assistant editor of the Vatican newspaper, several Italian bishops, and the abbot of the Order of St. Benedict.
- On November 27, 1983, the Pope retracted all Papal Bulls against Freemasonry and allowed Catholics to again become members of secret societies without fear of excommunication.

Cooper states that he believes Pope John Paul II is a member of the Illuminati: "The goal of the Illuminati to elect one of their own to the Papacy appears to have come to fruition."

## Role in the New World Order Alliance

Cooper asserts that in 1952 "an alliance was formed, bringing them all together for the first time in history. The Black Families, the Illuminati (the Order), the Vatican, and the Freemasons now work together to bring about the New World Order."

He notes that the Vatican is represented among the three committees of the Bilderberg Group's core 39-member structure: "The three committees are made up exclusively of members of all the different secret groups that make up the Illuminati, the Freemasons, the Vatican, and the Black Nobility."

Cooper states that "the Vatican, for instance, wants the Pope to head the world coalition" for the New World Order. He predicts that whoever else is selected as leader, "The Pope will have to approve him" — and that if this fulfills the prophecy in the Book of Revelation, "the Pope will ultimately win out and will reign as the second beast."

## Veto Power Over Papal Selection

Cooper claims that Prince Bernhard of the Netherlands — organizer of the Bilderberg Group — "has the power to veto the Vatican's choice of any Pope it selects" because his family, the Hapsburgs, are descended from the Roman emperors.

## U.S. Ambassador to the Vatican

Cooper raises concerns about the appointment of Knights of Malta members as U.S. Ambassadors to the Vatican, arguing this represents an irreconcilable conflict of interest since Knights of Malta are sworn to obey the Pope. He names William Wilson as the first such ambassador (and a Knight of Malta), Frank Shakespeare as his successor (also a Knight of Malta), and Thomas Melledy (appointed by President Bush) as another Knight of Malta in that role.

## The Pope John Paul II Center

Cooper notes that the Vatican founded the Pope John Paul II Center for Prayer and Study for Peace at 1711 Ocean Avenue, Springlake, New Jersey, in a mansion given to the New York Archdiocese by the estate of Elmer Bobst. He states the Center was directed by Kurt Waldheim (described as a former UN Secretary General and "ex-nazi war criminal"), Cyrus Vance, Clare Booth Luce (a Dame of the Knights of Malta), and J. Peter Grace (head of the Knights of Malta in the United States). Cooper states the Center "was set up by the Vatican as a part of the Pope's new peace plan, which will bring the world together."

## Israel and the Vatican

Cooper asserts that "the Vatican has not and will not recognize the state of Israel" and "The Vatican refuses even to call it Israel. Instead the Vatican says Palestine." He connects this to broader secret-society goals.

## Primary Textual Appearances

The Vatican is discussed throughout Chapter Two ("Secret Societies and the New World Order") of Cooper's book, primarily in connection with the Knights of Malta and the Bilderberg Group. Cooper also addresses Vatican infiltration at length in relation to Freemasonry and the Illuminati.
