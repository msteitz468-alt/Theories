## Identity and Nomenclature (from the source)

Per Doreal's preface, the pyramid priests descend from the guardians Thoth appointed over his secrets before withdrawing into Amenti. They deified Thoth as the God of Wisdom, and a delegation carried the Emerald Tablets out of Egypt c. 1300 B.C. as a "talisman" of authority, eventually placing them beneath a Maya sun-temple altar in the Yucatán.

## Primary Textual Appearances

- Preface (Doreal): their custody and the tablets' journey; Doreal claims to work with them through the "Great White Lodge."

## Source-Specific Portrait

Custodians and transmitters of the hidden wisdom across millennia, bridging Egypt and the Maya.
