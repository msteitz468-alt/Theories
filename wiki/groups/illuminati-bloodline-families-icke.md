**Identity and Nomenclature (from source)**

Icke uses "Illuminati" and "Illuminati bloodlines" interchangeably to refer to both the physical families and the network they operate. The term means "Illuminated Ones" — illuminated into knowledge denied to all others. Synonyms within the source include "the Nefilim" (Old Testament term for offspring of the "Sons of God" with human women), "the Anunnaki hybrids," "the 13 families," "the reptilian-Nordic hybrids," and "the bloodlines." The human-presenting form of these entities is described as a "genetic space suit" occupied by fourth-dimensional reptilian consciousness.

**Primary Textual Appearances**

- Chapter 1 introduces the 13-family structure and pyramid of secret societies
- Chapter 5 (Blood brothers) traces the bloodlines from Sumer through Egypt, Rome, and European royalty to present-day U.S. presidents and banking dynasties
- Chapter 8 (The shape-shifters) identifies specific named individuals as bloodline members and presents eyewitness shape-shifting accounts
- Chapter 17 presents the policy agenda being implemented by these families through Bilderberg, CFR, TC, RIIA

**Named Members/Families (per source)**

- The Rothschilds (described as one of the most prominent, with control over the Bank of England and the Federal Reserve)
- The Rockefellers (David, Laurance, Nelson — Bilderberg members; David described as co-ordinator of Bilderberg with Henry Kissinger)
- The Bush family (George H.W. Bush described as "of the Anunnaki/Merovingian bloodline"; named most frequently in shape-shifting accounts)
- The British Royal Family / Windsor Dynasty (described as reptilian bloodline going back to Egyptian pharaohs; Queen and Queen Mother specifically named as blood-drinkers)
- Prince Philip (identified as "the leading biological descendent of the 'reptilians'" at the peak of the ritual hierarchy per Phillip Eugene de Rothschild)
- Bill Clinton (Rhodes Scholar, Bilderberg; described as bloodline member and Bohemian Grove attendee; alleged to have been seen shape-shifting)
- Tony Blair (Bilderberg; described as installed to advance European monetary union)
- Henry Kissinger (Bilderberg, Trilateral Commission, CFR; identified as Bohemian Grove attendee)
- The Bronfman family (described as reptilian bloodline controlling Universal Music, Universal Studios, and using them for Illuminati conditioning)
- Alan Greenspan (Federal Reserve chairman; described as high in the ritual hierarchy, identified as making senior Rockefellers and Bushes "cower with just a glance" per de Rothschild)

**Source-Specific Portrait**

These families are described as psychologically characterised by traits of the reptilian brain: obsessive ritual behaviour, cold-blooded emotionlessness, top-down hierarchy, extreme aggression, and sexual violence. They have maintained their bloodlines through arranged marriages and "secret breeding programmes" to preserve hybrid genetics. All road lead up to these 13 families; the named public figures are mostly sub-elite "lackeys" serving the families.
