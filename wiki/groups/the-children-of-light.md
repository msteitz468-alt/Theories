## Identity and Nomenclature (from the source)

The "Children of Light" (also "Sons of Light," "Brothers of Light," "Suns of the Morning") are described as beings "formed from the space-dust, partaking of life from the infinite ALL" who descended and took human bodies to free mankind. Thirty-two dwell on thrones in the Halls of Amenti around the Flower; the Seven Lords of the Cycles are their overlords.

## Primary Textual Appearances

- Tablet 2: their descent, the thirty-two thrones, their incarnation "time after time… in the bodies of men."
- Tablet 6: as the "Brothers of Light," eternal antagonists of the Brothers of Darkness, "guardians of men… Masters of the SUN power."

## Source-Specific Portrait

Presented as benevolent, hidden guardians — "like man… and yet are unlike" — who guide humanity upward toward Light and freedom from the "veil of night."
