CIA-convened panel of scientists (January 1953), chaired by physicist Howard Robertson, that recommended a public 'training and debunking' program to reduce UAP reporting. Coulthart notes the CIA Director had been privately advised that the reports indicated 'something going on that must have immediate attention', making the panel's no-threat conclusion a 'cop-out'. J. Allen Hynek concluded the CIA feared UFO reports, not UFOs.
