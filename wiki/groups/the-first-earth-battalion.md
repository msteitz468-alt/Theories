## Description from Source

The conceptual New Age army unit envisioned by [[Jim Channon]] in his 1979 *First Earth Battalion Operations Manual*. Per Ronson, it imagined soldiers as peaceful "warrior monks" wielding intuition, telepathy, "sparkly eyes," indigenous music, and baby lambs to "melt the hearts of the enemy," with uniforms carrying ginseng regulators and divining tools. Channon declined to lead a literal battalion; instead the manual circulated through the army and inspired concrete spin-offs — Colonel **John Alexander**'s non-lethal weapons (Sticky Foam, acoustic weapons), Dr. Jim Hardt's brainwave "Zen master" machine, and the psychic-soldier programs championed by [[Albert Stubblebine III]]. Ronson argues its ideas were "assimilated into the fabric of the army" and later, far from Channon's pacifist intent, repurposed in War on Terror psyops.
