## Identity and Nomenclature

Bigelow Aerospace Advanced Space Studies (BAASS) was founded by aerospace entrepreneur Robert Bigelow to serve as the primary research and investigative contractor for AAWSAP. The $22 million Congressional appropriation flowed through the DIA to BAASS, which conducted fieldwork (including investigations at Skinwalker Ranch), case analysis, and scientific research on behalf of the program.

## Primary Textual Appearances

Referenced in Chapter 1 in the context of AAWSAP's funding and contractor structure. Bigelow's role as BAASS founder and his relationship with Hal Puthoff are described as central to AAWSAP's operational capability.
