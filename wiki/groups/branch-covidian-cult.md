The **"Branch Covidian Cult"** is the pejorative coined in chapter 10 of *[[Faucian Bargain (The Most Powerful and Dangerous Bureaucrat in American History)]]* for the segment of the public the authors say "turn[ed] COVID-19 into the apocalyptic event it never was," with [[Anthony Fauci]] as its "high priest." (The name puns on the Branch Davidians.)

## Description in the Source

Deace and Erzen argue this group satisfies seven traits of a cult, marking each "Check":
1. **Discourages critical thinking** — refusing dissenting data and seeking to de-platform skeptics.
2. **Isolates members** — lockdowns and indefinite masking (citing an Ohio State poll that 72% planned to keep masking even after COVID was "no longer a threat").
3. **Claims special knowledge** — treating "novel coronavirus" as wholly unprecedented while ignoring crossover/T-cell immunity research.
4. **Demands loyalty to the leader** — "In Fauci We Trust" merchandise, *SNL* and celebrity canonization.
5. **Detaches families** — Fauci's advice against Thanksgiving/Christmas gatherings.
6. **Crosses moral boundaries** — e.g., endorsing online hookups while restricting church communion.
7. **Separates from the church** — declining in-person worship attendance.

The chapter pairs this diagnosis with seven "antidote" steps (encourage critical thinking, re-engage the world, rely on established science, prioritize truth, reunite families, practice civil disobedience against "COVID fascism," and trust "faith, family, and freedom").

> Strict-extraction note: this is the source's polemical characterization, catalogued as the book's claim.
