## Identity and Nomenclature (from the Source)

In *Everything You Need to Know But Have Never Been Told* (2018), NATO is referenced primarily in the context of Operation Gladio — described as a post-WWII terrorist network created by "NATO and the CIA" based in Italy and expanding across Europe. The claimed public justification (preparation for Warsaw Pact invasion) is dismissed by Icke as a cover story; the real purpose, as described by operative Vincenzo Vinciguerra's testimony, was civilian terrorism to generate demand for greater state security powers.

NATO is also referenced in the context of ongoing involvement in Afghanistan: "The US and NATO are still in Afghanistan and asking for more troops with the excuse of the new buzz-term – 'stabilisation'."

Chapter 14 (Saying the Unsayable) on Operation Gladio. Chapter 10 (Advancing the Spell) on Afghanistan.
