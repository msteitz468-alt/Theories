## Overview from Source

The Gallery of Magick is the collective and publisher behind *Success Magick* and [[Damon Brand]]'s
other works. The source presents it as a group of magicians who develop and test practical magical
systems, sharing "what we confidently believe to be true about magick" and letting readers prove the
results to themselves. For *Success Magick* the group ran an unusually deliberate testing process —
distributing early drafts to volunteers (beginners, skeptics, and experienced occultists) to verify
that the simplified Enochian methods could be communicated and used successfully through a book. The
group's house style favors stripped-down, results-oriented ritual with minimal ceremony.
