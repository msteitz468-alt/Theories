## Identity and Nomenclature

Cooper asserts that the JASON Society — also called the JASON Scholars or the Order of the Quest — is "one of the highest degrees above the Skull & Bones and the Scroll & Key in the Illuminati." He insists the name is always spelled in capital letters when referring to the secret society; lower-case letters are never used.

Cooper is careful to distinguish the JASON Society from a separate entity he calls the JASON Group (administered publicly by the Mitre Corporation). He writes: "The JASON Group is a scientific organization formed and hired by the JASON Society and the U.S. Government for obvious reasons."

## Name and Symbolism

Cooper states that the JASON Society takes its name from the mythological story of Jason and the Golden Fleece, and that it is a branch of the Order of the Quest. He explains: "The golden fleece takes on the role of truth to JASON members. Jason represents the search for the truth. Therefore the name JASON Society denotes a group of men who are engaged in a search for the truth." Cooper also notes that the name may carry a deeper meaning, with Jason representing man searching for himself (the Golden Fleece).

## Role in the Power Structure

Cooper claims that "only members of the Order are initiated into the Order of the Quest, the JASON Society that makes up the executive members of the Council on Foreign Relations and, in fact, the Trilateral Commission as well." He writes that the inner core of the Council on Foreign Relations recruits its members from Skull & Bones and Scroll & Key, and that a "chosen few were later initiated into the secret branch of the Order of the Quest known as the JASON Society."

## The JASON Group (Scientific Body)

Cooper describes the publicly known JASON Group — distinct from the secret JASON Society — as founded by members of the Manhattan Project and made up mostly of theoretical physicists. He states:

- As of 1987, the membership included four Nobel Prize winners.
- Members are paid a $500-per-day consultant's fee.
- They hold "the highest and most restrictive security clearances in the nation."
- They are given the protocol rank of rear admiral (two stars) when visiting ships or military bases.
- JASON is administered by the Mitre Corporation.
- Members do not list JASON membership on their official resumes.
- The group guided decisions on Star Wars, submarine warfare, and predictions about the greenhouse effect.
- In the Naval Intelligence documents Cooper claims to have read, the JASONS predicted that the greenhouse effect would lead ultimately to an ice age, not warming.

Cooper states that the JASON Group was responsible for designing the electronic barrier between North and South Vietnam. He adds that the group's budget is allocated through the Defense Department as a Top Secret Army item, so the name JASON does not appear as a line item.

## Commissioned by Eisenhower

Cooper claims that "Top Secret documents that I read while with Naval Intelligence stated that President Eisenhower had commissioned the JASON Society to examine all of the evidence, facts, lies, and deception and find the truth of the alien question." He further states that the JASON Society confirmed the findings of the scientists regarding alien contact.

## Membership in Majesty Twelve

Cooper states that Majesty Twelve included "six men from the executive committee of the JASON Group." He also writes that the Wise Men (key CFR members) who served on Majesty Twelve "are all members of the Council on Foreign Relations and also members of a secret Order of the Quest known as the JASON Society."

## Primary Textual Appearances

The JASON Society is discussed extensively in Chapter Two ("Secret Societies and the New World Order") and Chapter Twelve ("The Secret Government") of Cooper's book. Cooper cites Top Secret Naval Intelligence documents as his source for claims about the society's role in the alien investigation.
