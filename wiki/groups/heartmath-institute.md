## Overview from Source

The HeartMath Institute (HMI) is a research organization based in Boulder Creek, California, that
[[Joe Dispenza]] names in *Becoming Supernatural* as his research partner for measuring **heart
coherence**. Per the source, HMI "helped us measure the responses of thousands of our participants,"
recording heart-rate variability (HRV) to determine when a student is "opening their heart" and
sustaining elevated emotions, producing a heart rhythm that is coherent ("rhythm, order, and
balance"). Dispenza draws on HMI's research for his claims that the heart generates a measurable
magnetic field carrying information, and for the **Global Coherence Initiative** behind
[[Project Coherence (collective meditation, Dispenza)]]. The wiki records HMI only as Dispenza
presents it.
