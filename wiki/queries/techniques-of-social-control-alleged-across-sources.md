## Question

Across the ingested sources, what specific named **techniques of mass social control** are
alleged — and which books advance each? This synthesizes a thread that runs through many otherwise
unrelated books but is not captured by any single comparison page. Per the wiki's contradiction
protocol, every technique is attributed to the source(s) that assert it and is **not adjudicated**
as fact.

## Answer: the recurring techniques

### 1. Manufactured crisis → pre-planned "solution" (the dialectic family)
The most cross-cutting technique. Several authors describe deliberately *engineering a problem* so a
frightened public will accept a pre-planned response.
- **Icke** names it "Problem-Reaction-Solution": [[Claim: Icke's Problem-Reaction-Solution technique — Illuminati create crises to justify pre-planned power centralisation]]
  and [[Problem-Reaction-Solution Is the Primary Elite Manipulation Technique]].
- **Sutton** frames the same idea as the Hegelian dialectic of *managed conflict* —
  [[The Order manipulates society through the Hegelian dialectic of managed conflict (Sutton)]] —
  documented concretely as both-sides financing in [[Wall Street financed both the Bolsheviks and their White opponents]].
- **Bramley** gives the ancient version: [[The Custodians breed conflict between humans through the Machiavellian third-party technique]].
- **Marrs** and **Coleman** apply it to history: [[The hidden network engineered the 20th century's wars and economic crises (Marrs)]]
  and [[The Club of Rome engineered the 1973 oil embargo to spread its anti-growth doctrine]].

### 2. Pressure from above + pressure from below
- **Allen**: [[The conspirators utilize a pincer strategy of pressure from above (government regulation) and pressure from below (street mobs) to force the socialization of America]].

### 3. Mass psychology / brainwashing institutions
- **Coleman**: [[The Tavistock Institute is the Committee of 300's mass psychological-warfare arm]] and
  [[The Club of Rome runs mass brainwashing and social engineering through the Tavistock Institute]].
- **Icke**: media as a perception-control system — [[Mainstream Media Is a CIA and Intelligence Perception Control System]].

### 4. Covert mind control and drugging
- **Icke**: [[MKUltra and Project Monarch as Elite Mind Control Networks]].
- **Lee & Shlain** (*Acid Dreams*) and **O'Neill** (*Chaos*) document the CIA/LSD programs:
  [[LSD has a covert CIA history that converges with the 1960s counterculture]] and
  [[The CIA funded and protected a network of academic LSD researchers who tested it on captive subjects]].

### 5. Engineered fear / false-flag terror
- **Icke**: [[False-Flag Terrorism Is Conducted or Enabled by State Networks]].

### 6. Belief manipulation / "subliminal seduction"
- **Vallée** (*Messengers of Deception*) argues UFO belief is engineered as a control instrument —
  the [[The control system (Vallée's functioning lie)]] wielded by [[The Manipulators (Vallée)]],
  using absurdity itself as a tool ([[The absurd behavior of UFO entities keeps science away and gives the myth religious overtones]]).
- **Cattle mutilations** are read by Vallée as engineered *terror* messaging:
  [[Cattle mutilations are real surgical acts delivering a message of terror]].

## What the synthesis shows

Two observations emerge from laying these side by side:

1. **The "manufactured crisis" technique is the single most common claim**, appearing under
   different names (Problem-Reaction-Solution, the Hegelian dialectic, the Machiavellian
   third-party, "order out of chaos") across authors who otherwise share little — from a
   document-driven analyst (Sutton) to a reptilian theorist (Icke) to a UFO researcher (Vallée).
2. **The sources split on agency** exactly as the
   [[Is history steered by a deliberate cabal or an emergent network? Models compared]] page
   describes: document-anchored authors (Sutton) present these as *patterns/methods*, while the
   maximalists (Icke, Cooper, Coleman) present them as the deliberate toolkit of a unified controlling
   group.
