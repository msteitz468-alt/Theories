## Biographical Details from Source

American investigative journalist who, from 1999, reported on UFOs for mainstream outlets (Boston Globe, Providence Journal) and organized a 2007 Washington press conference of former high-ranking officials with filmmaker James Fox. In *UFOs: Generals, Pilots, and Government Officials Go on the Record* (2010), she compiles and contextualizes first-person testimony from credible military and government witnesses, advancing an evidence-based, deliberately agnostic case for taking the phenomenon seriously and investigating it officially.

## Primary Source Appearances

Author/editor/narrator throughout the book; she frames each witness chapter.
