## Biographical Details from Source

The pen name of Mark Booth, a long-time commissioning editor at a major London publisher, who wrote *The Secret History of the World* (2007) after "nearly twenty years' research." He describes being educated and "prepared for initiation" by an unnamed mentor — "clearly of a different order of being" — who had been initiated to the highest level of at least one secret society and helped him read Rosicrucian and esoteric sources. Booth says he ultimately *declined* formal initiation, partly to keep working things out for himself and partly to avoid an oath that "forbad me to write."

## Primary Source Appearances

First-person author/narrator throughout *The Secret History of the World*.
