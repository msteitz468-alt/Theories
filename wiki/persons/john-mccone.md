## Biographical Details from Source

Cooper mentions John McCone in a single passage within his discussion of the Knights of Malta and their penetration of key government positions. The source does not provide biographical background on McCone beyond the roles Cooper attributes to him.

## Knight of Malta and CIA Director

Cooper states: "Knight of Malta John McCone was President Kennedy's envoy and he was also the Director of the CIA during the early '60s." This passage appears in the context of Cooper's argument that U.S. Ambassadors and presidential envoys to the Vatican who are also Knights of Malta face an irreconcilable conflict of interest, since their oath to the Knights of Malta requires obedience to the Pope.

## Primary Textual Appearances

McCone appears only in this single passage in Chapter Two ("Secret Societies and the New World Order") of Cooper's book, in a list of individuals whose dual loyalties Cooper considers disqualifying for government service.
