## Biographical Details from Source

Kennedy's vice president, whom Talbot portrays as a humiliated, sidelined figure by late 1963 — facing the Bobby Baker scandal and possible removal from the 1964 ticket — with longstanding ties to national-security hard-liners including Dulles (who visited the LBJ Ranch in summer 1963). In Hunt's account LBJ "signed off" on the plot; Talbot argues Johnson may have been an accessory but that Dulles, who "outranked Johnson," was the mastermind, and disputes LBJ's later claim that RFK asked him to appoint Dulles to the Warren Commission.
