## Biographical Details from Source

A favored Dulles protégé and former Bern OSS/CIA officer whom the Soviets had reportedly blackmailed into becoming a double agent (via a "honey trap" exploiting his sexuality). On March 31, 1953, Dulles confronted him over dinner with two CIA security men listening nearby; Kronthal was found dead the next morning with an empty vial. Talbot presents it (per CIA officer Robert Crowley) as a possibly assisted suicide arranged "for the good of the agency."
