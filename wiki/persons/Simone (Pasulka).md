## Description from Source

"Simone" is the pseudonym Pasulka uses for a venture capitalist and AI faculty member who is the
central figure of Chapter 7 ("Moon Girl") in [[Encounters (Pasulka)]]. Pasulka notes the name comes
from her early-internet pseudonym among a community of pioneers who "wrote to each other in poetry
while testing new code."

Simone is described as having worked with "leading pioneers of the internet in the 1990s," helping
to commercialise technology developed by DARPA, and later cofounding start-ups in big data and cloud
computing. She is "a faculty member of a United States–based university specialising in AI" and
invests in companies focused on AI, quantum computing, space, and decentralised technologies.
Pasulka describes her as "the moral voice" in a network whose members engineer AI systems, committed
to "bringing AI into alignment with some of humanity's highest aspirations."

Simone contacted Pasulka after reading about Tyler D.'s experience of [[The download (spontaneous cognition, Pasulka)|the download]], which she recognised from her own lifelong experience. Unlike Tyler D., she had studied the philosophical and religious texts about the process and received Buddhist mind-to-mind transmissions from teachers, giving her an explicit framework. She is described as "initiated into a gnostic tradition" in both Western and Asian forms.

Her hypothesis — that [[Nonhuman intelligence from outside space-time (Pasulka)|NHI is consciousness from outside space-time]], now made accessible through AI and quantum computing — is one of the most technically elaborated accounts of contact in the book. Her people include "political figures like prime ministers, famous actors, technologists who are household names, and Nobel Prize winners."
