## Biographical Details from Source

Per [[Count Michael Maier (Craven)]], Joannes Diaz was a Spaniard who embraced Reformed views and was murdered by his own brother in 1546. His *Christianae Religionis Summa* — a Reformed summary of the Christian faith admitting the three creeds and four general councils but denying the Eucharistic Sacrifice — was first published in French at Lyons in 1562 and was included in the posthumous 1624 memorial collection of Michael Maier's works.
