Melbourne nuclear physicist hired by Australia's Directorate of Air Force Intelligence (DAFI) in 1954 to review UAP sightings; he concluded the RAAF evidence was 'suggestive of extra-terrestrial origin'. As head of the nuclear-science section of the Joint Intelligence Bureau's DSTI, his 1971 paper alleged a deliberate US ridicule policy and proposed an Australian rapid-intervention team. He also investigated sightings at Maralinga while serving as a health physics officer on the British nuclear tests.
