## Biographical Details from Source

Roger Stone is the author of the foreword to *Hidden History*. He describes himself there as a
"political insider" of forty years who "worked on a senior level in nine American presidential
campaigns, from Richard Nixon to Donald Trump," and a former top aide in the US House and Senate. He
praises Jeffries for providing an "alternate narrative for the most important events in our nation's
recent history," endorsing the book's conspiratorial reading of events from the JFK assassination
through the Clinton scandals and Obama. The source presents him as a sympathetic insider witness.
