## Biographical Details from Source

Swiss author whose *Chariots of the Gods?* (1968) launched the modern ancient-astronaut movement and became an international bestseller. Writing as a self-taught investigator (he thanks NASA personnel and Wernher von Braun in his introduction), von Däniken presents the thesis that prehistoric humanity was visited and shaped by extraterrestrials remembered as gods, and urges science to re-examine ancient texts and monuments with that possibility in mind. He later founded the Ancient Astronaut Society.

## Primary Source Appearances

Author/narrator throughout *Chariots of the Gods?*. (He is invoked admiringly in Paul Wallis's "Eden" books.)
