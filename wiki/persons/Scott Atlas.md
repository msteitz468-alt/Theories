## Biographical Details from Source

**Dr. Scott Atlas** is identified in *[[Faucian Bargain (The Most Powerful and Dangerous Bureaucrat in American History)]]* as a physician from **Stanford University** whom President Trump invited onto the White House Coronavirus Task Force "toward the end of his administration" to "reset the narrative" away from [[Anthony Fauci]]. The authors interviewed him for the book.

## Portrayal in the Source

Atlas is cited approvingly as a credentialed dissenter from lockdown and mask policy:
- He argued the original short-term shutdown was appropriate to "flatten the curve," but that prolonged lockdowns "got out of hand" and merely "delay the infection."
- He itemized lockdown harms (undiagnosed cancers, missed transplants and screenings, skipped chemotherapy and immunizations, UN starvation projections, unreported child abuse, tuberculosis deaths).
- On masks he said population mask mandates "do not stop cases... That is just super naïve, wrong, and just garbage science," likening them to "wearing a copper bracelet."

He supports [[Credentialed experts dissented from Fauci's lockdown approach]] and [[Mask mandates are not supported by real-world data]].
