## Biographical Details from Source

Hungarian-born nuclear physicist who co-invented the thermonuclear bomb and tested variants at numbered Nevada Test Site sectors near Area 51.

Met Bob Lazar at Los Alamos in June 1982 when Lazar showed him a Los Alamos Monitor page-1 story about Lazar's jet car. In 1988, Teller referred Lazar to EG&G for employment at Area 51.
