## Biographical Details from Source

Peter J. Carroll is the author of *Liber Null* (1978) and *Psychonaut* (1981), foundational texts of
[[Chaos Magic]]. Within the source he writes as a practicing magician transmitting a "tradition with
no past," presenting the books as a sourcebook "written originally as a sourcebook for a secretive
group of chaos magicians" now released "for those who wish to work alone or who wish to establish
working chaos magic groups."

The text itself supplies little biography (consistent with its anti-dogmatic stance); Carroll
appears as the systematizer who recasts techniques drawn from shamanism, the Golden Dawn,
[[Aleister Crowley]], and [[Austin Osman Spare]] into "a practical skill for the contemporary
world." (The work is historically associated with his co-founding of the magical order the
Illuminates of Thanateros, which the source gestures toward in references to "autonomous satraps of
the Illuminati.")
