# Dion Fortune (Violet Mary Firth, 1890–1946)

## Biographical Details from Source

In the Foreword and opening chapters of *The Mystical Qabalah*, the author identifies herself as the founder of the **Society of the Inner Light** (formerly the Fraternity of the Inner Light) and as a former member of the organisation founded by **MacGregor Mathers** — i.e. the [[Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn]] — which she joined after Mathers's death. She states she was never personally associated with **Aleister Crowley**, though she frequently cites both Mathers and Crowley as authorities on Qabalistic mysticism. She presents the Qabalah not as a historical or scholarly study but as the living theoretical basis of the practical training given to pupils in her fraternity — the "Yoga of the West."

## Primary Source Appearances

- Foreword: her editorial choices (following Mathers's transliteration table from *The Kabbalah Unveiled*; the traditional capitalisation of technical terms).
- Chapter I ("The Yoga of the West") and throughout: her framing of the Qabalah as the Western esoteric tradition's ground-plan.
