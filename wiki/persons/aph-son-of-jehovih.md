## Biographical Details from Source

Aph, "Son of Jehovih, God in the arc of Noe," is the etherean God who carries
out [[The Submersion of the Continent of Pan (the Flood)]] some 24,000 years before kosmon, after the earth's heavens had
filled with [[Drujas, Fetals, and the Bondage of Hells]]. At Jehovih's command he ringed the doomed continent
of [[Pan (Whaga, the submerged continent)]] with "a wall of pillars of fire" and a net "a thousand miles"
high, then "the vortex of the earth closed in... and the land sank down beneath
the water, to rise no more," delivering billions of bound spirits on
"birth-blankets." His companion is Nin'ya, Daughter of Jehovih. The cycle/arc of
Aph is one of the great dan'ha markers in the prophetic system ([[The Cycles of Dan and Dan'ha]]).
