## Biographical Details from Source

New York abstract painter who became the pioneering investigator of UFO abductions. From 1976 Hopkins investigated nineteen abduction cases (thirty-seven people), working with co-investigator Ted Bloecher and with hypnosis-practising psychologists (notably Dr. Aphrodite Clamar) to recover suppressed memories via regression. *Missing Time* (1981) was the first systematic study to define the "missing time" marker and the abduction pattern; Hopkins went on to become the central figure of the abduction-research movement. He drew on J. Allen Hynek's Center for UFO Studies and its HUMCAT catalogue.
