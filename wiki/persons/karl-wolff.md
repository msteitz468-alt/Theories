## Biographical Details from Source

Himmler's former chief of staff and SS/Gestapo commander in Italy, and Dulles's partner in Operation Sunrise. A former advertising man who ran Himmler's "circle of friends" of industrial donors, Wolff was, per Talbot, the "Bureaucrat of Death" — a 1942 letter thanked a transport official that a train left daily "for Treblinka with 5,000 members of the chosen people." Dulles rescued him from partisans in April 1945 and shielded him from Nuremberg; he received only a denazification sentence reduced to time served (free June 1949).
