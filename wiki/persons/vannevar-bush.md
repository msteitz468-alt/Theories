## Biographical Details from Source

Cooper mentions Vannevar Bush in a single passage within his account of the alien survivor recovered from the 1949 Roswell crash. The source does not provide substantive biographical information about Bush beyond this attribution.

## Coining the Term "EBE"

Cooper states that the alien survivor found wandering in the desert after the 1949 crash was given the name EBE, and that "The name had been suggested by Dr. Vannevar Bush and was short for Extraterrestrial Biological Entity." This is the only substantive claim Cooper makes about Vannevar Bush in the text.

Cooper explains that EBE "had a tendency to lie, and for over a year would give only the desired answer to questions asked," and that the information EBE eventually provided "became the foundation of what would later be called the 'Yellow Book.'" The alien later fell ill; medical personnel were unable to determine the cause, given that "EBE's system was chlorophyll-based and he processed food into energy much the same as plants." EBE died on June 2, 1952.

## Primary Textual Appearances

Vannevar Bush appears only in this single passage in Chapter Twelve ("The Secret Government") of Cooper's book, credited solely with suggesting the acronym EBE for the alien entity recovered after the Roswell crash.
