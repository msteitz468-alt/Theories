## Biographical Details from Source

Gregg Braden is the author of the foreword to *Becoming Supernatural*. He is identified in the book's
front matter as a *New York Times* best-selling author (works cited include *The Divine Matrix* and
*Human by Design*). In the foreword he endorses Dispenza's project of uniting science and mysticism
and the claim that ordinary people can access extraordinary human potential. The source supplies
little further biography.
