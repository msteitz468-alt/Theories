## Biographical Details from Source

Native Floridian; appeared on Las Vegas Eyewitness News with George Knapp in November 1989 at age thirty, breaking Area 51's secrecy oath publicly—the only individual among tens of thousands of workers to do so in that manner per Jacobsen.

Met Edward Teller at Los Alamos in June 1982. Was fired from Los Alamos, had financial problems, moved to Las Vegas, marriage to Carol Strong failed (she committed suicide by carbon monoxide two days after Lazar remarried Tracy Murk). Teller referred him to EG&G in 1988.
