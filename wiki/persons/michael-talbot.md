## Biographical Details from Source

Author of *The Holographic Universe* (1991) and earlier books on quantum physics for general readers. Talbot says he "grew up in a psychic family" and experienced paranormal phenomena firsthand from an early age, which he offers as openly anecdotal evidence. His project is to synthesize David Bohm's and Karl Pribram's work into a single "holographic paradigm" capable of accommodating the paranormal. (Not to be confused with David Talbot, author of *The Devil's Chessboard*.)

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator/author throughout *The Holographic Universe*.
