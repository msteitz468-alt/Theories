## Biographical Details from Source

Michael Barkun is an American political scientist (professor emeritus, Syracuse University) and a
leading scholar of millennialism, the radical right, and conspiracy movements; he has also consulted
for the FBI on these subjects. In *A Culture of Conspiracy* he writes as an analyst — describing and
categorizing the conspiracist subculture and its beliefs rather than endorsing or simply ridiculing
them — and builds explicitly on Richard Hofstadter's "paranoid style."
