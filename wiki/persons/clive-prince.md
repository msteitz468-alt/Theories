## Biographical Details from Source

Clive Prince is the co-author, with [[Lynn Picknett]], of *The Forbidden Universe* (2011) and their
earlier collaborations (*The Templar Revelation*, *The Masks of Christ*). The source presents the
research and authorship jointly ("we"), with Prince as co-developer of the thesis that the Hermetic
tradition was the hidden inspiration of the Scientific Revolution and remains relevant to modern
cosmology. The text supplies little independent biography beyond his shared authorship.
