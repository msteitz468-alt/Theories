Scottish minister of Aberfoyle (d.1692) who in 1691 wrote *The Secret Commonwealth of Elves, Fauns and Fairies*, the first systematic description of the fairy races. Vallée reproduces a 16-point summary of Kirk's findings (intermediate nature between man and angels; fluid bodies; ability to carry things off; underground dwellings with self-lit lamps; whistling speech; tribal organization) and argues it matches the modern ufonauts.
