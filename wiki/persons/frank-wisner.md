## Biographical Details from Source

A former Wall Street lawyer and OSS veteran whom Dulles installed as chief of the [[Office of Policy Coordination (CIA)]] in 1949. Under Wisner the OPC became a largely unsupervised covert-action arm running sabotage, subversion, and assassination, with many ex-Nazi recruits. He ran Operation Splinter Factor with Dulles and helped buy the 1948 Italian election. His career ended in 1962 amid worsening mental illness.
