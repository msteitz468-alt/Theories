## Biographical Details from Source

The author (a pen name) and first-person narrator of *The Gods of Eden*. He recounts that he began researching the origins of human warfare in 1979 — initially dismissive of UFOs — and was led to the thesis that Earth is owned by an extraterrestrial "Custodial" society that keeps humanity enslaved through perpetual war and spiritual repression. He presents himself as a grass-roots researcher building on Charles Fort and Zecharia Sitchin, and closes the book with his own speculative spiritual doctrine.

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator throughout *The Gods of Eden* (1990).
