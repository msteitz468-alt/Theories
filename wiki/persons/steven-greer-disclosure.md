Emergency physician who founded CSETI (1990) and the Disclosure Project (1992). Greer organised the April 1997 Pentagon briefing of Admiral Wilson and the 2001 National Press Club disclosure event. Coulthart credits his access but is openly sceptical of his more extreme claims (an electromagnetic 'death ray' from Utah, assassination plots) and notes Greer's habit of publicly naming officials who spoke with him.
