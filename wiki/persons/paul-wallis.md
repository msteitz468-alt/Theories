## Biographical Details from Source

Australian pastor and theologian (formerly of the "Church on the Range," Victoria, and an Anglican archdeacon), author of the "Eden series" — *Escaping from Eden* (2020), *The Scars of Eden* (2021), and *Echoes of Eden* (2022). Wallis presents himself as a long-serving preacher who, while recovering from a leg injury, re-studied Genesis and concluded that its Hebrew "anomalies" point to a plural *elohim* — a council of non-divine "Powerful Ones" / "Sky People." He frames the books as a "preacher's journey," openly an amateur in linguistics, archaeology, and genetics who "stands on the shoulders" of specialists, and as a believer who keeps faith in the God of Jesus while distinguishing that God from the engineering "elohim." In *Scars* he also discloses his own unexplained 1985 experiences (grey figures in Bath; a "Nordic" couple in Chichester).

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator/author throughout *Escaping from Eden* and *The Scars of Eden*.
