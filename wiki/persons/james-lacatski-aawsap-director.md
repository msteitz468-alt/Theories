## Biographical Details from Source

James "Jim" Lacatski is described by Elizondo as the director of AAWSAP — the predecessor program to AATIP, funded at $22 million through the Defense Intelligence Agency with the support of Senators Harry Reid, Ted Stevens, and Daniel Inouye. Lacatski was one of the first DoD officials Elizondo met when being recruited into the UAP program world in 2009.

Lacatski's intellectual contribution to AATIP's theological dimension is described in Chapter 5: he created a chart positioning UAP on a vertical axis between God/Angels at the top and Humans at the bottom, situating NHI as entities occupying the space between human and divine — a framework for thinking about the ontological status of non-human intelligence that he shared with the AATIP team.

Lacatski had extensive access to classified UAP material through his DIA role and was deeply involved in the SCIF-based briefings that formed the intellectual core of AATIP's early work, including sessions with Hal Puthoff.
