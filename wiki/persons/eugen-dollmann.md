## Biographical Details from Source

Wolff's principal intermediary in the Operation Sunrise talks; a cultured, gay SS colonel who served as the link between Hitler's and Mussolini's courts. After the war, [[James Jesus Angleton]] extracted him from prosecution and used him (briefly) as an asset; Talbot tracks his colorful, blackmail-prone postwar career across declassified CIA memos until the agency cut ties in 1955.
