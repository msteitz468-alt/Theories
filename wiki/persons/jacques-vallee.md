French-born astronomer and computer scientist, author of *Passport to Magonia* (1969) and a central figure in the shift of ufology toward the folklore / interdimensional interpretation of UFOs. In the book he combines scientific training, folklore scholarship and case files to argue that UFO encounters are the modern form of an ancient phenomenon, while carefully withholding any claim that the objects are physical or extraterrestrial.
