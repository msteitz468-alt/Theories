## Biographical Details from Source

**Todd Erzen** is the co-author of *[[Faucian Bargain (The Most Powerful and Dangerous Bureaucrat in American History)]]* (2021). The book's "About the Authors" note identifies him as co-host of *The Steve Deace Show* on **The Blaze** alongside [[Steve Deace]], states he lives in **Iowa** with his wife and children, notes that *Faucian Bargain* is "Erzen's first" book, and that he had a newspaper-reporting background before broadcasting.

## Contribution in the Source

- Co-author and co-narrator of the book; the text is written in a collective first person ("the authors of this book").
