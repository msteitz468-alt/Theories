## Biographical Details from Source

British journalist and author of *The Men Who Stare at Goats* (2004), written with first-hand access to the principals of the U.S. military's psychic programs. Ronson's method is wry, skeptical-but-curious first-person reportage; he foregrounds his own uncertainty and the difficulty of verifying his subjects' claims.

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator/author throughout *The Men Who Stare at Goats*.
