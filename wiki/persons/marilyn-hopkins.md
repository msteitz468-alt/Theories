## Biographical Details from Source

Marilyn Hopkins is the co-author, with [[Tim Wallace-Murphy]], of *Custodians of Truth* (2005) and
the earlier *Rex Deus* (2000). The source presents the research and authorship in the first-person
plural ("we"), with Hopkins as joint developer of the [[Rex Deus]] hypothesis and the historical
research underpinning the book. The text supplies little independent biographical detail about her
beyond her shared authorship.
