## Biographical Details from Source

Talbot presents Nixon as the Dulles circle's "political weapon" — from the 1948 Hiss case (coached at the Roosevelt Hotel) through his vice presidency, when he served as the administration's mediator with Joe McCarthy. Per the Loftus allegation, Dulles financed Nixon's first 1946 campaign in exchange for silence on Nazi money-laundering. As president, Nixon's "whole Bay of Pigs thing" remark to Haldeman is read by Talbot (via Haldeman) as code for the Kennedy assassination.
