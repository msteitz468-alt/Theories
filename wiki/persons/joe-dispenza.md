## Biographical Details from Source

Dr. Joe Dispenza is the author of *Becoming Supernatural* (2017) and earlier works the source names —
*You Are the Placebo* and *Evolve Your Brain*. Within the book he presents himself as a teacher who
runs multi-day "advanced workshops" worldwide, where he and assembled scientific teams measure
participants' brain activity (EEG), heart-rate variability, and "energy fields" during meditation. He
frames his mission as "bridging the world of science with the world of mysticism" and demonstrating
that "common people can do the uncommon" — heal themselves and access mystical states — while
acknowledging that mainstream skeptics "might call my work pseudoscience." He partners with the
HeartMath Institute for the heart-coherence research.
