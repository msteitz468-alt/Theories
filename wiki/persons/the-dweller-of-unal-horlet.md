## Biographical Details from Source

A being "from a cycle beyond us" — "never one with the children of men" — who appeared as Atlantis rose, bringing the "Key of Wisdom," dividing the land into ten kingdoms, and building (from the ether, by the power of "YTOLAN") a vast Temple on Unal. Formless yet appearing in the image of men, he chose Three messengers as his links to mankind, initiated Thoth, and — when adepts opened a "forbidden gateway" — descended to Amenti and used the Seven Lords' powers to sink Atlantis. He remains in trance ("AGWANTI") in the Halls beneath the Temple.
