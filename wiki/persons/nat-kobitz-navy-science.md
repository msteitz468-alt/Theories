Former Director of Science and Technology Development for the US Navy (retired 1994), interviewed by Coulthart in early 2020 shortly before his death in April 2020. Kobitz said he was 'read into' a program involving crashed UFOs, that the US had recovered alien spacecraft 'several times', and that he was shown an anomalous piece of metal at Wright-Patterson whose bond appeared integral/cast rather than welded. Coulthart treats him as a credible, named insider.
