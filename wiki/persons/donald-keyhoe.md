## Biographical Details from Source

A retired U.S. Marine Corps aviator turned journalist, commissioned by *True* magazine in 1949 to investigate the flying-saucer mystery. His January 1950 *True* article and this book made him the first prominent public advocate of the view that flying saucers are interplanetary craft and that the Air Force is hiding the fact. A pilot himself, he began "skeptical of flying disks" but was persuaded by the volume of competent pilot and officer reports and by the contradictions in the Air Force's "Project Saucer." (He would later found the civilian investigative group NICAP, whose board came to include former CIA director [[Roscoe Hillenkoetter (CIA/NICAP)|Roscoe Hillenkoetter]].)

## Primary Source Appearances

Author/narrator of *The Flying Saucers Are Real*. He is also a recurring figure in Richard Dolan's cover-up history and is cited by Hynek.
