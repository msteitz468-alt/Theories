## Biographical Details from Source

Griffin is the author of *The Creature from Jekyll Island: A Second Look at the Federal Reserve* (1994/1998). The text is written in the first person and advances the thesis that the Federal Reserve is a banking cartel with a government facade. Beyond his authorship, the book provides no autobiographical detail. He cites extensive correspondence and research (including private correspondence with Andrew L. Gray, grand-nephew of A. Piatt Andrew, regarding the Jekyll Island attendee list).
