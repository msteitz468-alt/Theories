## Biographical Details from Source

Victorian author of the two-volume *The Secret Societies of All Ages and Countries* (new edition 1897). Heckethorn presents himself as a comprehensive, evidence-minded surveyor of more than 160 secret organisations, sceptical of partisan mythologising. His repeated method is to set the "plain history" of a society against the embellished version its members or detractors promote, and he is openly dismissive of modern Freemasonry's claims to secret knowledge.

## Primary Source Appearances
