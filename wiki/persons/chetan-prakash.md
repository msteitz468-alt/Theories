## Biographical Details from Source

**Chetan Prakash** is the mathematician who supplied the formal proofs underpinning Donald Hoffman's argument in *[[The Case Against Reality (Why Evolution Hid the Truth from Our Eyes)]]*. The book identifies him only by his role; no biographical detail beyond his collaboration is given.

## Contribution in the Source

- Proved the **[[Fitness-Beats-Truth (FBT) Theorem]]**, which Hoffman conjectured: "Fitness drives Truth to extinction with probability at least (N–3)/(N–1)" (chapter 4).
- Proved the **[[Invention of Symmetry Theorem]]**, also conjectured by Hoffman: symmetries in perception entail nothing about the structure of objective reality (chapter 7).
- Co-author (with Hoffman and **Manish Singh**) of the 2015 paper "The Interface Theory of Perception," published in *Psychonomic Bulletin & Review*, the technical statement of [[Interface Theory of Perception (ITP)]].
- Acknowledged among the colleagues Hoffman thanks for insights and comments on drafts.
