**Biographical Details from Source**

Enki (Akkadian Ea) is a son of [[Anu (Sitchin)]] but not the legal firstborn — a grievance Sitchin foregrounds in Enki's rivalry with his half-brother [[Enlil (Sitchin)]]. In Sitchin's reconstructed chronology Enki **led the first landing party to Earth (~445,000 years ago) and founded Eridu**, the first Earth station ([[Eridu (first Earth settlement)]]), establishing sea routes to southern Africa and organising the gold-mining operations in the AB.ZU (the "Lower World" / mining domain). He is cast as the gods' chief scientist: it is Enki who, with the mother-goddess Ninhursag, **fashions man** by "binding the image of the gods" upon an existing creature ([[Modern man was genetically engineered by the Nefilim from Homo erectus about 300,000 years ago]]). In the Deluge narrative Enki, sympathetic to mankind, **breaks the gods' oath of secrecy and warns his faithful servant Ziusudra/Noah** to build the ark.

**Source-Specific Portrait — Bramley, *The Gods of Eden* (1990)**

Bramley adopts Sitchin's Ea/Enki as the genetic engineer of *Homo sapiens* but adds a distinct role: Ea was the founder of the original, benevolent [[The Brotherhood of the Snake]], the serpent that tried to give humanity spiritual knowledge in Eden. After his faction lost to rival Custodians, Bramley says, Ea was demonized (recast as Satan / "Prince of Darkness") and his Brotherhood corrupted into a tool of repression. See [[The Brotherhood of the Snake was founded by Ea then corrupted into a tool of spiritual repression]] and [[The Garden of Eden story symbolizes the Custodial enslavement of humanity]].
