**Biographical Details from Source**

Coleman calls Willis Harmon "the high priest of the COR" and bases Chapter 9 ("A Return to the Dark Ages") on a Harmon paper about changing the "industrial technological image of man." Coleman quotes Harmon:

> "We must change the industrial technological image of man — fast. Our analyses ... lead to the conclusion that the images of man that dominated the last two centuries will be inadequate for the postindustrial era."

Coleman interprets this as a program to deliberately **destroy industry** so that the population becomes "unmanageable" and must be "corralled and culled" — "a return to the Dark Ages, a New Dark Age, under a one World Government dictatorship." He associates Harmon with "the Luciferians ... the members of the Cult of Dionysus, the 'Olympians.'"
