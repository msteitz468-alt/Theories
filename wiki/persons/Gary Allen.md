## Biographical Details from Source

The source provides two distinct passages about Allen.

**Preface (front matter)**
The book's preface identifies Allen as "a California based free-lance journalist." It states that after "majoring in history at Stanford University and doing graduate work at California State College at Long Beach, he became aware through independent research that his college courses had been highly slanted" and that "many of the most important facts had been left out." The preface characterizes the book itself as the result of Allen's "personal post graduate studies" in finding out "who's who" in American politics.

**Back matter bio**
The source gives Allen's dates as August 2, 1936 – November 29, 1986. It states that as a student he majored in history at Stanford University and studied at California State University, Long Beach. It describes him as "a prominent member of the John Birch Society, of which he was a spokesman," and notes that he contributed to magazines including *Conservative Digest* and *American Opinion* since 1964. It also states he was "the speech writer for George Wallace during the Alabama Governor Presidential campaigns" and was "adviser to the conservative Texas millionaire Nelson Bunker Hunt."

**Works listed in the source**

- *Richard Nixon: The Man Behind the Mask* (1971)
- *None Dare Call It Conspiracy* (1972)

- *The Rockefeller File* (1976)
- *Tax Target, Washington* (1978)
- *Ted Kennedy: In Over His Head* (1981)
- *Say "No!" to the New World Order* (1987)
