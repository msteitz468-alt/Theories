## Biographical Details from Source

Cmdr. Dave Fravor was the commanding officer of VFA-41 (Black Aces) aboard the USS Nimitz in November 2004. Flying as FASTEAGLE 01 on November 14, 2004, Fravor was vectored by USS Princeton's Combat Information Center to investigate a radar contact that had been observed for more than two weeks. He visually acquired the Tic Tac object, maneuvered to intercept, and had a direct engagement before the object accelerated away at extreme speed.

Fravor's account is presented by Elizondo as one of the most credible UAP witness testimonies in the record: he is a decorated career Navy aviator with thousands of flight hours who described observing a craft that violated every known principle of aerodynamics.

Fravor later testified before Congress. In the May 2022 public UAP hearing he testified as the primary visual witness to the Tic Tac encounter. In the July 2023 hearing, he testified again alongside David Grusch and Lt. Ryan Graves, providing sworn testimony about the physical characteristics of the UAP.
