## Biographical Details from Source

Retired Major General of the Belgian Air Force. As Chief of the Operations Division of the Air Staff (then a colonel), De Brouwer was tasked by Belgium's defense minister with handling the 1989–90 Belgian UFO wave. A twenty-year fighter pilot who also served in NATO Strategic Planning and later as a UN logistics consultant, he contributes the central first-person account in Kean's book — emphasizing factual accuracy, the credibility of the witnesses, and the failure to identify the triangular craft.

## Primary Source Appearances
