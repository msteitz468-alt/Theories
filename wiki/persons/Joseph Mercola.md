## Biographical Details from Source

Osteopathic physician and natural-health writer, co-author (with Ronnie Cummins) of *The Truth About COVID-19* (2021). Within the book Mercola supplies the medical/health material — the lab-engineering and Event 201 arguments, the critique of mortality statistics and pharmaceutical responses, and the prescriptive "metabolic health," diet, and sunlight/vitamin-D advice for "taking back control." He authored an earlier book on the benefits of sunlight exposure.

## Primary Source Appearances

Co-author of *The Truth About COVID-19*; bylines several chapters.
