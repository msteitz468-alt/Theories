## Biographical Details from Source

Michael Shermer is a science writer, historian of science, and founder of the Skeptics Society and
*Skeptic* magazine. In *Why People Believe Weird Things* he writes as a professional skeptic and a
former evangelical Christian turned scientific rationalist, combining hands-on investigation of weird
claims (psychics, abductions, creationism debates, Holocaust deniers) with a psychological account of
belief formation. He presents skepticism not as cynicism but as a method, and is candid that he,
too, once held beliefs he now considers weird.
