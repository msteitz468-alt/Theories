## Biographical Details from Source

Robert Lanza is an American physician and stem-cell scientist (a pioneer of regenerative medicine) who, with astronomer Bob Berman, authored *Biocentrism* (2009). The book argues that biology, not physics, is the true "theory of everything": that **life and consciousness create the universe** rather than arising within it, and that the puzzles of quantum mechanics and cosmic fine-tuning dissolve once the **observer** is recognized as central. Lanza draws on his own scientific background (including early work with B. F. Skinner) to press a consciousness-first reframing of reality.
