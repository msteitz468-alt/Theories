## Biographical Details from Source

Historian and author of *UFOs and the National Security State* (Vol. 1, *Chronology of a Cover-up, 1941–1973*, 2002; Vol. 2, *The Cover-Up Exposed, 1973–1991*, 2009). Dolan approaches the UFO question as a documentary historian — building a chronological narrative from FOIA-released records, participant accounts, and scholarship — and frames it within the structures of power he calls the "national security state." He is careful to separate documented fact from inference and to avoid overclaiming about extraterrestrial origin or a present-day control group, though by Vol. 2 he argues the secret-keepers have grown "beyond nations." (Dolan is also cited as a contemporary witness/researcher in Paul Wallis's *The Scars of Eden*.)

## Primary Source Appearances

Author/narrator throughout *UFOs and the National Security State*, Vols. 1 and 2.
