## Biographical Details from Source

Kabalactes is the Triune of the **[[The Confederacy of the Holy Ghost (the Triunes)]]** who "by inspiration,
founded what is now called Buddhism," ruling the heavenly capital Horactu over
**[[Vind'yu (India)]]** (India). Oahspe distinguishes him sharply from the historical
sage [[Sakaya (the historical Buddha)]] (whom his followers wrongly called "Buddha" a thousand years
after death). "The terrible conflicts in the heavens of Kabalactes... were also
manifested on the mortals of Vind'yu." He "is finally cast into hell."
