## Biographical Details from Source

British author (writing in the 1920s) who specialized in the history of revolution as conspiracy, following Robison and Barruel. In *Secret Societies and Subversive Movements* (1924) — a sequel to her *World Revolution* — she presents herself as a documentary historian exposing a continuous "secret force of revolution," and explicitly folds an alleged Jewish role into that thesis. Her work is a notoriously antisemitic landmark of modern conspiracy literature and a primary source for later Illuminati/New-World-Order writers. (The wiki documents her claims with attribution and flags, without endorsing or rebutting them.)

## Primary Source Appearances

Author/narrator of *Secret Societies and Subversive Movements*.
