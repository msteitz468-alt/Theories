## Biographical Details from Source

Kenneth L. Feder is an American archaeologist and professor of anthropology, author of the standard
textbook *Frauds, Myths, and Mysteries: Science and Pseudoscience in Archaeology*. He writes as a
working archaeologist defending the discipline against pseudo-archaeological claims (ancient
astronauts, lost continents, fringe diffusionism), combining a debunker's wit with instruction in
archaeological method and the epistemology of how the past is actually known.
