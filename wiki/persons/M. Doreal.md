## Biographical Details from Source

The modern author who presents the work. In the preface and interpretive sections he writes in the first person as the man who, "having a connection with the Great White Lodge," was instructed to recover the tablets from a Maya temple in the Yucatán, return them to the Great Pyramid, and was permitted to translate them in 1925 and publish part. He supplies the framing history of Thoth, the physical description of the tablets, and an esoteric commentary urging the reader to "read between the lines."

## Primary Source Appearances

Preface and introductory interpretation; commentary framing all tablets.
