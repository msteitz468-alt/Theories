## Biographical Details from Source

According to *Stalking the Wild Pendulum*:
* **Background:** Itzhak Bentov (1923–1979) was a Czech-born Israeli-American inventor and researcher who specialized in bio-medical engineering. He holds numerous patents, including the invention of the steerable cardiovascular catheter.
* **Approach to Consciousness:** Bentov combined his engineering background with meditation and metaphysical inquiry to build a mechanical and physical model of consciousness. He aimed to demystify altered states, out-of-body experiences, and subjective time perception using standing waves, resonance, and holography.
* **Death:** Bentov was among the passengers who died in the crash of American Airlines Flight 191 in Chicago on May 25, 1979.
