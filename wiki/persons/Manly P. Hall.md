## Biographical Details from Source

Canadian-American writer and lecturer on esoteric and occult philosophy who, while still in his twenties, compiled *The Secret Teachings of All Ages* (1928) — the encyclopedic synthesis of Masonic, Hermetic, Qabbalistic, and Rosicrucian symbolic philosophy. Hall writes as a transmitter and defender of the Western esoteric tradition, presenting the world's myths, scriptures, and rituals as repositories of a single concealed wisdom. (He later founded the Philosophical Research Society in Los Angeles.)
