## Biographical Details from Source

Ervin Laszlo is a Hungarian-born systems philosopher and theorist of consciousness who developed the idea of the **Akashic field** (or "A-field") — a fundamental, cosmos-wide information field that records and conveys the experience of all things, recovering the ancient Sanskrit notion of *Akasha* in the language of contemporary physics and systems science. In *The Akashic Experience* (2009) he gathers first-person testimonies of "akashic experiences" (transpersonal, healing, intuitive, past-life) and argues they are evidence of an underlying in-formed, interconnected reality, part of an emerging post-materialist scientific paradigm.
