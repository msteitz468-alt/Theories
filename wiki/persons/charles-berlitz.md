## Biographical Details from Source

Best-selling author (known for *The Bermuda Triangle*) and co-author, with William L. Moore, of *The Roswell Incident* (1980). Berlitz lent his commercial profile and narrative framing to Moore's investigation of the 1947 crash.

## Primary Source Appearances

Co-author of *The Roswell Incident*.
