## Biographical Details from Source

Brian P. Copenhaver is a historian of philosophy and the Renaissance whose 1992 *Hermetica: The Greek
Corpus Hermeticum and the Latin Asclepius* (Cambridge University Press) is the standard scholarly English
translation used in this ingest. His introduction situates the treatises in Greco-Roman Egypt of the
first centuries AD, surveys the Thoth-Hermes syncretism and the modern interpretive debates
(Reitzenstein, Festugière), and his extensive notes form the bulk of the volume. His role here is
translator and editor; the doctrines extracted are those of the Hermetic texts, not his own views.
