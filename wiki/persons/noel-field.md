## Biographical Details from Source

Idealistic American Quaker, former State Department officer and wartime OSS asset with Communist sympathies, whom Dulles knew since meeting him as a boy in Zurich. In Operation Splinter Factor, Dulles fed Stalin the false story that Field ran a U.S. spy network in the Eastern bloc; Field, his wife, brother, and foster daughter were all imprisoned and tortured behind the Iron Curtain. Released in 1954, Noel and Herta chose to remain in Hungary.
