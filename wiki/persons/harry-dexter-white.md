## Biographical Details from Source

Griffin presents Harry Dexter White as the second guiding theoretician of the [[Event: Bretton Woods Conference (1944)]] and "the first Executive Director for the United States at the IMF." Per the source, "it was learned in later years that Harry Dexter White was a Member of a Communist espionage ring," making the two intellectual founders of Bretton Woods (with [[John Maynard Keynes]]) "a Fabian Socialist and a Communist, working together to bring about their mutual goal: world socialism."
