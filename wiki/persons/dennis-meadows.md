## Biographical Details from Source

Co-author of *The Limits to Growth* and leader of the original MIT team that built the World3 model for the Club of Rome. A former faculty member at Dartmouth's Tuck School of Business, he is cited (with the others) to rebut the "anti-market" charge. In the 2004 edition's concluding divergence, Dennis (with Randers) holds the **pessimistic** view that "a decline in the average quality of life is now inevitable," and that global population and economy will likely be forced to fall.

## Primary Source Appearances
