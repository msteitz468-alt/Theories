## Biographical Details from Source

Frances Yates is the historian whose scholarship underpins *The Forbidden Universe*. The source builds
on her work — above all *Giordano Bruno and the Hermetic Tradition* (1964) — and the so-called "Yates
thesis": that the Renaissance revival of [[Hermes Trismegistus|Hermeticism]] (following Marsilio
Ficino's translation of the *Corpus Hermeticum*) was a major driving force behind the intellectual
transformation that produced modern science. Picknett & Prince present themselves as extending Yates's
argument — both backward (to the genuine antiquity and Egyptian character of the Hermetic outlook) and
forward (to its relevance for present-day cosmology).
