US Navy aerospace engineer at the Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division who filed a series of exotic patents (2016–2019) assigned to the Navy: an inertial-mass-reduction 'hybrid craft', a room-temperature superconductor, a high-frequency gravitational-wave generator and a plasma compression fusion device. The Navy's Dr James Sheehy vouched to the Patent Office that several were 'operable'. Coulthart and the physicists he consulted remain deeply sceptical.
