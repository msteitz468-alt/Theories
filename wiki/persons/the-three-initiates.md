## Biographical Details from Source

"The Three Initiates" is the pseudonym under which *The Kybalion* (1908) was published. The book
gives no names: the authors describe themselves only as "humble students of the Teachings, who,
while bearing the title of Initiates, are still students at the feet of HERMES, the Master." They
present their role as transmitting and explaining an older oral Hermetic tradition rather than
originating doctrine: "The original maxims, axioms, and precepts of 'THE KYBALION' are printed
herein, in quotation marks… Our own work is printed in the regular way, in the body of the work."

The source itself offers no further identification, consistent with the Hermetic "principle of
secrecy" it describes. (The wiki records only what the text states; it does not adjudicate the common
external attribution to William Walker Atkinson, which the source does not make.)
