## Biographical Details from Source

The CIA chemist — "a slight man with short gray hair and a clubfoot" — who ran Operation MK-ULTRA from the Technical Services Staff. In *Acid Dreams* he is the architect of the CIA's LSD experimentation: he organized the in-house surprise-dosing program, approved giving LSD to unwitting citizens via [[George Hunter White]]'s safehouses, spiked the 1953 retreat that preceded [[Frank Olson]]'s death, developed poisons for assassination plots, and carried LSD overseas to dose foreign statesmen. He testified (under immunity, suffering a convenient "loss of memory") at the 1977 Senate MK-ULTRA hearings, conceding "the bottom line on this whole business has not yet been written."

## Primary Source Appearances

*Acid Dreams*, Part One (Midnight Climax, Enter LSD); the Prologue's account of the 1977 hearings. Also appears in [[The Devil's Chessboard (Talbot)]]'s MKULTRA chapter.
