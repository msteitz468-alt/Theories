## Biographical Details from Source

Described as a septuagenarian nuclear-physicist-turned-ufologist, often called America's leading UFO expert by Larry King and others. Began Roswell research in 1978 with Bill Moore; interviewed sixty-two original witnesses by 1980.

## Primary Source Appearances

- **Chapter 1**: Rates Roswell's importance low before 1978; disputes Bob Lazar as "total fraud" over MIT credentials; arranged lunch with Lazar in 1990 that Lazar did not attend.
