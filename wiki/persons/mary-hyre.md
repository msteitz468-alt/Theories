## Biographical Details from Source

The veteran Point Pleasant–area stringer for the Athens, Ohio *Messenger* and Keel's chief local collaborator and clearinghouse for sightings ("if you wanted to find out anything about the area… ask Mary Hyre"). Her office became a focal point for witnesses, reporters, and the strange visitors Keel documents — including the dark-complexioned "twin" Men in Black and the odd "Jack Brown," who repeatedly asked what she would do if ordered to stop printing UFO stories ("I'd tell them to go to hell"). The book is dedicated "To Mary Hyre and the people of West Virginia."

## Primary Source Appearances

Recurs throughout, especially in the Men in Black episodes and the Silver Bridge aftermath.
