## Biographical Details from Source

Texas journalist and author (1943–2017) of *The Illuminati: The Secret Society That Hijacked the World* (2017), as well as *Crossfire*, *Rule by Secrecy*, and *Our Occulted History*. In this book he is the narrator and synthesizer, arguing that the defunct Bavarian Illuminati gave way to a living doctrine of "Illuminism" rooted ultimately in the Sumerian Anunnaki. He is comparatively careful for the genre — quoting skeptics, warning against antisemitic readings, and debunking the Pike "three world wars" hoax.

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator throughout *The Illuminati* (2017).

In *Rule by Secrecy* (2000) he advances the broad-canvas version of the same thesis, tracing a connective thread from the Trilateral Commission/CFR/Fed back through the Freemasons and Templars to the Mystery schools and the Sumerian Anunnaki.
