## Biographical Details from Source

CIA/Army biochemist who worked on biological warfare and MKULTRA-related research and grew morally troubled after witnessing brutal interrogations in Germany. Secretly dosed with LSD at a CIA retreat in November 1953, he fell to his death from a New York hotel window a week later. A 1994 second autopsy found injuries "suggestive of homicide." Talbot holds he was murdered as a security risk.
