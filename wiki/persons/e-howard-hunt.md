## Biographical Details from Source

CIA officer involved in the Guatemala coup and the anti-Castro campaign, later leader of the Watergate "plumbers." Per Talbot, near death (2003–2007) Hunt told his son Saint John Hunt of a 1963 CIA plot to kill Kennedy ("the big event"), naming William Harvey and David Morales while loyally omitting Dulles. He helped ghostwrite Dulles's *The Craft of Intelligence*. He left video/audio confessions, the memoir *American Spy*; his son published *Bond of Secrecy*.
