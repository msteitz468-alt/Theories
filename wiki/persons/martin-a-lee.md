## Biographical Details from Source

Co-author (with Bruce Shlain) and lead investigative voice of *Acid Dreams: The Complete Social History of LSD* (1985). The authors based the book on roughly 20,000 pages of declassified CIA, FBI, and military documents reviewed after a 1977 FOIA release, describing themselves as "archeologists rather than muckrakers" excavating "a lost history buried underneath layers of secrecy." Lee advances the dual-history thesis linking the CIA's mind-control programs to the 1960s counterculture.

## Primary Source Appearances

Narrator/author throughout *Acid Dreams*.
