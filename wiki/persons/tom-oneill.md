## Biographical Details from Source

Journalist and author of *Chaos: Charles Manson, the CIA, and the Secret History of the Sixties* (2019, with Dan Piepenbring), the product of a roughly 20-year investigation that began as a 1999 magazine assignment for *Premiere*. O'Neill interviewed "more than a thousand people," faced threats and a threatened libel suit from Vincent Bugliosi, and describes his own fear of becoming "an obsessive, a conspiracy theorist, a lunatic." His stance is deliberately negative and self-limiting: he aims to disprove the official story, not to assert a new one.
