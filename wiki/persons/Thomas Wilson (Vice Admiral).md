Career US Navy intelligence officer who, as Deputy Director of the Defence Intelligence Agency, took the April 1997 Pentagon meeting with Steven Greer and Edgar Mitchell. The disclosure activists say Wilson admitted he was blocked from a contractor-run UAP program ('you don't have a need to know'); the 'Admiral Wilson Memo' attributes the same account to a 2002 meeting with Eric Davis. Wilson has categorically denied the 2002 meeting ever happened and called the memo 'pure fiction'.
