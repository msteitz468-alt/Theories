## Biographical Details from Source

Allen Dulles's wife of decades, who underwent long Jungian analysis (with Jolande Jacobi) and left intimate journals quoted extensively by Talbot. She called her husband "The Shark"; her diaries record the emotional emptiness of the marriage and Dulles's "drive for absolute control" as "the only passion that truly gripped" him. She befriended his mistress [[Mary Bancroft]].
