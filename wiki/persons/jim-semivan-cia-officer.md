## Biographical Details from Source

Jim Semivan is a retired CIA operations officer with 25 years of service whom Elizondo meets in Chapter 17 while considering his resignation from the DoD. Semivan played a key role in connecting Elizondo to Tom DeLonge and the concept of taking UAP disclosure directly to the public via To The Stars Academy. Semivan's view, which influenced Elizondo's decision, was that the institutional path within the DoD was closed and that public engagement was the only viable route.

Semivan is quoted in the appendix to *Imminent*: "There is a nonhuman intelligence that is living with us on this planet. . . . We are not alone, and we never have been." This represents one of the most direct public statements about NHI from a career CIA official.

Semivan co-founded To The Stars Academy (TTSA) with Tom DeLonge, Hal Puthoff, Steve Justice, and others.
