## Biographical Details from Source

Damon Brand is the author of *Success Magick* (2020) and a member of [[The Gallery of Magick]], a
collective of practising magicians. Within the source he presents himself as a long-time practitioner
who has "spent a lifetime studying and exploring success" and seeks to make the notoriously complex
[[Enochian Magick (Gallery of Magick approach)|Enochian magic]] usable by ordinary people. He writes
that the methods were tested with Gallery of Magick volunteers and beta readers to confirm they work
for beginners and skeptics alike. The text gives no further biographical detail (the Gallery of
Magick authors write semi-anonymously).
