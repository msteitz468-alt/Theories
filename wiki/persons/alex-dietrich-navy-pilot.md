## Biographical Details from Source

Lt. Cmdr. Alex Dietrich flew as FASTEAGLE 02 (callsign Noodle) during the November 14, 2004 USS Nimitz Tic Tac encounter. She was wingman to Cmdr. Dave Fravor (FASTEAGLE 01). Dietrich observed the encounter from a different vantage point than Fravor, providing an independent visual witness account. Her testimony is treated by Elizondo as corroborating Fravor's account from a separate angle of observation.

Dietrich was one of the first Navy pilots to speak publicly on record about the Tic Tac encounter, granting a 2021 CBS News interview that helped establish the story's credibility with mainstream audiences.
