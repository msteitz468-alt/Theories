## Biographical Details from Source

Grandson of Theodore Roosevelt; the Groton- and Harvard-bred CIA officer who ran the 1953 coup against Mossadegh on the ground, hiring mercenaries and bribing officers. He later joined Gulf Oil and became a consultant representing the Shah. Talbot quotes him saying Dulles "would give his… index finger if he could go somewhere in the field and engineer a coup d'état himself."
