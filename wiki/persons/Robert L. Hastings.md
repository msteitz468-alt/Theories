## Biographical Details from Source

American researcher who has devoted decades to documenting UFO activity at nuclear weapons sites. Hastings traces his interest to 1967, when as a teenager — the son of an Air Force officer stationed at Malmstrom AFB, Montana — he witnessed UFOs being tracked on radar at the base air-traffic control tower. Since the 1970s he has interviewed more than 150 former and retired U.S. military personnel about UFO incursions at missile fields and weapons-storage facilities, compiling their testimony in *UFOs and Nukes* (2008; expanded 2017). In September 2010 he organized a press conference at the National Press Club in Washington, D.C., at which seven veterans publicly described UFO encounters at nuclear sites.

## Primary Source Appearances

Author, narrator, and lead interviewer throughout *UFOs and Nukes*.
