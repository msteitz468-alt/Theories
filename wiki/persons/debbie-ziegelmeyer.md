**Biographical Details from Source**

Debbie Ziegelmeyer is [[Chuck Zukowski]]'s sister and his principal collaborator in *The 37th Parallel*. Based in Missouri and connected through MUFON, she maintains her own UFO research office and corroborates Chuck's work — supplying coordinates for mutilation cases (e.g. Norwood and Piedmont, Missouri) and helping confirm that incidents she and Chuck investigate repeatedly fall along the 37th parallel. She co-authored the Roswell-artifact press materials with Chuck ("My sister and I have found the only artifact from the famous Mac Brazel debris site...") and pushed the NIDS-style methodology of trying to correlate sightings with military-base locations.

**Primary Source Appearances**

The Roswell artifact chapters and the climactic telephone exchanges (Chs. 27–28) in which the 37th-parallel pattern is assembled.
