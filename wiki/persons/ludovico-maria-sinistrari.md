Italian Franciscan theologian (1622–1701), councillor to the Supreme Tribunal of the Inquisition, author of the manuscript *De Daemonialitate, et Incubis, et Succubis*. Vallée uses Sinistrari's careful argument that incubi and succubi are a distinct, embodied aerial species — not the devil, since they ignore exorcism and relics — as a 17th-century parallel to the genetic / sexual dimension of modern UFO contact.
