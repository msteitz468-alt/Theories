Senior radar operator aboard the USS *Princeton* who coordinated air defence for the *Nimitz* carrier group during the November 2004 Tic Tac encounter. He tracked clusters of objects descending from above 80,000 feet, told Coulthart some objects 'came from orbit', and said the radio comms recording was found wiped the next morning.
