## Biographical Details from Source

Iran's democratically elected prime minister who nationalized the Anglo-Iranian Oil Company and was overthrown in the August 1953 CIA/MI6 coup. Talbot portrays him as an aristocratic, Western-educated nationalist — "another Gandhi… not a Mao" — falsely cast by Dulles as a Communist "stooge." After the coup he was tried for treason and confined; he died under house arrest in 1967, buried beneath his sitting room near a picture of Gandhi.
