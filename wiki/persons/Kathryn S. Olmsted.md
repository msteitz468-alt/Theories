## Biographical Details from Source

Kathryn S. Olmsted is an American historian (University of California, Davis) specializing in U.S.
political history, intelligence, government secrecy, and conspiracy theories. In *Real Enemies* she
writes as a historian documenting *actual* government plots, lies, and cover-ups across the twentieth
century, and analyzing how that record of proven official deception nourished American conspiracy
theories and eroded democratic trust.
