## Biographical Details from Source

Author of *The Journey of Robert Monroe* (2007), the authorized biography of [[Robert Monroe (1915–1995)]]. Russell writes as a sympathetic but measured biographer, drawing on Bayard Stockton's earlier *Catapult* (1989), Monroe's taped reminiscences, and Institute materials. He foregrounds the tension in Monroe's work between raw experience and after-the-fact rationalization.
