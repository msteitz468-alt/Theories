Senior Canadian government radio engineer who, in a November 1950 memo, recorded that US scientist Dr Robert Sarbacher told him flying saucers were 'the most highly classified subject in the United States government, rating higher even than the H-bomb', and that a small group under Dr Vannevar Bush was studying them.
