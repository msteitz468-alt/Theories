## Biographical Details from Source

Eric Davis is an astrophysicist described by Elizondo as a key figure in the classified UAP research community with connections to both AATIP and the Legacy Program contractors. He is identified as a former AATIP member and, at the time of the memoir's events, an employee of the Aerospace Corporation — one of the named Legacy Program contractors.

Davis is primarily significant to the source through the Wilson/Davis memo: a document memorializing a 2002 meeting in which Davis interviewed Admiral Thomas Wilson (then-Deputy Director of the DIA) about Wilson's failed attempt to access a contractor-held UAP Special Access Program. Davis recorded Wilson's account that he had located the program in DoD records, requested access, and been denied by the contractors themselves.

Davis is quoted in the appendix to *Imminent*: "We couldn't make it ourselves. Off-world vehicles not made on this earth."

The Wilson/Davis memo was entered into the Congressional Record in May 2022.
