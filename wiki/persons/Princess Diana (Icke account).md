**Biographical Details from Source**

Icke presents Diana, Princess of Wales, as a member of the **Spencer bloodline** — itself, in his account, an "Elite bloodline" of the [[Babylonian Brotherhood (Icke)]] — who was selected to play the role of the goddess **Diana** and led to a ritual death. He notes Earl Spencer's eulogy emphasis that his sister was "named after the ancient goddess of hunting," which Icke treats as confirmation of the symbolic design. Per Icke, "Diana's birth to the bloodline family the Spencers in 1961 could have led to her being chosen as a symbol of the goddess Diana who would be led to a ritual death," paralleling his claim that JFK was groomed for a ritual "Killing of the King."

He claims Diana privately said of the Windsors "They're not human," and relays [[Arizona Wilder]]'s testimony of a 1981 ritual ("The Awakening of the Bride") at Clarence House at which Diana was allegedly shown the Windsors shape-shift into reptiles and warned she would be killed if she revealed the truth.
