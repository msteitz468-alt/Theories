## Biographical Details from Source

Organic-food and consumer activist, co-author (with Joseph Mercola) of *The Truth About COVID-19* (2021). Cummins supplies the political-economy framing — the "global coup d'état" / "digital dictatorship" / Great Reset narrative, and the regenerative-food and "participatory democracy" alternative. Notably, he is more cautious than his co-author about the "plandemic" idea, writing that the pandemic was "long anticipated" rather than "necessarily planned," and that the engineered virus was "apparently accidentally… released."

## Primary Source Appearances
