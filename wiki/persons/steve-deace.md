## Biographical Details from Source

**Steve Deace** is the lead author of *[[Faucian Bargain (The Most Powerful and Dangerous Bureaucrat in American History)]]* (2021). The book's "About the Authors" note identifies him as host of *The Steve Deace Show*, broadcast each weekday for **The Blaze**, with co-host [[Todd Erzen]]. It states Deace lives in **Iowa** with his wife and children, that *Faucian Bargain* is "his seventh book he's either written or co-authored," and that he had a background in newspaper reporting before moving into broadcasting.

## Contribution in the Source

- Co-author and first-person narrator (with Erzen) of the book's argument that COVID-19 lockdown policy, personified by [[Anthony Fauci]], was an unconstitutional and catastrophic overreach by an unaccountable administrative state.
- The book is written from an explicitly stated **"biblical worldview"** emphasizing limited government, individual liberty, and the founders' fear of concentrated bureaucratic power.
- Reproduces an April 2020 essay Deace published on the show's Facebook page warning that prolonged lockdowns would devastate small business.
