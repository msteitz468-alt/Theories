## Biographical Details from Source

Scottish scientist, co-founder of the Club of Rome (with [[Aurelio Peccei]]) and its president at the time of writing. Lead co-author, with [[Bertrand Schneider]], of *The First Global Revolution* (1991), the Club's programmatic report. In it he advances the "world problematique"/"resolutique" framework and the call for improved global governance.

## Primary Source Appearances

Co-author/narrator of *The First Global Revolution*.
