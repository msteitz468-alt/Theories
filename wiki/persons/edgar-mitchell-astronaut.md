Sixth man to walk on the Moon (Apollo 14, 1971) and later a proponent of UAP research who sat on the science advisory board of [[National Institute for Discovery Science (NIDS)]]. Coulthart's source 'the Spaceman' says Mitchell privately confided he filmed anomalous blue lights on Apollo 14 and answered 'Treason' when asked why astronauts stayed silent. Mitchell's estate is the provenance of the 'Admiral Wilson Memo'. He died February 2016.
