## Biographical Details from Source

British UFO researcher and author of *Above Top Secret* (1987). Good built his case from documents obtained under the U.S. Freedom of Information Act and from official correspondence and contacts in defence and intelligence circles, assembling the first comprehensive international account of government UFO secrecy. He conducted a 1986 U.S. research trip investigating the Majestic 12 documents, corresponded with senior figures including Admiral of the Fleet Lord Hill-Norton, and is measured in approach — accepting that most sightings are mundane and that a genuine UFO need not be extraterrestrial.
