## Biographical Details from Source

Founder (1967) of the Haight-Ashbury Free Medical Clinic and a pharmacology researcher who studied amphetamines and LSD. In *Chaos*, "Dr. Dave" treated Manson's Family at the clinic, wrote about them in his 1971 memoir *Love Needs Care*, and developed the concept of a "psychedelic syndrome" (group LSD use reinforcing a shared worldview). O'Neill found Smith's rodent research — dosing crowded mice/rats with amphetamines, and (after first denying it) with LSD "to make them suggestible" — paralleled the Family's formation and echoed Calhoun's "behavioral sink." Smith gave Jolly West an office at the clinic. O'Neill notes Smith grew evasive and irritable when pressed on the gaps in his research ("delete the whole thing from your book").
