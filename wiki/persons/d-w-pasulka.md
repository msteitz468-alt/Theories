## Biographical Details from Source

D. W. (Diana Walsh) Pasulka is a professor of religious studies at the University of North Carolina,
Wilmington, whose research spans Catholic history to modern reports of UAPs/UFOs. She is the author of
*Encounters* (2023) and the earlier *American Cosmic: UFOs, Religion, Technology* (2019) and *Heaven
Can Wait*. In *Encounters* she writes as a participant-observer who studies experiencers of contact
with nonhuman intelligence using the witness-centered field method she credits to [[Jacques Vallée]],
and who treats UFO contact as a religious/anthropological phenomenon. She notes that one scientist she
had earlier anonymized as "Tyler D." corresponds to a milieu including the now-public
[[Garry Nolan (Stanford immunologist)]].
