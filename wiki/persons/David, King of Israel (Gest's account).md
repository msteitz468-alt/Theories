## Biographical Details from Source

In *[[The Secrets of Solomon's Temple (Gest)]]*, David is presented as Solomon's father who commenced
the design of the Temple, and — per David Rohl's revised chronology, which Gest cites approvingly — a
contemporary of the heretical Egyptian pharaoh Akhenaten rather than the conventionally later dating.
Gest also connects the Shechinah tradition associated with David/Solomon's reign to a forty-year Venus
pentagram cycle aligned with the Temple's dawn orientation.
