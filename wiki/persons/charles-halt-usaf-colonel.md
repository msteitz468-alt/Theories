Deputy commander of RAF Bentwaters who led a patrol into Rendlesham Forest in December 1980 and audiotaped his commentary of a luminous object that 'danced about' and shone beams into the nuclear weapons storage area. Interviewed by Coulthart in 2009, Halt remained adamant the object was intelligently controlled and probably non-human, and that there was an official cover-up.
