Knew Roswell disc from Stalin per Jacobsen; testified on Air Force UFO secrecy. In Timothy Good's *Above Top Secret*, Hillenkoetter — first CIA director (1947–50) and a NICAP board member — is documented for his public statement that "through official secrecy and ridicule, many citizens are led to believe the unknown flying objects are nonsense," and is named among the alleged members of Majestic 12.
