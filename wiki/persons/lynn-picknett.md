## Biographical Details from Source

Lynn Picknett is the co-author, with [[Clive Prince]], of *The Forbidden Universe* (2011). The source
identifies the pair as authors of earlier works including *The Templar Revelation* (1997) and *The
Masks of Christ* (2008) — which advance their "John the Baptist as the true Christ" heresy thesis —
and notes Picknett's solo *The Secret History of Lucifer* (2006) as a precursor to this book's theme
of "forbidden paths to both mystical and scientific enlightenment." In *The Forbidden Universe* she
and Prince write in the first-person plural, arguing for the Hermetic inspiration of modern science
and against the "scientism" of Dawkins and Hitchens.
