## Biographical Details from Source

A blunt, hard-drinking, pistol-carrying ex-FBI man who became a CIA legend (the Berlin spy tunnel, exposing Kim Philby). Put in charge of the ZR-RIFLE program to assassinate Castro (working with Mafia figure Johnny Rosselli), he was banished to Rome in 1963 after clashing with Robert Kennedy. In Hunt's deathbed account he is named as the operational "quarterback" of "the big event." CIA colleague F. Mark Wyatt suspected Harvey was in Dallas in November 1963.
