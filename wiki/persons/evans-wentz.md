Scholar whose 1909 study *The Fairy-Faith in Celtic Countries* gathered first-hand Celtic accounts of the Gentry, fairy food, fairy rings and abductions. Vallée quotes Evans-Wentz extensively to establish the detailed correspondence between the fairy-faith and modern UFO reports.
