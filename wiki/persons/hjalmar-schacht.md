## Biographical Details from Source

According to Antony Sutton's research:
* **Wall Street Origins:** Hjalmar Horace Greeley Schacht spoke fluent English and had deep Wall Street roots. His father had worked in New York for the Berlin office of the Morgan-controlled Equitable Trust Company. Schacht's middle names ("Horace Greeley") were chosen to honor the prominent American Democratic politician. Throughout his career, Schacht maintained close connections to Wall Street financial elites, including Owen D. Young (General Electric) and Standard Oil executives.
* **Reparations Architect:** Schacht was a key German organizer for the Dawes Plan (1924) and Young Plan (1928), working closely with American J.P. Morgan delegations to establish the Bank for International Settlements (B.I.S.). According to Schacht, he personally gave Owen D. Young the idea that eventually became the post-WWII International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (World Bank).
* **Funding the Nazi Takeover:** As Reichsbank president and Hitler's Minister of Economics, Schacht served as the primary link between the German corporate cartels and the Nazi Party. In 1933, Schacht established the "National Trusteeship" account (*Nationale Treuhand*) at the Delbrück, Schickler Bank, which served as the central depository for election campaign contributions from General Electric (A.E.G.), I.G. Farben, and other cartels to Rudolf Hess, bankrolling Hitler's final consolidation of power.
* **Occult and Intelligence Links:** Schacht had associations with early Nazi occult circles, including members of the Thule Society, and was a key advisor to the Keppler Circle.
