## Biographical Details from Source

UFO researcher and co-author (with Charles Berlitz) of *The Roswell Incident* (1980), the investigation — conducted substantially with nuclear physicist Stanton T. Friedman — that recovered and assembled the witness testimony establishing the modern Roswell narrative. Moore is the investigative engine of the book, tracing participants such as Major Jesse Marcel, Lydia Sleppy, and Johnny McBoyle.

## Primary Source Appearances

Co-author/narrator of *The Roswell Incident*.
