Former Director of [[Lockheed Skunk Works]] (died 1995) who repeatedly dropped remarks suggesting a major undisclosed technological breakthrough — 'we have things out in the desert that are 50 years beyond what you can comprehend' and 'we now have the technology to take ET home'. Coulthart notes these may equally have been disinformation.
