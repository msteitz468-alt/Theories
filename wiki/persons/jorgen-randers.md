## Biographical Details from Source

Co-author of *The Limits to Growth* updates and, the book notes, president of the Norwegian School of Management for eight years (cited to rebut the "anti-market" charge). In the 2004 edition's concluding divergence, Randers (with Dennis Meadows) takes the **pessimistic** view that a decline in average quality of life is now inevitable.

## Primary Source Appearances
