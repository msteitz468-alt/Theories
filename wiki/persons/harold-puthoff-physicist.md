## Biographical Details from Source

Harold "Hal" Puthoff is described throughout *Imminent* as one of the most credentialed and connected figures in the classified UAP research world. He holds a PhD in physics and conducted foundational work in quantum vacuum and zero-point energy research. He was a key scientist in the CIA's Project Stargate remote viewing program.

Puthoff served as the scientific anchor for AAWSAP, commissioning 38 Defense Intelligence Reference Documents (DIRDs) from academic researchers. He was the scientific architect of Elizondo's understanding of UAP physics. Key contributions described in *Imminent*:

- Told Elizondo in classified SCIF briefings that the Roswell 1947 crash was real and that four nonhuman bodies were recovered (Chapter 4)
- Developed an EMP theory for the Roswell crash mechanism — US military radar interfered with the craft's electromagnetic propulsion (Chapter 9)
- Presented a unified warp bubble theory in a classified SCIF briefing that explained all Six UAP Observables simultaneously (Chapter 16)
- Proposed water/deuterium as the likely UAP fuel source
- Identified bismuth-magnesium layered meta-material as a candidate structural component of a warp bubble generator
- Published an academic paper on ultraterrestrial models (cited in appendix)

Puthoff co-founded To The Stars Academy (TTSA) with Tom DeLonge and eventually departed TTSA as the organization encountered financial difficulties.
