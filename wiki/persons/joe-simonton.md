Sixty-year-old Wisconsin chicken farmer who, on 18 April 1961 at Eagle River, said he gave water to occupants of a landed saucer and received three pancakes in return; Air Force analysis (via J. Allen Hynek) found them an ordinary terrestrial buckwheat pancake. Vallée makes the case the centerpiece of his fairy-food parallel.
