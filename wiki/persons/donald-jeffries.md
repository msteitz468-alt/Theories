## Biographical Details from Source

Donald Jeffries is the author of *Hidden History* (2014). Within the source he writes as an
independent researcher and self-described conspiracy author who rejects the "court historians" and
the mainstream media's account of modern American events, presenting an "alternate narrative" of
assassinations, scandals, and cover-ups from 1963 onward. The book's foreword (by [[Roger Stone]])
characterizes his work as "meticulously researched and sourced." The text supplies little further
biography.
