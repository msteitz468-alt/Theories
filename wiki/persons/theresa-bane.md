## Biographical Details from Source

Theresa Bane (b. 1969) is the author/compiler of the *Encyclopedia of Demons in World Religions and
Cultures* (2012). She describes herself as a "professional vampirologist—a mythologist who specializes
in cross-cultural vampire studies," and the author of an earlier *Encyclopedia of Vampire Mythology*
(2010). In the preface she states her goal is "to contribute to the academic studies in the fields of
anthropology, folklore, mythology, and religion," explaining that her vampire research repeatedly
surfaced demonic entities, and "a little bit of research turned into a great deal of research, and a
book of demonology began to write itself."
