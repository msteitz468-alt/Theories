## Biographical Details from Source

Sandra Kynes is the author/compiler of *Llewellyn's Complete Book of Correspondences* (2013) and other
Llewellyn titles she cites, including *Change at Hand* (a method of energy work based on the four
elements, combining palmistry and reflexology). She writes from a **Celtic Pagan** background and
practice, describes correspondences as "fundamental to how I view the world" ("there are connections
everywhere; nothing exists in isolation"), and recounts using them discreetly for "office spellwork"
during years in the corporate world. She compiled the book from her own findings plus the sources in
its bibliography, aiming at a practical "middle ground" for general Pagans and Wiccans.
