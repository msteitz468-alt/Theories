## Biographical Details from Source

A CIA officer from a poor Mexican American family in Phoenix, described by colleagues as the agency's "go-to guy" for "things that were repugnant to a lot of people." In Hunt's account Morales attended the Miami meeting that pitched "the big event" and worked under Harvey on ZR-RIFLE. Talbot reports Morales told his attorney Robert Walton in 1973, "We took care of that son of a bitch, didn't we?"
