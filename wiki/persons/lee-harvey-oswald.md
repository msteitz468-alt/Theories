## Biographical Details from Source

The man charged with killing President Kennedy, shot dead by Jack Ruby two days later. Talbot argues Oswald was a likely-innocent intelligence "patsy" who behaved like a trained operative ("I'm just a patsy!"), pointing to forensic inconsistencies, his attempted call to a Raleigh intelligence contact, and a suppressed CIA photo-analysis finding of conspiracy.
