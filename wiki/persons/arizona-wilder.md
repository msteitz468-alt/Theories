**Biographical Details from Source**

Arizona Wilder (whom Icke says was formerly named Jennifer Ann Greene) is presented as a **deprogrammed mind-control survivor** who claims to have been trained from childhood to be one of only "three Mother Goddesses in the world," officiating at Satanic rituals "at the highest levels of the Brotherhood all over the world." Icke says he received her testimony "on audio and video tape" via a friend who was deprogramming her, and that her descriptions of secret underground facilities were independently verified.

Her central claims as relayed by Icke:
- The reptilians "cannot hold human form in this dimension" without human blood, and **shape-shift into reptiles** during rituals.
- She personally saw named figures — the Queen, the Queen Mother, Prince Philip, Lyndon Johnson, Al Gore, Earl Spencer — at sacrifice rituals, some shape-shifting.
- She officiated at rituals at **Glamis Castle** and **Balmoral**, and described the "Awakening of the Bride" ritual involving Diana in July 1981.
- She claimed **Guy de Rothschild** (under the pseudonym "Dr Barrington") said he was at the Pont de l'Alma tunnel to "steal Diana's soul," and that Catherine de Medici was a "previous incarnation of the Queen Mother."
