## Biographical Details from Source

Lead author of *The Limits to Growth* (1972) and its updates, and a systems scientist trained in the MIT system-dynamics tradition. In the 2004 edition's concluding divergence, Donella is the **optimist**: she "believed all her life that humanity will develop the insights, institutions, and ethics it needs to achieve an attractive, sustainable society." The book notes she had died before its publication (she died in 2001); her co-authors completed the update.

## Primary Source Appearances

Co-author/narrator of *The Limits to Growth* and *Beyond the Limits*.
