## Biographical Details from Source

Aleister Crowley appears in *Liber Null & Psychonaut* as a major influence on the modern magical
tradition that [[Chaos Magic]] draws upon. Carroll (and Hutton's foreword) credit Crowley with
having "played a pioneering role in popularizing tantric and Taoist magical techniques in Western
magic," and identify his order — "the Crowleyan Argentum Astrum" (A∴A∴) — alongside the Golden Dawn
and Spare's Zos Kia Cultus as a source of the practical techniques chaos magic distils.

Carroll also adopts and adapts Crowley's well-known definition of magic — "Magic is the science and
art of causing change to occur in conformity with will" — as a working basis in *Liber MMM*. The
source treats Crowley as a source of *technique*, not of dogma to be followed.
