## Biographical Details from Source

Tim Wallace-Murphy is the lead author of *Custodians of Truth* (and the earlier *Rex Deus*, 2000),
writing with co-author [[Marilyn Hopkins]]. Within the source he appears as a researcher and lecturer
on Rosslyn Chapel and esoteric symbolism who, in 1994, was approached after a talk by an Englishman
("Michael") claiming descent from Hugues de Payen and membership in the [[Rex Deus]] families. From
those conversations Wallace-Murphy constructed and tested the "Rex Deus hypothesis" against the
historical record. The source presents him as the investigator who, over years of research (the
Pyramid Texts, Dead Sea Scrolls, Nag Hammadi scrolls, and medieval history), developed the bloodline
thesis the book sets out.
