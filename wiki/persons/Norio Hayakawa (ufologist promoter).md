## Biographical Details from Source

Japanese American mortician in Los Angeles who heard Lazar on KVEG radio in early 1990. Contacted Japanese magazine Mu and Nippon TV; arranged Lazar interview and filmed orange light near Groom Lake. Nippon TV program reached thirty million Japanese viewers in prime time, introducing Japan to Area 51 per Hayakawa's account.
