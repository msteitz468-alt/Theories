## Biographical Details from Source

Psychiatrist (1924–1999) whom O'Neill calls his "white whale." A former Air Force colonel who deprogrammed Korean War POWs, West was named by the New York Times as one of seven scientists who secretly participated in MKULTRA under academic cover. In *Chaos* he is documented running a fake "hippie crash pad" (the Haight-Ashbury Project) out of David Smith's clinic in 1967, studying LSD and hypnosis; his Oklahoma project "Mass Conversion" was funded by [[Sidney Gottlieb]]. West examined **Jack Ruby** alone in his cell before Ruby's incoherent Warren Commission testimony, and argued people could be hypnotized into killing against their morals with no memory. He denied MKULTRA ties until his death (an assisted suicide) in 1999; O'Neill was the first to examine his donated UCLA papers.
