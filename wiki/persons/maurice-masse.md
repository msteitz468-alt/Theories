Valensole (France) lavender farmer who on 1 July 1965 reported encountering two small beings beside a landed egg-shaped craft; one pointed a tube at him and he was paralyzed yet conscious for the encounter, afterwards suffering weeks of compulsive drowsiness. Vallée treats it as a model 'close-proximity' case (paralysis, benign impression, after-effects).
