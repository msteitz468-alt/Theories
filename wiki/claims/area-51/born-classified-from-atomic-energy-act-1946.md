## Claim Statement

The concept "born classified" came from the Atomic Energy Act of 1946, under which a civilian Atomic Energy Commission maintained secrets outside presidential executive order systems.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "It is from the Atomic Energy Act of 1946 that the concept 'born classified' came to be..."

Donald Woodbridge (AEC classifications officer) quoted: born classified "give[s] the professional classificationist unanswerable authority."
