## Claim Statement

The U.S. government has never admitted that Area 51 exists.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "The U.S. government has never admitted it exists." (Prologue)

Jacobsen places this alongside descriptions of Area 51 as the nation's most secret domestic military facility at Groom Lake, 75 miles north of Las Vegas.

## Contexts Within the Source

Framed as foundational to the book's subject—secrets, black operations, and classification. Contrasted with public fascination and later disclosure efforts by individuals such as Bob Lazar.
