## Claim Statement

Hall presents the Qabbalah as "the secret doctrine of Israel": an emanationist cosmology in which all things proceed from **AIN SOPH** — the Incomprehensible, unconditioned Absolute "discovered only through the process of eliminating, in order, all its cognizable attributes" — and ultimately return to it.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He writes that "that which remains — when every knowable thing has been removed — is AIN SOPH, the eternal state of Being," which "permeates all space" yet is "without substance, essence, or intelligence." His image: AIN SOPH is "a great field of rich earth out of which rises a myriad of plants" — "universes, gods, and man" — all rooted in "one definitionless essence" and "doomed… to return to the black ground — AIN SOPH, the only Immortal — whence they came." It is "The Most Ancient of all the Ancients," sexless, its symbol "a closed eye," of which "to define It is to defile It." From AIN SOPH the Qabbalists derive the emanations (the Sephiroth / Tree of Life).

## Contexts Within the Source

The Hebrew expression of the one secret doctrine ([[A single secret doctrine has been preserved by the Mystery schools across all ages]]); its descent of all being from a hidden Absolute parallels the descent-into-matter motif Hall shares with Jonathan Black ([[History is a descent of spirit into matter]]).
