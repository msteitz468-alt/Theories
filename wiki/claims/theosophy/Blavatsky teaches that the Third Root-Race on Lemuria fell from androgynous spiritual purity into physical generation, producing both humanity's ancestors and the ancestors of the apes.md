> **Sensitivity note.** This claim involves Blavatsky's racial hierarchy and attributions of "sin" to
> specific proto-human populations. Recorded descriptively, not endorsed.

## Claim Statement

Blavatsky asserts that the Third Root-Race — the first fully physical humanity, inhabiting the lost
continent of Lemuria — began as androgynous, egg-born giants and then separated into male and female
beings. A significant portion of the "mindless" (un-ensouled) sub-races subsequently mated with
animal-forms, producing monsters that became the ancestors of modern anthropoid apes. The spiritually
advanced sub-races, having received the divine spark from the Manasaputras, became the forebears of
higher humanity. Lemuria was eventually destroyed by volcanic fires and subsidence.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Stanza VIII (Vol. II) states directly: "And those which had no spark took huge she-animals unto them.
They begat upon them dumb races. Dumb they were themselves. But their tongues untied. The tongues of
their progeny remained still. Monsters they bred. A race of crooked, red-hair-covered monsters going
on all fours. A dumb race, to keep the shame untold." The commentary explains: "The ancestor of the
present anthropoid animal, the ape, is the direct production of the yet mindless Man, who desecrated
his human dignity by putting himself physically on the level of an animal."

On the separation of sexes: "The animals separated the first. They began to breed. The two-fold man
separated also. He said: 'Let us as they; let us unite and make creatures.' They did."

On androgynous origins: "The early Third Race, then, is formed from drops of 'sweat,' which, after
many a transformation, grow into human bodies" — and previously from egg-like structures. "First
male-female, then man and woman."

On the destruction of Lemuria: "The sinking and transformation of Lemuria beginning nearly at the
Arctic Circle (Norway), the Third Race ended its career in Lanka... The small remnant now known as
Ceylon is the Northern highland of ancient Lanka."

On the "Sons of Will and Yoga": "The Third Race became the Vahan (vehicle) of the Lords of Wisdom.
It created 'Sons of Will and Yoga,' by Kriyasakti it created them, the Holy Fathers, Ancestors of
the Arhats."

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Blavatsky acknowledges the difficulty of the claim that "the sin of the mindless men" produced the
apes, noting that "science will deny" any cross-breeding between human and animal species. She
concedes the physiological differences between present humans and apes, but argues these existed
*because* conditions were so radically different in the Third Race epoch, 18 million years ago, that
"neither the human Atlantean giants, nor yet the 'animals,' were the physiologically perfect men and
mammalians that are now known to us."
