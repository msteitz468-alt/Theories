## Claim Statement

Blavatsky asserts that the mythologies, cosmogonies, and religious symbols of every ancient civilisation
— Hindu, Egyptian, Chaldean, Hebrew, Greek, Zoroastrian, Norse, Aztec, and Chinese — are not
independent inventions, nor (as Max Müller contended) mere "diseases of language." They are exoteric,
outward "veils" concealing a single primordial wisdom-religion. The inner doctrine they all encode is
identical, and the evidence is the universal recurrence of the same symbols: the serpent, the cross,
the lotus, the zodiac, solar and lunar imagery, sacred numbers, and geometrical figures. When decoded
with the esoteric key of occult science, these symbols reveal one original cosmological and spiritual
teaching that predates all existing religions.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

*The Secret Doctrine*, Vol. I, Book I, Part II (*Evolution of Symbolism*), lines ~3902–5476 of the
raw text:

- **On the universal symbol-language:** "Recent discoveries made by great mathematicians and
  Kabalists thus prove, beyond a shadow of doubt, that every theology, from the earliest and oldest
  down to the latest, has sprung not only from a common source of abstract beliefs, but from one
  universal esoteric, or 'Mystery' language." (§ I, line ~4020)

- **On one original religion:** "There was, during the youth of mankind, one language, one knowledge,
  one universal religion, when there were no churches, no creeds or sects, but when every man was a
  priest unto himself." (§ III / "One Tree of Knowledge", line ~4315)

- **On the esoteric key:** "Occult Science — having survived even the great Flood... still holds the
  Key to all the world problems. Let us apply that Key to the rare fragments of long-forgotten
  cosmogonies and try by their scattered parts to reestablish the once Universal Cosmogony of Secret
  Doctrine. The Key fits them all."

- **On design, not coincidence:** "No one can study ancient philosophies seriously without perceiving
  that the striking similitude of conception between all — in their exoteric form very often, in
  their hidden spirit invariably — is the result of no mere coincidence, but of a concurrent design."

- **On mythological narratives:** Blavatsky states that she "is one of those who feel convinced that
  no mythological story, no traditional event in the folk-lore of a people has ever been, at any time,
  pure fiction, but that every one of such narratives has an actual, historical lining to it." (§ I)

The cross-civilisational reach of her argument is illustrated by the parallelism she traces between
Chaos-Theos-Kosmos across Hindu (Brahmâ/Vishnu/Shiva), Egyptian (Kneph/Osiris/Isis), Greek
(Æther/Chaos/Kosmos), Chaldean, Hebrew (Elohim/Jahveh), and Aztec (Popol-Vuh) traditions, all
read as exoteric encodings of the same triad: Absolute Spirit, its emanative Logos, and manifested
Matter.

## Cross-source connections

- **[[The Secret Teachings of All Ages (Hall)]]** — Hall's entire project of encyclopaedic
  comparative symbolism is a direct application of Blavatsky's claim: that ancient symbols across
  cultures encode a single philosophical teaching. Hall cites Blavatsky as a foundational source.

- **[[Hermes Trismegistus]]** — Blavatsky identifies the Hermetic corpus as one of the clearest
  surviving fragments of the universal wisdom-religion. The Aryan, Hermetic, Orphic, and Pythagorean
  cosmological doctrines are "all based upon one irrefutable formula."

- **[[The Forbidden Universe (Picknett & Prince)]]** — traces the same *prisca theologia* tradition
  from Ficino and Bruno through the Hermetic revival into modern esotericism; Blavatsky is a key node
  in this lineage.

- **[[Where did civilization and advanced knowledge come from? Models compared across sources]]** —
  Blavatsky's "one original wisdom-religion" is the theosophical model in that comparative synthesis;
  contrasts with the ancient-astronaut model (external seeding) and the independent-invention model.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Blavatsky acknowledges that existing religious scriptures survive only in fragmentary, corrupted, or
deliberately obscured form — the "rare fragments of long-forgotten cosmogonies." She does not claim
that any single existing tradition has preserved the doctrine intact; the Secret Doctrine itself is
her reconstruction from those fragments, guided by access to the Mahatmas and to texts (especially
the *Stanzas of Dzyan*) she claims are not in the public domain. This makes the claim non-falsifiable
on its own terms within the source.
