> **Sensitivity note.** The claim involves racial characterizations of Atlantean "sub-races." These
> are recorded as Blavatsky's assertions, not endorsed as fact or as racial categorization.

## Claim Statement

Blavatsky asserts that Atlantis — the continent of the Fourth Root-Race in the mid-Atlantic region —
was home to a civilization of physically powerful, psychically gifted giants who inherited occult
knowledge from the Third Race. The dominant Atlantean sub-races gradually turned this knowledge to
sorcery and material self-worship ("Black Magic"), built giant idols to the human body, and bred
monsters. As punishment by karmic law and geological necessity, the continent was destroyed in stages
by deluges, the major submergence occurring approximately 850,000 years ago, with a final remnant
(Plato's island-Atlantis) sinking around 12,000 years ago. Only the spiritually pure were saved.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Stanza X (Anthropogenesis): "Then the Fourth became tall with pride. 'We are the kings,' it was said;
'We are the gods.' They took wives fair to look upon. Wives from the mindless, the narrow-headed.
They bred monsters... They built temples for the human body. Male and female they worshipped. Then
the third eye acted no longer."

Stanza XI: "They built huge cities. Of rare earths and metals they built... they cut their own images
in their size and likeness, and worshipped them... Inner fires had destroyed the land of their fathers.
The water threatened the Fourth."

Stanza XI, verse 45: "The first great waters came. They swallowed the seven great islands."

Commentary: "The secret teachings show that the 'Deluge' overtook the Fourth, giant Race, not on
account of their depravity, or because they had become 'black with sin,' but simply because such is
the fate of every continent, which — like everything else under our Sun — is born, lives, becomes
decrepit, and dies."

On sorcery: "With the masses they degenerated into Sorcery, taking later on the shape of exoteric
religions, of idolatry full of superstitions, and man-, or hero-worship." And from the commentary:
"Those children of Heaven and Earth were endowed at their birth... with extraordinary faculties both
moral and physical. They commanded the Elements, knew the secrets of heaven and the earth... All
these beings are marked with a character of MAGIC and SORCERY."

On the saved elect: "Alone the handful of those Elect, whose divine instructors had gone to inhabit
that Sacred Island — 'from whence the last Saviour will come' — now kept mankind from becoming
one-half the exterminator of the other... Like the Lemurians — alone the ungodly Atlanteans perished."

On Asuramaya: Blavatsky describes the legendary Atlantean astronomer Asuramaya as having "calculated
and recorded all the astronomical and cosmic cycles to come," with Brahmanical chronological
computations derived from his records. This is the scientific legacy of Atlantis preserved in
Indian tradition.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Blavatsky herself notes the problem of evidence: the great deluges would have buried and dissolved
skeletal and material remains over hundreds of thousands of years, meaning archaeology cannot be
expected to find confirmation. She also takes pains to distinguish the Atlantean fall as partly a
natural geological process rather than simple divine punishment, calling it the operation of
"karmic law and natural law" together rather than moral retribution alone.
