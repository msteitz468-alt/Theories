## Claim Statement

Coulthart reports that after the November 1986 JAL flight 1628 sighting near Anchorage, FAA division chief John Callahan said CIA agents confiscated all the radar evidence at a Washington meeting and ordered that the meeting be denied — but that Callahan kept copies of the radar, voice and paper data.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Quotes the CIA's stated rationale that revealing 'a UFO' would panic the public ('So therefore you can't talk about it'), and notes Callahan's account 'has never been challenged by any official'.

## Contexts Within the Source

Used as an explicit, on-record account of official suppression of UAP sensor evidence.
