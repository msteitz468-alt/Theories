## Claim Statement

Kean's policy conclusion: the United States should end its institutional neglect of UFOs and establish a small, serious government agency to collect and scientifically investigate reports — as France and other nations have done — and should declassify its files.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

She points to France's official **GEPAN/SEPRA/GEIPAN** (run within the national space agency CNES) as a working model, and to the COMETA Report's authors — retired French generals and officials — as proof that serious people in government take the issue seriously. The foreword by **John Podesta** (former White House Chief of Staff) presses the same case, calling for government transparency and declassification. Kean frames U.S. inaction as an abdication of responsibility over an unexplained aviation-safety and national-security matter, rather than (as cover-up theorists argue) a deliberate conspiracy.
