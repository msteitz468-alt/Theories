## Claim Statement

Former Air Force Lt Bob Jacobs told Coulthart that telescopic film he directed in 1964 of a dummy nuclear warhead launched from Vandenberg showed an object circle the warhead at high speed and fire four beams of light, after which the warhead tumbled and fell.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Jacobs says his CO Major Mansmann ordered him never to repeat that it was a UFO and later confirmed the account in signed letters Jacobs provided to Coulthart, asserting the object was an extra-terrestrial craft using 'directed energy'. Coulthart notes the viciousness of sceptics (Klass, Oberg) toward Jacobs.

## Contexts Within the Source

Presented as a firsthand, corroborated military case suggesting coordinated suppression of UAP-military interactions.
