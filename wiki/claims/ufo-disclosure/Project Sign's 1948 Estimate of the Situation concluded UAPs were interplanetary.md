## Claim Statement

Air Force officer Edward Ruppelt, later head of Project Blue Book, said he saw a classified 1948 Project Sign report — the 'Estimate of the Situation' — that concluded the best explanation for the flying-saucer sightings was that the craft were interplanetary.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Coulthart quotes Ruppelt: 'The situation was the UFOs; the estimate was that they were interplanetary.' He notes any such finding was absent from the heavily redacted version released in 1987.

## Contexts Within the Source

Presented as an early instance of the gap between private assessments and the public debunking posture (Sign → Grudge → Blue Book).
