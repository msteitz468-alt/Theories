**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that a 1957 symposium confirmed the planet would self-destruct by approximately 2000 due to overpopulation and environmental exploitation. By secret Executive Order, Eisenhower tasked the JASON Scholars to study the problem. They presented three alternatives. All three alternatives included birth control, sterilization, and the introduction of deadly microbes — including AIDS — to control population. AIDS was specifically targeted at Blacks, Hispanics, and homosexuals. A Mars colony was established following a May 22, 1962 probe confirming life-supporting conditions.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

On the symposium: "A symposium was held in 1957 which was attended by some of the great scientific minds then living. They reached the conclusion that by, or shortly after, the year 2000 the planet WOULD self-destruct due to increased population and man's exploitation of the environment WITHOUT ANY HELP FROM GOD OR THE ALIENS."

On the three alternatives:
- "Alternative 1 was to use nuclear devices to blast holes in the stratosphere from which the heat and pollution could escape into space... decided to be the least likely to succeed."
- "Alternative 2 was to build a vast network of underground cities and tunnels in which a select representation of all cultures and occupations would survive and carry on the human race. The rest of humanity would be left to fend for themselves on the surface... We know that these facilities have been built and are ready and waiting."
- "Alternative 3 was to exploit the alien and conventional technology in order for a select few to leave the earth and establish colonies in outer space... The Moon, code-named ADAM, was the object of primary interest, followed by the planet Mars, code-named EVE."

On AIDS: "As a delaying action, ALL THREE ALTERNATIVES included birth control, sterilization, and the introduction of deadly microbes to control or slow the growth of the Earth's population. AIDS is only ONE result of these plans. It was decided BY THE ELITE that since the population must be reduced and controlled, it would be in the best interest of the human race to rid ourselves of the undesirable elements of our society. Specific targeted populations included BLACKS, HISPANICS, and HOMOSEXUALS."

On funding: "The ruling powers decided that one means of funding the alien-connected and other 'black' projects was to corner the illegal drug market."

On Mars: "On May 22, 1962, a space probe landed on Mars and confirmed the existence of an environment which could support life. Not long afterward the construction of a colony on the planet Mars began in earnest. Today I believe a colony exists on Mars populated by specially selected people from different cultures and occupations taken from all over the Earth."

On the moon base: "In fact, a joint alien, United States, and Soviet Union base existed on the Moon at the very moment Kennedy spoke the words [about landing a man on the Moon]."

On the space program as cover: "This mandate enabled those in charge to funnel vast amounts of money into black projects and conceal the REAL space program from the American people."

On Soviet cooperation: "A public charade of antagonism between the Soviet Union and the United States has been maintained over all these years in order to fund projects in the name of national defense when in fact we are the closest allies."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper presents Alternatives 2 and 3 as proceeding simultaneously from the late 1950s. The Rand Corporation's 1959 Deep Underground Construction Symposium is cited as evidence — machines described that could "bore a tunnel 45 feet in diameter at the rate of 5 feet per hour in 1959." Cooper states he is "now in possession of official NASA photographs of one of the moon bases."
