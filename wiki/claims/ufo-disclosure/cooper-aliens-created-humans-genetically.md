**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that aliens — during diplomatic contact with Majesty Twelve — confirmed two things: (1) that they created the human race through genetic manipulation in a laboratory; (2) that they had manipulated the human race throughout history through religion, satanism, witchcraft, magic, and the occult. They also confirmed the Fatima prophecy (obtained by U.S. intelligence via Vatican moles), showed a hologram they claimed was the actual crucifixion of Christ, and stated they could time-travel. Project RAINBOW (time travel) subsequently confirmed the Fatima prophecy.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

On alien genetic creation: "The aliens confirmed that it was true [the Fatima prophecy]. The aliens explained that they had created us through genetic manipulation in a laboratory. They stated that they had manipulated the human race through religion, satanism, witchcraft, magic, and the occult."

On time travel: "They further explained that they were capable of time travel, and the events would indeed come to pass if the conditions were not met. Later exploitation of alien technology by the United States and the Soviet Union, utilizing time travel in a project named RAINBOW, confirmed the prophecy."

On the hologram: "The aliens showed a hologram, which they claimed was the actual crucifixion of Christ. The Government filmed the hologram. We did not know whether to believe them. Were they using our GENUINE religions to manipulate us? Or were they indeed the source of our religions with which they had been manipulating us all along? Or was this the beginning scenario of the genuine END TIMES and the RETURN OF CHRIST which had been predicted in the Bible? I DO NOT KNOW THE ANSWER."

On the Fatima prophecy: Cooper states U.S. intelligence penetrated the Vatican and obtained "the entire Vatican study, which included the prophecy." The prophecy stated: "if man did not turn from evil and place himself at the feet of Christ the planet would self-destruct... a child would be born who would unite the world with a plan for world peace and a false religion... World War III would begin in the Middle East with an invasion of Israel by a United Arab nation using conventional weapons, which would culminate in a nuclear holocaust."

On alien deterioration: "Their genetic structure had deteriorated and they were no longer able to reproduce. They stated that if they were unable to improve their genetic structure, their race would soon cease to exist." This explains their use of humans and animals "for a source of glandular secretions, enzymes, hormonal secretions, blood plasma and possibly in genetic experiments."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper places this in the context of Majesty Twelve's impossible situation: weapons were "literally useless against the aliens," so friendly diplomatic relations had to be maintained while secretly developing countermeasures (Projects JOSHUA and EXCALIBUR — low-frequency sound weapon and a nuclear bunker-buster respectively). The alien deterioration claim is described as viewed "with extreme suspicion."
