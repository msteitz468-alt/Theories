## Claim Statement

Coulthart reports that former Lockheed Skunk Works director Ben Rich made remarks to associates suggesting a major undisclosed breakthrough — 'we have things out in the desert that are 50 years beyond what you can comprehend' and, ending a 1993 lecture, 'we now have the technology to take ET home'.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Sources include aviation writer Jim Goodall and lecture attendees Jan Harzan and T. L. Keller; Coulthart explicitly cautions the remarks may have been disinformation rather than literal truth.

## Contexts Within the Source

Used to weigh the possibility that a US contractor secretly cracked exotic propulsion, a hypothesis the author neither endorses nor dismisses.
