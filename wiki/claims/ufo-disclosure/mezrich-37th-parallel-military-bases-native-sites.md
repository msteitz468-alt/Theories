## Claim Statement

The same 37th-parallel corridor that concentrates UFO/mutilation anomalies also threads through a striking number of **major military installations** and **Native American sacred/historic sites** — a triple correlation Zukowski treats as significant.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Following the NIDS tactic of correlating sightings with bases, Debbie notes "Area 51 is on the 37th. And so is NORAD, at Cheyenne." Zukowski adds bases and landmarks to his map, including: the Pentagon (38.87° N), Fort Knox (37.91° N), Area 51 (37.62° N), Cheyenne Mountain (38.74° N), Mammoth Cave (37.19° N), Zion NP (37.20° N), Monument Valley (37.00° N), Chaco Culture NHP (36.07° N), the Ancient Bristlecone Pine Forest (37.18° N), and Cahokia Mounds (38.66° N). He "found himself noticing other landmarks ... American Indian sites, both sacred and historic," wondering "if there might be some reason for so many of their important sites to be linked to this same paranormal highway."

Mezrich's framing: the 37th was "the latitude of choice for government bases and American Indian holy sites."

## Contexts Within the Source

This widens the thesis from a UFO/mutilation correlation to a claimed convergence of the anomalous, the military, and the sacred — setting up the "runway" reinterpretation.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

As with the highway claim, the correlation is acknowledged to mix possible coincidence and selection bias; the coordinates listed include several nearer the 36th or 38th than the 37th.
