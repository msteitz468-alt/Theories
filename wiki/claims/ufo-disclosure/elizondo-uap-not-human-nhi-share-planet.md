## Claim Statement

Elizondo asserts, as the foundational conclusion of his work at AATIP, that UAP (unidentified aerial phenomena) are not manufactured by any human nation or corporation, and that non-human intelligence (NHI) is present on or around Earth and constitutes a national security threat.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

In the Introduction to *Imminent*, Elizondo states directly: UAP are not made by humans, humanity is not the only intelligent life in the universe, and NHI represent a national security concern that the US government has been aware of and has actively suppressed. He frames this not as speculation but as the conclusion of a classified government investigation using intelligence community tradecraft.

Former CIA senior officer Jim Semivan is quoted in the appendix: "There is a nonhuman intelligence that is living with us on this planet. . . . We are not alone, and we never have been."

Eric Davis, former AATIP member and then-employee of the Aerospace Corporation, is quoted: "We couldn't make it ourselves. Off-world vehicles not made on this earth."

## Contexts Within the Source

Elizondo frames this as the output of structured intelligence analysis — Intelligence Preparation of the Battlespace (IPB) — applied to the UAP problem. He is not claiming personal UFO sightings as the basis but rather the aggregated, multi-sensor evidentiary record reviewed by AATIP over eight years. He states these conclusions were briefed to senior officials inside the DoD.
