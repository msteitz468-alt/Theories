## Claim Statement

Coulthart quotes former Senator Harry Reid telling *The New Yorker* in 2021: 'I was told for decades that Lockheed had some of these retrieved materials', and that the Pentagon refused to grant him the classified access he requested to examine them.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Reid links this to his unsuccessful 2009 request to give AATIP Special Access Program status, and confirmed to George Knapp he knew of 'other programs . . . including different pieces of evidence'. Coulthart notes Reid, a former 'Gang of Eight' member, stopped just short of asserting the US holds alien technology.

## Contexts Within the Source

A key on-record statement from a senior senator suggesting retrieved materials are held by a contractor beyond his oversight.
