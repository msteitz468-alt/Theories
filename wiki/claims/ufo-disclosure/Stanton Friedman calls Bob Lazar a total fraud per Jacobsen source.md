## Claim Statement

Stanton Friedman contends Bob Lazar is a "total fraud" who falsely claimed an MIT degree and nuclear physicist credentials.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Bob Lazar is a total fraud," Friedman contends. "He has no credibility as a scientist. He said he went to MIT. He did not."

Friedman spoke with Lazar by telephone in 1990 and arranged lunch in Nevada but Lazar did not attend.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Jacobsen reports Lazar never changed his S-4 story; lie detector administrator said Lazar appeared to believe what he said; Norio Hayakawa filmed lights near Groom Lake as Lazar predicted.
