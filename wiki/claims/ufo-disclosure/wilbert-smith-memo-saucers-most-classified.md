## Claim Statement

A November 1950 memo by senior Canadian government engineer Wilbert Smith recorded that US scientist Dr Robert Sarbacher told him flying saucers were 'the most highly classified subject in the United States government, rating higher even than the H-bomb', that they exist, and that a small group under Dr Vannevar Bush was studying them.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Coulthart quotes the memo and notes Sarbacher, before his death, wrote that recovered materials were 'extremely light and very tough' and that recovered 'aliens' seemed 'constructed like certain insects'. He flags it as intriguing but ultimately hearsay.
