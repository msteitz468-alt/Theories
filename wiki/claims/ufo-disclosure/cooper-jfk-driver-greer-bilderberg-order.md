**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that President Kennedy was murdered by William Greer, the Secret Service agent who drove his limousine in the Dallas motorcade, and that this act is "plainly visible in the Zapruder film." The assassination was ordered by the Bilderberg Policy Committee because Kennedy had issued an ultimatum to Majesty Twelve demanding disclosure of the alien presence and a cleanup of the CIA drug operation. All close witnesses were murdered within two years. The Warren Commission was a CFR operation designed to cover it up.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

On motive: "At some point President Kennedy discovered portions of the truth concerning the drugs and the aliens. He issued an ultimatum in 1963 to Majesty Twelve. President Kennedy assured them that if they did not clean up the drug problem, he would. He informed Majesty Twelve that he intended to reveal the presence of aliens to the American people within the following year."

On the assassination order: "President Kennedy's decision struck fear into the hearts of those in charge. His assassination was ordered by the Policy Committee and the order was carried out by agents in Dallas."

On the shooter: "President John F. Kennedy was murdered by the Secret Service agent who drove his car in the motorcade and the act is plainly visible in the Zapruder film. WATCH THE DRIVER AND NOT KENNEDY WHEN YOU VIEW THE FILM."

On witnesses: "All of the witnesses who were close enough to the car to see William Greer shoot Kennedy were themselves all murdered within two years of the event... At the present time over 200 material witnesses or people actually involved with the assassination are dead. The odds against the first 18 to die within two years of the assassination were calculated at one hundred thousand trillion to one."

On the Warren Commission: "The Warren Commission was a farce, and Council on Foreign Relations members made up the majority of its panel."

On film provenance: Cooper describes obtaining a video titled "Dallas Revisited" from John Lear, who received it from CIA acquaintance Lars Hansson. Cooper showed it at lectures including Hollywood High School (November 5, 1989) to approximately 800 people.

On the Zapruder narration: Lars Hansson is quoted on the film: "The driver of the car turns with his left arm over his right shoulder with a pistol and fires. You see the .45 automatic, .45-caliber nickel-plated automatic weapon in his left hand. He's firing over his right shoulder; you see it in relief."

On Linda Moulton Howe: Cooper states Howe was shown the same Navy documents he saw, including "the same information on the Kennedy assassination naming Greer as the assassin."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper ties this to the alien cover-up structure: Kennedy "was not a member of the Council on Foreign Relations and knew nothing of Alternative 2 or Alternative 3." This made him a threat. Internationally the operation was supervised by the Bilderberg Policy Committee; in the U.S. by the CFR Executive Committee.

Cooper also notes: "When President Nixon gave his farewell speech, he said he would tell the American people the truth about UFOs. Like James Forrestal, Richard Nixon found himself a prisoner on the mental ward of Bethesda Naval Hospital. Unlike Forrestal, Nixon survived. He has remained silent."

**Opposing Information Within the Source**

Cooper acknowledges that Lars Hansson later reversed his position and claimed Greer did not shoot Kennedy. Cooper dismisses this as disinformation: "Hansson, Lifton, Grodin, and the other agents of the Secret Government are running out of time."
