## Claim Statement

The mutilation phenomenon is not isolated: per the book it comprises **more than ten thousand individual, unexplained incidents** spread across roughly half a dozen western states over more than fifty years.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Mezrich: what occurred on Glenda's ranch "was part of a phenomenon made up of more than ten thousand individual incidents, in a half dozen states — all of them completely unexplained." And: "incidents like this had been going on for more than fifty years — this exact same scene had been witnessed and documented on ranches all over Colorado — as well as in New Mexico, Arizona, Utah, and many other places — decade after decade." Zukowski had spent "the past ten years ... pursuing the truth behind these kills."

## Contexts Within the Source

The figure establishes the scale that, for Zukowski, demands a systematic (rather than coincidental) explanation and motivates the 37th-parallel mapping project.
