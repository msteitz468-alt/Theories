## Claim Statement

Coulthart reports that Robert Bigelow's NIDS bought Skinwalker Ranch in 1996 and ran years of surveillance, with scientists (Eric Davis, Colm Kelleher, John Alexander) reporting UAPs, a creature emerging from a glowing 'tunnel of light', and surgically precise, bloodless cattle mutilations echoed across the US and Australia.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He cites NIDS reports preserved in Edgar Mitchell's archive (Dulce, NM investigations; cow-pat displacement tests) and Australian rancher Mick Cook's Cloverly Station mutilations (2018–2021), while noting that the extensive camera data did not capture the most dramatic claims.

## Contexts Within the Source

Coulthart records both the witness testimony and the absence of supporting instrument data, leaving the phenomena unresolved.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Coulthart notes that the large body of NIDS data (video, EM readings, soil and blood tests) 'did not support these extraordinary claims', and that sceptics attribute the events to credulous over-interpretation.
