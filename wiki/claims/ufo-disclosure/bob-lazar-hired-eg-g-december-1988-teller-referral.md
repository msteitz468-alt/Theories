## Claim Statement

In December 1988, on Edward Teller's instruction, Bob Lazar reported to the EG&G building at McCarran Airport, met supervisor Dennis Mariani, and was flown by unmarked Janet Airlines 737 to Groom Lake.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "On Teller's instruction, Lazar called a telephone number... to go to McCarran Airport... in December—to the EG&G building there... Inside the EG&G building, he was introduced to a man called Dennis Mariani who would soon become his supervisor."
