## Claim Statement

On Wallis's "Plural Genesis" reading, the Powerful Ones terraform the earth and then **fashion human beings from clay/earth to look like their makers**, animating the inert bodies by breathing "spirit" into them, and put the human males to work on a vegan subsistence diet inside an "enclosed zone" in Eden. When the males prove lonely, the Powerful Ones generate a female from the male's body. Wallis treats this as a literal account of engineered origins rather than divine creation.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Wallis re-reads the names as common nouns: *adam* = "earthling/the-earth-thing," *eve* = "the living." He flags the otherwise-pointless detail of high-grade **gold** and mineral deposits at Havilah (Gen 2:11–12) as a clue to a resource motive, and notes Eden sits by the Tigris and Euphrates. He reads the loss of the "enclosed zone" — and thus of its "healing botanicals" — as why the locked-out humans "begin to die," and the limiting of human lifespans (Gen 6:3) as a deliberate Council decision.
