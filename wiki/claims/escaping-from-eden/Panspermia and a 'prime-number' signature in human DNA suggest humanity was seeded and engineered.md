## Claim Statement

Wallis presents the theory of **panspermia** — that life's genetic code was deliberately seeded across the galaxy — together with the claim that human "junk" DNA carries a non-random, mathematical **"prime-number" signature**, as scientific support for his reading that humanity is "a seeded, engineered hybrid" rather than the product of unguided chance.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Wallis cites the credentialed proponents (Chapter 10): "Cambridge Astronomer Professor Sir Fred Hoyle, Francis Crick who won the Nobel prize for his co-discovery of DNA, Leslie Orgel… who with Francis Crick co-authored a paper arguing for 'panspermia'… and Hungarian astrophysicist George Marx." He notes Crick and Orgel's 1981 paper and that its journal (*Icarus*) "was edited at that time by our friend Carl Sagan."

On the DNA signature: **Maxim Makulov and Vladimir shCherbak** of al-Farabi Kazakh National University, after "thirteen years" on the Human Genome Project, "concluded that human beings were intelligently designed with… 'arithmetic patterns' and 'symbolic language' encoded into our DNA." Wallis reports their finding that numbers "that are multiples of 37" recur throughout the code — nine such multiples — a pattern Makulov called "very hard to ascribe to natural processes," with "the chances of such a repetition… 1:10 Trillion." He quotes Makulov: "all life on Earth carries the genetic code of our extraterrestrial cousins and… evolution is not what we think it is." He draws the analogy to the prime-number signal in Sagan's *Contact*.

He adds the corroborating data point of the *Rosetta* comet probe (ESA, landed 12 November 2014 on comet 67P), which "detected the presence of phosphorous and the amino acid glycine" — "an awesome cocktail of organic material that… could then go on to form life" — and the Zulu *Unkulunkulu* seed-pod legend as "a powerfully cinematic version of panspermia."

## Contexts Within the Source

Wallis frames the engineered-DNA argument as scientific convergence on his biblical reading — comets "carrying the DNA coding of the parent culture through the galaxy," with senders "literally sowing the seeds of civilizations" they would later "follow up" with "further tweaking." It is the modern-science wing of his case that the Powerful Ones engineered humanity (see the Genesis-engineering claims).
