## Claim Statement
Fortune states that the zigzag line connecting the Sephiroth in their order of development is called the Lightning Flash, and that it graphically shows the Sephiroth emanating successively as positive, negative, and equilibrated forces — a better representation of creation than a straight vertical line because it shows how the Spheres group, reflect, and react upon one another.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter VIII: "It will be observed that the line which indicates the successive development of the Sephiroth zigzags from side to side of the glyph and has been aptly named the Lightning Flash in consequence. This indicates graphically that the Sephiroth are successively positive, negative, and equilibrated." She adds this is "a far better representation of the process of creation than if the Spheres were represented one above another in a straight line."

## Contexts Within the Source
She develops this alongside the doctrine that each Sephirah is bi-sexual: "negative" (feminine) toward the Sephirah it emanates from, and "positive" (masculine) toward the one it transmits Force to — like a magnet with one negative and one positive pole. The actual course of evolution is said to follow this Lightning Flash / "Flaming Sword" track from Kether to Malkuth (Chapter VIII, §14).
