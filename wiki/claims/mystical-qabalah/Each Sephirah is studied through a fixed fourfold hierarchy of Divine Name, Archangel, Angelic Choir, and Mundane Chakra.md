## Claim Statement
Fortune specifies that the "most important forms upon the Tree" are the four Names of Power assigned to each Sephirah, ranked hierarchically: the God-name (manifesting in Atziluth, dominating all cosmic, evolutionary, and subjective aspects of the Sphere), the Archangel's name (the organised consciousness that inaugurated and directs that phase of evolution), the name of the Angelic Choir (a whole class of intelligent natural forces, not one being), and the Mundane Chakra (the celestial object — planet, element, or sign — that is the visible product of that Sephirah's phase of evolution).

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter XIII: "The most important forms upon the Tree, however, are those associated with the four Names of Power assigned to each Sephirah... The highest of these is the God-name, which manifests in Atziluth... and dominates all its aspects, whether cosmic, evolutionary, or subjective... The second Name of Power is that of the Archangel of the Sphere, and represents the organised consciousness of the being through the activities of which the evolution of that phase was inaugurated and directed... The third Name of Power denominates, not one being, but a whole class of beings, the choirs of angels... The fourth denominates what we have called the Mundane Chakra, that is to say the celestial object which is looked upon as the product of the particular phase of evolution which took place under the presidency of that Sephirah."

## Contexts Within the Source
She pairs this fourfold hierarchy with each Sephirah's own Magical Image and geometrical form (e.g. Geburah/Mars is symbolized by a pentagon in a Mars colour), and states the archangelic beings, though pictured in etherealised human form, are not conscious "as we know [consciousness]" but are "individualised, intelligent, and purposive" natural forces of a wholly different order from human minds.
