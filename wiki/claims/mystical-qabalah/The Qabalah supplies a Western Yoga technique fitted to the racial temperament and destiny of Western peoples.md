## Claim Statement
Fortune asserts that "the adepts of those races whose evolutionary destiny is to conquer the physical plane have evolved a Yoga technique of their own which is adapted to their special problems and peculiar needs," and that this technique is based upon the Qabalah, "the Wisdom of Israel." The Qabalah is therefore the Yoga of the West, a system tailored to Western psychic constitution as Eastern Yogas are tailored to Eastern constitutions.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter 1, section 2: "The adepts of those races whose evolutionary destiny is to conquer the physical plane have evolved a Yoga technique of their own... This technique is based upon the well-known but little understood Qabalah, the Wisdom of Israel." Section 19: "It is for this reason that I would recommend to the white races the traditional Western system, which is admirably adapted to their psychic constitution. It gives immediate results... it produces a unique vitality."

## Contexts Within the Source
Fortune frames this at the outset of the book as her thesis: that Western occultism has its own indigenous Yoga, unrecognized by most students of occultism, rooted in Hebrew mysticism rather than borrowed uncritically from the East.
