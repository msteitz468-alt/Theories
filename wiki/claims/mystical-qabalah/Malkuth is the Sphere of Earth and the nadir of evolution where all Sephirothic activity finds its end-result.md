## Claim Statement
Fortune states that Malkuth, unlike every other Sephirah, participates in no functional triangle, yet receives the emanations of all other Sephiroth and represents "the nadir of evolution, the outermost point on the outgoing arc, through which all life must pass before returning whence it came." Malkuth is the Sphere of Earth — meaning the "Earth-soul," the subtle psychic aspect of matter, not merely the physical globe — divided into four quarters assigned to Earth, Air, Fire, and Water, and it achieves the stability that is its great virtue.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter XXV, §1-2: "It will be observed that the conformation of the Tree falls naturally into three functional triangles, but that Malkuth participates in no such triangle... it is also the only Sephirah that is represented as parti-coloured... And although it is not functional in any triangle, it represents the end-results of all the activities of the Tree. It is the nadir of evolution, the outermost point on the outgoing arc." §5: "The great point to remember in connection with Malkuth is that herein is achieved stability. It is in the inertia of Malkuth that its virtue lies."

## Contexts Within the Source
Introduces the Malkuth chapter's extended treatment of matter as "visible drapery upon an invisible framework," and Fortune's analogy of Malkuth to the "marking-buoy in a yacht race" that the soul must round before turning homeward, illustrating that the discipline of matter cannot be bypassed.
