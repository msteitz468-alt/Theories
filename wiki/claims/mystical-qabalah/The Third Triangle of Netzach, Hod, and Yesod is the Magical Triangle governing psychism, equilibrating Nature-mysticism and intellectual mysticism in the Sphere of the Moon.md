## Claim Statement
Fortune describes the Third Triangle — Netzach (Victory, Sphere of Venus), Hod (Glory, Sphere of Mercury), and Yesod (Foundation, Sphere of the Moon) — as "an exact replica of the Second Triangle upon a lower arc," but calls it the "Magical Triangle" (versus the Second Triangle's "Ethical" character). Netzach is the sphere of nature mysticism ("the Lady of Nature," "the inebriating Dionysian type"), Hod the sphere of intellectual/occult mysticism ("the Lord of Books," Thoth), and these are equilibrated in Yesod, "the Mistress of Witchcraft," the sphere of the Moon (Luna, Hecate, Diana), governing psychism where "subconsciousness and super-consciousness correlate." Fortune further proposes Yesod as the Sphere of the Holy Spirit within a Christian-Trinity attribution to the Middle Pillar (Kether=undivided Unity, Tiphareth=Son/Redeemer, Yesod=Holy Spirit/Enlightener), citing Mathers that the Hebrew word for Spirit, Ruach, is grammatically feminine.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter 7, section 26: "The meaning of Netzach is Victory, and to it is assigned the Sphere of the planet Venus; the meaning of Hod is Glory, and to it is assigned the Sphere of the planet Mercury; the meaning of Yesod is Foundation, and to it is assigned the Sphere of the Moon." Section 27: "While the Second Triangle might not inaptly be termed the Ethical Triangle, the Third may well be called the Magical Triangle... we may be justified in referring to Yesod the Sphere of the Holy Spirit, the Enlightener." Section 29: "there are three paths of superconsciousness—devotional mysticism, which correlates with Tiphareth; nature mysticism, of the inebriating Dionysian type, which equates with the Venus Sphere of Netzach; and intellectual mysticism of the occult type, which equates with Hod."

## Contexts Within the Source
Fortune treats the correct fit of correspondences across the whole Tree as the test of a given symbolic attribution's validity, contrasting her Yesod-as-Holy-Spirit attribution with what she considers the less coherent placement of the Trinity onto the Three Supernals directly.
