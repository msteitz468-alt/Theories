## Claim Statement
Fortune prescribes a fixed order for safely investigating a Sephirah: first adore the Deity under its Divine Name in that Sphere, then invoke the Archangel of the Sphere and ask that the Order of Angels assigned to it be friendly, only afterward following out the association-chains of its correspondences. She warns that if the top-down order is not followed, elemental-level forces "may get out of hand and cause difficulties," whereas "starting under the presidency of the Divine Name... no evil can enter."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter IX: "we always start at the top and try to get into spiritual touch with the aspect of Deity which emanated that Sphere and manifests in it. If this is not done, the forces belonging to the Sphere on the elemental levels may get out of hand and cause difficulties. Starting under the presidency of the Divine Name, however, no evil can enter. Having adored the Creator and Sustainer of All under His Holy Name in the Sphere we are investigating, we next invoke the Archangel of the Sphere... We ask the blessing of the Archangel, and beg that he will bid the Order of Angels assigned to that Sphere that they shall be friendly and helpful."

## Contexts Within the Source
She illustrates the technique's efficacy with two examples: a pupil who meditated well on Binah, Saturn, and Tiphareth but poorly on Geburah-Mars, and her own initiatory experience treading the Thirty-second Path of Saturn, after which "the forces and problems symbolised by that Path... had become harmonised in my soul."
