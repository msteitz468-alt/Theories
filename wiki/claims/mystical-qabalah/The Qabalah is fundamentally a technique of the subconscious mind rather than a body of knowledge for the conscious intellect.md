## Claim Statement
Fortune teaches that possessing Qabalistic knowledge without acquiring the Qabalistic "technique of mentation" is of little use, because the Tree does not appeal to the conscious mind but to subconsciousness — specifically to "the racial subconsciousness, the oversoul of the peoples, the Earth-spirit" — which the adept enters through meditation on the prescribed symbols.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter IX: "the Qabalah is as much a method of using the mind as a system of knowledge. If we have the knowledge without having acquired the Qabalistic technique of mentation, it is of little use to us... it is not the conscious mind to which the Tree appeals, but subconsciousness, for the logical method of the Qabalah is the logical method of dream association; but in the case of the Qabalah the dreamer is the racial subconsciousness, the oversoul of the peoples, the Earth-spirit. Into communion with this Earth-soul the adept enters by means of meditation on the prescribed symbols."

## Contexts Within the Source
This underlies her prescribed method for studying any Sephirah: first review possible symbols in "half meditation, half reverie" without straining, so as to let subconscious association-chains rise into consciousness "free from any admixture of extraneous ideas."
