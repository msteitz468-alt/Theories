## Claim Statement
Fortune teaches that the contacts of Netzach are not made "by means of conceiving its life philosophically, nor by means of ordinary image-making psychism, but by 'feeling with'"; it is by dance, sound, and colour that the Netzach angels (the Elohim) are contacted and evoked, and that any magical operation of Hod must have a Netzach element if it is to be "ensouled effectually."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter XXII, §13: "The contacts of Netzach are not made by means of conceiving its life philosophically, nor by means of ordinary image-making psychism, but by 'feeling with,' as Algernon Blackwood has so graphically expressed it... It is by means of dance and sound and colour that the Netzach angels are contacted and evoked... All rites which have rhythm and movement and colour in them are a working in the Sphere of Netzach. And as Hod, the Sphere of magical workings, draws its force from Netzach, it follows that any magical operation of the Sphere of Hod must have a Netzach element in it if it is to be ensouled effectually."

## Contexts Within the Source
Presented within the discussion of Netzach's macrocosmic aspect, following the discussion of god-forms and worship "by means of the arts, not conceived by means of philosophies," and preceding Fortune's extended discussion of Netzach in the microcosm as the sphere of polarity and the Aphrodite cult.
