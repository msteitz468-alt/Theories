## Claim Statement
Fortune describes the path of the Lightning Flash proceeding from Kether to Chokmah and then across to Binah, forming "a triangular figure upon the glyph... called the Triangle of the Three Supernals, or the First Trinity," which "is separated from the rest of the Sephiroth by the Abyss, which normal human consciousness cannot cross." She calls this triangle the place where "are the roots of existence, hidden from our eyes."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter 6, section 16: "The result is a triangular figure upon the glyph, and it is called the Triangle of the Three Supernals, or the First Trinity and is separated from the rest of the Sephiroth by the Abyss, which normal human consciousness cannot cross. Here are the roots of existence, hidden from our eyes."

## Contexts Within the Source
This closes Chapter 6's overview of the Tree's layout and sets up Chapter 7's dedicated examination of Kether, Chokmah, and Binah as a related triad, and of Daath, "situated astride the Abyss."
