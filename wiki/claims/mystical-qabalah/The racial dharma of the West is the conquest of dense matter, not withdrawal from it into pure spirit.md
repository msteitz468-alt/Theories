## Claim Statement
Fortune states that "the racial dharma of the West is the conquest of dense matter," and that Westerners are endowed by racial heritage with a body and nervous system suited to that task, as other races are endowed with types suited to theirs. The normal Western temperament does not seek "to cease upon the midnight with no pain" and escape the wheel of birth and death, as the pathological or Eastern-oriented type might, but demands "life, more life," seeking to conquer the earth-plane and "bring the Godhead down into manhood."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Chapter 2, section 5: "The racial dharma of the West is the conquest of dense matter... In order that we may conquer dense matter and develop the concrete mind we are endowed by our racial heritage with a particular type of physical body and nervous system." Section 7: "Withdrawal from the earth-plane is not his line of progress... the normal Western temperament demands 'life, more life.'" Section 8: "He does not try to escape from matter into spirit... he wants to bring the Godhead down into manhood and make Divine Law prevail even in the Kingdom of the Shades."

## Contexts Within the Source
This is offered as the reason Eastern mystical methods of withdrawal are often unsuitable for Westerners, and as the root-motive for the Right-hand Path of White Magic rather than pure mystic Divine Union.
