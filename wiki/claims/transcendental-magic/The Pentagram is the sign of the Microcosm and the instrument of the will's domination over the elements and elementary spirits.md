## Claim Statement
Lévi presents the Pentagram as the kabalistic "Sign of the Microcosm," representing "the domination of the mind over the elements," by which "the demons of air, the spirits of fire, the phantoms of water and ghosts of earth are enchained"; he attributes to Paracelsus the teaching that all magical figures reduce to two supreme signs — the Seal of Solomon (Macrocosm) and the Pentagram (Microcosm, "more potent even than the first").

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
Opening of Chapter V: "The Pentagram signifies the domination of the mind over the elements, and the demons of air, the spirits of fire, the phantoms of water and ghosts of earth are enchained by this sign. Equipped therewith, and suitably disposed, you may behold the infinite through the medium of that faculty which is like the soul's eye, and you will be ministered unto by legions of angels and hosts of fiends."

Later: "The Pentagram is called in Kabalah the Sign of the Microcosm, that sign so exalted by Goethe in the beautiful monologue of Faust," quoting Goethe's Faust at length.

On Paracelsus: "Paracelsus... affirms that every magical figure and every kabalistic sign of the pantacles which compel spirits, may be reduced to two, which are the synthesis of all the others; these are the Sign of the Macrocosm or the Seal of Solomon... and that of the Microcosm, more potent even than the first — that is to say, the Pentagram."

Lévi also states: "The direction of the points of the star is in no sense arbitrary, and may change the entire character of an operation," and that "by the Pentagram also is measured the exact proportions of the great and unique Athanor necessary to the confection of the Philosophical Stone."

## Contexts Within the Source
The Pentagram doctrine follows directly from the doctrine of the Astral Light and the Diaphane/imagination (Chapter V), which explains how the sign operates: spirits "recognize by intuition... whether a sign is the expression of a real power or of a precipitate will," so the Pentagram's efficacy depends on the operator's science and will, not the figure alone.
