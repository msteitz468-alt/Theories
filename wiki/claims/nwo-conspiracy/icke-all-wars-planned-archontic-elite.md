## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that all major wars of the past 6,000 years have been orchestrated by the Archontically-controlled Reptilian hybrid bloodlines. War serves multiple overlapping purposes: (1) financial profit for arms manufacturers owned by the elite; (2) geopolitical "creative destruction" — removing target governments, redrawing maps, centralising power; (3) the most important reason — generating fear, suffering, and low-vibrational emotional energy on which Archontic entities feed. The Hidden Hand controlled all sides of both World Wars, creating them to serve step-by-step centralisation: League of Nations from WWI, United Nations from WWII. A Third World War is described as planned to complete the transformation of the UN into a full world government. The Old Testament Yahweh/Jehovah is identified as the "Lord Archon" — a death-and-sacrifice-demanding entity whose wars continue through modern military operations.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 12** establishes the Archontic feeding motive: "war and conflict of any kind keep happening to energetically feed the beast through the human emotional response. Continual war and conflict are themselves the main priority to the Archontic distortion. What are they fighting about? We don't really care so long as they are fighting."

"Creative destruction" process: "involves using war to change a status quo and install a new one. Then you destroy that status quo and install a new one. Each new status quo takes you further along the road to global domination and the Hunger Games Society."

WWI → League of Nations → WWII → United Nations: "Establishment of the European Economic Community – now the EU – was sold to a very significant extent as a way of stopping another war in Europe when the force claiming this had started the others."

Third World War: "Plans for a Third World War are in large part to morph the current United Nations into a fully-fledged world government to 'stop war happening again' through a centrally-controlled tyranny enforced by a world army."

**Chapter 13** opens: "Human history over the past 6,000 years since the emergence of the new psyche has been defined by war. Archontically-controlled Reptilians and Greys have manipulated wars and conflicts across the world through their hybrid El-lite with the human population pitched against itself to divide and rule and create an ongoing frenzy of violence, fear, suffering and death on which the hidden controllers feed."

US defence spending: "The United States spends enough on 'defence' to provide every homeless American with a one-million-dollar home." One week in April 2017: $100 million for 59 Tomahawk missiles on a Syrian airbase and £16 million for the "Mother of All Bombs" in Afghanistan.

Trump administration arms connections detailed: Secretary of Defense Mattis (General Dynamics), Homeland Security Kelly (DynCorp), NSA McMaster (CSIS/Lockheed/Raytheon/ExxonMobil).

Old Testament as Lord Archon: Book of Joshua quote on destruction of Jericho is compared directly to the US Marines' assault on Fallujah (April 2004), both commanded by "Mad Dog" Mattis. "The Old Testament 'Demiurge' or Yahweh/Jehovah has long demanded death, destruction, sacrifice and war."

Canadian arms lobby: Canadian Global Affairs Institute (CGAI) campaigns for Canadian involvement in overseas conflicts; funded by General Dynamics, Lockheed Martin, and others.

He presents both World Wars as Hidden Hand creations using his own prior books (*...And The Truth Shall Set You Free*) as further documentation. The chapter connects contemporary US military policy (Syria, Afghanistan, 2017) to the same pattern.
