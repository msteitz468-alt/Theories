**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that Harvard's Skull & Bones and Yale's Scroll & Key are both secret branches of the Illuminati, specifically "Chapter 322 of the Order" (also called "the Brotherhood of Death"). Their members are recruited into a higher degree called the Order of the Quest, also known as the JASON Society. The executive committee of the Council on Foreign Relations is composed exclusively of JASON Society initiates. George Bush is named as a member. Cooper states he learned this while with Naval Intelligence.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

"The real power are men who are always recruited without exception from the secret societies of Harvard and Yale known as the Skull & Bones and the Scroll & Key. Both societies are secret branches (also called the Brotherhood of Death) of what is otherwise historically known as the Illuminati."

"They are connected to parent organizations in England (The Group of Oxford University and especially All Souls College), and Germany (the Thule Society, also called the Brotherhood of Death)."

On Bush: "George Bush is a member of the Order. Surprised? You shouldn't be. His father was also a member who helped finance Hitler."

On the oath: "Members of the Order take an oath that absolves them from any allegiance to any nation or king or government or constitution... They swear allegiance only to the Order and its goal of a New World Order. George Bush is not a loyal citizen of the United States but instead is loyal only to the destruction of the United States."

On the JASON Society: "The JASON Society, or JASON Scholars, takes its name from the story of Jason and the Golden Fleece... one of the highest degrees in the Illuminati." Cooper states that "President Eisenhower had commissioned the JASON Society to examine all of the evidence, facts, lies, and deception and find the truth of the alien question."

On the JASON Group (distinct from the JASON Society): "The JASON Group is a scientific organization formed and hired by the JASON Society and the U.S. Government... administered by the Mitre Corporation... so that the name JASON does not ever appear in documents which may come under public scrutiny." As of 1987, four Nobel Prize winners were members.

On the CFR: "Every Director of the Central Intelligence Agency has been a member of the CFR. Most Presidents since Roosevelt have been members... The CFR does not conform to government policy. The government conforms to CFR policy."

On CFR and aliens: "President Eisenhower had appointed six of the Executive Committee members of the CFR to sit on the panel called Majesty Twelve... Majesty Twelve is the secret group that is supposed to control extraterrestrial information and projects."

On the Trilateral Commission: "The real reason for its formation was the decline of the Council on Foreign Relation's power as a result of the people's dissatisfaction with the Vietnam War... formed by Rockefeller's man Zbigniew Brzezinski in 1972 [not 1973 as claimed]."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper traces a structural hierarchy: Illuminati → Skull & Bones / Scroll & Key (Chapter 322) → Order of the Quest / JASON Society (highest initiation) → CFR Executive Committee → Trilateral Commission → Bilderberg Group (the summit). All are described as faces of one network.

Samuel P. Huntington's "Seminal Peace" for the Trilateral Commission is cited: "We have come to recognize that there are potential desirable limits to economic growth. There are also potentially desirable limits to the indefinite extension of political democracy."
