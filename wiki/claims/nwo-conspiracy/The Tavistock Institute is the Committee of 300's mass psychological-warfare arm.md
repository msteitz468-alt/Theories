## Claim Statement

Coleman claims the **Tavistock Institute for Human Relations** is "the premiere brainwashing institution in the world" and the Committee of 300's instrument for mass psychological manipulation — engineering culture, crises, and public opinion across Britain and the United States.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He says Tavistock-style "profiling" was developed in 1922 on the order of the Royal Institute for International Affairs, with Major John Rawlings Reese building "the largest brainwashing facility in the world" as Britain's Psychological Warfare Bureau. He credits Tavistock (via Stanford Research Institute, under Willis Harmon) with the Club of Rome's "post-industrial" and counterculture programs ("drugs, rock, sex… environmentalism," the "Aquarian Conspiracy"), with "crisis management" theory (Kurt Lewin), and with the founding of FEMA — alleging the Three Mile Island incident was "not an accident but a deliberately designed crisis test for FEMA." He claims Tavistock runs "over 30 research institutions in the United States."

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Coleman presents these as the results of his own pioneering research (noting others later "imitate" it) rather than documented institutional records; the causal links are his attributions.
