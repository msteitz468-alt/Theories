## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that the narrative of human-caused global warming (or "climate change") is a deliberate construction — a "Big Lie" — whose purpose is not environmental protection but the justification of Agenda 21 and Agenda 2030, which are described as blueprints for global centralised control over every aspect of human life. The United Nations is the primary vehicle, and it was itself created by the Rockefeller family as a "stalking horse for world government." The UN's IPCC drives the climate narrative. Al Gore is described as the "Climate Change Liar-in-Chief." Once the climate hoax is accepted as the justification, all the Agenda 21/2030 centralisation goals — abolition of private property, state-raised children, restrictions on movement, mass depopulation — follow as necessary measures to "save the planet."

## Contexts Within the Source

Icke situates the climate hoax as a Problem-Reaction-Solution operation: fabricate the problem (human-caused climate disaster), generate the reaction (worldwide public fear), offer the pre-planned solution (Agenda 21/2030 centralisation). He distinguishes between genuine concern for the environment (which he does not dismiss) and the manufactured political narrative. The chapter is explicitly framed as answering the question "Why would they do that?" — Icke's answer is world government, not environmental cynicism.
