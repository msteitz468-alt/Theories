## Claim Statement

Sutton argues that The Order deliberately imported the statist **Prussian/Hegelian education system** into the United States to produce a compliant, "dumbed-down" populace trained "to serve the State" rather than to think independently.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He attributes the import to a Skull & Bones "troika" — **Daniel Coit Gilman, Andrew Dickson White, and Timothy Dwight** — "and then financed by Rockefeller," and credits Bonesmen with promoting the **Dewey** system ("Dewey was an ardent statist, and a believer in the Hegelian idea that the child exists to be trained to serve the State"). The result, he says, "requires suppression of individualist tendencies and a careful spoon-feeding of approved knowledge," yielding an education system that "is little more than a conditioning mechanism… a lot to do with control of the individual." He cites Iserbyt's *The Deliberate Dumbing Down of America* but traces the system further back, to the Yale Bonesmen.
