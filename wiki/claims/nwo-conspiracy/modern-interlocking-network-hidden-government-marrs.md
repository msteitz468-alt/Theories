## Claim Statement

Marrs argues that an interlocking set of elite organizations — the **Trilateral Commission, the Council on Foreign Relations, the Bilderberg group, the Federal Reserve, the Round Table / Royal Institute of International Affairs, and Skull & Bones** — funded and overlapping through the **Rockefeller, Morgan, and Rothschild** dynasties and the great tax-exempt foundations, functions as a hidden government steering policy behind the elected one.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Part I devotes chapters to each node, tracing shared memberships and money: the Trilateral Commission and CFR as policy-formulating bodies, the Bilderbergers as their international counterpart, the banking dynasties behind the **Federal Reserve System**, the Rhodes–Ruskin imperial vision behind the **Round Table/RIIA**, and **Skull & Bones** as the American collegiate node — with the mass media ("It's News to Us") keeping the connections obscure.
