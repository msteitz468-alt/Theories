## Claim Statement

Marrs's ultimate claim: the secret tradition traces back through the ancient Mystery schools and the **Cabala** to ancient Sumer — and beyond it to the **Anunnaki**, the non-human "gods" of Sumerian myth, positioning the entire chain of secret societies as the inheritance of a knowledge (and possibly a controlling influence) of extraterrestrial origin.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Part V proceeds through "The Road to Rome," "The Cabala," "Ancient Secrets and Mysteries," "Was There More to Moses?", and "**All Roads Lead to Sumer**" to "**The Anunnaki**" and "Floods and Wars." Drawing on Zecharia Sitchin, Marrs presents the Sumerian Anunnaki as the originators of the knowledge guarded by later secret societies, the deepest root of his "hidden history."

## Opposing Information Within the Source

This is the most speculative link in the book, relayed from Sitchin's contested interpretations; Marrs presents the Anunnaki origin as a question/inference (cf. his later, hedged claim that the elite "may be guided by, or be" the Anunnaki).
