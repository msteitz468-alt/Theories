**Claim Statement**

Cooper claims that a New World Order is actively being implemented and that short of mass awakening and collective action, "nothing short of civil war will stop the planned outcome." He presents this not as advocacy but as a sober appraisal based on the apathy of the American public.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

From the Preface:

> "My research has shown, at this point, that the future laid out for us may be just about impossible to change. I do not agree with the means by which the powerful few have chosen for us to reach the end. I do not agree that the end is where we should end at all. But unless we can wake the people from their sleep, nothing short of civil war will stop the planned outcome. I base that statement not on defeatism but on the apathy of the majority of the American people. Twenty-five years ago I would have believed otherwise — but twenty-five years ago I was also sound asleep."

Cooper extends the warning:

> "To cling to the past is guaranteed suicide. To remain apathetic is assured enslavement. To learn the truth and then act on it is the only means of survival at this moment. To shrug off the information contained in this book and to disregard its warning will result in the complete destruction of the Republic of the United States of America."

He also states: "The old way is in the certain process of destruction and a New World Order is beating down the door."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper presents this as the stakes justifying the book's existence. The urgency is absolute — no second warning, no second chance. He references the toll already visible in children and the welfare state as evidence the destruction is already underway.

Cooper frames his motivation in religious terms: "I believe with all my heart that God put me in places and in positions throughout my life so that I would be able to deliver this warning to His people."
