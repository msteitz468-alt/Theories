## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that the long-planned outcome of Archontic elite manipulation is what he calls the "Hunger Games Society" — a three-tier global hierarchy modelled on the dystopian film series: (1) an ultra-rich less-than-one-percent elite that controls everything; (2) a police/military enforcement layer that carries out the elite's will; and (3) an enslaved majority in perpetual poverty and dependency with no meaningful freedom, private property, or self-determination. This structure is described as being actively installed "by the day" through financial engineering (designed wealth concentration, engineered financial collapses, civil asset forfeiture), through a police/military state justified by manufactured terrorism, and through the progressive elimination of all intermediate institutions and the middle class.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 12** introduces the term: "I refer to this long-planned Brave New World as the Hunger Games Society after the movie series that portrayed a dystopia in which a tiny few mega-rich people employed a vicious and merciless police/military state to impose their will upon a poverty-stricken population maintained in ongoing intergenerational slavery."

Wealth concentration data cited: "62 people worth the same as the poorest half of the human population combined by early 2016. This 62 figure has since plummeted ... 2017 studies put the number as low as five or six – Bill Gates (Microsoft), Mark Zuckerberg (Facebook), Jeff Bezos (Amazon), Warren Buffet (Berkshire Hathaway investments), Amancio Ortega (fashion and real estate) and Carlos Slim Helu (Latin America telecom). The top five each has the wealth equivalent to 750 million people."

Amazon monopoly: "nearly one dollar in every two that Americans spend online going to Amazon and four pounds in every ten in the UK."

Financial theft: Greek Finance Ministry amending laws to allow confiscation of bank accounts and safe deposit boxes. The derivatives market (worth "hundreds of trillions") is predicted as an engineered collapse mechanism. 2014 US civil asset forfeiture: "law enforcement seized more property from Americans than burglars did – $5 billion worth."

45% of Americans using half their monthly pay to service debt (Northwestern Mutual 2017 survey cited).

**Chapter 13** adds the war dimension: "The United States spends enough on 'defence' to provide every homeless American with a one-million-dollar home." War generates immense profits for "armament giants owned by the El-ite." Arms industry influence in Trump administration is documented: Secretary of Defense Mattis (paid $242,000 + $500,000 vested stock options as General Dynamics director); Homeland Security chief John Kelly (advisor to DynCorp); National Security Advisor McMaster (connected to CSIS, funded by ExxonMobil, Chevron, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Raytheon).

**Chapter 15** connects the structure to Agenda 21 and 2030: the elimination of private property, state-raised children, restrictions on movement, "human settlement zones," and mass population reduction are all described as Hunger Games Society implementation goals.

## Contexts Within the Source

The "Hunger Games Society" concept synthesises Icke's political and cosmological claims: it is the material-world expression of the Archontic control agenda, implemented by the hybrid bloodline El-ite, sustained by the media perception-control system, and intended to culminate in the transhumanist takeover of human consciousness. Icke notes that those who currently have "nice houses and well-paid jobs" are not exempt from this agenda: "The one percent and its controlling less-than-one-percent want everyone's wealth and assets."
