**Claim Statement**  
Major historical events, including world wars, revolutions, economic depressions, and foreign policy shifts, are not accidental or the result of arbitrary historical tides, but are systematically planned and engineered by a self-perpetuating clique of international bankers and elites known as the Insiders.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**  
According to *None Dare Call It Conspiracy* (Chapter One):
* **Rejection of the Accidental Theory:** Gary Allen argues that the "accidental theory of history" promoted by the academic and media Establishment is a deception to shield political leaders from accountability. He asserts: "If we were merely dealing with the law of averages, half of the events affecting our nation's well-being should be good for America... If we were dealing with mere incompetence, our leaders should occasionally make a mistake in our favor. We shall attempt to prove that we are not really dealing with coincidence or stupidity, but with planning and brilliance."
* **Carroll Quigley's Revelation:** To prove the existence of this planned network, Allen cites Professor Carroll Quigley's 1,300-page book *Tragedy and Hope*. Quigley admits to having studied this secret banking network for twenty years and examined its papers, stating: "I know of the operations of this network... I have no aversion to it or to most of its aims... my chief difference of opinion is that it wishes to remain unknown, and I believe its role in history is significant enough to be known."
* **Father Pedro Arrupe's Quote:** Allen cites a December 27, 1965 declaration by Father Pedro Arrupe, head of the Jesuit Order, charging that a "godless society... follows a perfectly mapped-out strategy" and "holds almost complete sway in international organizations, in financial circles, in the field of mass communications."

**Contexts Within the Source**  
Gary Allen presents this claim as the foundational premise of his book, urging readers to look past the "camouflage" landscapes painted by the media. He argues that by identifying the cause-and-effect relationship behind political disasters, the public can begin to see that the Insiders are orchestrating events to gradually strip national sovereignty and establish a central global dictatorship.
