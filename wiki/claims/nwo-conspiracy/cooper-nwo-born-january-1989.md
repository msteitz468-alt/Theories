**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that the New World Order was formally born in secrecy on January 19, 1989 — the day before George H.W. Bush was inaugurated as the 41st President of the United States.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

"I believe that the New World Order was born in secrecy on January 19, 1989. I believe that the implementation of the New World Order began with the presidency of George Herbert Walker Bush."

Cooper presents this date as the operational commencement of the NWO agenda after decades of preparation through the CFR, Trilateral Commission, Knights of Malta, and Skull & Bones networks he describes throughout Chapter 2.

**Contexts Within the Source**

This claim appears at the conclusion of the Secret Societies chapter, after Cooper's extended cataloguing of interlocking elite networks. It functions as his synthesis claim — that the described organisations converged to produce a singular political event: the Bush inauguration.
