**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that two classified U.S. government programs — Garden Plot (population control) and Cable Splicer (orderly takeover of state and local governments by the federal government) — represent a blueprint for an internal coup. The programs are backed by: 25 million civilian files at the Pentagon's domestic intelligence center; 760,000 files on organizations; civilian detention camps designated as "emergency detention centers" (never actually repealed); and a 300th Military Police POW Command in Livonia, Michigan, whose camps are described as intended for "enemies of the United States" including U.S. citizens.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

"Garden Plot is the program to control the population. Cable Splicer is the program for an orderly takeover of the state and local governments by the federal government."

On files: "The Army has over 350 separate record centers containing substantial information on civilian-political activities... There are 25 million cards on individuals and 760,000 on organizations held by the Defense Central Index of investigations alone. And this information includes political, sociological, economic and psychological profiles."

On detention: "The cadre of specialized persons to enforce this plan are found in the U.S. Army Reserves — Military Police POW Command at Livonia, Michigan. Mr. Fennerin, of the 300th Military Police POW Command, at Livonia, told me, when I called him from the Federal Information Center at Houston, that the camps in the Command were for foreign prisoners-of-war and for 'enemies of the United States'." Cooper states: "I asked him if enemies of the United States included U.S. citizens. He became angry, wouldn't deny it."

On the repeal trick: "One congressman — when the hearings were held for the repeal of the Emergency Detention Act — mentioned that there are 17 other bits of law that provided for the same thing. So it didn't matter whether they ever repealed the Emergency Detention Act. The public was in fact tricked by the Congress of the United States!"

On California's L.E.A.F.: Cooper describes a 1,000-member law enforcement assault force with helicopters, SWAT teams, and "twelve-gauge shotguns" deployed in California — described as a product of the "Cable Splicer conferences." A former L.E.A.F. administrator is quoted: "Local law enforcement has failed and must be replaced by a national police force."

On Cable Splicer handbook: "The Cable Splicer handbook is composed of 6 loose-leaf 3-ring binders that are merely an outline for the impending takeover and destruction of our Constitution."

On psychosurgery: "If you don't cooperate with their programs, you are merely operated on so that you be as cooperative as an adding machine. Or, the L.E.A.A. supports drug research for the same purpose — to neutralize neurological sources of violence."

On weapons confiscation: "The plan also specifically includes a proposition for confiscation of privately-owned weapons and ammunition."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper presents this as the domestic enforcement infrastructure for the broader NWO plan. FEMA is described as the entity that would "take over" under presidential emergency orders, with Mt. Weather, Clark County, West Virginia named as the primary backup command facility. He situates these programs as the American equivalent of what the Bilderberg Group planned economically via Silent Weapons.
