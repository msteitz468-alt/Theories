**Claim Statement**  
The Rockefeller family and their allied corporate interests actively finance, trade with, and transfer advanced industrial technology to the Soviet Union and other Communist regimes, cooperating to divide global markets and establish centralized monopolies under a unified world government.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**  
According to *None Dare Call It Conspiracy* (Chapter Six):
* **Early Support via Hoover:** Allen claims that the Bolshevik regime was originally saved from collapse by Herbert Hoover, who raised funds to buy food that was appropriated by Lenin to subdue starving peasants.
* **Vanderlip and Rockefeller Support:** Soon after, Frank Vanderlip (a Rockefeller agent and Jekyll Island participant) compared Lenin to George Washington, and Rockefeller interests began investing in the USSR.
* **Chase Manhattan and Technology Transfers:** Chase Manhattan Bank and Rockefeller family members have consistently promoted trade and technology transfers to Communist nations. This includes financing and exporting machinery for the giant Kama River truck factory, which had military transport capabilities.
* **David Rockefeller and Khrushchev:** Allen points to a vacation David Rockefeller took in the Soviet Union in October 1964. A few days after Rockefeller met with Kremlin leaders, Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev was abruptly fired. Allen suggests this event indicates that Soviet leaders are figureheads ultimately subordinate to the real power centers in New York.
* **CFR Promoting Trade with the Reds:** Allen asserts that the CFR leads the promotion of technology transfer and credit lines to Communist countries, which are ultimately backed by American taxpayers.

**Contexts Within the Source**  
Allen presents this claim to refute the idea that corporate capitalism and state Communism are mortal enemies. He argues that under the Insiders' model, Communism acts as a regional holding company or monopoly manager. By helping Communist regimes build industrial capacity, the Rockefellers ensure that major sectors of the global market are closed to free-market competitors and instead run as state-controlled monopolies with which they can partner.
