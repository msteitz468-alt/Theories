## Claim Statement

Marrs argues that the modern secret-society network grew out of the revolutionary era: **Freemasonry** and the **Bavarian Illuminati** played decisive hidden roles in both the American and French Revolutions, linking the founders' Masonry and Bacon's *New Atlantis* vision to the Illuminist agitation behind 1789.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Part III treats the American Revolution alongside the Freemasonry of the founders and "Sir Francis Bacon and the *New Atlantis*"; the French Revolution alongside "**The Illuminati**," "Jacobins and Jacobites," "Masonic Plots," and the Anti-Masonic Movement; plus the Rosicrucians and "Count Saint-Germain and Other Magicians." Marrs presents the revolutions as the moment the esoteric/Masonic current entered active politics.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Consistent with his profile, Marrs is comparatively cautious — distinguishing documented Masonic membership from sweeping claims and (elsewhere) debunking the Pike "three world wars" hoax.
