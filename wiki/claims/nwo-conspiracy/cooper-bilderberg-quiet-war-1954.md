**Claim Statement**

Cooper reproduces a document titled *Silent Weapons for Quiet Wars: An Introductory Programming Manual, Operations Research Technical Manual TM-SW7905.1*, dated May 1979, which he states was found on July 7, 1986, in an IBM copier purchased at a surplus sale. He further states that Top Secret documents he read while with Naval Intelligence identified this as "the doctrine adopted by the Policy Committee of the Bilderberg Group during its first known meeting in 1954." Cooper titles it "The Illuminati's Declaration of War upon the People of America."

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

The document's own preamble states: "This publication marks the 25th anniversary of the Third World War, called the 'Quiet War,' being conducted using subjective biological warfare, fought with 'silent weapons.'"

The document's historical introduction states: "The QUIET WAR was quietly DECLARED by the INTERNATIONAL ELITE [THE BILDERBERG GROUP] at a meeting held in 1954."

On the mechanism of control: "It was decided to privately wage a quiet war against the American public with an ultimate objective of permanently shifting the natural and social energy (wealth) of the undisciplined and irresponsible many into the hands of the self-disciplined, responsible, and worthy few."

On population management: "The latter option has been taken as the obviously better option... reduce the economic inductance of the world to a safe level by a process of benevolent slavery and genocide."

On social welfare as control: "The social welfare program is nothing more than an open-ended credit balance system... the recipients become state property in return for the 'gift,' a standing army for the elite."

On economic shock testing: "By shock testing, it is found that there is a direct relationship between the availability of money flowing in an economy and the psychological outlook and response of masses of people." Specific test commodities named: beef, coffee, gasoline, sugar.

On the planned body-number system: "LATER A PERMANENT 'TATTOOED' BODY NUMBER invisible under normal ambient illumination" [Cooper's emphasis].

On education of the lower class: "The quality of education given to the lower class must be of the poorest sort, so that the moat of ignorance isolating the inferior class from the superior class is and remains incomprehensible to the inferior class."

**Contexts Within the Source**

The Rockefeller Foundation funded a four-year Harvard Economic Research Project beginning 1948. The U.S. Air Force joined in 1949. In 1952 "a high-level meeting of the ELITE [Illuminati] was held to determine the next phase of social operations research." The document traces the economic control methodology back to Mayer Amschel Rothschild's discovery of "economic inductance."

Cooper's bracketed editorial notes appear throughout, identifying institutions: "[THE COUNCIL ON FOREIGN RELATIONS]" inserted next to references to "those in positions of power"; "[Illuminati]" next to references to "the elite."

Cooper states: "I do recognize this document, based upon the document's own admission, as a formal Declaration of War by the Illuminati upon the Citizens of the United States of America."
