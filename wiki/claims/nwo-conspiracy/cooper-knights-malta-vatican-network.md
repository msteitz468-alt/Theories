**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts the Knights of Malta is a global power network whose members hold diplomatic immunity, can ship goods across borders without customs checks, swear allegiance to the Pope above all national loyalties, and penetrate the CIA, banking, politics, media, and intelligence organizations. Every U.S. Ambassador to the Vatican has been a Knight of Malta. CIA Director William Casey was a Knight and helped plan to suspend the Constitution during Iran-Contra. Cooper identifies this as "one of the oldest branches of the Order of the Quest in existence."

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

On immunity: "The Knights of Malta ALL have diplomatic immunity. They can ship goods across borders without paying duty or undergoing customs check."

On membership: "Nearly half of the 10,000 members belong to Europe's oldest and most powerful families. This cements the alliance between the Vatican and the 'Black Nobility.'"

On allegiance: "Membership in the Knights of Malta entails obedience to one's superior in THE ORDER and ultimately to the Pope. Therefore, a U.S. ambassador who is also a member of the Knights of Malta faces a conflict of interest."

On Vatican ambassadors: "The first U.S. Ambassador to the Vatican was William Wilson, a Knight of Malta... Knight of Malta Myron Taylor was President Roosevelt's envoy. Knight of Malta John McCone was President Kennedy's envoy and he was also the Director of the CIA... Frank Shakespeare is a Knight of Malta."

On Casey: "William Casey was a member of the Knights of Malta. William Casey, with the help of Vice President Bush, Anne Armstrong and Donald Regan, caused the President's Foreign Intelligence Advisory Board to be emasculated... They had also developed a plan to suspend the Constitution of the United States and were preparing to implement the plan when they were caught... I believe Casey was murdered."

On the 1930s plot: "In the 1930s General Smedley Butler was recruited to help take over the White House... At the top of the list was John J. Raskob, who was a founding member of the U.S. branch of the Knights of Malta. He was board chairman of General Motors." Cooper notes congressional hearings were held but no one named was ever called to testify.

On Vatican infiltration: "In 1884 Pope Leo XIII issued a proclamation stating that Masonry was one of the secret societies attempting to 'revive the manners and customs of the pagans' and 'establish Satan's kingdom on Earth.'" Cooper cites Piers Compton's *The Broken Cross* documenting over 70 Vatican officials as members of secret societies.

On November 27, 1983: "The Pope retracted all of the Papal Bulls against Freemasonry and allowed Catholics, after several hundred years, to again become members of secret societies without fear of excommunication."

On the Vatican computer: "The Center was set up by the Vatican as a part of the Pope's new peace plan... The computer is hooked to the world capitals via satellite. All nations have agreed to relinquish sovereignty to the Pope and submit future problems to the computer for solution."

On the Polish salesman / Pope John Paul II: Cooper identifies the future Pope John Paul II as having sold cyanide to the Nazis for Auschwitz while working for I.G. Farben, then joining the Catholic Church. He states: "I believe, according to my research, that [this Pope] is a member of the Illuminati."
