## Claim Statement

Sutton's central mechanism: The Order is guided by **Hegelian philosophy** and engineers history through *managed conflict* — deliberately financing and promoting opposing forces ("right" and "left") so that, by "controlling the opposites," it "dominates the nature of the outcome."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He writes that "progress in the Hegelian State is through contrived conflict: the clash of opposites makes for progress. If you can control the opposites, you dominate the nature of the outcome." Hegelianism, he argues, "glorifies the State" and is "the source of both Naziism and Marxism." He reads the U.S. "two-party Republican-Democrat… system" as "one Hegelian party" (controlled opposition), and gives the drug trade plus the "so-called 'war on drugs'" as a thesis/antithesis pair benefiting the same Bonesmen. He adds that "reductionist" fragmentation of science and learning made "it easier to control the whole through the parts."
