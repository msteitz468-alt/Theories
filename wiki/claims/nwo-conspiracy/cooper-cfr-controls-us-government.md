**Claim Statement**

Cooper asserts that the Council on Foreign Relations (CFR) — an off-shoot of the British Royal Institute of International Affairs — controls all branches of the U.S. government. Every Director of the CIA has been a CFR member. Most Presidents since Roosevelt have been members. The CFR forbids note-taking and expels any member who divulges meeting content. Its executive committee is composed of JASON Society initiates drawn from Skull & Bones and Scroll & Key.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

"The Council on Foreign Relations controls our government. Through the years its members have infiltrated the entire executive branch, State Department, Justice Department, CIA, and the top ranks of the military. EVERY DIRECTOR OF THE CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE AGENCY HAS BEEN A MEMBER OF THE CFR. MOST PRESIDENTS SINCE ROOSEVELT HAVE BEEN MEMBERS."

"The members of the CFR dominate ownership of the press and most, if not all, of America's top journalists are members. The CFR does not conform to government policy. The government conforms to CFR policy."

On secrecy: "The CFR is a secret society in that it forbids the taking of notes or the publishing of minutes of its meetings. Any member who divulges the subject or any part of any conversation or talk that took place during a meeting is terminated."

On founding: "It did not take on its present power until 1921, when it merged with the Royal Institute of International Affairs and received its financial base from J.P. Morgan, the Carnegie Endowment, the Rockefeller family, and other Wall Street banking interests."

On Kissinger: "Rockefeller gave Kissinger a grant of $50,000 in the early '50s, a fortune in those days, and made dear old Henry a member of the CFR."

On the Trilateral Commission relationship: "The real power has always remained solidly in the hands of the Council on Foreign Relations. The Rockefeller family was, is and always will be the benefactor of both organizations."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Cooper situates the CFR within a cascade of nested organizations: CFR → Bilderberg Group → Knights of Malta → Vatican. The Bilderberg Group is described as the summit, with the CFR as its primary American instrument. Cooper states Eisenhower appointed six CFR Executive Committee members to Majesty Twelve to handle the alien question.
