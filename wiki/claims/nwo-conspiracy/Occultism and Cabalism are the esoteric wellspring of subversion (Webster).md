## Claim Statement

Webster argues that occultism — and especially the **Jewish Cabala** — supplies the esoteric doctrine running beneath the whole subversive tradition, feeding the secret societies from antiquity onward.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

She states that "the Jewish Cabala played an important part in the occult and anti-Christian sects from the very beginning of the Christian era," and treats the legendary "Christian Rosenkreutz" as "a purely mythical personage," invented "to disguise the real sources whence the Rosicrucians derived their system" — which she calls "a compound of ancient esoteric doctrines of Arabian and Syrian magic, and of Jewish Cabalism, partly inherited from the Templars but reinforced by direct contact with Cabalistic Jews in Germany." On this reading the occult societies are vehicles, knowingly or not, of a Cabalistic current.
