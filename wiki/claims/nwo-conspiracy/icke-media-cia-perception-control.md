## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that the mainstream media is not a journalistic enterprise but a perception-control system operated by the same elite network — and specifically the CIA and allied intelligence agencies — that runs governments, banks, and corporations. Six corporations own virtually all American mainstream media; those corporations are themselves owned by a handful of billionaires who are controlled by the "Web" (the elite network). The CIA maintains hundreds of assets among foreign journalists and has direct influence over major American media outlets. This is not a historical artefact from the Cold War but an ongoing and expanding system of what Icke calls "psychological warfare on the public."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 9** states: "A handful of giant corporations own the American media and those corporations are themselves owned by a handful of billionaires." The six named corporations: News Corp, Viacom, Time Warner, Disney, CBS Corporation, and NBC Universal. "Go deep enough in the Web and all six will be controlled by the same hidden network."

Church Committee (1976 Senate Select Committee): "about 50 'agency assets' were individual American journalists or employees of American media organisations" and "more than a dozen United States news organizations and commercial publishing houses formerly provided cover for CIA agents abroad." The report acknowledged the CIA "currently maintains a network of several hundred foreign individuals around the world who ... provide the CIA with direct access to a large number of newspapers and periodicals, scores of press services and news agencies, radio and television stations, commercial book publishers, and other foreign media outlets."

Jeff Bezos conflict: Bezos owns both Amazon and *The Washington Post*, and "negotiated a $600 million deal for Amazon with the CIA while The Washington Post reports stories about the CIA."

Anderson Cooper: described as "CNN stalwart Anderson Cooper from the El-lite Vanderbilt bloodline" who "was an intern for the CIA before turning to what he calls journalism."

PSYOPS embedded in news: "CNN stalwart Anderson Cooper ... 'Zionist-controlled and incredibly arrogant CNN and the publicly-funded US National Public Radio (NPR) have admitted that eight members of the US Army 4th Psychological Operations (PSYOPS) Group worked in their news divisions and other areas during the Kosovo war.'"

Udo Ulfkotte: "Leading German journalist Udo Ulfkotte, former assistant editor for major German newspaper, Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung, publicly exposed control of the media by intelligence agencies before he died of a heart attack in January 2017 at the age of 56." Ulfkotte is quoted: "I've been a journalist for about 25 years, and I was educated to lie, to betray, and not to tell the truth to the public." He revealed being "forced to publish articles under his own name that were handed to him by intelligence agencies." He published *Journalists for Hire: How the CIA Buys the News*.

Dr Wolfgang Herles (retired head of German public-service television network ZDF Bonn): quoted as saying networks "are told what to report (and not) by the German government."
