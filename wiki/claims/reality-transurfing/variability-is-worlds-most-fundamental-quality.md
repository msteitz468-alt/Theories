## Claim Statement

Variability is asserted to be the world's most fundamental quality — not stability, not determinism, but the perpetual availability of different alternatives. This grounds the entire Transurfing model: if reality were fixed, no alternative could be selected.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. I: "Variability is the world's most fundamental quality."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim serves as the philosophical premise for the alternatives space model. It implies that at every moment, multiple alternative scripts and sceneries exist and any of them could be realized depending on the parameters of thought energy present. The current physical reality is not the only one possible — it is simply the one currently "lit up" by the collective and individual thought energy present.
