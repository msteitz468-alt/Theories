## Claim Statement

The law of balance is the most fundamental operative law in the Transurfing model. All difficulties a person experiences are linked to balanced forces acting to eliminate the excess potential that person has created through the attribution of importance.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. IV: "The law of balance is primary; all difficulties linked to balanced forces eliminating excess potential."

Ch. IV: "The only obstacle on the path to fulfilling your desires is the projection of artificial importance."

## Contexts Within the Source

The source presents the law of balance as non-personal and mechanical — balanced forces do not punish, they correct imbalance. The implication is that personal difficulties are not caused by bad luck, hostile forces, or inadequacy, but by the person's own creation of excess potential through importance.

This framing makes importance reduction the single most important practical intervention across all domains: relationships, money, goals, health, social situations.
