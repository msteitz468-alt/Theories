## Claim Statement

The Egyptian pyramids were built using outer intention. Similarly, the civilization of Atlantis was destroyed by balanced forces after its inhabitants perfected outer intention. The Holy Grail, philosopher's stone, and related historical quests are described as attempts to harness outer intention. Telekinesis is asserted to be a unique human capacity for outer intention.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. VII (Outer Intention section): "Egyptian pyramids built with outer intention; Atlantis destroyed by balanced forces after perfecting it; Holy Grail, philosopher's stone = attempts to harness it; telekinesis = unique capacity for outer intention."

"Ability to direct outer intention has atrophied in contemporary humans — suppressed by pendulums." (Ch. VII)

## Contexts Within the Source

These claims serve as illustrations of outer intention's historical potency rather than as primary claims. They are presented as historical examples showing that outer intention was once more widely accessible and that its power has been recognized (and sought) across civilizations.

The Atlantis example also functions as a warning about the [[Law of Balance]]: even achieving mastery of outer intention generates excess potential if importance is attributed to that mastery, triggering balanced forces.

The claim about telekinesis connects outer intention to documented anomalous phenomena, framing telekinesis not as a violation of physical law but as a shift of "the point of material realization from one sector to another in the alternatives space" (Ch. VII).
