## Claim Statement

When the parameters of a person's thought energy change — in frequency, quality, or characteristic emotional tone — the person shifts from one life line to another. The person cannot change the script within a given life line; they can only shift to a different alternative.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. I: "The destiny of a person flows along a life line in the alternatives space. When the parameters of a person's thought energy change, they shift from one life line to another."

Ch. I: "You cannot change the script within a given life line; you can only choose a different alternative."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim establishes the structural reason why "trying harder" within a current situation (inner intention applied to the existing life line) has limited effect: the script of the current life line is fixed. Change requires shifting to a different life line, which requires shifting one's thought energy parameters — not effortful action within the current line.

The claim also explains the Transurfing emphasis on managing attention, importance, and emotion: these directly affect thought energy parameters, and therefore which life line is inhabited.
