## Claim Statement

The space experienced in dreams is identical to the alternatives space. Dreaming is the soul's travel through the alternatives space, where all types of script exist. Dreams are not illusions — "The mind genuinely sees them" — but actual experiences of unrealized sectors of the alternatives space.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. VII: "Dreaming is the soul's travel through the alternatives space, where all types of script exist."

Ch. VII: "The subconscious directly connects to the alternatives space and the information it contains."

Ch. VII: "The brain does not store information itself. It stores the address to where the information is located in the alternatives space."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim serves as the theoretical bridge between the cosmological model (the alternatives space) and practical guidance about dreaming and lucid dreaming. If the dream space is the alternatives space, then:
- Dreams can show what could have happened or what could happen (they show adjacent sectors)
- [[Lucid Dreaming (Transurfing)|Lucid dreaming]] is training for [[Outer Intention]] because the dream is where outer intention is most accessible
- Hallucinations and psychotic perceptions are not fantasy but experiences of unrealized sectors
- Dreams should not be interpreted as signs — they are not messages but travel experiences

The one notable risk stated: "Soul will not necessarily return if it flies into a realized sector of alternatives space." (Ch. VII Summary)
