## Claim Statement

All energy pendulums — without exception — are inherently destructive. They consume the thought energy of their adherents for their own sustenance, regardless of whether the pendulum appears to serve positive or beneficial purposes.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. II: "All pendulums are inherently destructive — they consume adherent energy."

The claim is stated categorically with no exceptions. Even pendulums associated with positive causes (religion, art, science, social movements) are included.

## Contexts Within the Source

The source grounds this claim in the structural nature of pendulums: their survival depends on adherents' uniformity of thought and continued energy input. A pendulum does not care about the wellbeing of individual adherents — their welfare is irrelevant to the pendulum's survival imperative. The pendulum may appear to serve adherents, but this appearance serves only to sustain adherent energy flow.

"The pendulum is most comfortable when its adherents are thinking and acting in the same way." (Ch. IX)

The "puppet strings" by which pendulums control adherents are listed: fear, guilt, duty, inferiority/warrior/truth-lover complexes, justice, pride, ambition (Ch. II).
