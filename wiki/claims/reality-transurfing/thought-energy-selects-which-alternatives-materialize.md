## Claim Statement

Human thought energy transforms possible alternatives into physical reality by "lighting up" a corresponding sector of the alternatives space. The frequency/quality of thought energy determines which sector becomes physically manifest.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. I: "Thought energy transforms possible alternatives into physical reality by lighting up a sector."

Ch. I: "The destiny of a person flows along a life line in the alternatives space. When the parameters of a person's thought energy change, they shift from one life line to another."

The claim establishes the mechanism connecting subjective mental states (thought energy) to objective outcomes (which life line is inhabited). It is the causal core of the model.

## Contexts Within the Source

The source uses this claim to explain both desired outcomes (choosing favourable life lines through intention) and undesired outcomes (shifting to negative life lines through fear, guilt, or pendulum entrainment). The principle is symmetrical: thought energy matching a negative sector will shift one there just as readily as thought energy matching a positive one.

The claim is repeatedly illustrated: negative thinking attunes to negative life lines ("you get what you do not want"); heart–mind unity in negative expectation easily realizes feared outcomes (Ch. VII Summary).
