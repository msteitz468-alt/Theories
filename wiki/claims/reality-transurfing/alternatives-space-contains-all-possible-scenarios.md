## Claim Statement

The alternatives space is a gigantic material information structure that contains all possible life scenarios that ever have existed or could exist. Every point in the space has both a script (sequence of events) and scenery (physical conditions).

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Ch. I: "The alternatives space is a gigantic information field containing scripts and scenery for all possible events."

Ch. VI: "Information Field: alternatives space = information field / energy matrix = template for possibilities."

The claim is presented as the cosmological foundation of the entire Transurfing framework. All subsequent claims about life lines, thought energy, pendulums, and outer intention presuppose it.

## Contexts Within the Source

The source frames this claim as a model rather than as empirically verifiable fact, but asserts it as the true description of reality. Zeland presents it as beyond conventional materialism ("The majority of what we call common sense is nothing of the kind") and explicitly acknowledges it will seem implausible from a conventional standpoint.

The claim is structural: without the alternatives space, the concepts of life lines, sector shifts, outer intention, and transfer chains have no referent.
