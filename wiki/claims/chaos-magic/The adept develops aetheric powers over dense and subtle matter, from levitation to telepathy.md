## Claim Statement

Carroll holds that the adept's primary practical work is mastery over [[The Aether (Carroll)]] at two levels of density. At the dense level, the adept is said to achieve gross physical feats including levitation, fire-walking, and extraordinary physical force. At the subtle level, the adept is said to develop telepathy, mind influence, the projection of subjective invisibility, and the projection of charisma or fascination.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

*Liber AOM*, "Aetherics":

> "The aethers in their various degrees of density are the means by which magic and miracles are affected. The adept may require these abilities to accomplish his will on earth, or he may simply wish to develop them as a test of the mystic abilities."

On dense aether:

> "Operation of the dense aether is used to accomplish gross feats such as levitation and the short-range occult martial arts. The force responsible for this resides in the belly just above the navel and may be extruded as lines of force to any part of the body and to locations outside the body's surface to a distance of several meters. Levitation (which includes the ability to walk on water and fire, as well as in the air, or at fantastic rates across the earth) is accomplished by supporting the body's weight with these aetheric force lines."

Carroll offers a concrete training method: the adept sits cross-legged and attempts to "jump like a frog" from that position, practised in darkness with lungs fully inflated, claiming results "within as little as three years' practice."

On subtle aether:

> "Operation of the subtle aethers involves the transference of thoughts and emotions by the agency of the aethers which normally link the life force to the brain … One of the classical tests of psychic ability is the power of casting an aura of subjective invisibility about oneself … The complementary ability to this is the projection of an aura of charisma or fascination, which may have application in the formation of sundry cults and messianic sects."

Carroll also describes healing and disease suppression: "The adept should be able to achieve freedom from the attack of virtually any form of disease and ensure longevity by directing the psychic force to any site of weakness."

## Contexts Within the Source

- *Liber NOX*, "Sorcery" — earlier initiate-level use of aetheric material bases (talismans, amulets, magical weapons), which the adept supersedes with direct aetheric projection.
- *Liber NOX*, "The Double" — the double as an aetheric vehicle is a precursor skill to the adept's aetheric operations.
- *Psychonaut*, "Magical Combat" — applications of aetheric force in offensive and defensive magic (projecting disruptive energy through hands, voice, or breath).

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Carroll catalogs these as real abilities but notes that "destructive effects are much more easily created than constructive ones." He frames the training timelines (e.g., "within as little as three years' practice") as estimates for beginning to learn rather than guarantees.
