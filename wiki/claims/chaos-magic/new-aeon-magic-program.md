## Claim Statement

Carroll holds that "New Aeon Magic" represents the next major advance of human civilization, superseding both monotheistic religion and materialist science. It has two pillars: rigorous experimental investigation of actual magical techniques divorced from their religious symbolism, and the development of a distinct magical philosophy ("spirituality of magic") grounded in chaotic good, individual liberty, and psychological anarchy.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

*Psychonaut*, "New Aeon Magic":

> "The next great advance that humanity will make will be into the psychic domain … In this new field of endeavor, we shall rediscover much of the magical knowledge that the ancient animists and shamans once possessed."

On the two pillars:

> "There is a twofold aspect to the most important magical work now being done. Foremost is an experimental investigation into the actual techniques as opposed to the mere symbolism of magic. The methods of magic are remarkably uniform throughout history and across cultures. It is time to unearth them and make them work. Secondly, it is essential that what might be called a spirituality of magic is evolved."

On the shifting of magical force from matter to psyche:

> "The magic force is now manifesting with increasing strength on the psychic levels. The creativity of consciousness has mushroomed so enormously that the totality of human ideas seems to double with each decade … On the magical level, the psychic powers are becoming much more accessible."

Carroll aligns New Aeon Magic against religion, politics-as-domination, ideology, and pure materialism, describing its commitment as "chaotic good":

> "Magic's commitment to the good is reflected in its concern with individual freedom and consciousness and its interest in all other life forms on this planet."

He further identifies its method as "psychological anarchy":

> "The chaotic aspect of new aeon magic is psychological anarchy. It is a species of operation mindfuck applied to ourselves as much as the world. The aim is to produce inspiration and enlightenment through disordering our belief structures. Humor, random belief, counter-information, and disinformation are its techniques."

## Contexts Within the Source

- *Psychonaut*, "Introduction" — frames *Psychonaut* as a companion to *Liber Null*, oriented toward group/shamanic magic rather than individual sorcery.
- *Liber NOX*, "The Millennium" — the aeonic historical analysis that motivates the claim that a new magical epoch is imminent.
- *Psychonaut*, "Group Magical Experiments" and "Levels of Consciousness" — the practical chapters implementing the new aeon's experimental approach.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Carroll acknowledges the possibility of failure or delay: the magical force could be captured by technological states and used for enslavement ("Chemicals, electronics, and surgery will only tend to enslave") rather than liberation. The fifth aeon "may develop into … a time of totalitarian tyranny." He also notes that most wizards "end up crying in the wilderness or being put to the stake in various ways."
