## Claim Statement

Strieber recounts that the December 26, 1985 encounter was not isolated: under hypnotic regression he recovered an **earlier episode of October 4, 1985**, and presents the verbatim session transcript as evidence that the visitor experiences were recurring and that hypnosis surfaced buried memories.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He publishes "an actual transcript of my first hypnosis. Nothing has been left out" — a March 1, 1986 session with psychiatrist **Dr. Donald Klein**, who walked him from Labor Day "forward… to the beginning of the month of October… Right around October first, 1985." Strieber, initially placing himself mundanely ("I'm working on the Russian book"), is drawn toward the night in question and breaks down in terror as the memory surfaces ("Scared the devil out of me… I just can't believe that this happened. But it did happen"). The session establishes the October event behind his initially-remembered "turmoil."

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Strieber presents the raw transcript precisely to let readers judge the hypnosis, while elsewhere conceding such memories are contested and that he cannot finally say what the visitors are.
