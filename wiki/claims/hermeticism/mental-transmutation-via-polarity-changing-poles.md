## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that because emotional and mental states exist as pairs of poles on a single scale (not as categorically distinct qualities), a Hermetic practitioner can deliberately "slide" from an undesirable mental state to its opposite by raising or lowering the rate of mental vibration along the axis of that quality. This is presented as the foundational mechanism of all [[Mental Transmutation (Kybalion)]].

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "To destroy an undesirable rate of mental vibration, put into operation the Principle of Polarity and concentrate upon the opposite pole to that which you desire to suppress. Kill out the undesirable by changing its polarity." — The Kybalion.

> "Mind (as well as metals and elements) may be transmuted from state to state; degree to degree; condition to condition; pole to pole; vibration to vibration." — The Kybalion.

The Three Initiates illustrate the practical method: "By raising his mental vibrations along the line of Fear-Courage, he [a Fearful man] can be filled with the highest degree of Courage and Fearlessness." The analogy from Ch. XV: one does not sweep darkness from a room but opens the shutters to admit light. Concentrating upon the Positive pole causes the Negative to dissolve, rather than needing to directly combat the negative state.

Mental Induction (the influence of one mind upon another) is explained by the same mechanism: a trained Hermetic practitioner raises their own polarity and transmits that vibration to another person by induction, raising the other person's polarity.

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim bridges Ch. X (Polarity) and Ch. XV (Hermetic Axioms), and is presented throughout as the chief practical application of the Principle of Polarity. It is described as the key insight underlying "the results of many of the 'mental treatments'" practiced by Mental Science schools.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The text insists that the change is "not in the nature of a transmutation of one thing into another thing entirely different — but is merely a change of degree in the same things." Cross-category transmutation (e.g., Fear into Love, Courage into Hate) is explicitly ruled out. Results also depend on "persistent patience and practice": "the results obtained depend upon the persistent patience and practice of the student."

The Principle of Polarity is described as able to shift polarity but not to "destroy" the Principle of Rhythm — the pendulum continues to swing; only its effects on consciousness can be neutralized.
