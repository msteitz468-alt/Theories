## Claim Statement

At death the human "rushes up through the cosmic framework," surrendering at each of the **seven
planetary zones** the vice/energy it acquired there; stripped bare, it enters the **ogdoad**, hymns the
Father with the powers, and finally — "this is the final good for those who have received knowledge" — is
**"made god."**

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

C.H. I.25–26: "at the first zone surrendering the energy of increase and decrease; at the second evil
Machination… at the seventh zone the deceit that lies in ambush. And then, stripped of the effects of
the cosmic framework, the human enters the region of the ogdoad… and, having become powers, they enter
into god. This is the final good for those who have received knowledge: to be made god."

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The reward is reserved for "those who have received knowledge"; the unreverent are handed to "the
avenging demon," so the ascent is conditional on gnosis, not universal.
