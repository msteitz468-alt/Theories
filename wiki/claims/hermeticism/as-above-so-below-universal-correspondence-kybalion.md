## Claim Statement

The Kybalion holds that the Second Hermetic Principle — Correspondence — is not a poetic metaphor but a structural fact: because all phenomena in the Universe emanate from the same source (THE ALL) and are governed by the same ultimate reality, identical Laws and Principles operate on the Physical, Mental, and Spiritual Planes alike. This is what makes inference from one Plane to another valid. The axiom "As above, so below; as below, so above" encapsulates this cross-Plane identity and serves as a practical tool for understanding the unknown by reference to the known.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The Kybalion maxim opening Chapter VIII:

> "As above, so below; as below, so above."
> — THE KYBALION

The Three Initiates define the Principle: "The great Second Hermetic Principle embodies the truth that there is a harmony, agreement, and correspondence between the several planes of Manifestation, Life and Being. This truth is a truth because all that is included in the Universe emanates from the same source, and the same laws, principles, and characteristics apply to each unit, or combination of units of activity, as each manifests its own phenomena upon its own plane."

Chapter VIII concludes with an enumeration of all Seven Principles operating on every Plane: "The Principle of Mental Substance of course applies to all the planes, for all are held in the Mind of THE ALL. The Principle of Correspondence manifests in all, for there is a correspondence, harmony and agreement between the several planes. The Principle of Vibration manifests on all planes... The Principle of Polarity manifests on each plane... The Principle of Rhythm manifests on each Plane... The Principle of Cause and Effect manifests on each Plane... The Principle of Gender manifests on each Plane."

## Contexts Within the Source

The Three Initiates present Correspondence as the epistemological key of the entire Hermetic system. It licenses the analogical method throughout the Kybalion: the way a finite artist is "wrapped up" in a mental creation is used to illuminate how THE ALL is immanent in its Universe; the way a spinning wheel appears motionless at high speed is used to illustrate the vibration of Spirit. In each case, the Principle of Correspondence is what makes the analogy more than mere illustration — it makes it structurally valid.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The Kybalion explicitly qualifies the reach of Correspondence: it applies to "The Aspect of BECOMING" in THE ALL, but not to "The Aspect of BEING" — the deepest nature of THE ALL, where "all Laws are lost in LAW; all Principles merge into PRINCIPLE." The axiom "As above, so below" does not extend infinitely upward into THE ALL's innermost nature.

The text also notes that the Three Great Planes and their sub-divisions are "more or less artificial and arbitrary" — they shade into each other, and "no hard and fast division may be made between the higher phenomena of the Physical and the lower of the Mental."
