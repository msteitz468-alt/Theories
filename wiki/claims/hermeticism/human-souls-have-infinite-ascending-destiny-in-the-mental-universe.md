## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that individual human souls are not destroyed at death but continue ascending
through higher planes of existence across vast aeons of time, ultimately to be drawn back into THE
ALL in conscious At-One-ment at the end of the "Grand Cycle of Aeons." This teaching follows
directly from the Mental Universe doctrine: because humans exist within the Infinite Mind of THE ALL,
they are protected, destined for growth, and fundamentally imperishable.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Within the Father-Mother Mind, mortal children are at home."
> — THE KYBALION

> "There is not one who is Fatherless, nor Motherless in the Universe."
> — THE KYBALION

The Three Initiates state (Ch. V): "Death is not real, even in the Relative sense—it is but Birth
to a new life—and You shall go on, and on, and on, to higher and still higher planes of life, for
aeons upon aeons of time. The Universe is your home, and you shall explore its farthest recesses
before the end of Time. You are dwelling in the Infinite Mind of THE ALL, and your possibilities
and opportunities are infinite, both in time and space."

"And at the end of the Grand Cycle of Aeons, when THE ALL shall draw back into itself all of its
creations—you will go gladly, for you will then be able to know the Whole Truth of being At One
with THE ALL. Such is the report of the Illumined—those who have advanced well along The Path."

The text also places humanity within a larger cosmic hierarchy: "there are beings with powers and
attributes higher than Man has ever dreamed of the gods' possessing. And yet these beings were once
as you, and still lower—and you will be even as they, and still higher, in time, for such is the
Destiny of Man as reported by the Illumined."

## Contexts Within the Source

This eschatological teaching is presented as the practical and personal implication of the entire
Mental Universe doctrine. Dwelling in the Infinite Mind of [[THE ALL (Kybalion)]] means safety,
protection, and infinite potential. The passage closes Ch. V with reassurance: "rest calm and
serene—you are safe and protected by the Infinite Power of the FATHER-MOTHER MIND."

The teaching is attributed to "the Illumined" — those who have advanced on The Path — rather than
presented as the Three Initiates' personal discovery.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The Kybalion notes (with gentle irony) the danger of premature identification with THE ALL — those
who declare "I AM GOD" misread the doctrine. The At-One-ment at the end of the Aeons is a future
eschatological event, not a present reality accessible to ordinary humans. The text does not specify
the mechanism of ascent through planes or the duration of the "Grand Cycle."
