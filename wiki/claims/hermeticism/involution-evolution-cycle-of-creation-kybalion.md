## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that the creation of the Universe follows a two-phase cycle: Involution (also called the "Outpouring"), in which THE ALL projects its Will and its vibrations lower until the grossest possible Matter is manifested; and Evolution (the "Indrawing"), in which vibrations rise again and all created things move progressively Spiritward. At the end of the cycle, all is withdrawn back into THE ALL, and "the Spirit of each soul is not annihilated, but is infinitely expanded — the Created and the Creator are merged."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The Three Initiates describe the Involuntary stage: "the process consists of the lowering of Vibration until a very low degree of vibratory energy is reached, at which point the grossest possible form of Matter is manifested. This process is called the stage of Involution, in which THE ALL becomes 'involved,' or 'wrapped up,' in its creation."

On the return: "From the beginning of the Evolutionary or 'Indrawing' stage, there is manifested the Law of Individualization — that is, the tendency to separate into Units of Force, so that finally that which left THE ALL as unindividualized energy returns to its source as countless highly developed Units of Life, having risen higher and higher in the scale by means of Physical, Mental and Spiritual Evolution."

On the final absorption: "At the end of countless cycles of æons of time, THE ALL withdraws its Attention — its Contemplation and Meditation — of the Universe, for the Great Work is finished — and All is withdrawn into THE ALL from which it emerged."

The Kybalion characterizes all of cosmic time as instantaneous from the perspective of THE ALL: "the entire creation, including Involution and Evolution, of an Universe, is but 'as the twinkle of the eye' to THE ALL."

## Contexts Within the Source

This teaching appears within Chapter VII's broader discussion of how THE ALL is immanent in its creation. The Involution/Evolution model is the Kybalion's account of how THE ALL can be simultaneously "wrapped up" in Matter (immanent) while remaining THE ALL. The metaphor used is an artist or author who becomes so absorbed in a mental creation as to "almost forget his own existence."

The text explicitly aligns this with the Principle of Rhythm (the "pendulum-swing"): "the beginning of the Evolutionary stage is regarded as the beginning of the return swing of the pendulum of Rhythm — a 'coming home' idea being held in all of the Hermetic Teachings."

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The Kybalion stops short of claiming the cycle is fully understood: "The true Meaning of Life is not known to men on this plane — if, indeed, to any." It also resists treating Involution/Evolution as a mechanical necessity imposed on THE ALL: "we prefer to think of THE ALL as acting entirely FREE from any influence, internal as well as external."
