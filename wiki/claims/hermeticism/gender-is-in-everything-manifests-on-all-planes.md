## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that Masculine and Feminine Principles — understood not as biological sex but as universal creative polarities — are present and active at every level of existence: in subatomic particles, in chemistry, in gravitation, in the formation of crystals, and on the mental and spiritual planes. Gender, in this sense, is the engine of all creation and generation in the universe.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Gender is in everything; everything has its Masculine and Feminine Principles; Gender manifests on all planes." — THE KYBALION.

The Three Initiates distinguish Hermetic "Gender" from biological "Sex": the latter is "merely a manifestation of Gender on a certain plane of the Great Physical Plane—the plane of organic life." Gender derives from the Latin "to beget; to procreate; to generate; to create; to produce" and is asserted to be of universal application.

Physical evidence cited: atoms are formed by "the clustering of negative corpuscles around a positive one," which the text identifies as Feminine electrons clustering around a Masculine nucleus. The Cathode (formerly "Negative") pole is reframed as the Feminine Mother Principle — the source of the creative electron-streams that constitute matter. Gravitation, chemical affinity, and atomic attraction are all stated to be manifestations of Gender: "the very Law of Gravitation—that strange attraction by reason of which all particles and bodies of matter in the universe tend toward each other—is but another manifestation of the Principle of Gender."

## Contexts Within the Source

This is the opening thesis of Ch. XIII. The chapter deliberately sets up the distinction from Sex to protect the teaching from being conflated with "wild and fanciful, and often reprehensible, theories and teachings regarding Sex" by writers who had "acquired a smattering of the Hermetic Philosophy." The chapter then moves to the physical and scientific illustrations before transitioning in Ch. XIV to the Mental Plane application.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The Kybalion acknowledges that it cannot fully back its scientific claims: "we are unable to back up the same with scientific proof, for the reason that science has not as yet progressed thus far." The text presents its physical examples (electron behavior, crystal formation) as suggestive evidence rather than proof, and appeals to the reader to "examine the phenomena in the light of the Hermetic Teachings" and judge for themselves. The claim that gravitation is a manifestation of Gender is explicitly offered without scientific proof: "We cannot offer you scientific proof of this at this time."
