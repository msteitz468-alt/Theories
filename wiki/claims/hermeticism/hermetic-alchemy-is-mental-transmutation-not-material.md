## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that the true Hermetic art called "alchemy" was never primarily concerned with
the transmutation of physical metals. It was always a mental art: the transmutation of mental
states, conditions, and vibrations. The legends of the Philosopher's Stone and base metal into gold
were allegorical veils for an inner, psychological-spiritual practice.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Mind (as well as metals and elements) may be transmuted, from state to state; degree to degree;
> condition to condition; pole to pole; vibration to vibration. True Hermetic Transmutation is a
> Mental Art."
> — THE KYBALION

The Three Initiates state in Ch. I: "These teachings really constituted the basic principles of
'The Art of Hermetic Alchemy,' which, contrary to the general belief, dealt in the mastery of
Mental Forces, rather than Material Elements—the Transmutation of one kind of Mental Vibrations
into others, instead of the changing of one kind of metal into another. The legends of the
'Philosopher's Stone' which would turn base metal into Gold, was an allegory relating to Hermetic
Philosophy, readily understood by all students of true Hermeticism."

In Ch. III: "'Mental Transmutation' means the art of changing and transforming mental states, forms,
and conditions, into others… a form of practical Mystic Psychology." Because [[The Principle of Mentalism (Kybalion)]] holds that "the Universal is Mental in its nature," Mental Transmutation
becomes "the art of CHANGING THE CONDITIONS OF THE UNIVERSE, along the lines of Matter, Force and
Mind" — equated with "the 'Magic' of which the ancient writers had so much to say."

## Contexts Within the Source

The claim establishes the central practical program of the Kybalion. [[Mental Transmutation (Kybalion)]] is the applied art that flows from the metaphysical doctrine of [[THE ALL (Kybalion)]]
and [[The Principle of Mentalism (Kybalion)]]. The Kybalion further claims that all "psychic
phenomena," "mental influence," "mental science," and "new-thought phenomena" are imperfect,
incomplete versions of this same Hermetic art.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The text claims advanced Masters can also transmute gross physical conditions — "the control of the
elements of Nature; the production or cessation of tempests; the production and cessation of
earthquakes." This edge case partially complicates the "mental, not material" framing, though the
Kybalion insists even these powers "operate along the lines of the higher Mental Transmutation."

Modern practitioners of mental science are characterized as "quite ignorant compared to the ancient
masters" — the Kybalion frames itself as restoring the foundational knowledge those movements lack.
