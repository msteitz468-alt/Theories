## Claim Statement

Salvation in the Hermetica is **knowledge (gnosis)**: to know God is to be transformed by him and
ultimately made divine, whereas ignorance is a drunken sleep and the root of evil. Worship is contemplative
realization, not ritual transaction.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Poimandres commissions Hermes to wake humanity: "People, earthborn men, you who have surrendered
yourselves to drunkenness and sleep and ignorance of god, make yourselves sober" (C.H. I.27). The reborn
"recognizes himself as constituted of the intelligibles and rejoices" (C.H. XIII.10), and the ascent
ends in being "made god" for "those who have received knowledge" (C.H. I.26). C.H. X ("The Key") makes
knowledge of God the soul's highest good.

## Cross-source connections

This salvific gnosis is the engine behind the Kybalion's promise of mental mastery and the
[[The cosmic Mind of God (Forbidden Universe)|Forbidden Universe]] thesis that Hermetic knowledge confers
god-like power over nature; see [[God is Mind and knowledge (gnosis) divinizes the human (Hermetica)]].

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The knowledge is esoteric and graded — most treatises are "preparatory," and the highest is "a secret kept
in silence," not available to "the mob."
