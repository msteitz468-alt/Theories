## Claim Statement

The Kybalion teaches that while the Principle of Rhythm is invariable and cannot be destroyed, Hermetic Masters have discovered a method — called the **Law of Neutralization** — by which its effects on consciousness can be escaped. By rising mentally to a Higher Plane of Consciousness and polarizing at the Positive Pole of the Self (the "I Am" pole), the adept allows the backward swing of the mental pendulum to occur on the Unconscious Plane only, so that it does not register in waking awareness.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Rhythm may be neutralized by an application of the Art of Polarization." — The Kybalion.

The Three Initiates describe the Law of Neutralization in Ch. XI: "Its operations consist in the raising of the Ego above the vibrations of the Unconscious Plane of mental activity, so that the negative-swing of the pendulum is not manifested in consciousness, and therefore they are not affected. It is akin to rising above a thing and letting it pass beneath you."

In Ch. XV the axiom is expanded: "The advanced Hermetist polarizes himself at the Positive Pole of his Being—the 'I Am' pole rather than the pole of personality, and by 'refusing' and 'denying' the operation of Rhythm, raises himself above its plane of consciousness, and standing firm in his Statement of Being he allows the pendulum to swing back on the Lower Plane without changing his Polarity."

The caveat is explicit: "Remember, always, however, that you do not really destroy the Principle of Rhythm, for that is indestructible. You simply overcome one law by counter-balancing it with another, and thus maintain an equilibrium."

## Contexts Within the Source

This teaching appears first in Ch. XI and is reaffirmed in the practical axioms of Ch. XV. The Kybalion frames it as available "to a great degree" to all who have attained self-mastery — even unconsciously: "All individuals who have attained any degree of self-mastery, accomplish this, more or less unknowingly, and by refusing to allow their moods and negative mental states to affect them, they apply the Law of Neutralization." Masters do so consciously and to a far higher degree.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

The claim is explicitly limited: the Principle cannot be destroyed, only counterbalanced. The Law of Neutralization operates only on the Mental Plane (it does not exempt the adept from physical rhythms such as mortality). The teaching also implicitly assumes the doctrine of reincarnation: the full working-out of Rhythm's compensation is said to span "several lives."
