## Claim Statement

Icke asserts the **British House of Windsor is the "highest profile reptilian family on the planet,"** a Black Nobility bloodline of the [[Babylonian Brotherhood (Icke)]] whose genealogy runs back through the Venetian Black Nobility, the Phoenicians, Egypt and Sumer to the reptilian Anunnaki, and whose coronation rituals encode that origin.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

"The Windsors are the highest profile reptilian family on the planet and they operate at the heart of the global manipulation," though "still 'gofers' of a kind and not at the very top." Icke reads the **1953 coronation** as overt Brotherhood ritual: regalia stored overnight in the Jerusalem Chamber, the Stone of Destiny ("Jacob's Pillar"), the anointing oil "symbolic of the messeh fat used in Egypt by the Royal Court of the Dragon," and a crown set with "12 stones ... the same stones, in the same order" as the Breastplate of Aaron.

He traces the bloodline through **William of Orange** (who "signed into existence the Bank of England"), William the Conqueror, the Hanoverians and Saxe-Coburg-Gotha, asserting the European royal houses "are not families at all, they are one family." He repeatedly puns "El-lizard-birth" / "El-lizard-birth Bowes-Lyon," and via [[Arizona Wilder]] alleges the Queen, Queen Mother and Prince Philip **shape-shift into reptiles** at rituals (e.g. the 1981 "Awakening of the Bride," and rituals at Glamis and Balmoral, said to sit over "entrances ... into the inner-Earth where the physical reptilians live").

## Contexts Within the Source

The Windsor chapter is Icke's worked example of the whole thesis ("the House of Windsor encapsulates almost every element of this story"), connecting bloodline, ritual, Nazi-era ties (Prince Philip's Hesse relatives) and the reptilian claim.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

The reptilian/shape-shifting portion rests entirely on the uncorroborated testimony of ritual witnesses (Wilder, Christine Fitzgerald); Icke presents the genealogical material as documented but the reptilian conclusion as inference and witness claim.
