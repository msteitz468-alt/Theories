## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that the families who hold the "most important positions of power worldwide" are not human in the ordinary sense but are "biological software programs" — hybrid waveform information fields combining human DNA codes and Reptilian DNA codes. Their Reptilian dimension allows them to be more powerfully possessed by the Archontic counterfeit spirit than the general population. They can switch from their human waveform information field to their reptilian field, causing observers to perceive what appears to be physical shapeshifting. This shapeshifting occurs not in the body but in the decoding processes of the observer's brain. Their full-spectrum Archontic programming means they have no empathy, remorse, or shame — psychopathic traits — and this explains how global leaders can order mass killings, impose austerity while accumulating wealth, and otherwise behave in ways incomprehensible to empathic humans.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 6** states: "I have been writing for decades about specific bloodlines that do the bidding of the Archontically-possessed Reptilians/Greys within human society in the frequency band of the five senses. These 'bloodlines' can be accurately described as biological software programs which are a hybrid combination of human and Reptilian waveform information fields."

On shapeshifting: "What I have described there is the phenomenon of human-reptilian shapeshifting, which does not mean that a physical body changes into another physical body because there is no physical. Hybrid bloodlines or software are really hybrid waveform information fields ... The appearance of 'physical' shapeshifting only happens in the small region of the brain that decodes visual reality." Triggers listed: "ritualistic blood fests, by extreme states of fury ... and by using their own minds to switch the codes."

On Agent Smith analogy: "Agent Smith in the Matrix movies is a great analogy for what I am describing with regard to El-ite software bloodlines. Smith is a software program imposing control on humanity as dictated by the architect of the simulation. He and his fellow agents look like humans, but they are not human."

On psychopathic traits: Archontic traits listed as "obsessive compulsive behaviour; personal day-to-day rituals and superstitious acts; slavish conformance to old ways of doing things; ceremonial re-enactments; obeisance to precedent, as in legal, religious, cultural, and other matters and all manner of deceptions." The British Royal Family is cited as a primary example of ritual-driven bloodline behaviour.

The question "How could anyone order the bombing of countries to kill and maim the innocent?" is answered: "Those with no capacity for empathy or compassion because their software is programmed not to compute such basic humanity and expressions of Spirit."

On social parasitism: "What are banks, but a means to parasite off the labour of the same people by lending them money that doesn't exist – 'credit' – and charging them interest?"

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim updates and re-frames Icke's earlier (2001) bloodline thesis from *Children of the Matrix*. The 2018 version adds the simulation/software framing: bloodlines are not merely genetically special humans but are hybrid waveform constructs. The control hierarchy is explicit: Archontic distortion → Reptilian/Grey entities → human-reptilian hybrid El-ite → human lackeys. The Archontic El-ite occupy "government, banking, corporations, military, media and other institutions."
