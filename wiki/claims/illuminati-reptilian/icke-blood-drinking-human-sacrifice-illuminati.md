**Claim Statement**

The Illuminati bloodlines must consume human blood to maintain their human form; without mammalian blood the reptilian DNA codes reassert themselves. They also require the adrenaline released by extreme terror, hence their preference for traumatised victims. Fourth-dimensional reptilian "demons" simultaneously feed on the vibrational frequency of fear and negative emotion released during sacrifices. Ritual human sacrifice is presented as a regular occurrence involving named public figures including U.S. presidents, British prime ministers, and members of the British royal family. Bohemian Grove in Northern California is identified as one such ritual site.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

From Chapter 8 ("The blood drinkers"):

> "Insiders have told me that the reptilians need to drink human (mammalian) blood to maintain human form and stop their reptilian DNA codes from manifesting their true reptilian state... This includes people of the bloodline like George Bush, Al Gore, Bill Clinton, Henry Kissinger, the Rockefellers, Rothschilds, British prime ministers like Ted Heath, and the British royal family. Yes, including, indeed especially, the Queen and Queen Mother."

On Bohemian Grove:

> "I have been writing for years about the ancient Satanic rituals performed by the elite of the United States at a place called Bohemian Grove. This is 2,700 acres of secluded and guarded redwood forest in northern California... Alex Jones, an American journalist, documentary maker, and radio presenter, managed to get into the Grove during their ritual disguised in one of the hooded robes the participants wear."

Icke lists Bohemian Grove attendees: George H.W. Bush, George W. Bush, Al Gore, Ronald Reagan, Richard Nixon, Jimmy Carter, Gerald Ford, Eisenhower, Lyndon Johnson, Herbert Hoover, Teddy Roosevelt, the Rockefellers (David, Laurance, Nelson), Henry Kissinger, Mikhail Gorbachev, Walter Cronkite, Bing Crosby, Bob Hope, Ray Kroc, Mark Twain, John Muir.

From Chapter 14, on what the sacrifice achieves at the fourth-dimensional level:

> "At the same time, the lower fourth-dimensional 'demons' or psychic vampires absorb the deeply negative energy generated by such horrors. The vibrational frequency of negative emotion, like fear and terror, resonates to the lower fourth-dimensional range or density and so those emotions generate energy for the fourth-dimensional reptilians and demonic entities."

From a Phillip Eugene de Rothschild account (quoted in Chapter 8):

> "I can recall the Rockefellers and the Bushes attending rituals, but never having the supremacy to lead them... At the forefront of the rituals were [the royal families of Europe], Prince Philip at the pinnacle. He stands, like most of the contemporary European monarchy, in the Charlemagne, Merovingian, Aenean bloodline... Prince Philip...is the leading biological descendent of the 'reptilians'."

**Contexts Within the Source**

Icke relies on: (1) his own unnamed "insider" sources; (2) Phillip Eugene de Rothschild, identified as an unofficial Rothschild offspring and former Illuminati member; (3) mind-control survivors including Cathy O'Brien and "Arizona Wilder"; (4) the Alex Jones Bohemian Grove footage (available on Jones's website). He also claims historical support from the Book of Enoch's descriptions of Nefilim consuming blood and flesh.

The July timing of Bohemian Grove is connected to the heliacal rising of Sirius (July 23rd), which Icke identifies as the most sacred date in Illuminati astrology.

**Opposing Information Within the Source**

Icke acknowledges that his primary eyewitnesses are mind-control survivors whose testimony could be products of programming. He maintains the evidence base is too large and cross-cultural to explain away entirely.
