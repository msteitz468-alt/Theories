## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that Jesus Christ did not exist as a historical person but is "just another name for a recurring hero figure across the world each of them placed in a different historical and cultural setting long before the version called 'Jesus' appeared." Similarly, Muhammad's prophethood is described as resting entirely on faith (repeated assertion with no hard evidence) rather than documentation. Major religious texts — Bible, Koran, Talmud, Vedas — are presented as unknown-origin documents whose true authorship is "lost" and whose fake origins were invented as control mechanisms. The entire edifice of organised religion is framed as a form of intergenerational perception programming, functionally identical to any other downloaded software program, that captures human minds from birth and prevents connection with Infinite Awareness.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 18** states: "There are 2.2 billion Christians whose whole perception of life and reality is founded on the existence of 'Jesus' who did not exist." The basis given: "'Jesus' is just another name for a recurring hero figure across the world each of them placed in a different historical and cultural setting long before the version called 'Jesus' appeared."

On gospel authorship: "Not even the Church claims" that the disciples Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John wrote the gospels bearing their names. "Why do only Matthew and Luke mention the virgin birth? Did Mark and John have the night off?"

On Islam: "1.6 billion advocates for a religion founded on the 'teachings' of a man called Muhammad who claimed to be the prophet of god or Allah. How do Muslims know that and so justify calling him the Prophet Muhammad for 1,400 years? Well, it can't be based on the hard evidence and so it must be 'faith'. What is faith? It is a belief gleaned from constant repetition by other believers."

On religious identity as software: "If belief or faith is derived in and of itself without external influence then how come people from Muslim families overwhelmingly become Muslims and the same with Jews, Hindus, Christians, the lot of them? Had today's Christians been born into a Muslim culture they would now be Muslims." Religious identity is framed as a "downloaded software program."

Wahhabism origin: "The most vehement Christians would be the most vehement Muslims ... Beliefs of modern Muslim extremists originate from the 18th century and a Dönmeh Jewish man Muhammad ibn ͑Abd al-Wahhab, who the British Empire teamed up with the founder of their Dönmeh Saudi dynasty."

On imposing religion on children: "The way Jewish parents impose their faith and limitless behaviour laws on their children is child abuse in my opinion and the same with Muslims, Christians and every other religious tyranny."

Science, academia, medicine, and finance as religions: "science, academia, medicine, finance and all the rest are religions every bit as much as Islam, Judaism or Christianity" — all based on authority, repetition, and suppression of dissent rather than open-ended inquiry.
