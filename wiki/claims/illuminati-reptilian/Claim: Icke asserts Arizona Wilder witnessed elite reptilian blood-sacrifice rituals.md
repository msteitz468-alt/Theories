## Claim Statement

Icke asserts that **Arizona Wilder** (formerly Jennifer Ann Greene), a deprogrammed mind-control survivor and self-described Brotherhood "Mother Goddess," personally conducted and witnessed **Satanic blood-sacrifice rituals at which royal and political figures shape-shifted into reptiles**.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Icke says Wilder "performed rituals for the Brotherhood at the highest level" and told him "without human blood the reptilians cannot hold human form in this dimension." He relays that she "officiated at Satanic rituals at **Glamis Castle** ... and also at **Balmoral**," and "described to my friend the inside of many secret and underground facilities ... accurate every time."

Specific claims he attributes to her:
- She "saw the shape-shifting Johnson [LBJ] at sacrifice rituals," and saw Al Gore shape-shift into a reptilian.
- She witnessed the **"Awakening of the Bride"** ritual at Clarence House in early July 1981, at which the Queen Mother, Queen, Prince Philip, Earl Spencer, Charles and Camilla were present, and "Prince Philip and the Queen Mother then shape-shifted into reptiles to show Diana who they really were."
- **Guy de Rothschild** (as "Dr Barrington") tried to reprogramme her and said he had been at the Pont de l'Alma tunnel to "steal Diana's soul."
- Catherine de Medici was "a previous incarnation of the Queen Mother."

## Contexts Within the Source

Wilder's testimony is the evidentiary backbone Icke uses to connect the reptilian, royal, and Diana threads; he stresses it was recorded on audio/video and "continually confirmed" by other survivors (Cathy O'Brien, Brice Taylor, Christine Fitzgerald).

## Opposing Information Within the Source

The testimony is uncorroborated by any independent or physical evidence; Icke's "verification" consists of mutual agreement among other claimed survivors and his friend's checks of facility descriptions. He concedes the material will "attract ridicule."
