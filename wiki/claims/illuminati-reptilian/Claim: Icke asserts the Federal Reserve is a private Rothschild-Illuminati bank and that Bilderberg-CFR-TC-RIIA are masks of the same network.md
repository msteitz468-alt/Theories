**Claim Statement**

The U.S. Federal Reserve is not a government institution but a private cartel of banks controlled by the Rothschild banking dynasty and the Illuminati. It was established in 1913 and has never produced audited accounts. All national government debt in the U.S. consists of loans from this private entity, on which the public pays interest for money created from nothing. The Bilderberg Group, Council on Foreign Relations (CFR), Trilateral Commission (TC), and Royal Institute of International Affairs (RIIA) are all "masks on the same Illuminati face," co-ordinated by the Round Table secret society. Together they implement the same policies through apparently unconnected national governments, banks, and militaries.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

On the Federal Reserve:

> "The Federal Reserve is neither federal nor has any reserve. It is a cartel of private banks controlled from Europe, i.e. the Rothschilds and the Illuminati. It has not produced audited accounts since it was formed in 1913... When the government wants to borrow money it issues a government 'bond' or 'IOU' to the bank, say for a billion dollars. The bank then prints a billion dollars or simply creates it on a screen and this costs the 'Fed' nothing... From that moment the US taxpayer begins to pay the privately-owned 'Fed' interest on a billion dollars."

On the Bilderberg / Round Table network:

> "The Bilderberg Group (Bil) is part of a network of organisations, which are all masks on the same Illuminati face. They are controlled by the same people and have different roles to play in advancing the agenda of global centralisation. The others are the Royal Institute of International Affairs in London (RIIA), the Council on Foreign Relations (CFR) in the United States, and the Trilateral Commission (TC), which operates in the United States, Europe, and the Far East."

> "The Secretary-General of NATO, the head of the biggest military force in the world, is appointed by the Bilderberg Group, a private organisation that 99.9999% of the human race has never heard of."

Icke names six consecutive NATO Secretaries-General (Luns, Carrington, Claes, Woener, Solana, Robertson) as all Bilderberg members. He similarly names: James Wolfensohn (World Bank president, Rothschild partner), WTO heads Peter D. Sutherland and Renato Ruggerio, Bill Clinton, Tony Blair, Gerhard Schroeder, and multiple past UK and German prime ministers — all identified as Bilderberg members.

On the Kennedy/Lincoln connection:

> "Both President Kennedy and President Lincoln began to issue interest-free money. What else do these two men have in common?"

Icke argues the UK's Tony Blair (elected 1997) was installed to advance European monetary union and was confirmed as Illuminati through: his first policy announcement giving interest-rate control to the Bank of England (identified as Rothschild-controlled), and the support of nominally "opposing" Conservatives for the same policy.
