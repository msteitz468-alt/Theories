## Claim Statement

David Icke asserts that MKUltra — the CIA mind control programme — was not an aberration but the foundation of an ongoing and expanded system of creating mind-controlled human assets for the following purposes: (1) producing assets including children who would not remember their abuse or famous abusers; (2) creating lone-nut "assassins" explainable as random individuals; (3) programming targets to carry out terrorist attacks blamed on others. Project Monarch (an MKUltra offshoot) creates mind-controlled sex slaves for prominent figures including US presidents, and its assets include major names in Hollywood and the music industry. The programme was built on Nazi trauma-based mind control techniques carried to the US by Josef Mengele and 1,600 other German scientists, doctors, and technicians under Project Paperclip — which also created NASA.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 7** begins with Arizona Wilder (Jennifer Greene), described as a mind-controlled asset who provided Icke with testimony about elite Satanism and the Reptilian connection.

MKUltra's scope: "The programme involved at least 80 institutions in the United States and Canada with colleges, universities, hospitals, prisons and the pharmaceutical cabal." CIA Director Stansfield Turner testified in 1977 it was "to explore 'the use of biological and chemical materials in altering human behaviour'."

Nazi connection: "'MK' stands for mind control, but they used the German spelling in deference to the Nazis who ran the operation." Josef Mengele ("Angel of Death") is identified as an MKUltra operative under the alias "Dr Green," working in the US after WWII, confirmed by Arizona Wilder's testimony. One location cited: China Lake Naval Weapons Station, California. Mengele is stated to have been "among 1,600 German mind controllers, geneticists, scientists, engineers and technicians taken secretly to the United States after the war under a US Intelligence/Vatican network operation called Project Paperclip."

Hitler survival claim: "Adolf Hitler (who didn't die at the end of the war)" and Mengele were "relocated to Argentina in South America."

NASA origin: "Project Paperclip personnel who were helped to escape from Germany created NASA (NAZI)." Werner von Braun, "a member of the Nazi Party ... designed the Saturn V booster rockets for the Apollo Moon programme." He also "was the brains behind the deadly V-2 rockets that targeted Britain."

Project Monarch: "An offshoot of MKUltra was called Project Monarch and named after the monarch butterfly. Monarch involves the creation of mind-controlled sex slaves for the rich and famous, including American presidents." Major entertainment celebrities are described as Monarch assets, identified by butterfly imagery and one-eye-covered publicity photos.

Trauma-based methodology: Children targeted before age six while "brain pathways are still forming." Trauma causes dissociation, creating separate personality compartments. President Gerald Ford (described as connected to MKUltra) appointed Vice-President Nelson Rockefeller to head the "inquiry" to suppress the full truth.

Rockefeller family funded eugenics: "The Rockefeller family funded a whole floor of a German university for Hitler's race purity 'expert' Ernst Rudin to pursue his eugenics programmes."

## Contexts Within the Source

Icke presents this claim as partly documented (the Church Committee's 1970s exposures; published MKUltra records after FOIA) and partly based on witness testimony from Arizona Wilder and unnamed others he encountered during his 1990s research. He explicitly states the public 1970s revelations represented only a fraction of the true programme: "I say 'were' – but all this continues today under other names."
