## Claim Statement

Icke's resolution and the source of the title: humanity can break out of the perceptual "Mind Prison" / soul trap by awakening **expanded awareness** — shifting identity from the program-bound, left-brain "Body-Mind" to the infinite consciousness ("the Divine Spark") it really is — with the **heart** (not the head) as the gateway. This awakening is "the next stage of human awareness," the "Reveal."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Icke contrasts the entrapping "perceptual prison cell of 'I know'" with the liberating humility of "I don't know — so what don't I know?", and associates expanded awareness with right-brain "maverick" perception over left-brain literalism. The closing chapter, "Heart of the Matter," locates the escape in heart-centred awareness rather than intellect. The book is positioned as the culminating third volume of his "Reality Trilogy" (after *The Trap* and *The Dream*).
