## Claim Statement

The Trap's foundational claim: "We are NOT human. We are consciousness having a brief experience called 'human.'" Icke argues that "'Human' is the Great Illusion and the foundation of our mass servitude" — humanity is infinite consciousness ("Infinite Possibility") that has been manipulated into a "myopic" perceptual "reality bubble," and the purpose of life is to **remember** what we truly are, which "will set us free."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Icke writes that "humanity has been manipulated into a reality bubble through thousands of years of what we call 'time' to see self and life in a myopia so small… that only the basic functions of survival constitute the perception of 'living'." He frames his own role as a mission "to wrest control of human perception from the clutches of a deeply evil (inverted) force," dating his awakening to 1990–91 ("my consciousness exploded awake"), and insists "the intellectual mind must be the servant of expanded states of consciousness," not the master.
