## Claim Statement

Icke argues the COVID-19 era was the Cult's engineered "New World" — that the "pandemic was deliberately planned" (a "fake pandemic"), that lockdowns and "fake vaccines" were instruments of control and harm, and that the WEF's "Great Reset" / "Build Back Better" agenda is the blueprint for a transhumanist, total-surveillance "Hunger Games Society."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Icke (writing in 2022) asserts the "'pandemic' was deliberately planned and the fake vaccines were killing" and injuring people; he points to rising 2021 mortality/insurance data, "self-replicating synthetic genetic material" and "crystalline substances" he says are in the vials, and Pfizer's pre-rollout awareness of "Vaccine-Associated Enhanced Disease." He names **Klaus Schwab** and the WEF as Cult operatives behind the **Great Reset** ("Build Back Better," "own nothing"), reads it through his long-standing **Problem-Reaction-Solution** technique (and a "No-Problem-Reaction-Solution" variant), and casts political leaders as Cult "handlers'" front-men.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Icke frames these as vindicated predictions and pattern-inference, conceding no contrary evidence; the claims are recorded here strictly as he asserts them, not as established fact. (Icke's "fake pandemic / fake vaccine" framing is his own.)
