## Claim Statement

Icke asserts that the assassination of President John F. Kennedy was a **Masonic/Brotherhood ritual sacrifice** — the ancient "Killing of the King" (or Killing of the Sun King) — staged in an "outdoor temple of the Sun" at Dealey Plaza, encoded with Freemasonic symbolism and numerology.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Icke (drawing on James Shelby Downard and Bill Cooper) argues Dealey Plaza is "a Sun temple" shaped like a "pyramid with the capstone missing," officially named for 33rd-degree Freemason George Bannerman Dealey ("Dealey means 'goddess line'"). He claims: Kennedy "was shot just after noon when the Sun was 'most high'"; his wounds (back, head, throat) match those of the Masonic legend figure **Hiram Abiff**; Jackie represented "the goddess, the Queen of Love and Beauty" and JFK "the scapegoat Sun King"; and the site sits near "the 33rd degree of latitude" (the 33rd Masonic degree). He stacks numerological "synchronicities" (the 11/22 date as the anniversary of the 1307 papal bull against the Templars; the Lincoln–Kennedy coincidences; gate 28 / "Beale" sun-god numerology) and reads the Dealey Plaza obelisk as "the penis of Osiris."

Quoting Downard: "Masonry ... in the JFK assassination ... took great risks in order to make this heinous act correspond to the ancient fertility oblation of the Killing of The King."

## Contexts Within the Source

The JFK case is presented as the explicit template for the Diana claim ("For Lee Harvey Oswald ... read Henri Paul"): both, Icke argues, are centuries-planned ritual murders by the same network.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Icke notes Robert Anton Wilson called Downard's theory "the most absurd ... Illuminati theory of them all" — which Icke takes as evidence Downard "must definitely be on to something" rather than as a rebuttal.
