## Claim Statement

Icke argues that perceived physical reality is not solid but a **holographic simulation** — a "Plasma Matrix" — and that "human life" as we experience it is an illusion. The body and brain *decode* this information field into the apparent solid world, much as a device decodes Wi-Fi; "our reality is holographic rather than solid."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The opening "Mind Prison" chapter holds that "we are fed a constant stream of illusion and our illusion-infested thoughts and emotions then manifest the illusory experience that we falsely believe to be real." Icke describes the "body/simulation decoding sequence" working like Wi-Fi, and reads the body as "60 percent water… a decoded holographic expression." He says he has spent "35 years" scanning the illusion "for patterns" to establish that it is an illusion, what creates it, and what lies behind it.
