**Claim Statement**

The Anunnaki — the "gods" of Sumerian cuneiform tablets — were a physically real reptilian extraterrestrial race. Their Sumerian names (AN.UNNAK.KI = "Those who from Heaven to Earth Came"; DIN.GIR = "The Righteous Ones of the Blazing Rockets") reveal their extraterrestrial nature. They interbred with humanity and the Nordic alien race to create the hybrid bloodlines that ruled Sumer, Egypt, Babylon, and all subsequent empires. Zechariah Sitchin's translations document this, though Icke disputes Sitchin's refusal to acknowledge their reptilian nature.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**

From Chapter 2:

> "Central to these Sumerian accounts were the 'gods' the Sumerians called the Anunna ('Sons of An'). Their later Semitic names were AN.UNNAK.KI ('Those who from Heaven to Earth Came') and DIN.GIR ('The Righteous Ones of the Blazing Rockets'). They are best known as the Anunnaki and so I shall use that term in the book. The Anunnaki, as we shall see, were a reptilian race from the stars."

On Sitchin:

> "The Sumerian tablets describe, according to the author and translator, Zechariah Sitchin, a collision between the moons of a planet they called Nibiru and one orbiting between the present Jupiter and Mars. The debris from this stupendous collision, Sitchin's translations say, created what the Sumerians called 'the Great Band Bracelet' — the asteroid belt."

Icke notes a key disagreement with Sitchin's approach: "it is worth remembering, however, that for whatever reason, Sitchin will simply not accept the existence of a reptilian or serpent race, despite the scale of evidence."

From Chapter 3 (Ruled by the Gods):

Icke describes three main extraterrestrial physical types operating on Earth: (1) "white race or the 'blue-eyed blonds'"; (2) reptilians "of various expressions"; (3) "greys" of modern UFO folklore. He also mentions "insectoids" and an "advanced black race." The reptilians are the dominant Illuminati faction:

> "This reptilian race is the dominating force behind the Illuminati (at one level anyway), but with considerable involvement from the greys and some elements of the extraterrestrial white race or 'Nordics' as they are known in UFO research circles."

He describes the Dogon tribe of Mali (per Robert Temple's *The Sirius Mystery*) as evidence of ancient ET contact: they possessed accurate astronomical knowledge about the Sirius star system — including Sirius B (invisible to the naked eye) and Sirius C (confirmed by astronomers in 1995) — which they attributed to amphibious "serpent-featured" beings from Sirius.

**Contexts Within the Source**

Icke situates the Anunnaki as the primary post-cataclysm force that rebuilt civilisation after the destruction of Atlantis/Lemuria (~10,000 BC), establishing Sumer as the "re-start" of their project. He relies on Sitchin's translations while extending them to include the reptilian identification Sitchin avoided. He also cites R.A. Boulay (*Flying Serpents and Dragons*) and Mark Amaru Pinkham (*The Return of the Serpents of Wisdom*) for additional support.

**Opposing Information Within the Source**

The source notes that Sitchin — the primary academic translator of the Sumerian tablets used by Icke — "will simply not accept the existence of a reptilian or serpent race." This is the most significant internal tension: Icke's reptilian identification goes beyond what his most cited translator supports.
