## Claim Statement

The text states that after fleeing Atlantis to Egypt by "spaceship," Thoth buried the craft deep beneath the rock and "erected a marker in the form of a lion yet like unto man" — i.e., the Sphinx — over it, to be retrieved "when need shall arise," and prophesies future "invaders… from out of the deep" against whom the awakened wise should bring forth the ship.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Deep 'neath the rocks, I buried my spaceship, waiting the time when man might be free. Over the spaceship, erected a marker in the form of a lion yet like unto man. There 'neath the image rests yet my spaceship… Know ye, O man, that far in the future, invaders shall come from out of the deep. Then awake, ye who have wisdom. Bring forth my ship and conquer with ease." (Tablet 5)

The secret is said to be reachable via the pyramid — "deep in the passage that ends in a wall" — opened by "the KEY of the SEVEN."

## Contexts Within the Source

Earlier in Tablet 1 Thoth describes rising into the sky in "the great ship of the Master" during the Atlantean evacuation. This claim ties the Sphinx, the Great Pyramid, and the [[The Seven Lords of the Cycles]] together as "keystones." See [[The Great Pyramid of Giza]].
