## Claim Statement

The tablets give techniques for leaving the body at will — for conscious death ("willing his Death as he wills his own life," as "the Avatar" does), for retaining the memory of past lives across death, and for astral travel to the higher planes, Amenti, and "Shamballa," each unlocked by spoken "words of power."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

- **Conscious death** (Tablet 13): lie with head eastward, hands at the solar plexus, divide consciousness north and south, and "forth from thy form will thy silver spark fly, upward… to the Sun of the morning."
- **Past-life memory** (Tablet 13): at death hold "the flame of thy Soul" at "the place between thine eyebrows… until the fingers of Death grasp thy Soul," so "the memory of all be retained."
- **Astral travel / Amenti / Shamballa** (Tablet 15): the words "Dor-E-Lil-La" (pass the barriers of space for six hours), "La-Um-I-L-Gano" (free from flesh), "Mekut-El-Shab-El Hale-Sur-Ben-El-Zabrut Zin-Efrim-Quar-El" (be called to Amenti), and the "Secret of Secrets" — "Edom-El-Ahim-Sabbert-Zur Adom" — to pass "the Twelve and the One" to Shamballa, "the place where my Brothers live in the darkness."

## Contexts Within the Source

Tablet 15 frames man as "threefold in nature, physical, astral and mental," with nine qualities plus a fourfold Spiritual Self — "thirteen in one, the mystical number"; physical channels are blood, "magnetism," and "Akasa." See [[Man is space-born — a star bound to a body]].
