## Claim Statement

The text describes the Halls of Amenti as a realm deep in Earth's heart, "far 'neath the islands of sunken Atlantis," created by the Children of Light — beings who descended from "the space-dust" and took human bodies to free mankind from "bondage." Thirty-two Children of Light sit on thrones around a central radiant "Flower" / "ray of great potence," periodically re-quickened by its light ("one hundred years out of each thousand"), their souls incarnating among men as teachers.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Deep in Earth's heart lie the Halls of Amenti… Halls of the Dead and halls of the living, bathed in the fire of the infinite ALL." (Tablet 2)

"Thirty and two were there of the children, sons of Light who had come among men." A soul that "has grown from the darkness… free is he made of the Halls of Amenti, free of the Flower of Light and of Life."

## Contexts Within the Source

Amenti is also where Thoth was made immortal and "free… not bound by death to the circle of life" (Tablet 1–2), and where the [[The Seven Lords of the Cycles]] preside. Tablet 13 reframes the Flower as Earth's living spirit-source. See [[Halls of Amenti]], [[The Flower of Life]].
