## Claim Statement

The text recounts that Atlantis (ten island-kingdoms) was destroyed because proud Atlantean adepts "delved into the forbidden" and "opened the gateway that led to below," threatening to bring "great woe" to Earth. The Dweller, [[The Dweller of Unal (Horlet)]], roused from his detached trance ("AGWANTI"), called on the powers wielded by the Seven Lords, "changed the Earth's balance," and sank Atlantis beneath the waves — sparing only Unal and the Dweller's people.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Deep delved they into the forbidden, opened the gateway that led to below… Called HE then on the powers the Seven Lords wielded; changed the Earth's balance. Down sank Atlantis beneath the dark waves. Shattered the gateway that had been opened." (Tablet 5)

Tablet 1 gives a parallel account: the Dweller "speaking The Word, calling the power… Over the world then broke the great waters," leaving "only the Temple of Light… standing on the great mountain on UNDAL."

## Contexts Within the Source

After the cataclysm Thoth was commanded to gather "the sons of Atlantis" and flee to "the people of the rock caves… the land of the Children of KHEM" (Egypt). See [[Event: The sinking of Atlantis]], [[Atlantis (Undal and Keor)]].
