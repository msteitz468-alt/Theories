## Claim Statement

Seven Lords "from the Space-Times above," titled by the numbers **THREE, FOUR, FIVE, SIX, SEVEN, EIGHT, NINE**, sit within the Flower in Amenti and guide the destiny of mankind, beneath a "Veiled Presence, Lord of Lords, the infinite Nine." Each Lord has a distinct office; the Logos issues from them.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Tablet 3 assigns offices: THREE "holds the key of all hidden magic, creator… of the halls of the Dead"; FOUR "looses the power… Lord of Life"; FIVE is "the Lord of all magic… Key to The Word"; SIX "Lord of Light, the hidden pathway"; SEVEN "Lord of the vastness, master of Space"; EIGHT "orders the progress; weighs and balances"; NINE "the father… forming and changing from out of the formless."

Tablet 6 gives their secret names invoked in ritual: "UNTANAS, QUERTAS, CHIETAL, and GOYANA, HUERTAL, SEMVETA—ARDAL."

## Contexts Within the Source

The Lords are "Suns of the cycles, Masters of Wisdom… brought from the future of infinity's end." Their names and numbers are used in the protective magic of Tablet 6 and the Amenti-entry rite of Tablet 15. See [[The Seven Lords of the Cycles]].
