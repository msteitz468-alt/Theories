## Claim Statement

In Tablet 12, having "conquered the Law of time-space," Thoth prophesies a future "great warfare." The signs: man "again shall conquer the ocean and fly in the air on wings like the birds" and "learned to harness the lightning" — then "the time of warfare begin." The **Dark Brothers** "open the warfare between Light and the night"; "Nation shall rise against nation using the dark forces," and "Weapons of force shall wipe out the Earth-man until half of the races of men shall be gone." Then "the **Sons of the Morning**" come forth and give their edict to men to "cease from thy striving against thy brother." Men cease their striving; "the ancient home of my people" — **Atlantis** — "shall… rise from its place beneath the dark ocean waves"; and "the Age of Light" unfolds, in which "the Brothers of Light rule the people" and men become "Children of Light."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Then shall there come unto man the great warfare that shall make the Earth tremble and shake in its course. Aye, then shall the Dark Brothers open the warfare between Light and the night." (Tablet 12)

> "When man again shall conquer the ocean and fly in the air on wings like the birds; when he has learned to harness the lightning, then shall the time of warfare begin… Weapons of force shall wipe out the Earth-man until half of the races of men shall be gone." (Tablet 12)

> "Then shall come forth the Sons of the Morning and give their edict to the children of men, saying: O men, cease from thy striving against thy brother." (Tablet 12)

> "Then shall the ancient home of my people rise from its place beneath the dark ocean waves. Then shall the Age of Light be unfolded… Then shall the Brothers of Light rule the people." (Tablet 12)

## Contexts Within the Source

The prophecy is qualified by Tablet 12's framing doctrine: "all of the future is an open book to him who can read," yet the future "is not fixed" but "varies with man's free will" as cause produces effect. After delivering the prophecy Thoth announces his departure to dwell in the Halls of Amenti "in the future when Light shall come again to man," and gives the name of power for summoning him: "Chequetet, Arelich, Volmalites."
