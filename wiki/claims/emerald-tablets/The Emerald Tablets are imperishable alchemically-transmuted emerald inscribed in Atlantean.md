## Claim Statement

Doreal describes the tablets' physical nature: twelve tablets of "emerald green," formed by alchemical transmutation, imperishable and chemically inert ("in effect, the atomic and cellular structure is fixed… they violate the material law of ionization"), inscribed with ancient Atlantean characters that "respond to attuned thought waves, releasing the associated mental vibration in the mind of the reader," and bound with hoops of a "golden-colored alloy."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "They consist of twelve tablets of emerald green, formed from a substance created through alchemical transmutation. They are imperishable, resistant to all elements and substances." (Preface)

Doreal advises the tablets "should be read, not once, but a hundred times for only thus can the true meaning be revealed."
