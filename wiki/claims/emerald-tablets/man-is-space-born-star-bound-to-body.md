## Claim Statement

The tablets teach that humans are fundamentally beings of light and star-origin — "man is a star bound to a body" (Tablet 3) and "man is truly space-born, a Sun of the Sun, a child of the stars" (Tablet 4) — temporarily trapped by a "veil of night," whose destiny is to free the inner flame and merge with the universal Light/ALL, governed throughout by immutable Law and "Order and balance."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Man is a star bound to a body, until in the end, he is freed through his strife." (Tablet 3)

> "Not of the earth are ye—earthy, but child of the Infinite Cosmic Light… Thy bodies are nothing but planets revolving around their central suns." (Tablet 4)

Thoth recounts freeing his soul from the body and traveling through space, finding "Law, in all of its beauty" among other inhabited worlds, including beings who "constructed… a planet" by condensing the ether through will.

## Contexts Within the Source

Tablet 4 supplies a soul-projection technique (silence, fasting, visualization, "vibrate back and forth with thy power") to free the soul, prefiguring the death/projection methods of Tablets 13 and 15.
