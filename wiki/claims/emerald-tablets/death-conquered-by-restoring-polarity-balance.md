## Claim Statement

The text teaches that death occurs only when the body's polarity balance is "shaken" — "one pole has been lost" — and that the Masters' secret of long life is to restore that balance, drawing on the balance of the living Earth (whose spirit is the [[The Flower of Life]]). It prescribes a concrete daily practice to "hold off the fingers of Death" and freshen the aged body.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Know that the secret of life in Amenti is the secret of restoring the balance of poles." (Tablet 13)

The practice: lie one hour daily with the head to the north (positive pole), holding consciousness "from the chest to the head"; one hour with the head to the south, holding thought "from chest to the feet"; "hold thou in balance once in each seven" days. "Aye, if thou be old, thy body will freshen and thy strength will become as a youth's." Neglecting it after passing one hundred years "will mean the coming of Death."
