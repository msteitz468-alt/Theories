## Claim Statement

The tablets describe an "age-old" cosmic war between Light and Darkness: hidden "Brothers of Darkness" (dark adepts) who secretly bind and enslave human souls through dark magic and dream-intrusion, opposed by the "Brothers of Light" (the Children of Light) who free men. Light must ultimately conquer. The text supplies counter-techniques and warns the dark brothers especially target those advanced on the path of Light.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Banded together as in order, BROTHERS OF DARKNESS, they through the ages, antagonist they to the children of men… Into man's mind-space reach the DARK BROTHERS." (Tablet 6)

Defenses given: examine whether a dark feeling is one's own (banish it) or externally projected; "Send through thy body a wave of vibration… from thine head to thy foot"; and a circle-and-invocation rite calling the Seven Lords by name ("UNTANAS, QUERTAS, CHIETAL, and GOYANA, HUERTAL, SEMVETA—ARDAL") to "free me from darkness and fill me with LIGHT."

## Contexts Within the Source

The dualism resolves in Tablet 15's monism: "Darkness and light are both of one nature… Darkness is disorder. Light is Order. Darkness transmuted is light." See [[The Brothers of Darkness]], [[The Children of Light]].
