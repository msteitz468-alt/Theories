## Claim Statement

Doreal supplies the tablets' chain of custody and his own role: pyramid priests carried the tablets from Egypt c. 1300 B.C. as a "talisman" of authority, emigrating to South America, where the Maya placed them beneath the altar of a Yucatán sun temple. Doreal — claiming connection to the "Great White Lodge" which works through the pyramid priesthood — says he was instructed to recover and return them to the Great Pyramid, translated them in 1925, and was given "permission to retain a copy."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "The writer (who has a connection with the Great White Lodge which also works through the pyramid priesthood) was instructed to recover and return to the Great Pyramid the ancient tablets. This… was accomplished… This was done in 1925 and only now has permission been given for part to be published."

Doreal also claims the Great Pyramid "has been and still is a temple of initiation… Jesus, Solomon, Apollonius and others were initiated there."

## Contexts Within the Source

This provenance narrative gives the work its claimed authenticity. Doreal anticipates skepticism: "It is expected that many will scoff. Yet the true student will read between the lines and gain wisdom."
