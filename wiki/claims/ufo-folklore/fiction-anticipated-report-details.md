## Claim Statement

Vallée points out that works of fiction anticipated specific later UFO report features: Arthur Koestler's 1933 play *Twilight Bar* contains the first UFO-caused blackout, and Bernard Newman's 1950 novel *The Flying Saucer* the first UFO car-ignition failure.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He calls the coincidence between these imaginative works and the actual details later reported by the public 'remarkable,' opening 'the way to unlimited speculation' — then deliberately stops speculating.

## Contexts Within the Source

Cited as evidence the phenomenon's content tracks human imagination and technology.
