## Claim Statement

Vallée argues that the Western cattle-mutilation wave is *real* and surgically precise — not
the work of predators or "delusionary spirals" — and that, whatever its source, it functions as
a **message of terror** aimed at the public and especially at the military and intelligence
communities, fitting the same manipulation pattern as the UFO phenomenon.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He cites ~700 mutilations in 15 western states in an 18-month period (180+ in Colorado in 1975),
with consistent features: blood drained, specific organs removed (eyes, ears, tongue, genitals,
rectum), no tracks, carcasses apparently dropped from the air, and use of standard autopsy
technique (the **Rokitansky** procedure — "Coyotes and delusionary spirals do not use the
Rokitansky technique"). He quotes officials (Colorado Gov. Richard Lamm: "one of the greatest
outrages in the history of the western cattle industry"; a coroner: "an expert job") and
investigator **Frederick W. Smith**, whom he credits with the only interpretation that "makes
sense": "Someone has been delivering a message." A 1,500-lb buffalo was mutilated by the
Cheyenne Mountain (NORAD) zoo, and a cow at the NORAD entrance gate.

Vallée notes "few weapons are as effective as terror," recalls the 1918 Munich Thule-Society
mutilation hoax, and observes the organs taken are "concerned with communication and with
reproduction" — "good surgeons, but also good psychologists." In Ch. 10 he reports the recanted
Bankston/Dugan "Satanic cult" confessions and the documented military use of faked
vampirism/"evil eye" mutilation in the Philippines and Vietnam.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Vallée is candid that "I cannot give a conclusive answer": he concedes some cases are predators
or wolves, that evidence "may have been planted to mislead us," and that "I do not have any
proof that a single group of men is responsible." He lists three competing explanations
(natural/predator, secret human organization, UFO occupants) without endorsing one fully.
