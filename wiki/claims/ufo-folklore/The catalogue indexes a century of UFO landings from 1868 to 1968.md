## Claim Statement

The appendix presents 'A Century of UFO Landings (1868–1968),' an indexed catalogue of landing / close-encounter cases compiled from cross-indexed international sources, of which about 35% include descriptions of occupants.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Vallée describes building the index from 1961, drawing on Aimé Michel, Guy Quincy, the *Flying Saucer Review* 'Humanoids' special issue, and re-examined ATIC files (witness names deleted per official procedure), with the 1954 French/Italian wave the best-documented section.

## Contexts Within the Source

Offered as a research tool, with an explicit warning that inclusion implies no claim of physical reality.
