## Claim Statement

Vallée argues the periodic-yet-unpredictable pattern of UFO waves matches B. F. Skinner's most effective "schedule of reinforcement" — the kind that produces slow but **irreversible** learning. On this reading the phenomenon is conditioning humanity, wave after wave, toward a new world-view and a "higher image of man," and — like Skinner's apparatus with a rat — it *must* mislead its subjects for the conditioning to work.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Vallée cites Ferster and Skinner's *Patterns of Reinforcement* (ONR-funded): "the best schedule of reinforcement is one that combines periodicity with unpredictability. Learning is then slow but continuous… And it is irreversible." He plots a 1947–1962 curve of UFO activity (trend removed) and asks "Is this a schedule of reinforcement?" "If the phenomenon is forcing us through a learning curve then it MUST mislead us… Man is hungry for knowledge and power, and if there is an intelligence behind the UFOs it must have taken this fact into account."
