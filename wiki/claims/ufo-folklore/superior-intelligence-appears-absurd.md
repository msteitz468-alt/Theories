## Claim Statement

Vallée argues, following Aimé Michel, that the organized action of a superior race must appear absurd or random to an inferior one, so scientists who dismiss UFO reports because 'intelligent visitors would not behave like that' have not thought seriously about nonhuman intelligence.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He likens human attempts to find purpose in the behavior to a dog confronted with a mathematician writing on a blackboard, and states this absurdity 'has had the effect of keeping professional scientists away.'
