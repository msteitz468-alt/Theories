## Claim Statement

Vallée argues that large, formal UFO organizations waste energy on overhead and feuds and bury or bias their data, and that real progress would come instead from many small, informal circles gathering and rapidly publishing good first-hand reports.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He states that only local residents can properly weigh a sighting, praises outlets such as the *Flying Saucer Review*, GEPA Bulletin and *Lumières dans la Nuit*, and insists 'neither a crash-program ... nor computer correlations ... will easily dispose of' the problem.

## Contexts Within the Source

His prescription for turning the material into something studiable.
