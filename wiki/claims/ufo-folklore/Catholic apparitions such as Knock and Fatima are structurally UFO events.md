## Claim Statement

Vallée argues that apparition cases bearing the Catholic Church's stamp — Fatima, Guadalupe, and the 1879 Knock apparition — are, if the definitions are applied strictly, UFO phenomena in which the entity delivered a religious rather than a technological message.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He details Knock (stationary changing golden light, figures and a lamb, a witness whose embrace met only the wall, dry ground under the light during heavy rain, subsequent cures) as structurally identical to a landing.

## Contexts Within the Source

Stated as Fact 5 in *Magonia*: the apparition mechanism 'is standard and follows the model of religious miracles.' In *The Invisible College* (Ch. 7, "A Morphology of Miracles") Vallée develops the Fatima case in much greater detail and ties it explicitly to his control-system thesis — see [[The Fatima apparitions of 1917 were a UFO-type event]].
