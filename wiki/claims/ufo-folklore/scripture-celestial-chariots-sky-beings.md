## Claim Statement

Vallée notes that the foundational texts of religion describe contact with a 'superior race' from the sky using luminous craft ('celestial chariots'), citing the Bible and Ezekiel's cherubim, and surveys ancient imagery (the Palenque sarcophagus, the Jomon Dogu figurines) that ancient-astronaut writers read as space visitors.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He quotes Psalms ('The chariots of God are twenty thousand, even thousands of angels') and Ezekiel, while treating the ancient-astronaut readings as speculation he reports rather than endorses.
