## Claim Statement

Under MK-ULTRA, Sidney Gottlieb's Technical Services Staff progressed from self-experimentation to dosing fellow CIA personnel by surprise, and finally to giving LSD to **unwitting members of the public**. The field operation was run by narcotics officer [[George Hunter White]], who set up CIA safehouses in Greenwich Village and San Francisco. In Operation **Midnight Climax**, drug-addicted prostitutes lured men back to a CIA-financed bordello and spiked their drinks with LSD while White observed through two-way mirrors.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The authors quote White's diary ("Gloria gets horrors… Janet sky high") and his code word for LSD, "Stormy." White was paid the prostitutes $100/night and promised to fix any arrests. The program ran until CIA Inspector General John Earman stumbled onto it in 1963 and warned it placed "the rights and interests of U.S. citizens in jeopardy"; Helms defended and sought to continue it ("no answer to the moral issue," but necessary "to keep up with Soviet advances"). White later wrote Gottlieb: "Where else could a red-blooded American boy lie, kill, cheat, steal, rape, and pillage with the sanction and blessing of the All-Highest?"
