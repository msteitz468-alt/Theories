## Claim Statement

Beyond research, Lee and Shlain document that the CIA deployed LSD operationally: as a third-degree interrogation aid (a "marked anxiety and loss of reality contact" used as leverage), and — under the operational code "P-1" — to dose "socialist or left-leaning politicians in foreign countries so that they would babble incoherently and discredit themselves in public." The decision to use it operationally reported directly to Richard Helms, who called the drug "dynamite."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The authors report that **Fidel Castro** was targeted for "surprise acid attacks" (and the thallium-salts plot to make his beard fall out before the CIA "shifted its focus… to eliminating Castro altogether" with Gottlieb's poisons and the Mob), and that Egyptian president **Gamal Abdel Nasser** "figured high on the CIA's hallucinogenic hit list." CIA documents (cited via ABC News) confirm Gottlieb "carried a stash of acid overseas on a number of occasions… with the intention of dosing foreign diplomats and statesmen."
