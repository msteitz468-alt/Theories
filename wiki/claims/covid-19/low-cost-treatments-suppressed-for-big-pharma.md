## Claim Statement

The authors argue that "the mass media, Big Pharma, the WHO, and the tech giants" censored information about cheap, effective COVID-19 treatments in order to protect the market for expensive patented drugs and vaccines — "pharmaceutical failures" promoted while "successful protocols [were] suppressed."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The low-cost treatments they say were suppressed include: **quercetin and zinc** (the "Swiss Protocol"); **hydroxychloroquine** "at proper low doses with zinc… and azithromycin"; **ivermectin** (which they say "appears particularly effective at preventing SARS-CoV-2 infection"); **vitamin D** supplementation; nebulized hydrogen peroxide; and the **MATH+ / "COVID-19 Critical Care"** protocol. They contrast these with what they call the failures and harms of Big Pharma products and the profit motive driving the vaccine rollout.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

The authors present these as treatments used "by doctors across the world" and as contested/censored at the time of writing (2021), citing the suppression itself as part of their argument rather than claiming settled regulatory acceptance.
