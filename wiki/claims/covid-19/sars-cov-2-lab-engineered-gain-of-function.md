## Claim Statement

Mercola and Cummins argue that SARS-CoV-2 was not a natural zoonotic spillover but a virus **engineered through "gain-of-function" research** — "a joint Chinese/US creation… a genetically engineered, mutant offspring of a decades-long biological arms race, disguised as gain-of-function biomedical, vaccine, or biosafety research" — most likely escaping from a Wuhan lab. They state the release was "apparently accidentally, rather than deliberately" caused.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

They point to: gain-of-function work at the **Wuhan Institute of Virology** (Dr. Shi Zhengli, the "Bat Woman"); the **EcoHealth Alliance** under Peter Daszak channeling research there; reporting that Anthony **Fauci's NIAID gave $3.7 million** to Wuhan-linked work, allegedly continuing after the 2014–2017 US gain-of-function moratorium; and what they call suppression of evidence (≈300 deleted Chinese coronavirus studies, the removed "patient zero" report on lab worker Huang Yanling). They dispute the official natural-origin account (the bat→pangolin recombination story) and note Daszak's later appointment to both the *Lancet* and WHO origin inquiries as a conflict of interest.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

The authors present this as "the preponderance of evidence" rather than proof, stress the release was likely **accidental**, and acknowledge the natural-origin hypothesis is the position of bodies like the EcoHealth Alliance — which they then argue is compromised.
