## Claim Statement

Dispenza reports four case studies in which workshop participants claim complete or near-complete
remission of serious medical conditions following attendance at his advanced meditation workshops.
He frames these as evidence that "when you get beyond your body, your environment, and time," the
autonomic nervous system steps in and performs healing. The cases involve chronic pain from disc
injury, electromagnetic hypersensitivity, multiple autoimmune disorders, and severe eczema.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Ginny** (herniated discs L4/L5, two years of near-constant pain, scheduled for lumbar surgery):
During a meditation at a Seattle workshop, Ginny reports feeling energy move up her spine and out of
her head, followed by a state in which she felt "connected to everybody, everyone, everything, every
place, and every time." After the meditation she stood without pain for the first time in two years,
walked straight without limping, and subsequently cancelled her surgery. Dispenza reports she
"has remained pain free."

**Daniel** (electromagnetic hypersensitivity, EHS — chronic headaches, fatigue, inability to work):
After reading Dispenza's earlier book *You Are the Placebo*, Daniel began doing meditations
(Changing Beliefs and Perceptions; Blessing of the Energy Centers) and reports progressive pain
reduction over months. He ultimately moved to a shared office space surrounded by Wi-Fi and
electronics, meditating in that environment daily, until headaches and fatigue resolved. Dispenza
reports Daniel considers himself "100 percent healed" and has returned to work and eliminated debt.

**Jennifer** (lupus erythematosus, Sjögren's syndrome, celiac disease, kidney disease, arthritis,
asthma, and additional conditions; weight 108 lbs): Attended a weekend workshop then an advanced
workshop in Australia. By the end of the advanced workshop she reports walking "like a healthy
person," no longer needing asthma medication, and tolerating normal foods. She subsequently reports
walking up to 10 miles a day, lifting 45 pounds, and training toward a half marathon.

**Felicia** (medical doctor with chronic eczema and skin infections since infancy): After
surrendering to the breathing exercise at an advanced workshop and entering what observers called a
"seizure"-level gamma brain-wave state (described as 190 standard deviations above normal), Felicia
reports her eczema resolved within days. Brain-scan photos cited as evidence (Graphics 7A–7D).
She subsequently takes no medication and her skin remains clear.

## Contexts Within the Source

Dispenza presents these narratives as practical demonstrations of the theoretical framework
established in Chapters 1–5. He attributes the healing mechanism to the practitioner getting "beyond
their body, environment, and time" so that the autonomic nervous system "stepped in and did the
healing for her/him." The chapter explicitly states the purpose is to make other readers believe
"a similar experience can happen for us."

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

These are self-reported case narratives, not controlled studies. No independent medical verification
is provided within the text. The EHS case (Daniel) notes that EHS itself is "still controversial in
the medical community." The sheer severity of the portrayed symptoms and speed of resolution are
presented uncritically by Dispenza. The author himself has elsewhere acknowledged critics who label
his framework "pseudoscience."
