## Claim Statement

Dispenza reports first-person accounts from workshop participants who, following sustained practice of the meditations described in prior chapters (Mind Movies, kaleidoscope, elevated-emotion cultivation), experienced dramatic spontaneous events: energy "downloads" entering through the crown of the head, complete relief from chronic physical pain, dissolution of longstanding emotional trauma, and states of timelessness and other-dimensional reality. Dispenza frames these as evidence that consistent daily practice eventually produces measurable "supernatural" shifts.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Three cases are reported in Ch. 10:

**Tim (Seattle advanced workshop):** After sustained Mind Movie practice focused on worthiness, Tim felt energy enter through the top of his head during meditation, moving out through his hands. He reported an "ecstatic, exalted state" and conviction that he was "rewriting his DNA." Days later, an acquaintance bequeathed him $110,000 in gold and silver coins — the exact imagery from his Mind Movie — which Dispenza presents as synchronistic confirmation of the practice.

**Sarah (Cancun advanced workshop, Labor Day 2016):** Sarah attended with a severe back injury (scheduled for surgery) and a Mind Movie depicting herself doing aerial yoga. On the third day, after kaleidoscope meditation, she reports reaching for the floor and finding it "no longer there" — entering what she described as "another reality, a different time and space," where she was performing aerial yoga. Upon returning to ordinary consciousness, she reports all pain was gone: "I knew I was healed."

**Terry (Sunshine Coast, Australia, September 2016):** During a walking meditation, Terry felt "an intense shaking from the inside — a voltage of energy that my body had never experienced," energy entering through the crown of the head and surging through her body. She interpreted this as her body releasing "trauma from not only my lifetime, but also past lifetimes," and described it as "true liberation." She subsequently reported feeling reconnected to her physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual self.

All three are presented as self-reports gathered by Dispenza.

## Contexts Within the Source

Chapter 10 precedes the theoretical chapters (11 on space-time/time-space, 12 on the pineal gland, 13 on Project Coherence) and serves as "proof of concept" from practitioners. The chapter introduction frames these stories as examples of people "more in love with their future than their past." They are explicitly linked to Mind Movies (see [[Mind Movies and mental rehearsal (Dispenza)]]) and breathing/energy practices.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

These are self-reported accounts without independent medical verification presented by Dispenza. Sarah's reported complete pain relief following surgery-level back injury, and Tim's synchronistic inheritance, are presented as facts rather than as anecdotes requiring confirmation. Dispenza does not note that emotional catharsis during intense retreats can produce temporary pain relief via known psychological and physiological mechanisms unrelated to "rewriting DNA" or interdimensional travel.
