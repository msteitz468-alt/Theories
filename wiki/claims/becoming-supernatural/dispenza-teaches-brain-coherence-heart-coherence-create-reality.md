## Claim Statement

Dispenza teaches that the combination of a coherent brain (clear intention — an electric charge) and a coherent heart (elevated emotion — a magnetic charge) produces an electromagnetic field or "signature" that the person broadcasts into the quantum field. When this signature achieves a "vibrational match" with a potential in the unified field, that potential is drawn into the person's life. He summarizes this in the Introduction: "It takes a clear intention (a coherent brain) and an elevated emotion (a coherent heart) to begin to change a person's biology from living in the past to living in the future. That combination of mind and body — of thoughts and feelings — also seems to influence matter. And that's how you create reality."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

The Introduction states that Dispenza partnered with the HeartMath Institute (HMI) to measure thousands of participants' heart-rate variability (HRV), finding that elevated emotions (gratitude, inspiration, joy, kindness, appreciation, compassion) cause the heart to beat in a "coherent fashion — that is, with rhythm, order, and balance." He claims the heart in coherence produces a measurable magnetic field that extends beyond the body.

Chapter 2 provides the underlying model: thoughts create electrical charges in neural networks; feelings create magnetic charges; together they produce an electromagnetic field equal to the person's state of being. Living by the same thoughts and feelings broadcasts the same electromagnetic field, re-creating the same life.

Chapter 3 makes the quantum-field application explicit: intention is the electric charge sent into the unified field; elevated emotion is the magnetic charge. Together they create "a whole new electromagnetic signature" that — when matched to a potential in the field — draws that potential into physical reality. He calls this mechanism the basis for students healing diseases, manifesting new jobs, resolving problems, and having mystical experiences.

Dispenza cites his 2016 Tacoma workshop study (117 participants, EEG measurements) showing significant shifts from high-range beta to delta brain waves over four days as empirical support for the brain-coherence claim.

## Contexts Within the Source

This is the operational core of the method repeated in nearly every chapter. It underpins the design of every meditation in the book.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Dispenza acknowledges the framework may be called pseudoscience. The HeartMath partnership and EEG data are offered as evidence, but the claim that an individual's electromagnetic field can influence matter at a distance by "vibrational match" with an immaterial quantum field is not standard physics. He frames the quantum-field application as experiential and spiritual as much as scientific.
