## Claim Statement

Wallis argues that the once-mainstream knowledge of ET intervention in human origins was lost not by accident but through a series of deliberate suppressions across history — "a lot of forgetting and none of it by accident."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

His stated sequence: the knowledge was *commanded* out of Judaism (the Ten Commandments); *edited* out of the Hebrew Scriptures in the 6th century BCE (the "J" redaction, per Book 1); *excommunicated* out of the early Church in the 1st–2nd centuries CE; *illegalized* from the Roman Empire by Emperor Theodosius in 381 CE (its texts hidden in the caves of **Nag Hammadi** to escape destruction); *burned* out of Central and South American libraries by the conquistadors in the 15th century; its proponents *burned at the stake* by the Roman Catholic Church in the 16th century; and *suppressed* in modern times when President Truman signed the **National Security Act of 1947**, a policy Wallis says continued "unabated" until recent US Department of Defense UFO disclosures. He also recounts a Carl Sagan briefing giving Apollo 14 astronauts a secret ET "codeword."
