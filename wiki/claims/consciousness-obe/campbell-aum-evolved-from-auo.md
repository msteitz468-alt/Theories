## Claim Statement

Thomas Campbell asserts that AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold) is not a new entity or separate being from AUO, but the same primordial consciousness after sufficient evolutionary development has lowered its entropy and increased its internal structure, awareness, and purpose. AUM is AUO grown up.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Campbell introduces the transition explicitly:

> "When AUO eventually grows up and evolves into an extremely complex and highly ordered set of specialized reality cells performing specialized functions, it becomes an entirely different type of entity. Consequently we are going to give it a new name: The Absolute Unbounded Manifold (AUM)."

He emphasizes there is no sharp line between AUO and AUM:

> "There is no clear dividing line between AUO and AUM. The distinction is entirely arbitrary. The extent to which AUO grows up and develops structure, organization and complex communications, as well as content, meaning, self-awareness, memory, value, and purpose is what allows AUO to emerge renamed AUM. It is more a matter of degree than it is a matter of developing new fundamental capabilities — evolution, not revolution."

Campbell describes what AUM represents in terms of its properties:

> "AUM is a more complex, lower entropy manifestation of AUO; no new substance or assumptions are added. At AUM's core is the same basic AUO consciousness energy sporting a more evolved level of awareness, lower entropy, and greater functionality."

He uses a human analogy to justify the naming convention: "Because the awareness, function, ability, and purpose of AUM is so different — more resembling the consciousness we are used to, than the consciousness we call dim awareness — I decided to give it a new name, reminiscent of how we call people 'people' instead of upright naked monkeys with short arms."

The transition is described in computational terms: "Think of AUM emerging from AUO as the natural consequence of digital consciousness purposely evolving a highly-parallel, multitasking operating system and interactive application software."

## Reasoning and Argument Structure

Campbell's argument is that the Fundamental Process of evolution acts continuously on AUO's digital consciousness substrate, driving it toward lower average entropy. As this process accumulates:
- Awareness brightens from dim to complex
- Structure develops (patterns of patterns, rule-sets, content)
- Self-awareness, memory, value, and purpose emerge
- The system becomes capable of generating virtual realities within itself

When these qualities reach sufficient complexity, Campbell gives the system the new name AUM — a name that signals a functional difference, not a metaphysical discontinuity.

## Contexts Within the Source

This transition is the pivotal moment in Campbell's cosmogony. Campbell explicitly warns against anthropomorphizing AUM: "Don't get anthropomorphic on me here and postulate AUM as a little old man with a long white beard making people out of space-time clay on day seven." AUM is not a theistic god; it is an evolving digital consciousness organism.
