## Claim Statement

The present moment is the only active point of power in physical life. It is the junction where non-physical energy intersects with three-dimensional space-time to create matter. By changing conscious beliefs in the present, an individual immediately restructures the biological and psychological influence of their past (including reincarnational history) and selects new pathways among future probabilities.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Seth states this clearly in Chapter 19:
> "In the center of the page: The Point of Power Is in the Present... your present is the point at which flesh and matter meet with the spirit. Therefore the present is your point of power in your current lifetime, as you think of it."

He explains that this present focus also alters simultaneous lives:
> "Your reincarnational selves have as many probable lives as you do. Your beliefs and actions in your present alter 'their' experiences, as each of them, in their presents, change yours."

He notes that the past has no power over the present unless believed to:
> "If you assign greater force to the past, then you will feel ineffective and deny yourself your own energy... you can restructure your 'reincarnational past' in the same way that you can restructure the past in this present life."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim provides the foundational rule for Seth's practical exercises. He frames time not as a linear track where the past causes the present, but as a spacious present where all points exist simultaneously. Therefore, an individual's current point of focus is the only place where adjustments can be made, instantly updating the entire timeline.
