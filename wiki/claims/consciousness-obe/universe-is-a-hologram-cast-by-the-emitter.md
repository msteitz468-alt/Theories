## Claim Statement

The climactic revelation of Monroe's quest for "the missing Basic": he reports perceiving that the physical universe is not the product of a "big bang" but an ongoing creative emission — a "hologram" cast by an energy source he calls **"the Emitter,"** through an **"Aperture,"** with a return flow forming "a closed loop… a circle." He concludes there is a Creator beyond the Emitter, though that Creator itself lay past where he could go.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Monroe describes bathing in the Emitter's radiation and "knowing what I am… a part of the Whole, the restless part that desires to return, yet lives to seek expression in doing, creating, building, giving… and above all desires to bring back gifts of love to the Whole." He states the "Known Basic": "The physical universe, including the whole of humankind, is an ongoing creative process. There is indeed a Creator." Characteristically, he marks the limit of his knowledge: "Who or what this Creator is lies beyond the Emitter and the Aperture, and I have not been there… that part I do not know. Not yet."

## Contexts Within the Source

The resolution of the book's driving search (after establishing [[The Earth Life System]] as a baseline). The "gifts of love to the Whole" theme connects to the "loosh"/energy material of *Far Journeys*.
