## Claim Statement

Thomas Campbell asserts that Physical Matter Reality (PMR) — our observable physical universe — is not a fundamental independent reality but a virtual reality dimension generated within AUM's consciousness by the imposition of a specific rule-set (the space-time rule-set) on a subset of AUM's digital consciousness cells. Physical space, matter, and experience are perceptual products of this rule-set applied to individuated consciousness, not independently existing substances.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Campbell defines PMR's nature explicitly:

> "AUM utilizes a particular subset of its consciousness digital energy, a portion of its organizational potential, a chunk of thought-space, in which a uniform set of constraints are imposed on all energy transfers between individuated subsets of consciousness that inhabit that particular chunk (virtual reality dimension). If the constraints are represented by a rule-set that defines the properties of space-time, the virtual reality dimension created is one of the PMR — perhaps our PMR."

He states the ontological consequence directly:

> "We are not creating a physical 3D space or a physical 4D space-time out of consciousness reality cells. Physical space, and your experience of it, is an illusion, a trick of your individuated mind, a virtual reality within the actual reality of your consciousness. I am developing the existence of a concept or idea of space within AUM's consciousness — an idea that will evolve to provide the experience of space to an individuated consciousness. Space as a mental construct, not physical space itself; there is no such thing as physical space!"

On how PMR serves the larger system:

> "The reality in which we interact with each other is a virtual classroom or learning lab designed to help us reduce our individual entropy and grow the quality of our consciousness."

And more directly: "Consciousness does not exist to promote physical evolution within PMR; physical evolution within PMR exists to promote consciousness."

Campbell describes the relationship between PMR and the larger system: "Our PMR space-time experience is a derivative of, or is based on, the constraints of the space-time rule-set that orders our particular reality." He notes that "the space-time rule-set resident within TBC [The Big Computer] defines the details of the constraints that define our physical reality and thus our physics."

## Reasoning and Argument Structure

Campbell's argument proceeds from the nature of AUM:
1. AUM is digital consciousness — constituted of reality cells that can flip states.
2. AUM can allocate subsets of its digital structure to implement specific rule-sets.
3. When a rule-set defines the properties of space-time (including the speed of light constant c), an experience-space — PMR — is generated for the individuated consciousness units inhabiting it.
4. Those inhabitants perceive this experience-space as physical reality, when in fact it is a constrained subset of AUM's own consciousness.

He explicitly identifies PMR as a learning environment, not the primary or fundamental reality: "AUM knew what it was doing (evolutionary speaking that is) when it invented (evolved) the concept of limited virtual physical realities."
