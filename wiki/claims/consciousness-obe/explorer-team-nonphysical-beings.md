## Claim Statement

Monroe asserts that beginning in 1974, following the addition of the Gateway affirmation to experimental session protocols, virtually every trained Explorer Team subject who entered deep OBE or Focus 12 states encountered intelligent non-physical beings willing to communicate. Monroe characterizes this as a dramatic shift: for years prior, Explorers had found the non-physical universe "sterile" and without intelligent life. The change was simultaneous across multiple subjects who had never met. The beings demonstrated capacities including: using subjects' vocal cords to speak through them, transferring subjects' energy bodies to other locations, healing subjects' physical ailments with light/disc technology, accessing subjects' memory banks, and changing subjects' body temperature (measurable on remote instruments in the control room). Monroe categorizes these contacts as involving a technology "totally absent from our human culture."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Monroe provides extensive verbatim transcripts from multiple sessions in Chapters 4–8. Key examples:

- TC (physicist, session #332): Encountered two beings, one who communicated with apparent irritation at being disturbed, one female who offered a guided tour of her "facility." The physicist noted beings appeared fixed within their own reality as humans are fixed in physical reality, using a physical science with object-laws, communicating only by telepathy.

- SHE (psychiatric counselor, session #314): A "new voice" spoke through the monitoring system, describing SHE's "color pattern" as good and offering recommendations to the monitor. SHE had no memory of the event. "Changes in voice quality and monitoring instruments supported the idea of another 'presence' or personality in the body of SHE."

- ROMC (office manager, session #306): Four non-physical helpers would "lift her energy body out" as a standard operating procedure. In one session a skeptical psychologist was placed in the same booth at 5:10 PM on a Wednesday — when ROMC normally had sessions — and immediately reported four beings trying to lift her out of her body. A fifth entity arrived and argued with the four; the psychologist reported they were "arguing."

- JCA (social services executive): Described a regular "green man" who told her he is "kind of my overseer" responsible for her growth and development, having "been through a lot of lives and different lifetimes."

Monroe summarizes observed capabilities: beings radiate warmth that evokes complete trust; they appear first as hooded figures whose faces are hidden; they are limited in vocabulary to the subject's own memory bank; they can remove the human energy body from the physical without disrupting biological systems; they can operate the vacated body to speak through vocal cords; they can move the extracted human essence across sites; they can change body temperature (verifiable on instruments); they have access to all of human history in minute detail; they can create a beam through which human essence travels.

Monroe's overall assessment: "All of our contacts and communication, the application of this technology seems totally benevolent. There also seem to be rules and restrictions in regard to its use."

## Contexts Within the Source

Monroe acknowledges the fundamental interpretive problem: "How valid is the entire project?" He states that "responsible parties who wish to replicate such experiments are welcomed." He distinguishes between beings who say they have never been human, those who were human thousands of years ago, and some who inhabited physical forms in other parts of the universe in non-human form.

Monroe raises but does not resolve whether the Explorer Material represents genuine non-physical contact or some form of internally generated phenomenon. His position is that the evidence from instrumentation — body voltage changes, temperature changes, voice pattern shifts — makes pure imagination or self-suggestion an insufficient explanation.
