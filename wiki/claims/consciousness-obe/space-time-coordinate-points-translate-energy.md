## Claim Statement

Coordinate points in space-time behave as primary energetic junctions, energy accelerators, and multidimensional transformers. They act as channels through which non-physical psychic energy flows, functioning as warp paths between realities and converting human thoughts and emotions directly into physical matter.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Seth describes the coordinate points in Chapter 5 (Session 524):
> "These points are not recognized as such, but they are points of what you could call double reality, containing great energy potential; coordinate points, indeed, where realities merge."

He explains their structure and cosmic role:
> "There are four absolute coordinate points that intersect all realities. These coordinate points also act as channels through which energy ows, and as warps or invisible paths from one reality to another. They also act as transformers, and provide much of the generating energy that makes creation continuous..."

In the Appendix (Session 593), he describes their function as white and black holes:
> "Electromagnetic properties are drawn into the black hole, and accelerated beyond imagination... The holes, therefore, or coordination points, are actually great accelerators that reenergize energy itself."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim provides the specific mechanism of action for Seth's "physical universe as idea construction" thesis. Rather than thoughts transforming into matter abstractly, Seth outlines a structured, mathematical template of coordinate points that permeate space, acting as localized nodes where mental energy gathers enough density to enter physical perception.
