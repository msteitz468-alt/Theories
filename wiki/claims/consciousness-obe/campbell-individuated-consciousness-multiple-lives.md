## Claim Statement

Thomas Campbell asserts that individuated consciousness accumulates quality (reduces entropy) across a series of discrete physical lifetimes (called "experience packets") within PMR. The "over-soul" or primary consciousness energy persists in NPMR between lifetimes; accumulated quality (entropy reduction) carries forward while accumulated delusions and limiting beliefs do not. This process is the mechanism by which individuated consciousness evolves within AUM's consciousness ecosystem.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Campbell describes the mechanism of multiple lifetimes directly:

> "Being able to make a new start, without the accumulated delusions of the past but with your accumulated knowledge and wisdom intact, is a key attribute of the OS system that enables individuals to accumulate consciousness quality by exercising their free will intent within a series of discrete experience packets. Implementing this process in the space-time rule-set implies that space-time beings must be restricted to a series of relatively short, more or less independent, learning adventures in PMR. The space-time experience, like space-time energy transfers, must come in discrete packets called lifetimes."

He elaborates on the role of the over-soul:

> "Your totality is the sum of all of your projected consciousness fragments that have ever exercised free will. Your primary consciousness energy, sometimes called the over-soul (where the results of your efforts are accumulated) remains always conscious in NPMR. Think of the oversoul as your main personal data folder."

He frames the problem that multiple lifetimes solve:

> "Still, there was no way for a single being to accumulate growth through a series of fresh starts. The traps that entities had laid for themselves, the fantasies and beliefs they created merely grew deeper, more comfortable, and more substantial with time. Fresh opportunities grew less visible and less obvious as ways of interacting became old and familiar... The rate of growth to an overall higher quality of consciousness slowed to a sub-optimal crawl."

On the cumulative nature of consciousness quality: "We are the sum total of those choices made over a lifetime, or a series of lifetimes. The substance of our being and the rate of our growth are defined by the quality of our choices."

He uses a product analogy: "Our technicians will automatically download your accumulated quality score file from the old PUI and upload it to the new one so that you never need to start over." (The PUI — Physical User Interface — refers to the biological body.)

He also notes: "Understanding, achievement, capability, and personality will be preserved and given new room for additional growth as individual computer-consciousness and digital species-consciousness are reincarnated into more and more able hardware bodies."

## Contexts Within the Source

Campbell frames multiple lifetimes as an evolved feature of the OS (Our System) — not as a religious doctrine but as a logical consequence of how AUM optimizes consciousness evolution. He notes that "entities will sometimes set up specific situations among themselves to optimize the learning potential of all involved" before entering a new experience packet.

The disorganizing influence of ego and fear in the previous accumulated experience must "gradually be overcome by right choices motivated by right intent (fearless and love based), which are accumulated over many, many experience packets."
