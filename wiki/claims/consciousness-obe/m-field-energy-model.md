## Claim Statement

Monroe presents the **(M) Field** as a single, largely unrecognized energy field that is "the only energy field common to and operational both within and outside time-space," present in varying degrees in all physical matter and concentrating in living organisms. Thought, emotion, and love are described as adjacent bands of this one field; the **I-There** (each human's higher cluster-self) "is composed solely of (M)"; and all living organisms use (M) to communicate. The model thus unifies physics, mind, the afterlife, belief systems, and "psychic" abilities under one substrate, with a small minority of humans possessing hidden control over their (M) Field radiation.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Monroe receives the material in Chapter 13 ("Fine Tuning") as a **ROTE** (Related Organized Thought Energy) from his EXCOM/I-There, which he then says he "rolled" and translated:

- "There is a broad field of energy which for convenience is called (M)…. It is the only energy field common to and operational both within and outside time-space and is present in varying degrees in all physical matter."
- "Because of the tendency of (M) to accumulate in living organisms, **LIFE—or Layered Intelligence-Forming Energy—** is a useful acronym for one band of the (M) Field spectrum."
- "Thought is a much-used perturbation of (M), and emotions are bands of (M) adjacent to thought. Love is also a band of (M) adjacent to thought."
- "The **I-There is composed solely of (M)**, 'There' being outside time-space but within the (M) Field."
- "All living organisms use (M) to communicate. Animals are more aware of (M) radiation than humans, who, with few exceptions, have no awareness of it at all."
- "The power of beliefs and belief systems lies in various manipulations of (M) Field energy. Your beliefs become Knowns when you recognize or experience this manipulation."

Monroe applies the model to explain charisma, "gut" feelings, instant likes and dislikes, contagious group emotion, the placebo effect, and "miracle cures," all as side-band (M) phenomena. He estimates that of "nearly six billion humans," perhaps six thousand have "incredible ability," reducible to roughly **six hundred** "on the loose and unrecognized" who possess near-total covert control of (M) Field radiation — adding "I believe that I may have met one of them; but I do not know."

## Contexts Within the Source

The ROTE is offered as the answer to Monroe's question about the influences "I have to watch out for." He frames the (M) Field as the long-overlooked "Basic": "It was as if one had been swimming in an ocean for years without feeling the water!" The model retroactively grounds his earlier teaching — the indestructible radiation of love, the H Band Noise, the necessity of "phasing" and shielding — in a single energetic field, and explains why "most human (M) Field energy is not love and light," requiring automatic shielding as insulation.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Monroe presents the (M) Field as received knowledge rather than demonstrated fact, conceding it is "virtually unrecognized in our contemporary civilization" and that worldwide exploration of it is "too little, too late." He ends the chapter on the six hundred hidden adepts with "Questions with no answers!"
