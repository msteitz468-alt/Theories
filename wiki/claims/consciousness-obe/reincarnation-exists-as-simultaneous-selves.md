## Claim Statement

Reincarnational lifetimes are not sequential events that occur in linear history. Instead, they exist simultaneously as "simultaneous selves" within a "spacious present." These individual selves are connected psychologically and electromagnetically through the parent entity, constantly exchanging information, skills, and emotional feedback—primarily during the dream state.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Seth outlines this in Chapter 19 (Session 668):
> "When you think in terms of reincarnation it seems that one tracing exists before the other, but the entire 'chart' exists at once, with all the individual life-tracings... You exist, say, in seven different centuries at once."

He explains that these lives are psychologically connected:
> "Since these offshoots or life-tracings each come from your entity, they are connected psychologically and in terms of electromagnetic energy patterns."

He details their constant interaction and exchange:
> "Your reincarnational selves have as many probable lives as you do. Your beliefs and actions in your present alter 'their' experiences, as each of them, in their presents, change yours... in the dreaming condition there is a great interchange of information with these other portions of your selves."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim qualifies the nature of reincarnation. By shifting the model from sequential lives to simultaneous selves, Seth explains that individuals can actively draw upon the talents, knowledge, and strengths of their "other" selves in the present moment by changing their current conscious beliefs.
