## Claim Statement

Deep meditation triggers a physical feedback loop in the human body, where the heart-aorta recoil generates a standing wave resonance of approximately 7 to 7.8 Hz. This micro-motion causes the skull to oscillate, which electromagnetically entrains the brain's sensory-motor cortex and locks the individual's field into phase with the planet's electrostatic field (Schumann resonance).

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Bentov outlines the heart-aorta recoil and brain entrainment in the Appendix:
> "...the recoil of the aorta creates a rhythmic, physical micro-motion or vibration in the body (approx 7 Hz)... this causes the skull to oscillate... stimulating a circular current in the sensory-motor cortex that activates pleasure centers..."

He explains the planet-resonance link in Chapter 3:
> "...a meditating body... will emit a simple harmonic motion or a 'sound' of approximately 7 Hz. through the electrostatic field of the planet. This emission will entrain others and help them in their effort to achieve the resonant state."

He describes how this acts as an emitter:
> "The electrostatic field of the planet vibrates at approximately 7.5 to 7.8 Hz (the Schumann resonance)... The meditating body locks into this frequency, sending a coherent signal through the planetary field."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim provides the physiological explanation of how meditation alters consciousness. Bentov, using his background in bio-medical engineering, shows that meditation is not just a psychological state, but a physical resonance that quietens the nervous system's sensory inputs, allowing the observer to achieve out-of-body states.
