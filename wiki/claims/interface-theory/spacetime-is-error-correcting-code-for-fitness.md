## Claim Statement

Having argued that spacetime is not fundamental, Donald Hoffman proposes a positive account of *what it is*: spacetime and physical objects are an evolved **data-compression and error-correcting code** for fitness payoffs. "Spacetime is not an ancient theater erected long before any stirrings of life. It is a data structure that we create now to track and capture fitness payoffs."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Chapter 7**:
- **Data compression**: fitness-payoff functions "can vary in hundreds of dimensions"; human vision compresses them into three dimensions of space, one of time, and objects with shapes and colors — "the happy tradeoff." Distances may encode the energy cost (relative to available energy) of acquiring a resource (supported by Dennis Proffitt's glucose/distance experiments).
- **Error correction** via redundancy: Hoffman links this to the **holographic principle** — "two dimensions contain all the information in any 3D space," interpreted as the redundancy a noisy fitness channel needs. He cites physicists Almheiri, Dong, and Harlow (and Preskill, Pastawski) finding the geometry of spacetime can be read as a **quantum error-correcting code**.
- The **perceive-decide-act (PDA) loop** (Figure 10) formalizes how an agent's experiences encode messages about fitness.
- **Visual demonstrations** of error correction in real time: illusory contours (glowing lines/squares filling "erased" gaps; the subjective Necker cube, which "inflates" 2D information holographically into a 3D cube with superposition and entanglement of its lines). Hoffman suspects this is "precisely the same" process by which, per van Raamsdonk and Swingle, "spacetime is woven together from threads of entanglement."
- The **[[Invention of Symmetry Theorem]]**: symmetries we perceive reflect compression/error-correction algorithms, not reality's structure.
- Applications: physical **causality is a useful fiction** (the cue ball doesn't "really" cause the eight ball to move, any more than dragging an icon causes a file to delete); this undercuts theories tying consciousness to objects' causal powers — Tononi's **Integrated Information Theory (IIT)** and Searle's biological naturalism. The Body Optix jeans line manipulates depth cues to send a "white lie" about fitness.

## Contexts Within the Source

This chapter answers the "if spacetime is doomed, what is it?" question raised at the end of chapter 6, and supplies a mechanism (compression + error correction) consistent with both the [[Interface Theory of Perception (ITP)]] and the holographic principle.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Hoffman contrasts his account with standard vision science (inverse optics / Bayesian estimation, which assume real 3D objects whose shapes are recovered) and with "radical embodied cognition" (which denies perception involves information processing) — arguing the latter conflicts with physicists' claim that all physical processes are information processes.
