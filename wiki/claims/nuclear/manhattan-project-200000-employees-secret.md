## Claim Statement

The Manhattan Project employed two hundred thousand people across eighty offices and dozens of production plants, including a sixty-thousand-acre Tennessee facility drawing more power than New York City on any given night—and no one knew the project was there.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence
