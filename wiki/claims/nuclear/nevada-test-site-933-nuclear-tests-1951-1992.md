## Claim Statement

Beginning in 1951 on President Truman's orders, 105 nuclear weapons were exploded aboveground at the Nevada Test Site and 828 underground. The last U.S. nuclear weapons test on American soil occurred there on September 23, 1992.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

> "Beginning in 1951, on the orders of President Harry Truman, 105 nuclear weapons were exploded aboveground at the site and another 828 were exploded underground... The last nuclear weapons test on American soil occurred at the Nevada Test Site on September 23, 1992."
