## Claim Statement

Marrs argues that today Illuminism operates through a small set of wealthy "bloodline families" and elite, screened societies — Yale's [[Skull and Bones]] (brought from Germany in 1832), the [[Council on Foreign Relations]], the Trilateral Commission, the Bilderberg Group, Bohemian Grove, the Knights of Malta — alongside the central banks, working toward a centralized "New World Order." He cites the 2011 Swiss Federal Institute of Technology study (a "super-entity" of 147 firms controlling ~40% of the corporate network) as reality-based confirmation of concentrated control.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Marrs relays self-described defectors/insiders — "Svali" (an Illuminati "programmer" describing regional councils, a Supreme World Council with Rothschild/Hapsburg/Rockefeller seats, and a planned financial-collapse-then-martial-law scenario), Cisco Wheeler (Springmeier's co-author on trauma-based mind control), and "Charles"/Project Camelot (a "Board of 33" bloodlines) — while noting most such whistleblowers are "dubious characters." He lists bloodline families per Fritz Springmeier (Astor, Bundy, Collins, DuPont, Kennedy, Rockefeller, Rothschild, Russell, Van Duyn, Merovingian, etc.). The "Hidden Hand" poster equated the group with "the Illuminati, Nephilim, Custodians, Watchers."

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Marrs stresses the whistleblowers' claims "tend to support" researchers but are largely unverifiable, and that Svali herself insists "the Illuminati agenda is not a Jewish conspiracy."
