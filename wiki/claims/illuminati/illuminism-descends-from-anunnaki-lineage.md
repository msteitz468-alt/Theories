## Claim Statement

Marrs argues the doctrines of Illuminism predate the Bavarian Illuminati "by millennia," traceable backward through Freemasons, Rosicrucians, Knights Templar, the Assassins, the Essenes, the Gnostics and Cabala, and the Mystery Schools of Greece and Egypt, to ancient Sumer and the "possibly extraterrestrial **Anunnaki**." The secret societies are presented as the conduits that carried fragments of the "sky gods'" knowledge down through history.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

In the Origins summary Marrs gives the Sumerian account — Anunnaki from Nibiru mining gold, engineering a slave race via Enki and Ninhursag, the Deluge — and ties it to ORME/"white powder of gold" (manna), Moses-as-Akhenaten, and the looting of the Baghdad museum in 2003 (allegedly to seize "ancient alien technology"). The Conclusions reaffirm: these traditions "can be traced back through the Freemasons, Rosicrucians, Knights Templar, Mystery Schools of Greece and Egypt, to ancient Sumer and their accounts of the possibly extraterrestrial Anunnaki."
