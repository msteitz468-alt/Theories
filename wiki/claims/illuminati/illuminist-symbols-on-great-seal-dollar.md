## Claim Statement

Marrs reports the long-standing claim that the reverse of the Great Seal of the United States — the unfinished pyramid, the All-Seeing Eye, and the mottoes *Annuit coeptis* and *Novus Ordo Seclorum* — encodes Illuminati/Masonic symbolism. The All-Seeing Eye is tied to the Eye of Horus/Ra; the pyramid to the order itself; and *Novus Ordo Seclorum* ("a new order of the ages") is read by many as "New World Order." He notes FDR (a 32nd-degree Mason) placed the reverse on the dollar bill in 1935.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Marrs cites 33rd-degree Mason Manly P. Hall ("the signature of the Mysteries may still be seen on the Great Seal") and anti-communist Emanuel Josephson, who argued the 1776 date (MDCCLXXVI) marks Weishaupt's founding of the order rather than the Declaration, and that *Annuit coeptis* means "our enterprise (conspiracy) has been crowned with success." He also notes the Owl of Minerva (Illuminati symbol) allegedly hidden on the dollar and echoed at Bohemian Grove, and that the original eagle may have been a Phoenix.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Marrs records the official explanations (the pyramid signifies strength/duration and the 13 colonies; the eye signifies divine favor) alongside the conspiratorial readings, presenting both. He treats the symbology as suggestive rather than proven.
