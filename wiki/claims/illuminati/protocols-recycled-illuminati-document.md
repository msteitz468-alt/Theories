## Claim Statement

Marrs treats *The Protocols of the Learned Elders of Zion* as a "list of procedures for usurping national governments," and argues it likely originated as an Illuminati document later rewritten into antisemitic propaganda — **not** as evidence of a genuine Jewish conspiracy. He traces it from Maurice Joly's 1864 satire *Dialogue in Hell Between Machiavelli and Montesquieu* (Joly was a Rosicrucian) through documents linked to the Illuminati-descended Haute Vente Romaine, to the 1890s reissue with antisemitic material by the Russian Ochrana and Sergei Nilus.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Marrs notes Joly's work was "crude plagiarism," that the documents tied to it came from "an Italian secret society… headed by an Italian prince who had been initiated by Weishaupt intimate Knigge" (the Haute Vente Romaine, "nothing more than a reincarnation of the Illuminati"), and that the Ochrana reissue aimed "to relieve public pressure on the czar by portraying Russian revolutionaries as pawns of an international Jewish conspiracy." He records that Hitler took the Protocols as genuine in *Mein Kampf*.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Marrs is explicit that the document was fabricated/forged and repeatedly warns against the antisemitic reading; he presents the Illuminati-origin idea as what "some researchers believe," not as established fact. See [[Marrs speculates the ruling elite may be guided by, or be, the non-human Anunnaki]] for his framing of the conspiracy as bloodline/elite rather than ethnic.
