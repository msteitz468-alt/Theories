## Claim Statement

Booth argues history is the **gradual hardening of spirit into matter** — the reverse of materialist evolution. Matter "was only precipitated out of the cosmic mind over very long periods" and slowly "became denser," and human perception dimmed in step: the spiritual "Third Eye" was once as vivid as physical sight but became "vestigial" as the skull and the solid world took their present form.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

"Matter was only precipitated out of the cosmic mind over very long periods and was only very gradually developing towards the sort of solidity we are familiar with today." As it hardened it "became a greater barrier to the free flow of the cosmic mind," and "two parallel dimensions evolved, the spirit world and the material world." Booth reads myth as a record of this descent — the expulsion from Eden, Lucifer as a light-bringer of individual consciousness, the **Nephilim** ("the gods who loved women"), Isis and Osiris — and holds the process is cyclical: the material world "exists only for a brief interval" and "will dissolve again."

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Booth presents this as avowedly esoteric, "an imaginative exercise," and concedes the idealist model has its own unproven step (how mind precipitates matter).
