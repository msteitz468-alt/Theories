## Claim Statement

Dolan situates the UFO cover-up within a broader, documented structure he calls the "national security state" — the U.S. military-intelligence-contractor complex — whose five defining traits are **secrecy, wealth, independence, power, and duplicity**. The UFO secret, he argues, was handled exactly like the apparatus's other secrets: "secretly, with great deception, and with significant resources."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He grounds each trait in well-documented history rather than UFO lore: the Manhattan Project's secrecy, biological-weapons and "terminal mind-control experiments," the importation of Nazi scientists, mail/cable interception, secret coups and assassinations (Secrecy); CIA proprietary airlines and organized-crime/narcotics ties (Wealth); Eisenhower's inability to control or even learn Strategic Air Command's nuclear war plan (Independence); and the 1948 "war crisis," the fabricated 1950s "missile gap," and the Gulf of Tonkin (Duplicity). The cover-up is thus, for Dolan, unremarkable in *kind* — an instance of how "those at the top of the heap" maintain the status quo.
