## Claim Statement

Brand teaches that Enochian magic — usually regarded as the most baffling and forbidding of magical
systems — can be stripped of its complexity and used effectively by ordinary people with no prior
skill, contrary to gatekeepers who insist it is too complex, too dangerous, or reserved for spiritual
or political elites.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He writes that "Enochian Magick is now something you can investigate," that people will try to stop
you ("too complex to be simplified, too powerful for ordinary people"), and rejects the claim that
the magic "must be left completely alone." He frames his contribution as "a more practical system
than has been revealed before," tested with Gallery of Magick volunteers and beta readers to confirm
"complete beginners," skeptics, and experienced occultists could all use it. "The skills you need
will develop effortlessly as you progress."

## Contexts Within the Source

The enabling premise of the whole book — see [[Enochian Magick (Gallery of Magick approach)]] and the

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Brand acknowledges he is "not the ultimate authority on Enochian Magick," that "this is not the only
way to perform Enochian, and many other methods work," and that experts may find his democratizing
approach hard to accept.
