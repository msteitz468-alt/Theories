## Claim Statement

Brand teaches a single, repeatable ritual structure used for all 49 workings: perform once a day for
seven (up to eleven) consecutive days, in private, with no special clothing, equipment, timing, or
mood required.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Per the "How to Use the Rituals" steps: (1) the ritual begins simply "when you open the book… with
the intention to perform the ritual"; (2) read the **Foundation** ideas and reflect briefly; (3) gaze
at the **sigil**, "letting all the letters enter your subconscious mind"; (4) speak the **divine name**
(angel ritual) or the **Aethyr name three times** (Aethyr ritual); (5) make
[[The offering to humanity (Gallery of Magick ritual method)|the offering to humanity]] — hand on
chest, then outward; (6) "trust that the magick is in motion," with "no formal ending." Brand notes
"there is never a need to perform the ritual more than once a day."

## Contexts Within the Source

The shared mechanism behind [[The forty-nine rituals of Success Magick]] and the practical core of
[[Success Magick (Damon Brand's Enochian system)]].

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Brand stresses the guideline (seven days) "is open to wide interpretation and variation, according to
your needs and intuition," and that doing more than once a day "would bring no harm" but is
unnecessary.
