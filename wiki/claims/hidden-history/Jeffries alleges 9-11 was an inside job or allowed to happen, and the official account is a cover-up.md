## Claim Statement

Jeffries alleges that the official account of the September 11, 2001, attacks is false on multiple counts: the NORAD stand-down that allowed four planes to fly for up to eighty minutes after being identified as hijacked was not accidental; the World Trade Center towers (including Building 7) were brought down by controlled demolition, not by jet fuel fires; the alleged hijackers' identities are questionable; key phone calls from the planes were fabricated or technically impossible; and the 9/11 Commission was a politically controlled whitewash analogous to the Warren Commission.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Per [[Hidden History (Jeffries)]], Chapter 9 presents the following:

**NORAD stand-down**:
- A 1994 GAO report cited 1,518 fighter intercept assignments in four years. A 1998 document promised interception "within 10 or so minutes" of unusual behavior.
- Between August 2000 and June 2001, NORAD scrambled sixty-seven fighters (AP, August 12, 2002).
- When golfer Payne Stewart's Learjet lost contact in October 1999, fighters were on its wing within minutes.
- On 9/11, Flight 77 flew for nearly an hour after being identified as hijacked before hitting the Pentagon — with no interception.
- NORAD happened to be running "Vigilant Guardian," a weeklong exercise simulating hijackings, on September 11. The 9/11 Commission concluded this "expedited" the response — which Jeffries calls logically impossible given the outcome.
- George H.W. Bush met with Shafig bin Laden (Osama's brother) at a Carlyle Group meeting on September 10.

**Controlled demolition of the WTC**:
- Steel does not melt until 1535°C; the BBC reported WTC fires at 800°C. No steel-frame building had ever collapsed from fire before 9/11, or since.
- Explosives expert Van Romero told the Albuquerque Journal on September 11: "My opinion is … there were some explosive devices inside the buildings that caused the towers to collapse." He retracted ten days later.
- Multiple NYC firefighters reported hearing explosions before and during the collapses. Firefighter Edward Cachia: "We thought there was like an internal detonation, explosives." Assistant Fire Commissioner Stephen Gregory testified to seeing "low level flashes … like when they demolish a building."
- Building 7 — not struck by any plane — collapsed in a symmetrical freefall at 5:20 p.m. Demolition expert Danny Jowenko called it a controlled demolition (Jowenko died in a car crash on July 16, 2011).
- WTC maintenance worker William Rodriguez reported a sub-basement explosion before the plane impact above; engineer Mike Pecoraro described a machine shop and parking garage in the sub-basement "completely gone, turned to rubble."

**Hijacker identity problems**:
- FBI Director Robert Mueller told CNN on September 20, 2002: "There is no legal proof to prove the identities of the suicidal hijackers."
- Multiple named hijackers were found alive in Saudi Arabia and elsewhere after 9/11 (reported by BBC, Washington Post, Saudi Embassy, among others).
- Several named hijackers had addresses at US military bases (Pensacola Naval Air Station, Maxwell Air Force Base, Brooks Air Force Base).
- Hani Hanjour, credited with executing the precise spiral descent into the Pentagon, had been deemed too incompetent to rent a Cessna at a Maryland flight school only three weeks earlier.

**Phone calls impossibility**:
- A 2003 study by Scientific American columnist A.K. Dewdney found cell phone calls were impossible from cruising altitude in 2001: the chance of one call succeeding was one in a hundred; two calls, less than one in ten thousand.
- FBI Moussaoui trial records (2006) show Barbara Olson's alleged call to husband Ted Olson lasted "0 seconds" and was "unconnected."

**Flight 93**:
- An anonymous Fort Meade whistleblower told researchers she heard military commanders order Flight 93 shot down.
- Somerset County coroner Wallace Miller stated at the Shanksville site: "There was no wreckage, no bodies, and no noise. It appeared as though there were no passengers or crew on this plane."
- Cleveland news on September 11 reported a United 767 identified as Flight 93 landing at Cleveland Hopkins Airport.

**Post-9/11 political exploitation**:
- The Project for a New American Century's September 2000 document called for "a catastrophic and catalyzing event — like a new Pearl Harbor."
- At a Council on Foreign Relations meeting on September 12, Senator Gary Hart said the attack was "a chance for the president … to carry out what his father … called a new world order."
- Paul Wolfowitz called for "ending states who sponsor terrorism" on September 13. Tom Brokaw told viewers on October 11: "We're now in a new world order."

**Suspicious deaths of witnesses**:
- Barry Jennings (deputy director of NYC Housing Authority Emergency Services), who witnessed explosions in Building 7 before the twin towers fell, died August 19, 2008, under unclear circumstances; a private detective hired to investigate quit, telling researcher Dylan Avery "never contact me again."
- Beverly Eckert, who lost her husband on 9/11 and knew the official story was false, died in a plane crash one week after meeting President Obama.
- Danny Jowenko (demolition expert who publicly called Building 7 a controlled demolition), killed in a car accident on July 16, 2011.
- These deaths are framed within [[The political "body count" pattern (Jeffries)]].

## Contexts Within the Source

Chapter 9 is the longest single-event chapter in *Hidden History*. Jeffries frames it as "the official conspiracy theory" (a term he uses deliberately). He draws explicit parallels to the JFK assassination cover-up throughout, invoking the Warren Commission, suppressed witnesses, falsified reports, and the "lone nut" template.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Jeffries presents but does not himself endorse the most extreme theories (that no planes hit the WTC, that victims were fabricated). He focuses on the controlled demolition evidence, the NORAD stand-down, and the phone call impossibilities as his strongest claims.
