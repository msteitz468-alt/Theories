## Claim Statement

Jeffries alleges that Republican Party insider Larry King, while operating the Franklin Credit Union in Omaha, Nebraska, organized a child sex ring supplying underage victims to politicians and powerful business figures, and that FBI and grand jury processes were used to suppress the investigation and imprison a victim-witness rather than prosecute the perpetrators.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Per [[Hidden History (Jeffries)]], drawing substantially on John DeCamp's *The Franklin Cover-Up*:

- Victim Paul Bonacci testified that he was transported across the country, forced to have sex, deliver drugs, and appear in snuff films at King's behest.
- Bonacci alleged participation in Bohemian Grove ceremonies and that a real child was murdered there (described before he had any knowledge of the location; his description was later corroborated by Alex Jones's 2000 infiltration video).
- Bonacci also alleged he had sex with Representative Barney Frank in the basement of Frank's Washington, D.C., home.
- Alisha Owen testified before a federal grand jury naming a Nebraska District Court judge and Larry King as among her abusers; she also claimed to have seen President [[George H.W. Bush]] at one of these parties with a young black male companion.
- Owen was sentenced to nine to twenty-seven years in prison for perjury — described by DeCamp's source Dr. Judianne Densen-Gerber as "unprecedented, probably in the history of the United States." Her seventeen-year-old brother Aaron was found hanged in a correctional center cell on November 9, 1990.
- The FBI refused to investigate the child abuse allegations. A grand jury labeled the charges a "hoax." Witness Troy Boner retracted his statements but later claimed FBI threats forced him to do so.
- Nebraska's Boys Town (the institution immortalized in the 1938 Spencer Tracy film) was allegedly used to recruit troubled youths. Monsignor Robert Hupp, Boys Town's executive director 1973–1985, told DeCamp he was "the wife who did not know, and was the last to find out," and that the Archbishop refused to act when Hupp raised concerns.
- Investigator Gary Caradori, chief of the Nebraska Senate's Franklin Committee, died in a plane crash on July 11, 1990; a farmer on the scene said the plane exploded in midair; child pornography was reportedly scattered across the crash field.
- Documentary *Conspiracy of Silence* (Yorkshire Television) was pulled from its scheduled May 3, 1994, Discovery Channel airing under pressure from US politicians; all copies were ordered destroyed.
- The June 29, 1989, Washington Times reported a related homosexual prostitution ring featuring underage males connected to "key officials of the Reagan and Bush administrations" — centered on lobbyist Craig Spence, who was found dead in Boston's Ritz-Carlton hotel on November 10, 1989.
