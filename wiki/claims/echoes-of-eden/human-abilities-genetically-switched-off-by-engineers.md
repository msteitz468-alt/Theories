## Claim Statement

Wallis argues that the non-human engineers who modified early humanity **deliberately downgraded** our species — performing "neurological interference" to switch off latent abilities (self-healing, telepathy, precognition, remote viewing) inherited from our "animal ancestors" — so as to confine humans to the five physical senses and render them "more amenable to being managed and to working for their feathered superiors." These switched-off capacities are the "secrets of human potential… buried with our ancestors' memories of ET contact."

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

His key text is the Mesoamerican **Popol Vuh** (Quiché Maya), which he reads as describing "a moment in prehistory when the cognitive abilities of our ancestors were deliberately downgraded," the engineer being "a feathered serpent" — Ququmatz / Kukulkan / Quetzalcoatl. He pairs this with an Aboriginal Australian tradition (via Steven Strong) that "we could all do things like this in the past… when we had more DNA… more of our DNA was activated." On Wallis's reading the downgrade left humanity "reliant on 'authorities' to tell them anything they needed to know" beyond the senses.
