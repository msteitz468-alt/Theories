## Claim Statement

Crowley teaches that two ancient formulae — IAO (Isis-Apophis-Osiris) and Tetragrammaton (Yod-He-Vau-He, the Hebrew divine name) — provide the structural backbone for all operative ceremonial Magick, encoding respectively the initiatory cycle of death and rebirth and the creative-to-material flow of magical force. He also presents an expanded Thelemic successor to IAO, the formula FIAOF (numerical value 93, the number of Thelema), suited to the new Aeon of Horus.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**On IAO** (Chapter V, *The Formula of I.A.O.*):

> "This formula is the principal and most characteristic formula of Osiris, of the Redemption of Mankind. I is Isis, Nature, ruined by A, Apophis the Destroyer, and restored to life by the Redeemer Osiris."

> "In beginning a meditation practice, there is always a quiet pleasure, a gentle natural growth… This stage represents Isis. Sooner or later it is succeeded by depression — the Dark Night of the Soul… This is the period of Apophis. It is followed by the arising not of Isis, but of Osiris. The ancient condition is not restored, but a new and superior condition is created, a condition only rendered possible by the process of death."

> "A ceremonial self-initiation may be constructed with advantage on this formula. The essence of it consists in robing yourself as a king, then stripping and slaying yourself, and rising from that death to the Knowledge and Conversation of the Holy Guardian Angel."

Crowley notes that IAO underlies the Third Degree of Masonry, the 5°=6° ceremony of the Golden Dawn, and parallels the myths of Jesus Christ, Dionysus, Prometheus, and all dying-and-rising god figures documented in Frazer's *Golden Bough*. He treats it as "a formula of Tiphareth" — the sphere of the Sun/Son on the Tree of Life.

**On FIAOF** (the Thelemic expansion):

> "The Master Therion, in the Seventeenth year of the Aeon, has reconstructed the Word I A O to satisfy the new conditions of Magick imposed by progress. The Word of the Law being Thelema, whose number is 93, this number should be the canon of a corresponding Mass. Accordingly, he Has expanded I A O by treating the O as an Ayin, and then adding Vau as prefix and affix. The full word is [FIAOF] whose number is 93."

> "FIAOF: 93, the full formula, recognizing the Sun as the Son (Star), as the pre-existent manifested Unit from which all springs and to which all returns. The Great Work is to make the initial [Vau] of Assiah (the world of material illusion) into the final [Vau] of Atziluth, the world of pure reality."

In the new formula, the Aeon of Horus differs from the Aeon of Osiris in that "there are now two sexes in one person throughout, so that each individual is self-procreative sexually." The formula is Love in all cases, and the end is the beginning on a higher plane.

**On Tetragrammaton** (Chapter III, *The Formula of Tetragrammaton*):

> "The formula of Tetragrammaton is the most important for the practical magician."

> "The formation of the Yod is the formulation of the first creative force, of that father who is called 'self-begotten'… The adding of the He to the Yod is the marriage of that Father to the great co-equal Mother… Their union brings forth the son Vau who is the heir. Finally the daughter He is produced."

> "It is the second half of the formula which symbolises the Great Work which we are pledged to accomplish. The first step of this is the attainment of the Knowledge and Conversation of the Holy Guardian Angel, which constitutes the Adept of the Inner Order."

In ceremony, Yod corresponds to a swift and violent creative energy; He to a calm powerful flow of Will; Vau to expansion of consciousness through all space; He-final to crystallization in matter.

## Cross-source connections

Peter Carroll's *[[Liber Null & Psychonaut (Carroll)]]* explicitly draws on Crowley's IAO cycle as one framework for understanding magical initiation, retaining its three-phase structure (beginning enthusiasm, dark night of the soul, new attainment) while stripping away its Osirian theological dressing.

## Opposing or Limiting Notes Within the Source

Crowley specifies that IAO "is now superseded by that of HORUS, the Crowned and Conquering Child" for those who have assimilated the Law of Thelema — though it "remains valid for those who have not yet assimilated the point of view of the Law of Thelema." He also warns that the formula of IAO must not be confused with a vulgar doctrine of individual immortality: "That which persists (if anything persist) is his real John Smithiness, a quality of which he was probably never conscious in his life." The resurrection is not of the ego-personality but of the essential self.
