**Claim Statement**  
The sudden and rapid transition of Egypt and Sumer from primitive neolithic tribal societies into advanced, fully formed, and highly organized civilizations with writing, monumental architecture, and complex administration was initiated and catalyzed by contact with amphibious extraterrestrial beings (the Nommos or Annedoti) from the Sirius star system.

**Source Attribution and Direct Evidence**  
According to *The Sirius Mystery* (Chapter One and Chapter Seven):
* **Sudden Emergence of Civilization:** Robert Temple argues that the civilization of Egypt and Sumer appeared "out of nowhere" without any discernible background of gradual development. He writes: "What we do know is that primitive people suddenly found themselves living in thriving and opulent civilizations and it all happened rather abruptly." (Chapter One)
* **W. B. Emery's Evidence:** Temple quotes archaeologist Walter B. Emery's *Archaic Egypt* to support the abrupt nature of Egyptian civilization: 
  > "At a period approximately 3400 years before Christ, a great change took place in Egypt, and the country passed rapidly from a state of advanced neolithic culture with a complex tribal character to two well-organized monarchies... At the same time the art of writing appears, monumental architecture and the arts and crafts developed to an astonishing degree... All this was achieved within a comparatively short period of time, for there appears to be little or no background to these fundamental developments in writing and architecture." (Chapter One)
* **Babylonian Records of Civilizing Amphibians:** Temple references the fragments of the Babylonian priest Berossus (c. 280 BC), who recorded that the amphibious creature Oannes (and other subsequent "Annedoti" or semidaemons) emerged from the Persian Gulf to teach humanity the arts of writing, sciences, agriculture, geometry, laws, and architecture. According to Berossus, before this contact, the people of Mesopotamia "lived like beasts without order or law," and after Oannes's instructions "nothing new has been invented." (Chapter Seven)
* **Sumerian and Egyptian Link:** Temple states that the earliest dynastic cultures of Sumer and Egypt shared a common astronomical and mythological foundation centered on the jackal and dog stars (Sirius), indicating a shared external instruction by the same visitors.

**Contexts Within the Source**  
Temple frames this claim to argue against the assumption of independent, gradual cultural evolution on Earth. He contends that the lack of archaeological transitional phases in writing and stone-masonry in both regions points to an imported, ready-made cultural system. Under his synthesis, the mythological civilizers described in ancient records (the Babylonian Oannes/Annedoti and the Dogon Nommos) are historical, physical extraterrestrials who possessed a watery physiology requiring them to interact with early humans from aquatic bases (like the Persian Gulf or Nile).
