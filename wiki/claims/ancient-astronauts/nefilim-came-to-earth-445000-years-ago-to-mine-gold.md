## Claim Statement

The Nefilim first landed on Earth about **445,000 years ago**, led by [[Enki (Sitchin)]], establishing Eridu as their first "Earth Station." Their purpose was the **extraction of gold and other minerals** from the AB.ZU (the "Lower World"), with operations reaching to southern Africa.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Sitchin's closing chronology table states:
- **445,000 years ago** — "The Nefilim, led by Enki, arrive on Earth from the Twelfth Planet. Eridu — Earth Station is established in southern Mesopotamia."
- **400,000** — "Enlil arrives on Earth, establishes Nippur as Mission Control Center. Enki establishes sea routes to southern Africa, organizes gold-mining operations."
- **360,000** — "The Nefilim establish Bad-Tibira as their metallurgical center for smelting and refining. Sippar, the spaceport, and other cities of the gods are built."

The mining domain is the **AB.ZU** (Akkadian *apsu*), which Sitchin re-analyses against the conventional "watery deep" to mean a place of mining/ores — "they dug in the Apsu to bring up the minerals of Earth," and texts speak of "the waters of the shiny lodes" where "gold could be river-panned." Enki is "Lord of the AB.ZU," depicted with smelting/fire imagery; one term is glossed "ship for ores of the Lower World."

## Contexts Within the Source

This economic motive (gold) is the premise from which the mutiny and the creation of man follow.
