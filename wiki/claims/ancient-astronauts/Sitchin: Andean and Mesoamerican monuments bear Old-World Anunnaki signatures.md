## Claim Statement

Sitchin argues that the great Andean and Mesoamerican monument complexes — most prominently Tiahuanacu/Puma-Punku, Sacsahuaman, Ollantaytambu, Machu Picchu, Chavin de Huantar, and the Izapan-Olmec sites — bear unmistakable engineering and astronomical signatures of the [[Nefilim (Sitchin)]] (Anunnaki), identical to those found in Old World Anunnaki works such as Baalbek, the Giza pyramids, and Sumerian temple precincts. These signatures include: 45-degree grid alignments keyed to landing corridors; solar/equinoctial observatories echoing Sumerian calendrics; hydraulic gold-panning waterworks of identical design; metal clamps of bronze as found at Near Eastern sites; turquoise inlay and Lost-Wax casting traceable exclusively to the Old World; Sumerian pictographs and zodiacal symbolism replicated in stone; and monolithic construction technologies requiring colossal transport of stone that only Anunnaki-directed labour could accomplish. Taken together these parallels indicate deliberate transatlantic and transpacific contact managed by the Anunnaki rather than independent invention.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

**Astronomical and calendrical parallels.** Sitchin demonstrates that Inca and pre-Inca astronomical knowledge precisely mirrors Sumerian originals. The zodiac names in Quechua and Aymara are cognates of Sumerian terms: "January, the month of Aquarius, was dedicated to Mama Cocha and Capac Cocha, Mother Water and Lord Water." Cuzco itself was divided into twelve wards keyed to the zodiac houses, "and for Aries to have been associated with the spring equinox, as we have shown, we have to turn the clock back more than 4,000 years." The Kalasasaya observatory at Tiahuanacu is found by a German astronomical commission to embody an obliquity angle of 23° 8' 48" — diverging from the present value and implying great antiquity — while Maria Schulten de D'Ebneth's grid study found that "a 45 degree line originating at Tiahuanacu, combined with squares and circles of definite measurements, embraced all the key ancient sites between Tiahuanacu, Cuzco, and Quito in Ecuador, including the all-important Ollantaytambu." Sitchin identifies the same 45-degree flight-path grid as the one the Anunnaki anchored their landing corridors on in the Old World.

**Sacred-wall cosmology identical to Sumerian sources.** The Coricancha's wall above the Great Altar is read as a direct Sumerian solar-system depiction: "What the sacred wall depicted, we must conclude, was not distant or mysterious constellations, but our own Solar System, with the Sun, the Moon, and ten planets, adding up to a total of twelve." The central oval — the orbit of Nibiru — is flanked by five outer and four inner planets exactly as in Sumerian cosmology. "Should we be surprised to find that this was exactly the Sumerian view of our Solar System?"

**Tiahuanacu as Anunnaki metallurgical capital.** The city's name is parsed as TI.ANAKU — "Tin City," after the Mesopotamian term Anaku (tin as the metal of the Anunnaki). Sitchin writes: "Tiahuanacu was there because it was the best place for it to be for what it was: the metallurgical capital of South America, of the New World." He marshals the bronze clamps at Puma-Punku (an alloy requiring advanced Old-World know-how: "bronze is a most difficult alloy to produce, requiring the combination of a certain proportion of copper... with tin"), the subterranean sluice-chambers for ore-washing that parallel Olmec and Chavin waterworks, and the gold-plated walls whose attachment holes Posnansky photographed, with golden nails still in situ, as evidence of an Anunnaki-built precinct parallel to Uruk's E.ANNA. The stairway symbol on Ollantaytambu's monoliths — "all archaeologists agree that the symbol... signified the ascent from Earth to Heaven or, in reverse, a descent from Heaven to Earth" — is the same glyph the Sumerians used and which Posnansky considered Tiahuanacu's "trademark."

**Megalithic engineering beyond known technology.** At Ollantaytambu the colossal monoliths (eleven to fourteen feet high, some weighing 250 tons) were quarried across the valley and raised by means of deep T-shaped cuts "intended to hold together two stone blocks with a metal clamp: as a precaution against earthquakes" — an identical technique to Baalbek, which Sitchin identifies as a [[Nefilim (Sitchin)]] landing platform. "The many similarities we find between Ollantaytambu and Baalbek include the origin of the megaliths... quarried miles away in a valley, then incredibly lifted, transported, and put in place to fit with other stones." Sacsahuaman's vast circular reservoir and sluice-chambers point to a large-scale gold-panning facility: "In the stone vats, what remained was gold."

**Sumerian pictographs, divine iconography, and linguistic traces.** Stone blocks at Chavin de Huantar bear "Egyptian motifs — serpents, pyramids, the sacred Eye of Ra" alongside "Mesopotamian motifs, such as deities within Winged Disks" and figures "wearing conical headdresses, the headgear by which Mesopotamian gods were identified." Sumerian mining pictographs are found incised on rocks along Colombian rivers. Andean metallurgical vocabulary — Anta (copper), ZI.ANA (Heaven), ZI.KI.A (Earth and Water) — is Sumerian. The God of the Golden Tears on the Gate of the Sun holds forked lightning and a metal wand "well known from the ancient Near East, where the god called Ramman... Adad or ISH.KUR ('He of the Far Mountains')... was depicted standing upon a bull... holding the metal tool in one hand and a forked lightning in the other." Bronze tools found near Tiahuanacu with handles shaped as the Sumerian goddess Ninti with twin umbilical cutters are described: "Significantly, some of the tools included in his examples had handles shaped as the head of the Sumerian goddess Ninti with her symbol of the twin umbilical cutters, later the Mistress of the Sinai mines." Turquoise inlaid in the gold tears of Tiahuanacu statues is conclusive because "there is no turquoise... anywhere in South America. It is a mineral whose earliest mining... is believed to have taken place in the Sinai peninsula and in Iran."

## Contexts Within the Source

This claim is the capstone synthesis of [[The Lost Realms (Sitchin)]]. It draws together the Olmec African presence (see [[Sitchin: the Olmec stone heads record Africans brought to the New World by the Anunnaki]]) and the tin-gold metallurgy argument into a single thesis: the [[Nefilim (Sitchin)]] established the New World as a deliberate extension of their Old World mining empire, circa 4000–2000 BC, managing transatlantic contact through divine intermediaries (Thoth/Quetzalcoatl, Adad/Viracocha, and Enlilite highland gods). The Anunnaki's need to resupply tin to Old World Bronze Age civilisations after circa 2600 BC — when Old World tin sources were exhausted — provides the economic motive for the entire New World enterprise, anchored at Lake Titicaca. Sitchin also links the 45-degree grid of Andean sites to Anunnaki flight-path geometry first described in The Stairway to Heaven, reinforcing the claim that Ollantaytambu and the Paracas Bay trident were Anunnaki landing and navigation markers.

## Opposing Information Within the Source

Sitchin acknowledges mainstream archaeology assigns all these monuments to identifiable pre-Columbian cultures (Chavin, Tiahuanacu, Inca, Olmec) with no transoceanic contact. Posnansky's extreme dates for Tiahuanacu (15,000 BC or 9300 BC) were rejected by the scientific community and even Posnansky himself later retreated to a range of 4050–10,150 BC. The German Astronomical Commission confirmed the Kalasasaya's anomalous obliquity but declined to endorse Posnansky's chronological conclusions definitively. The metallurgical study by Erland Nordenskidld, which Sitchin cites, establishes that South American bronze tools match Old World shapes — but Nordenskidld drew no transatlantic-contact conclusions and presented this as an unresolved anomaly, not evidence of Anunnaki intervention. The mainstream scholarly consensus is that the Andean monuments were built by indigenous peoples without external direction, and that cosmological and calendrical parallels reflect convergent development or limited documented contact, not an Anunnaki-coordinated civilisation programme.
