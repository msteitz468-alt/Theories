## Claim Statement

Bramley argues the corrupted Brotherhood institutionalized its teachings in the Egyptian "Mystery Schools," which turned a once-scientific, step-by-step spiritual education into an instrument of repression: access was restricted to an elite, advanced teachings were forbidden to be written down (guaranteeing their loss), and Akhenaton converted them into deliberately incomprehensible mystical symbols. The result is that genuine spiritual knowledge became "misidentified with bizarre symbols and mystery" — disgraced today as occultism and superstition.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

Bramley (citing Rosicrucian founder H. Spencer Lewis) says the first Mystery School temple was built by Cheops, that Thutmose III made the Brotherhood "a completely closed order," and that Akhenaton encoded the teachings in symbols and pioneered **monotheism**. He calls these restrictions, oral-only transmission, and symbol-coding successive pieces of "the Biblical 'revolving sword'" blocking access to the "tree of knowledge."
