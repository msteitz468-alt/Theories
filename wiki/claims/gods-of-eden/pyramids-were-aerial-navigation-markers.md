## Claim Statement

Bramley argues the Giza pyramids functioned as a precise aerial-navigation reference system for the Custodians. The Great Pyramid's sides face the compass points almost exactly, it sits near the 30th parallel at the center of Earth's landmasses, and (when limestone-cased) reflected sunlight to be visible from far away — allowing the planet to be sectioned into a 30/60/90-degree navigational grid. He links this to reported pyramid-like formations photographed on the Moon (Sea of Tranquility, 1966) and Mars (Cydonia, 1976).

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He quotes Soviet engineer Alexander Abromov that the lunar objects are "arranged in precisely the same way as the apices of the three great pyramids," and notes the first pyramid's design reputedly "descended… from heaven" to Imhotep, son of the god Ptah. Egyptian records, he says, indicate human labor built them — consistent with the slave-race thesis — and the pharaohs were fed the "cruel joke" that mummification would let them join the gods in the sky.
