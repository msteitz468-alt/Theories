## Claim Statement

Bramley argues the Custodians maintain control by the classic "divide and conquer" / third-party manipulation technique described by Machiavelli, operated covertly through the Brotherhood network. The instigator (1) erects conflicts that make people fight each other rather than the instigator, (2) stays hidden as the true cause, (3) lends support to all warring sides, and (4) appears as the benevolent solver of the conflicts.

## Source Attribution and Direct Evidence

He quotes *The Prince* on holding subject towns "distracted by factions" and supporting "now one, now the other party… until they got sick of their turbulent way of living." He notes Machiavelli warned the technique backfires if *both* sides discover the hidden manipulator — which is why Custodial secrecy (via the Brotherhood) is essential. The Bible's "hardened their hearts" passages are presented as Custodians manipulating peoples into war.

## Contexts Within the Source

This is the mechanism Bramley applies throughout — to the conquest of Canaan ([[Jehovah was a succession of Custodial management teams, not a Supreme Being]]), the religious wars, the revolutions, and modern geopolitics — fulfilling the Custodial aim from the Tower of Babel to keep humanity disunited.
