## Phenomenon Overview

The Roswell UFO Incident refers to the recovery of debris from a crashed object near Roswell, New Mexico, in early July 1947. On July 8, 1947, the Roswell Army Air Field (RAAF) issued a press release stating that they had recovered a "flying disc." Within hours, the War Department released a correction stating that the recovered object was actually a conventional weather balloon. Decades later, testimonies from military and civilian witnesses emerged, alleging that the recovered object was an extraterrestrial spacecraft containing alien bodies, leading to a major controversy with competing explanations.

## Competing Source-Specific Explanations

### Extraterrestrial Craft Crash & Cover-Up

* **Charles Berlitz & William L. Moore (*The Roswell Incident*):**  
  Asserts that the crashed object was a genuine extraterrestrial spacecraft. They document that the debris consisted of unearthly materials (indestructible foil-like metal and structural beams with physical symbols) and that the military recovered several dead alien bodies. According to this work, the weather balloon explanation was a rapidly substituted military cover-up designed to hide the reality of alien contact from the public. See [[A UFO crashed near Roswell in July 1947 and the AAF recovered unearthly debris]] and [[The military recovered dead extraterrestrial bodies at Roswell]].

* **Milton William Cooper (*Behold a Pale Horse*):**  
  Agrees that a spacecraft crashed at Roswell, claiming that it was one of several crashes between 1947 and 1952. Cooper states that the military recovered extraterrestrial bodies and that the incident prompted the establishment of a secret government control group (Majestic Twelve / MJ-12) to manage the alien presence and negotiate secret treaties. He also notes that the extreme secrecy surrounding the crash led to subsequent presidents, including Dwight D. Eisenhower, being denied direct access to the recovery files. See [[Even President Eisenhower was denied the clearance to access the Roswell crash-recovery files]] and [[Claim: Cooper asserts 16 crashed alien craft and 65 bodies were recovered by the US government 1947–1952]].

### Soviet Psychological Warfare & Human Experimentation

* **Annie Jacobsen (*Area 51: An Uncensored History*):**  
  Presents a radically different, non-extraterrestrial explanation based on testimonies from an anonymous EG&G engineer. Jacobsen asserts that the Roswell craft was a pilotless Horten-style flying wing sent by Soviet leader Joseph Stalin to spark mass panic in the United States, reminiscent of Orson Welles's *War of the Worlds* broadcast. The child-sized bodies recovered from the wreckage were not aliens but human guinea pigs (children around 13 years old) genetically altered by Nazi doctor Josef Mengele to appear deformed. According to this account, the craft featured Russian/Cyrillic-like writing on its interior and was intentionally flown into U.S. airspace before crashing. See [[Roswell disc child bodies Stalin Mengele hoax per EG&G engineer Jacobsen source]] and [[Roswell craft had Cyrillic writing per Jacobsen source]].

### Non-Human Intelligence & Legacy Programs

* **Luis Elizondo (*Imminent*):**  
  Frames Roswell as a historical reality involving the recovery of technology from a non-human intelligence (NHI). Citing briefings from physicist Harold Puthoff, Elizondo states that the Roswell crash did occur and that four non-human bodies were retrieved. He asserts that the materials recovered from this and other UAP crashes have been sequestered within highly classified legacy reverse-engineering programs managed by defense contractors, hidden from public oversight and standard congressional control. See [[Puthoff confirmed Roswell real with four nonhuman bodies to Elizondo]] and [[Legacy Program contractors hold nonhuman technology and materials]].
