## Phenomenon Overview

The assassination of President John F. Kennedy occurred on November 22, 1963, in Dealey Plaza, Dallas, Texas, while he was riding in a presidential motorcade. The official Warren Commission investigation concluded that Lee Harvey Oswald acted as a lone gunman, shooting the President from the Texas School Book Depository. Oswald was subsequently shot and killed by Jack Ruby two days later while in police custody. Because of the political climate, forensic and ballistics discrepancies, and the rapid elimination of the prime suspect, the assassination has generated multiple alternative explanations attributing the crime to a deep-state conspiracy, secret societies, or other hidden groups.

* **David Talbot (*The Devil's Chessboard*):**  
  Presents the assassination as a highly coordinated coup d'état engineered by the national security state and elements of the Central Intelligence Agency, with former director **[[Allen Dulles]]** as the likely organizer. Talbot argues that Kennedy's peace initiatives during the Cold War and his decision to fire Dulles after the Bay of Pigs threat created deep hostility within the military-intelligence establishment. He details E. Howard Hunt's deathbed confession naming a CIA-guided operation involving figures like William Harvey and David Morales, and argues that Dulles subsequently steered the Warren Commission to cover up the conspiracy and maintain the lone-gunman narrative. ### Alien Disclosure Suppression & Federal Reserve Resistance

* **Milton William Cooper (*Behold a Pale Horse*):**  
  Asserts that Kennedy was assassinated to prevent him from revealing the extraterrestrial presence to the American public and to stop his attempt to bypass the Federal Reserve via Executive Order 11110 (printing Treasury-backed currency). Cooper claims that during the motorcade in Dealey Plaza, the driver of the presidential limousine, Secret Service agent William Greer, turned around and shot Kennedy in the head using a gas-powered pressure weapon developed by the CIA's MKULTRA program. According to Cooper, the operation was ordered by the Bilderberg Policy Committee to protect the elite's monopoly on credit and alien technology.
