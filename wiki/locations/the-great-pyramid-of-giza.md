## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

In the tablets the Great Pyramid was built by Thoth — not Cheops — over the entrance to the Halls of Amenti, "patterned after the pyramid of Earth force," using "the power that overcomes Earth force (gravity)." It contains a force-chamber, a circular passage, a crystal set in the apex "sending the ray into the 'Time-Space,'" and hidden chambers holding "the keys to Amenti." Doreal calls it a "temple of initiation… [where] Jesus, Solomon, Apollonius and others were initiated."

## Textual Appearances

- Tablet 1: its construction over the Amenti gateway and the sarcophagus initiation rite.
- Tablet 5: the buried spaceship beneath a nearby lion-shaped marker (the Sphinx), reachable via a pyramid passage opened by "the KEY of the SEVEN."
