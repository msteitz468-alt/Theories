## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

The Nevada Test Site is a 1,350-square-mile parcel inside the larger Nevada Test and Training Range. Beginning in 1951 on President Truman's orders, 105 nuclear weapons were exploded aboveground and 828 underground. The facility contains the largest amount of weapons-grade plutonium and uranium in the United States not secured inside a nuclear laboratory, per Jacobsen.

Area 51 sits just outside the NTS, approximately five miles northeast of its northernmost corner. Numbered sectors (Area 12, Area 19, Area 20) inside the test site bear traces of thermonuclear testing.
