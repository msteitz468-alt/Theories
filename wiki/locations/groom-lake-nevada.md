## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

Groom Lake is the flat, dry lake bed around which Area 51 facilities were built. The CIA chose the site partly because the lake bed doubled as a natural runway for secret spy planes.

## Textual Appearances

- **Prologue**: Named as the geographic core of Area 51 construction.
- **Chapter 1**: Described as the visual center of the Box on approach; runway lights flash on at night for test flights; "the pole" pylon for radar cross-section measurement sits near the lake bed.
