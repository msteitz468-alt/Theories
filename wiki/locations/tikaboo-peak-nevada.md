## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

Tikaboo Peak is the mountain from which outsiders without clearance can occasionally glimpse Area 51 activity through binoculars. Nighttime viewing is described as best because daytime heat distortion obscures structures.

## Textual Appearances

- **Chapter 1**: Viewing instructions (ten-hour commitment, four-wheel-drive, hiking boots); Norio Hayakawa's film crew directed here by Lazar.
