## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

Jaffeth is Oahspe's name for **China**, one of the great divisions of the earth
peopled by survivors of [[Pan (Whaga, the submerged continent)]]. It is the land of the lawgivers [[Po]]
and [[Chine]] (after whom it is said to be named) and of [[Ka'yu (Confucius)]]
(Confucius). In the cycle of the false Gods it fell under [[Kabalactes (the false Buddha)]] and
the name **Te-in** was given for the Great Spirit there.

## Textual Appearances

Recurs throughout the histories as one of the four/five divisions of the earth.
