## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

The Texas city where President Kennedy was assassinated in Dealey Plaza on November 22, 1963. Talbot notes Dallas's reactionary political climate (the Dallas Morning News, Ted Dealey) and Dulles's anomalous late-October 1963 visit to the city.

## Textual Appearances

Site of the assassination, Oswald's arrest and killing, and (per F. Mark Wyatt's suspicion) Bill Harvey's possible presence in November 1963.
