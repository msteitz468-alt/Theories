## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

The Nevada Test and Training Range encompasses 4,687 square miles—described as slightly smaller than Connecticut, three times Rhode Island, more than twice Delaware. It is the largest government-controlled land parcel in the United States per Jacobsen.

Area 51 sits inside this range but outside the Nevada Test Site proper. Gray areas at the borders—craggy mountains and dry lake beds not controlled by the Department of Defense—hosted the most secret projects.
