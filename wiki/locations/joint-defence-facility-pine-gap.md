Joint US/Australian satellite ground station 18 km south-west of Alice Springs, central Australia, that collects Defense Support Program early-warning satellite data. Coulthart reports that operators sometimes detect objects ('Fast Walkers') that are not missiles and appear to change course under intelligent control, and that military insiders described anomalous sightings over Pine Gap stretching across decades.
