## Geographical and Historical Context from Source

The "mother country" of the tablets — a civilization of ten island-kingdoms. Thoth's birthplace is given as "the great city of KEOR on the island of UNDAL." Undal held the Temple of Light built by the Dweller; the Children of Light dwelt among the Atlanteans, who possessed advanced "magic-science." The land was destroyed in the cataclysm; only Undal (Unal) and the Dweller's people survived, after which the survivors fled to Egypt.

## Textual Appearances

- Tablet 1: Keor, Undal, the renewal of Atlantean life in Amenti, the great waters.
- Tablet 5: the Dweller's Temple, the ten kingdoms, the opening of the forbidden gateway and the sinking.
