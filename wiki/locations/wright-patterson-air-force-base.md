US Air Force base near Dayton, Ohio, long home to the Foreign Technology Division (later folded into the National Air and Space Intelligence Center) and central to UAP lore as the alleged repository of Roswell wreckage. Coulthart's source Nat Kobitz said he was invited there as a private welding expert and shown a strange titanium-alloy fragment whose bond appeared integral/cast rather than welded.
