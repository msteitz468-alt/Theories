## Source Description

*The Nature of Personal Reality: Specific, Practical Techniques for Solving Everyday Problems and Enriching Your Life* is a channeled book by Jane Roberts, acting as a medium for the non-physical entity Seth. The book was dictated between June 7, 1972, and July 11, 1973, and published in 1974 by Prentice-Hall. 

Serving as a direct sequel to *Seth Speaks*, the book focuses on how conscious beliefs, suggestions, and expectations construct individual physical reality, including biological health, daily circumstances, and the social environment. Seth presents the conscious mind as the director of unconscious energy and emphasizes that the present moment is the only active point of power.
