## Source Description

*The Truth About COVID-19: Exposing the Great Reset, Lockdowns, Vaccine Passports, and the New Normal* (2021) by osteopath Joseph Mercola and organic-food activist Ronnie Cummins (foreword by Robert F. Kennedy Jr.) is a COVID-skeptic polemic. Its argument has two intertwined strands: (1) that SARS-CoV-2 was **lab-engineered** through reckless "gain-of-function" research and (the authors say) *accidentally* released; and (2) that a "global elite" (Bill Gates, the WHO, the World Economic Forum, Big Tech, and Big Pharma) **anticipated and then exploited** the pandemic — via fear, censorship, lockdowns, and vaccines — to advance a "Great Reset" toward a "digital dictatorship." It contends COVID severity was overstated and comorbidity-driven, that lockdowns/mandates eroded liberty, that low-cost treatments were suppressed, and that the real defense is metabolic health.

The book is presented as exposing "the official story," and the authors explicitly distinguish "anticipated" from a fully scripted "plandemic." These are the authors' claims, recorded here as they frame them; the wiki does not adjudicate them.

### Chapter One: How the Pandemic Plans Unfolded (Ronnie Cummins)
Cummins lays out the book's dual thesis: SARS-CoV-2 was lab-engineered through reckless "gain-of-function" research and "apparently accidentally, rather than deliberately, released," while the pandemic was "long anticipated" and exploited by a "global elite" (Bill Gates, the WHO, the WEF, Big Tech, Big Pharma) to advance a "digital dictatorship" and "Great Reset." ### Chapter Two: Lab Leak or Natural Origin? (Ronnie Cummins)
A detailed case that the "official story" of natural zoonotic origin is "crumbling." Cummins argues the bat-to-pangolin recombination is implausible, that the furin cleavage site and high human-ACE2 affinity indicate the virus was "pre-adapted" to humans, and that "seamless" genetic-engineering techniques leave no trace. ## Internal Tensions or Multiple Accounts

The two authors differ in emphasis: Cummins (Ch. 1) writes that "the COVID-19 pandemic was not necessarily planned and then deliberately executed… but… was long anticipated," and that the virus was "apparently accidentally, rather than deliberately, released"; Mercola's chapters lean harder toward "a planned event… now being milked." The book frames its case as "the preponderance of evidence" against a censored "official story," acknowledges COVID-19 as "a genuine health threat" to the vulnerable, and presents its treatment and origin claims as contested at the time of writing (2021) — citing suppressed studies and pending investigations rather than settled conclusions.
