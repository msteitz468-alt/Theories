## Source Description

*The Book of Lies* (full ironic title *…which is also Falsely Called BREAKS…*) is a 1913 work by
[[Aleister Crowley]] of 93 short, numbered chapters — paradoxes, poems, and riddles, each with a
punning title and (in the annotated editions) a commentary. It is a mystical-instructional companion to
his Thelemic system, treating themes of non-duality, the dissolution of the ego, and the formulae of
attainment. Recorded descriptively, not adjudicated. See [[Thelema and Crowley's system of Magick]].
