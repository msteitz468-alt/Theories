**Source Description**

*Behold a Pale Horse* (Light Technology Publishing, Flagstaff AZ, 1991; ISBN 978-0-929385-22-8) is the primary work of Milton William Cooper, a former U.S. Navy petty officer who claimed access to classified intelligence. The title is drawn from Revelation 6:8. The book became one of the most widely read conspiracy texts of the 1990s.

The book comprises 17 chapters (Introduction through Protocols of the Elders of Zion) and seven appendices (A–G plus supplemental material on drug involvement and the Kurzweil vs. Hopkins affair). It purports to document: the existence of a secret global elite orchestrating a New World Order; extraterrestrial contact and government cover-up; suppressed documents including *Silent Weapons for Quiet Wars*; FEMA as a proto-police-state instrument; U.S. government drug involvement; and related topics.

Cooper writes in the Preface: "The ideas and conclusions expressed in this work are mine alone. It is possible that one or more conclusions may be wrong. The purpose of this book is to convince you (the reader) that something is terribly wrong."

**Chapter Overview**

- **Introduction / Preface** (pp. 1–4): Cooper's core worldview, USS Tiru UFO sighting, personal creed
- **Foreword** (pp. 5–34): Autobiography, family background, military service, death threats, disinformation campaigns against him
- **Chapter 1: Silent Weapons for Quiet Wars** (pp. 35–66): Reproduction of and commentary on a purported policy document found in an IBM copier in 1986, dated May 1979. Cooper identifies it as the Bilderberg Group's 1954 "Quiet War" declaration — a plan for population control through economic manipulation
- **Chapter 2: Secret Societies and the New World Order** (pp. 67–98): The Illuminati, Skull & Bones, Scroll & Key, JASON Society, CFR, Trilateral Commission, Knights of Malta, Vatican; their interlocking structure and shared NWO goal
- **Chapter 3: Oath of Initiation of an Unidentified Secret Order** (pp. 99–102): Reproduced oath text
- **Chapter 4: Secret Treaty of Verona** (pp. 103–108): 1822 treaty text and commentary
- **Chapter 5: Good-by USA, Hello New World Order** (pp. 109–120): Legislative and regulatory mechanisms for NWO transition
- **Chapter 6: H.R. 4079 and FEMA** (pp. 121–150): FEMA as martial-law infrastructure; Garden Plot and Cable Splicer programs; 25 million civilian files; detention camps
- **Chapter 7: Anti-Drug Abuse Act of 1988** (pp. 151–158): HR 5210; legislative provisions enabling police-state powers
- **Chapter 8: Are the Sheep Ready to Shear?** (pp. 159–162): Media control and public conditioning
- **Chapter 9: Anatomy of an Alliance** (pp. 163–178): US-Soviet cooperation concealed behind Cold War theatre
- **Chapter 10: Lessons from Lithuania** (pp. 179–182)
- **Chapter 11: Coup de Grace** (pp. 183–194): Nixon/MAJORITY messages; Apollo-Soyuz; phone conversations with sources
- **Chapter 12: The Secret Government** (pp. 195–238): The central UFO chapter — MJ-12 origin; 16 crashed craft / 65 bodies / 1 live alien 1947–1952; EBE captive; JFK assassination by driver Greer; three Alternatives; AIDS as population reduction; Mars colony; George Bush / CIA drug trade
- **Chapter 13: Treason in High Places** (pp. 239–250): UN treaty vs. US sovereignty
- **Chapter 14: A Proposed Constitutional Model for the Newstates of America** (pp. 251–266): Reproduced document showing planned replacement constitution
- **Chapter 15: Protocols of the Elders of Zion** (pp. 267–330): Full text with Cooper commentary
- **Chapter 16: The Story of Jonathan May** (pp. 331–360): May's alternative banking scheme and prosecution
- **Chapter 17: Army Intelligence / Satanic Church Connection** (pp. 361–380): Documentation of U.S. Army ties to Anton LaVey's Church of Satan
- **Appendix B: UFOs and Area 51** (pp. 397–441): Photocopied government documents (largely images in PDF, text extraction minimal)
- **Appendix C: Alien Implants** (pp. 442–444)
- **Appendix D: AIDS** (pp. 445–447)
- **Appendix E: New World Order** (pp. 448–472): Club of Rome "Confidential" 1973 report, regionalized world model, ten global "kingdoms"
- **Appendix F: U.S. Government Drug Involvement** (pp. 473–489)

**Internal Tensions or Multiple Accounts**

Cooper himself repeatedly acknowledges uncertainty. Regarding the alien scenario in Chapter 12 he offers five mutually exclusive conclusions, including the possibility that "the whole alien scenario is the greatest hoax in history designed to create an alien enemy from outer space in order to expedite the formation of a one-world government." He states: "I have found evidence that this could be true." This is the most explicit self-qualification in the book — Cooper is uncertain whether aliens are real but certain the secret power structure is real regardless.
