## Source Description

*The Practice of Enochian Magick* collects [[Aleister Crowley]]'s work on the **Enochian** (or
"Angelic") system of magic — the system of angelic invocations, the **Watchtowers** of the four
elements, the **Enochian Calls/Keys**, and the **thirty Aethyrs** — originally received by
[[John Dee (1527–1608-9)|John Dee]] and Edward Kelley in the 1580s and adapted by the Hermetic Order of
the Golden Dawn and then by Crowley. It includes the framework Crowley used for his own visionary
exploration of the Aethyrs (*The Vision and the Voice*). See [[Thelema and Crowley's system of Magick]].

This grounds the wiki's modern Enochian material — the
[[Success Magick (Damon Brand's Enochian system)|simplified Enochian]] systems descend from this
Dee→Golden Dawn→Crowley line. Recorded descriptively, not adjudicated.
