## Source Description

*My Big TOE* is Thomas Campbell's 2005 trilogy presenting a unified theory of everything (TOE) grounded in consciousness, digital physics, and the author's personal investigation of non-physical reality. The title stands for "My Big Picture Theory Of Everything." Campbell, a physicist who worked on simulation modeling and electromagnetic systems, co-led early out-of-body research with Robert A. Monroe beginning in 1972 and spent decades exploring non-physical reality through altered states.

The trilogy is organized into six sections across three books:
- **Book 1 (Awakening)**: Section 1 (author biography) + Section 2 (foundational concepts: AUO, AUM, evolution, entropy, time, space)
- **Book 2 (Discovery)**: Section 3 (consciousness-evolution interface: ego, love, free will, psi) + Section 4 (operational consciousness model; PMR derivation)
- **Book 3 (Inner Workings)**: Section 5 (NPMR mechanics, digital physics) + Section 6 (implications)

Campbell's explicit methodological claim: starting from only two assumptions (primordial consciousness, the Fundamental Process of evolution), he derives physics, the nature of space-time, the origins of human consciousness, and a working model of larger reality.

## Personal Background and Genesis

Campbell describes first encountering Robert A. Monroe's *[[Journeys Out of the Body]]* in 1972 when his boss, Bill Yost, gave him the book at a Department of Defense laboratory where Campbell worked as a physicist. After meeting Monroe at Whistlefield estate and being satisfied of Monroe's intellectual credibility and sanity, Campbell (with colleague Dennis) joined Monroe's early research, helping construct instruments for measuring physiological correlates of out-of-body states. Campbell and Dennis became part of Monroe's early research team alongside Nancy Lea Honeycutt.

Campbell describes his own prior experience with altered states through meditation (since college), which predisposed him toward openness to Monroe's claims. He describes being able to enter meditative states quickly and completely, to the point of appearing unconscious while apparently in deep concentration.

### Ontological

- Claim: Love is technically the expression of low-entropy consciousness
- Claim: The purpose of human life is consciousness quality evolution

- Claim: Free will is a necessary feature of a consciousness-evolution system

### Paranormal and Psi
- Claim: Psi phenomena are acausal effects of consciousness operating outside PMR rule-set
- Claim: The Psi Uncertainty Principle limits psi access to those who can use it constructively
- Claim: Precognition is access to computed probable reality surfaces by consciousness

## Internal Tensions or Multiple Accounts

Campbell acknowledges that his model is inherently difficult to verify from within PMR, since the very perceptual systems available to us are products of the PMR rule-set he is describing. He states explicitly that consciousness must be expanded beyond normal PMR awareness to fully comprehend or verify the larger reality framework.

He also notes multiple failed or "unprofitable" evolutionary experiments (including NPMRn beings who became trapped in intellectual loops without accountability), which he presents as AUM's design reasoning for creating the PMR space-time learning lab. Campbell does not treat his framework as certain but as a working model subject to ongoing exploration.
