## Source Description

*The Secrets of Solomon's Temple: Discover the Hidden Truth that Lies at the Heart of Freemasonry*
(2011) by **[[Kevin L. Gest]]** argues that Solomon's Temple was deliberately designed as a solar
instrument — its orientation, dimensions, and furnishings encoding sun-alignment and sacred-geometry
knowledge inherited from Egypt and Pythagorean mystery schools — and that this "sun-hypothesis" is the
concealed origin of Freemasonry's symbolism and ritual. The book works chapter by chapter from solar
symbolism in ancient religion, through the biblical narrative of Moses, David, and Solomon, to a
reconstructed architectural design of the Temple, the pillars Jachin and Boaz, the Knights Templar and
Hiram Abif, and the emergence of speculative Freemasonry in 1717.

## Chapter Summaries

### Introduction–Chapter Two: Why the Sun?; The Rise of the Sun

Gest frames the book as a personal investigation, as a Freemason, into why his lodge's ceiling bears a
zodiac and the floor a Mosaic pavement. He traces solar/astronomical symbolism to ancient civilizations
he argues tracked the precession of the equinoxes millennia before Hipparchus, and argues major
Christian festivals and symbols were adapted from earlier solar and pagan worship.

**Claims:** [[Gest argues ancient civilizations tracked precession of the equinoxes millennia before Hipparchus]] · [[Gest argues major Christian festivals and symbols were adapted from earlier solar and pagan worship]] · [[Gest argues the Royal Society was founded predominantly by Freemasons]] · [[Gest argues the Sussex Masonic Temple's zodiac and pavement encode ancient solar-astronomical knowledge]]

### Chapters Three–Six: Secret Knowledge; Solomon's Seal; Pythagoras; Ecclesiastical Influence

Gest develops sacred geometry — the Vesica Piscis and its derivatives (phi, the pentagram/hexagram,
the "Mason's secret square") — as the technical toolkit behind cathedral and Masonic-temple design,
proposing his own account of why both the hexagram and pentagram are called "Solomon's Seal." He
traces Pythagoras's biography, mystery-school teachings, and number symbolism as a claimed antecedent
of Masonic doctrine and biblical gematria, and reads the 1 Kings workforce totals for Solomon's Temple
as encoded Pythagorean numerology. Ecclesiastical material extends the vesica/geometry thesis to
Peterborough, Chartres, and Barcelona.

**Persons:** [[Pythagoras (Gest's account)]]
**Concepts:** [[Vesica Piscis as the generative form of sacred geometry (Gest)]]

### Chapters Seven–Eight: Sacred Knowledge Resurfaces; Things May Not Be as We Were Taught

Gest applies vesica/sacred-geometry analysis to the Brighton Masonic Temple and reads the number 666
as an encoding of the precession of the equinoxes rather than a mark of evil. He then argues biblical
civilization timelines were older and more advanced than mid-20th-century teaching allowed, offers
natural-event explanations for the Exodus plagues and the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah, and cites
Barbara Thiering's Essene-community reading of Mary's virgin conception and Jesus's marriage to Mary
Magdalene.

**Claims:** [[Gest argues the Brighton Masonic Temple's proportions encode Vesica Piscis, phi, and sacred numbers]] · [[Gest argues the number 666 encodes the precession of the equinoxes rather than evil]] · [[Gest argues biblical civilization timelines were far older and more advanced than mid-20th-century teaching allowed]] · [[Gest offers natural-event explanations for the Exodus plagues and the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah]] · [[Gest cites Barbara Thiering's claim that Mary's virgin conception and Jesus's marriage to Mary Magdalene are Essene-community readings]]

### Chapters Nine–Eleven: Moses Prepared the Foundations; David, Heresy, and Chronology; Solomon and His Wisdom

Gest argues Moses was raised as an Egyptian prince initiated into priestly ancient wisdom, and that the
Ark of the Covenant's dimensions were deliberately chosen to encode phi and sacred numbers. He cites
David Rohl's revised chronology identifying Saul and David as contemporaries of the heretical pharaoh
Akhenaten, reads biblical chronology (from Ussher onward) as repeatedly revised with the Red Sea
crossing misidentified, and connects the Shechinah to a forty-year Venus pentagram cycle aligned with
the Temple's dawn orientation. Solomon's reputed wisdom is presented as educated astronomical and
priestly knowledge rather than innate insight.

### Chapter Twelve: Who Was Solomon? What Was Moloch?

Gest argues Solomon was a title or role rather than a personal name, that his birth name was Jedidiah
and "Solomon" derives from "Sol Amon" (a temple name for the Sun and Moon), and that the Israelites
practiced child sacrifice to Moloch, possibly as part of the Temple's dedication ceremony.

**Claims:** [[Gest argues Solomon was a title or role rather than a personal name]] · [[Gest argues Solomon's birth name was Jedidiah and that "Solomon" derives from "Sol Amon," a temple name for the Sun and Moon]] · [[Gest argues the Israelites practiced child sacrifice to Moloch and that this rite may have been part of the Temple's dedication ceremony]]
**Persons:** [[Solomon (Jedidiah), King of Israel (Gest's account)]]

### Chapters Thirteen–Fourteen: Revealing the Design; Secrets in the Pillars

Gest reconstructs the Temple's ground plan from Vesica Piscis circles and cubit-based numerology,
argues its clerestory windows, cherubim, and five-sided door jambs functioned as a solar seasonal
calendar, and presents the pillars Jachin and Boaz as solar-lunar calendrical instruments with
mathematically encoded dimensions.

**Claims:** [[Gest reconstructs Solomon's Temple ground plan from Vesica Piscis circles and cubit-based numerology]] · [[Gest argues the Temple's clerestory windows, cherubim, and five-sided door jambs functioned as a solar seasonal calendar]] · [[Gest argues the pillars Jachin and Boaz were solar-lunar calendrical instruments with mathematically encoded dimensions]]

### Chapters Fifteen–Sixteen: The Knights Templar Legacy and a New Hiram Abif; The Closing

Gest identifies Hiram Abif not as a historical person but as an allegory for suppressed heliocentric
knowledge, describes the founding, persecution, and dissolution of the Knights Templar order, argues
Cambridge's Templar-built Round Church encodes solar-calendar alignments like Solomon's Temple, and
proposes the Grand Lodge of England was founded in 1717 to encode a Hebrew-numerological reference to
the number 20.

### Appendices 1–3: The Megalithic Yard; the Tabernacle as a Model of the Universe; a Speculative Chronology

Gest reproduces Lomas and Knight's method for reconstructing Alexander Thom's megalithic yard from
astronomical observation, reproduces Josephus's claim that the Tabernacle and priestly garments were
built as a symbolic model of the universe, and closes with his own speculative chronology tracing an
unbroken transmission of suppressed heliocentric and geometric knowledge from prehistory to modern
Freemasonry.
