## Source Description

*None Dare Call It Conspiracy* is a book by journalist Gary Allen and co-author Larry Abraham, published in 1971 (with first printings appearing in February 1972) by Concord Press. The book represents a key foundational text of late 20th-century alternative history and conspiracy literature, selling millions of copies through grassroots distribution channels. It features an introduction by U.S. Congressman John G. Schmitz.

The book proposes a cohesive synthesis of modern political history, arguing that major national and international events—including wars, economic depressions, revolutions, and political shifts—are not accidental but are deliberately planned and executed by a self-perpetuating, wealthy clique of international financiers and elites known as "the Insiders." Under this synthesis, the Insiders utilize central banking systems (such as the Federal Reserve) to manipulate economies, bankroll revolutionary movements (including the Bolshevik Revolution), establish front organizations (such as the Council on Foreign Relations and the Round Table groups) to control foreign policy, and promote state socialism as a tool for creating a monopolistic world system of total financial and political control.

## Chapter Summaries

### Preface and Introduction
Introduces the book's authors, its massive grassroots printing and distribution figures, and a warning by Congressman John G. Schmitz that the book will face media silence and ridicule by the Establishment's "intellectual" opinion makers.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[History is planned and engineered by a self-perpetuating clique of international bankers known as the Insiders]]

### Chapter 1: Don't Confuse Me With Facts
Critiques the "accidental theory of history" taught in academic institutions and promoted by major media outlets, arguing that it is statistically absurd to view decades of disastrous political and economic decisions as mere blunders. Allen presents the cause-and-effect or "conspiratorial theory of history" and quotes extensively from Georgetown Professor Carroll Quigley's *Tragedy and Hope* to confirm the existence of an international banking network working secretly to establish a world system of financial and political control.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[History is planned and engineered by a self-perpetuating clique of international bankers known as the Insiders]]

### Chapter 2: Socialism-Royal Road to Power for the Super-Rich
Redefines "Communism" not as a movement of the working class but as an international conspiratorial drive for power by wealthy elites. Allen argues that the Insiders utilize socialism as the ultimate tool to establish state monopolies, eliminate free-enterprise competitors, and centralize all political and economic power in the hands of the government, which they control.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[Socialism is not a movement of the downtrodden masses, but a tool utilized by the super-rich to establish monopolistic control over governments, resources, and individuals]]

### Chapter 3: The Money Manipulators
Examines the creation of the U.S. Federal Reserve System at the secret Jekyll Island meeting in 1910. Asserts that the Federal Reserve is a private banking cartel designed to control the money supply, inflate credit, and deliberately engineer economic panics and depressions (such as the 1920–21 agricultural depression and the 1929 stock market crash) to consolidate assets and control.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[The Federal Reserve Act was drafted in secret at Jekyll Island in 1910]]
  * [[The Federal Reserve deliberately caused the Great Depression of 1929-31]]
  * [[The Federal Reserve financed the Allies and brought the US into World War I]]

### Chapter 4: Bankrolling the Bolshevik Revolution
Asserts that international financiers in New York, London, and Paris financed the Bolshevik Revolution of 1917. Focuses on Jacob Schiff (Kuhn, Loeb & Co.) providing $20 million, Max Warburg financing Lenin, and Lord Alfred Milner (leader of the Round Table group) coordinating payments. Allen argues that the bankers do not fear Communism because they control it, using it as an artificial "enemy" to justify the socialization of Western democracies.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[Wall Street financiers financed the Bolshevik Revolution]]

### Chapter 5: Establishing the Establishment
Traces the origins of the Council on Foreign Relations (CFR) back to the secret society formed by Cecil Rhodes in 1891 and organized by Alfred Milner (the Round Table groups). Outlines how this group established the Royal Institute of International Affairs (RIIA) in Britain and the CFR in New York under the guidance of "Colonel" House. Asserts that the CFR dominates U.S. presidential administrations, the State Department, and major media networks.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[The Council on Foreign Relations and the Round Table groups were established by international bankers to guide U.S. and British policy toward a centralized world socialist government]]

### Chapter 6: The Rockefellers and the Reds
Documents the Rockefeller family's extensive financial support, trade agreements, and technology transfers to the Soviet Union and other Communist countries. Allen details how the Rockefellers cooperate with the Soviet regime to build industrial plants (such as the Kama River truck factory) and argues that these corporate alliances are designed to secure global market monopolies under a centralized world government.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[The Rockefeller family and their corporate partners finance and cooperate with Communist regimes to establish monopolies and partition global markets under a centralized world government]]

### Chapter 7: Pressure From Above and Pressure From Below
Describes the dual strategy of "pressure from above" (Establishment insiders in government enacting centralized regulations) and "pressure from below" (radical street groups, rioters, and activist organizations demanding socialist policies). Allen argues that the street groups are pawns manipulated by the Insiders to create crises that justify the expansion of state power, squeezing the middle class.
* **Claims Extracted:**
  * [[The conspirators utilize a pincer strategy of pressure from above (government regulation) and pressure from below (street mobs) to force the socialization of America]]

### Chapter 8: You Are The Answer
Presents a call to action for citizens, suggesting grassroots book distribution to bypass media censorship, demanding that local congressional candidates take a public stand on the CFR, and resisting the centralization of political and economic power.
