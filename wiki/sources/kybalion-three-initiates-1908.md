## Source Description

*The Kybalion: A Study of the Hermetic Philosophy of Ancient Egypt and Greece* is a 1908 esoteric
treatise published anonymously by "[[The Three Initiates]]" (widely attributed to William Walker
Atkinson). It presents itself as an explanation of an older, orally transmitted set of Hermetic
maxims — "THE KYBALION" — said to descend from [[Hermes Trismegistus]], the legendary "Master of
Masters" of ancient Egypt. The book interleaves quoted aphorisms (presented as the primary text, in
quotation marks) with the authors' own expository commentary.

Its core content is the exposition of **[[The Seven Hermetic Principles]]** — Mentalism,
Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause and Effect, and Gender — together with the
doctrine of **THE ALL** (an unknowable Infinite Living Mind of which the universe is a mental
creation) and the practice of **Mental Transmutation** ("mental alchemy"), the use of the principles
to change one's own and others' mental states.

## Strict-extraction note

The Kybalion is a doctrinal/esoteric text: its assertions are metaphysical teachings, not empirical
claims. The wiki records them as *what the Kybalion teaches*, attributed to the source, without
adjudicating their truth. Quoted maxims are the source's own "primary" aphorisms; surrounding
arguments are the Three Initiates' commentary.

## Structure and Chapter Summaries

Fifteen short chapters/lessons.

**I. The Hermetic Philosophy** — Frames the tradition as descending from ancient Egypt and
[[Hermes Trismegistus]] ("the Master of Masters"), transmitted secretly "from lip to ear," veiled in
alchemical/astrological terms; warns against letting the doctrine "crystallize into a creed." See
[[Hermetic Philosophy is the one Master-Key to all occult teachings]] and
[[The Hermetic principle of secrecy: wisdom given only to prepared ears]].

**II. The Seven Hermetic Principles** — Enumerates the seven axioms and their maxims (the umbrella
[[The Seven Hermetic Principles]]): Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause and
Effect, Gender.

**III. Mental Transmutation** — Defines the practical art: Hermetic "alchemy" is the changing of
mental states/conditions/vibrations, not metals; the Philosopher's Stone is an allegory. See
[[Mental Transmutation (Kybalion)]] and [[Hermetic alchemy is mental transmutation, not material chemistry]].

**IV. The All** — Argues by exclusion that the Substantial Reality (THE ALL) is Infinite, Eternal,
Immutable, and unknowable in essence, best conceived as Infinite Living Mind / Spirit. See
[[THE ALL (Kybalion)]] and [[THE ALL is an unknowable Infinite Living Mind — Spirit]].

**V. The Mental Universe** — The cosmology: the universe is neither THE ALL itself nor nothing nor a
fragment, but a **mental creation** held in the Mind of THE ALL. See [[The Mental Universe (Kybalion)]],
[[The Universe is THE ALL's mental creation — neither a fragment of THE ALL nor Nothing]],
[[THE ALL is Mind; the Universe is a Mental creation of THE ALL]], and the eschatology
[[Human souls have an infinite ascending destiny within the Mental Universe, ending in At-One-ment with THE ALL]].

**VI. The Divine Paradox** — The universe IS NOT (from the Absolute view) yet IS (from the Relative
view); the "half-wise" deny it and are destroyed, the wise *use* higher laws against lower. See
[[The Divine Paradox (Kybalion)]],
[[The Universe is simultaneously unreal from the Absolute viewpoint and real from the Relative viewpoint]], and
[[The half-wise deny the Universe's reality and are destroyed by it; the wise use higher Laws against lower Laws through transmutation]].

**VII. "The All" in All** — THE ALL is immanent in every part of its universe, yet no part is
identical to THE ALL; creation proceeds by an Involution (outpouring to Matter) and Evolution
(indrawing to Spirit). See [[THE ALL is immanent in every part of its Universe, yet the Universe is not THE ALL]] and
[[Creation proceeds through Involution (Outpouring, lowering vibration into Matter) then Evolution (Indrawing, rising back to Spirit)]].

**VIII. The Planes of Correspondence** — The Three Great Planes (Physical, Mental, Spiritual), each
with seven minor planes; "As above, so below" is the tool from the known to the unknown. See
[[The Planes of Correspondence (Kybalion)]], [[The Principle of Correspondence (Kybalion)]],
[["As above, so below" — the same Laws, Principles, and characteristics operate identically on all Planes of the Universe]], and
[[The Three Great Planes (Physical, Mental, Spiritual) are degrees of vibration, not separate places, and all are equally creations held within the Mind of THE ALL]].

**IX. Vibration** — "Nothing rests; everything vibrates"; all differences (Matter, Energy, Mind,
Spirit) are differences of vibration rate; thought is a transmittable vibratory force. See
[[The Principle of Vibration (Kybalion)]],
[[Nothing rests — all manifest differences between Matter, Energy, Mind, and Spirit are differences in rate and mode of vibration]], and
[[Every thought and mental state has a corresponding vibration that can be controlled by the will and transmitted to other minds]].

**X. Polarity** — "Opposites are identical in nature but differ in degree"; the basis of mental
transmutation by changing poles. See [[The Principle of Polarity (Kybalion)]],
[[Opposites Are Identical in Nature but Differ Only in Degree]], and
[[Mental Transmutation Is Achieved by Changing the Polarity of Mental States]].

**XI. Rhythm** — The pendulum-swing on all planes; "rhythm compensates" (Law of Compensation), and
the Law of Neutralization by which the Master escapes the backward swing. See
[[The Principle of Rhythm (Kybalion)]],
[[Rhythm Compensates: The Swing in One Direction Determines the Swing in the Other]], and
[[The Law of Neutralization: The Adept Escapes the Rhythmic Pendulum-Swing by Rising to a Higher Plane]].

**XII. Causation** — Nothing escapes Cause and Effect; "Chance is but a name for Law not
recognized"; Masters rise to become Causes rather than Effects. See
[[The Principle of Cause and Effect (Kybalion)]],
[[Nothing Escapes the Law of Cause and Effect — Chance Is Only Unrecognized Law]], and
[[The Hermetic Master Rises to Become a Cause, Not Merely an Effect, on the Lower Planes]].

**XIII. Gender** — Masculine and Feminine principles are present in everything and on all planes,
distinct from biological sex. See [[The Principle of Gender (Kybalion)]] and
[[Gender Is in Everything and Manifests on All Planes]].

**XIV. Mental Gender** — The mind has a Masculine ("I"/Will) and Feminine ("Me"/generative) aspect;
most people are polarized in the receptive "Me" and governed by others' Will. See
[[Mental Gender (Kybalion)]] and [[The Mind Has Two Principles: the Masculine 'I' (Will) and the Feminine 'Me' (Generative)]].

**XV. Hermetic Axioms** — Closing practical maxims operationalizing the principles, culminating in
"True Hermetic Transmutation is a Mental Art." See [[The Hermetic Axioms (Kybalion)]] and
[[True Hermetic Transmutation Is a Mental Art — the Universe Being Wholly Mental]].
