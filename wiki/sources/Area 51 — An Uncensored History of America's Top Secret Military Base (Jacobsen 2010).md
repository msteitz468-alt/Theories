## Source Description

Nonfiction book by Annie Jacobsen. The author states it is a work of nonfiction and that none of the people are invented. She interviewed seventy-four individuals with rare firsthand knowledge of the secret base; thirty-two lived and worked at Area 51.

Published 2010 (Hachette Digital, Inc.; ISBN 9781409141396 per file metadata).

## What the Source Contributes

- Full arc of Area 51 from pre-base Roswell/War of the Worlds framing through U-2, A-12/Oxcart, Black Shield, MiG reverse engineering, nuclear accidents, drone era, and post-9/11 Predator operations.
- Atomic Energy Commission role, Project 57, Plumbbob, NERVA, Palomares, Thule, Cosmos 954.
- UFO cover-up culture tied to classified aircraft (U-2, Oxcart).
- CIA–Air Force institutional rivalry; NRO creation; transition to Air Force control and F-117/Area 52.
- Bob Lazar S-4 claims; culminating anonymous EG&G engineer Roswell/Stalin/Mengele account and S-4 human-experiment claims (Ch. 21).
