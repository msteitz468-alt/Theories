## Source Description

*Success Magick: The Hidden Power of Enochian Rituals* (2020) by Damon Brand ([[Damon Brand]]),
published by [[The Gallery of Magick]], is a practical ritual **how-to manual**. It presents a
simplified, "democratized" form of **[[Enochian Magick (Gallery of Magick approach)]]** — the
angelic magic system received by [[John Dee (1527–1608-9)]] and his scryer [[Edward Kelley]] in
1582–1584 — repurposed toward worldly success. The book argues anyone can use the magic without
prior skill, by gazing at sigils and speaking divine/angelic names, and closes each working with a
distinctive **[[The offering to humanity (Gallery of Magick ritual method)|offering to humanity]]**.

Most of the book is a sequence of **49 short, structurally identical rituals** (see
[[The forty-nine rituals of Success Magick]]) grouped as the Ritual of the Angels (1–19) and the
Rituals of the First and Final **[[The Thirty Aethyrs (Enochian)|Aethyrs]]** (20–49), preceded by
theory chapters and a "How to Use the Rituals" section.

## Strict-extraction / sensitivity note

This is an instructional occult manual. Its claims (that the rituals summon angelic powers and
produce real-world results) are recorded as *what Brand teaches*, attributed and **not adjudicated**;
ritual procedures are catalogued descriptively, not as instructions endorsed by the wiki. The book's
own disclaimer states the information is "speculation and not professional advice, to be used at your
own risk."

## Structure

Theory: Discovering Success · A Complete Magickal System · Magickal Revelations · The Images and
Letters of Magick · How to Use the Rituals · The Words of Magick · The Foundations of Magick. Then
49 named rituals (Vision, Clarity, Pathway, … Light) across the Angels and the First/Final Aethyrs,
plus an Appendix of further reading.
