## Source Description

*Llewellyn's Complete Book of Correspondences* (2013) by Sandra Kynes ([[Sandra Kynes]]) is a
cross-referenced reference work of **magical correspondences** — the associations between intentions,
plants, stones, animals, deities, planets, colors, numbers, and elements used in ritual and
spellwork. Kynes' distinctive framing is to move past "rote tables" toward a **"web" of
correspondences** in which each item links not only to a magical intention but to the other
correspondences as well (see [[Magical correspondences (Kynes)]]). She presents it as
*sympathetic magic* grounded in how the mind works by association, aimed at general Pagans and
Wiccans (explicitly *not* ceremonial/high magicians).

## Strict-extraction note

Entries record *the correspondences Kynes compiles* from Pagan/Wiccan tradition and her cited sources —
folklore and magical practice, recorded descriptively, not as factual claims and not as endorsed
instructions.

## Structure

Introduction (why we use correspondences; the "web" concept) · Ch. 1 Issues, Intentions, and Powers
(the master correspondences indexed by magical intention) · Ch. 2 The Plant Kingdom · Ch. 3 The
Mineral Kingdom · Ch. 4 The Animal Kingdom · Ch. 5 Deities and Other Beings · Ch. 6 Astrological and
Time Reckoning · Ch. 7 Miscellaneous (elements, colors, numbers, directions) · Appendix: Guide to
Plants.
