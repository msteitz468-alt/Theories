## Source Description

*The Secret Doctrine: The Synthesis of Science, Religion, and Philosophy* (1888) by Helena Petrovna
Blavatsky ([[H. P. Blavatsky]]) is the foundational text of modern Theosophy and one of the most
influential esoteric works of the modern era. Presented as a commentary on the (claimed ancient)
**Stanzas of Dzyan**, it lays out a vast scheme of **cosmogenesis** (Vol. I — the evolution of the
cosmos from the One) and **anthropogenesis** (Vol. II — the evolution of humanity through seven
**Root-Races**, including the lost continents of Lemuria and Atlantis). See the umbrella
[[The Secret Doctrine: theosophical cosmogenesis and anthropogenesis (Blavatsky)]].

This is a key **primary root** for the wiki: its doctrines (the wisdom-religion underlying all faiths,
the Root-Races, Lemuria/Atlantis, cyclic cosmic evolution, the septenary constitution, the
re-reading of "fallen angels" as cosmic forces) directly underlie later sources — Manly P. Hall, the
Emerald Tablets tradition, the ancient-astronaut and lost-civilization writers, and David Icke's
root-race/Atlantis material — and are squarely *countered* by Feder on Atlantis/Lemuria.

## Strict-extraction / sensitivity note

Blavatsky's claims are recorded as *what the source asserts* — attributed and **not adjudicated**.
Note that her Root-Race scheme contains racial hierarchies and language that were later drawn on by
race ideologues; such content is recorded descriptively and flagged, never endorsed or amplified.

## Structure & ingestion scope

Two volumes, each in three parts. **Vol I (Cosmogenesis):** Proem (the Three Fundamental Propositions)
· Part I Cosmic Evolution (Stanzas of Dzyan I–VII) · Part II The Evolution of Symbolism · Part III
Science and the Secret Doctrine Contrasted. **Vol II (Anthropogenesis):** Part I Anthropogenesis (the
Root-Races) · Part II The Archaic Symbolism of the World-Religions · Part III Science and the Secret
Doctrine. *Ingest scope: heavy on the doctrinal core (Proem/Cosmogenesis Stanzas; the Root-Races/
Lemuria/Atlantis); selective on the symbolism and the 1888 anti-science polemics (theses only, not the
exhaustive symbol catalogs or dated science feuds).*

## Section summaries

Each entry is recorded as *what Blavatsky asserts/teaches*, attributed and not adjudicated. (Root-Race

**Vol I — Proem & Cosmogenesis** (**[[The Three Fundamental Propositions of the Secret Doctrine (Blavatsky)]]** · **[[The Stanzas of Dzyan and cosmic evolution (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts an Omnipresent Eternal Absolute Principle as the first foundation of all existence]] ·
[[Blavatsky teaches the law of cosmic periodicity — Manvantara and Pralaya as universal rhythm]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts the identity of all individual souls with the Universal Over-Soul and their obligatory pilgrimage through incarnation]] ·
[[Blavatsky teaches that the Stanzas of Dzyan are an archaic record of cosmic evolution from the ONE through the Dhyan-Chohans to the appearance of Man]] ·
[[Blavatsky teaches that Fohat is the dynamic cosmic electricity bridging Divine Mind and Matter]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts a septenary constitution of both cosmos and man reflecting the seven-fold nature of the Absolute]].

**Vol I — Evolution of Symbolism** (**[[The Secret Doctrine as the one wisdom-religion underlying all faiths (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts that all world religions and mythologies are exoteric veils of one original esoteric wisdom-religion]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that comparative symbolism decoded with the esoteric key reveals the common hidden source of all civilisations' wisdom]].

**Vol I — Science vs. the Secret Doctrine** (**[[Occult science versus materialist science (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts that occult science possesses a truer account of nature than materialist physics, because matter and force are rooted in conscious, living intelligence]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that modern science unknowingly rediscovers, in fragments, what ancient occult wisdom already possessed in full — from gravity and ether to the primordial element]].

**Vol II — Anthropogenesis** (**[[The seven Root-Races and the evolution of humanity (Blavatsky)]]** · **[[Lemuria and Atlantis as lost Root-Race continents (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts that humanity evolved through seven Root-Races from ethereal astral beings to physical mankind, inverting the Darwinian sequence]] ·
[[Blavatsky teaches that the Third Root-Race on Lemuria fell from androgynous spiritual purity into physical generation, producing both humanity's ancestors and the ancestors of the apes]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that the Sons of Mind (Manasaputras) incarnated in early Third-Race humanity to endow it with intellect, constituting humanity's true spiritual awakening]] ·
[[Blavatsky teaches that Atlantis was a continent of giant sorcerers who fell into black magic and were destroyed by successive deluges]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that the Third and Fourth Root-Races possessed a functional 'third eye' (pineal gland) granting direct spiritual perception, which atrophied as races materialized]].

**Vol II — Archaic Symbolism** (**[[Fallen angels, the Serpent, and Lucifer as misunderstood cosmic forces (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts that the Fallen Angels and Lucifer are the divine intelligences who gifted humanity with mind and self-consciousness, not evil rebels]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that orthodox Christianity deliberately demonized ancient cosmic wisdom-symbols — the Serpent, the Dragon, Lucifer — inverting their true meaning to consolidate theological power]].

**Vol II — Science vs. the Secret Doctrine** (**[[The great antiquity of man and the critique of Darwinian evolution (Blavatsky)]]**).
[[Blavatsky asserts that humanity is millions of years old and predates or is contemporaneous with the earliest mammals]] ·
[[Blavatsky asserts that apes are degenerate offshoots of early humanity and that Darwinian natural selection cannot account for the origin of man]].
