## Definition from Source

The Seven Hermetic Principles are the seven axioms on which, per *The Kybalion*, "the entire Hermetic
Philosophy is based": "The Principles of Truth are Seven; he who knows these, understandingly,
possesses the Magic Key before whose touch all the Doors of the Temple fly open." This is the
umbrella concept tying together the book's seven principle-chapters (each detailed on its own page).

## The Seven Principles (each with its Kybalion maxim)

1. **[[The Principle of Mentalism (Kybalion)]]** — "THE ALL is MIND; The Universe is Mental."
2. **[[The Principle of Correspondence (Kybalion)]]** — "As above, so below; as below, so above."
3. **[[The Principle of Vibration (Kybalion)]]** — "Nothing rests; everything moves; everything vibrates."
4. **[[The Principle of Polarity (Kybalion)]]** — "Everything is Dual; everything has poles; … opposites are identical in nature, but different in degree."
5. **[[The Principle of Rhythm (Kybalion)]]** — "Everything flows, out and in; … the pendulum-swing manifests in everything; … rhythm compensates."
6. **[[The Principle of Cause and Effect (Kybalion)]]** — "Every Cause has its Effect; every Effect has its Cause; … Chance is but a name for Law not recognized."
7. **[[The Principle of Gender (Kybalion)]]** — "Gender is in everything; everything has its Masculine and Feminine Principles; Gender manifests on all planes."
