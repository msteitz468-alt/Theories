## Definition from Source

The umbrella thesis and practice of *Becoming Supernatural*: that human beings are "not linear beings
living linear lives, but dimensional beings living dimensional lives," already equipped with "all the
anatomy, chemistry, and physiology" needed to transcend the material self. Through a trainable
combination of a **coherent brain** (clear intention) and a **coherent heart** (elevated emotion),
plus blessing the body's energy centers and activating the pineal gland, Dispenza claims a person can
move "from living in the past to living in the future," heal the body, and "influence matter" —
"that's how you create reality."

## Description from Source

The method's components (each detailed on its own page): becoming present and stepping out of
"survival mode" (the present moment); **[[Tuning in to the quantum field (Dispenza)]]**;
**[[Blessing of the energy centers (Dispenza)]]**; reconditioning the body to a new mind;
**[[Heart coherence and heart intelligence (Dispenza)]]**; mental rehearsal via
**[[Mind Movies and mental rehearsal (Dispenza)]]**; walking meditation; transcending space via
**[[Space-time and time-space (Dispenza)]]**; **[[The pineal gland as a transducer (Dispenza)]]**; and
collective practice through **[[Project Coherence (collective meditation, Dispenza)]]**. Dispenza
supports the framework with EEG/HRV measurements and case studies, framing it as a bridge between
science and mysticism.

## Strict-extraction note

The healing, gene-expression, energy-field, and group-effect claims are contested and recorded as
Dispenza's teaching, not as established science.
