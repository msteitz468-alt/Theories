## Definition from Source

"The download" is a recurring motif across *Encounters*: a process by which "extremely creative
people appear to receive, through spontaneous cognition, calculations, information about biomedical
products, or similar types of information without any apparent effort." Pasulka first identified it
among the scientists she worked with and finds it reappearing among later experiencers (e.g. Simone
the venture capitalist).

## Description from Source

Pasulka treats the download as one of the characteristic "effects" of
[[Contact with nonhuman intelligence (Pasulka)|contact]] — alongside precognition and visionary
dreams — and links it to innovation and invention, suggesting some technologists experience their
creativity as information received rather than generated. It is often connected with the
[[The organic network / noösphere (Pasulka)|organic network]] through which such information is
imagined to travel.
