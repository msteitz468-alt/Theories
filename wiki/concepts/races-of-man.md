## Definition from Source

Oahspe's anthropology arranges mankind in a sequence of races defined by spiritual
grade and descent:

- **[[The Asu (Asuans, the first race)]]** (Asu / Adam) — the first race, made from se'mu, soulless and
  mortal.
- **[[The I'hins (the sacred little people)]]** — the sacred little white-and-yellow people, born of angels +
  Asu; the first immortal race and the Faithists of antiquity.
- **[[The Druks]]** — the dark fallen race, from I'hins (or their seed) + Asu;
  "heirs that shall go out in darkness."
- **[[The I'huans]]** — the copper-colored warrior race, from I'hins + Druks (stock
  of Abram and Moses).
- **[[The Ghans]]** — the highest race, from I'huans + I'hins.

## Key Properties (from source)

- Race correlates with capacity for everlasting life and for hearing Jehovih's
  voice; the chosen "cohabit together" to "rise in wisdom and virtue," while
  mixing with the asuans brings "heirs in the descending grade of life."
- The grades are managed across generations by the [[The Loo'is and the Breeding of Prophets]].
