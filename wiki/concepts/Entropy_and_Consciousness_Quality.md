## Definition and Characterization from Source

In [[Thomas Campbell]]'s *[[My Big TOE]]*, **entropy** is borrowed from thermodynamics and applied as the primary metric for consciousness quality. High entropy = disordered, poorly organized, dim, fearful, self-serving consciousness. Low entropy = highly organized, bright, aware, loving, purposeful consciousness.

This technical definition makes spiritual and psychological categories (love, ego, wisdom, evil) precise:

> "Right growth and right learning are technically defined as growth or learning that leads to a decrease in the entropy of your consciousness. Personal growth, growing the quality of your being, or growing wisdom — all increase your personal power as they decrease the entropy of your consciousness. I am talking about the growing-up and maturing of your being — a process that increases your capacity to love. I am also talking about spiritual growth, the evolution of your soul."

## The Entropy Scale

Campbell describes a spectrum from maximum to minimum entropy in consciousness:

**High entropy (poor quality):**
- Fear-based orientation
- Self-serving ego dominance
- Power-control-force motivation
- Belief-trapped thinking
- Limited, closed awareness
- "Evil" in the extreme

**Low entropy (high quality):**
- Unconditional love
- Open, expansive awareness
- Value-based action
- Purpose-driven existence
- Contribution to the whole
- Expression of AUM's nature

Campbell states:

> "Quality runs the gamut from the severely limited, high-entropy, fear-based, self-serving ego, to the unlimited, low entropy, unconditional love exhibited by AUM-consciousness."

## Love as Technical Concept

A distinctive claim is that **love is not a sentiment but a physical/informational property** of consciousness:

> "Spirituality and Love are technically defined as functions of entropy content within consciousness."

And:

> "Love is the animating dynamic of AUM; it is AUM's most salient property. AUM is an evolving digital-love-consciousness-being-thing whose actions and intents are animated and motivated by unconditional love — which is the natural mode and expression of a low entropy consciousness."

This equates:
- Reduced entropy = higher love capacity
- Maximum love = minimum entropy
- Unconditional love = the natural state of fully evolved consciousness

## Ego as High-Entropy Structure

Campbell identifies **ego** as a specific manifestation of high-entropy consciousness. Ego is characterized by:
- Fear-based self-protection
- Belief in the primacy of the individual self
- Confusing the PMR-self with the total self
- Resistance to consciousness growth

Ego is not evil by design — it is a normal feature of early-stage consciousness development — but becomes the primary obstacle to entropy reduction at higher stages.

## Consciousness Quality and Personal Power

Campbell equates consciousness quality with genuine power (not power-control-force, but functional effectiveness):

> "Personal growth, growing the quality of your being, or growing wisdom — all increase your personal power as they decrease the entropy of your consciousness."

This creates a direct relationship: as entropy decreases, capability increases. The "paranormal" abilities associated with advanced consciousness (psi, precognition, etc.) are natural expressions of low-entropy consciousness, not supernatural exceptions.

## Positive and Negative Entropy Reduction

Campbell notes entropy can also decrease in a negative direction — toward power-control-force rather than love:

> "The negative intent consciousness critters and beings (evil) can evolve only toward power-control-force. That is as far as evil can go — the establishment of power-control-force. Minimizing entropy by growing or evolving in the negative direction is extremely limited and relatively worthless compared to the advantages gained by a consciousness evolving positively toward the expression of itself as love."

This frames "evil" as a less profitable evolutionary path — limited in potential, producing only control and domination, ultimately less viable than love-consciousness.

## How Entropy Is Reduced

Campbell argues entropy reduction happens through everyday choices, not dramatic revelations:

> "Consciousness evolution does not progress through a process of successive epiphanies. Significant entropy reduction is accomplished through an iterative process that slowly accumulates a large number of nearly infinitesimal profitable choices to produce significant growth. Trying to grow your consciousness in great leaps impedes progress by distracting you from the hundreds of small profitable choices you have the opportunity to make every day. Your consciousness quality rises and falls based upon the intent that animates your everyday interactions."

Key mechanisms:
- **Intent quality**: The motivation behind each choice matters most
- **Interaction outcomes**: How one's actions affect others provides feedback
- **Iterative accumulation**: Small, consistent improvements compound
- **PMR as learning environment**: Physical reality provides conditions for rapid feedback

## Persistence Across Lifetimes

Campbell claims consciousness quality (entropy) persists and accumulates across physical lifetimes:

> "Your learning is cumulative. You are a participant in, as well as a driver of, the consciousness cycle."

Physical death does not reset consciousness quality. The accumulated low-entropy state carries forward into subsequent PMR experiences, giving continuity to the evolutionary project.

## Measurement Implications

Campbell argues that consciousness quality has objective correlates:
- Consistent functional effects in the world (love produces measurable outcomes)
- Paranormal capability as a function of consciousness quality
- The quality of one's consciousness is not private but expressed outwardly through all interactions
