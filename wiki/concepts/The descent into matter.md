## Definition / Characterization from Source

Booth's central historical mechanism: matter "was only precipitated out of the cosmic mind over very long periods" and "gradually became denser," approaching the solidity we know only recently. As matter hardened it became "a greater barrier to the free flow of the cosmic mind," so two parallel dimensions diverged — a spirit world and a material world. Human perception changed in step: the **Third Eye** (organ of spiritual sight, the "Lantern of Osiris") was once as active as the physical eyes but became "vestigial" as matter solidified and the skull took its present form.

This makes the secret history a **mirror image of materialist evolution**: spirit comes first and condenses into matter (not matter giving rise, by chance, to mind). Booth also frames it as a cycle — the material world "exists only for a brief interval" and "will dissolve again."
