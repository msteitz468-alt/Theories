## Concept Definition

**Coordinate Points** (also referred to as **coordination points**) are invisible mathematical junctions in the structure of space-time where realities merge. They contain concentrated energy potential and serve as the mechanisms through which psychic energy is translated into physical matter. 

Seth distinguishes between two main types of coordinate points:
1. **Main Coordinate Points:** Four absolute coordinate points that intersect all realities. These act as primary channels for energy flow, warps or paths between dimensions, and cosmic transformers that provide the generating energy that keeps creation continuous.
2. **Subordinate Coordinate Points:** Countless minor coordinate points that permeate all physical space. These act as localized energy transformers, allowing human thoughts and emotions to be projected and condensed into physical objects.

## Mechanics of Translation

Physical matter is not solid or objective; it is a camouflage projection formed by consciousness. The brain and inner mind project thoughts outward, which are attracted to and amplified by subordinate coordinate points:
* **Energy Accumulation:** When thoughts or emotions match the coordinates of these points, they are drawn in, accelerated, and condensed into physical symbols (objects).
* **Space-Warping:** Coordinate points act like "black holes" and "white holes" turned inside out. They draw in electromagnetic properties, accelerate them beyond imagination, draw additional energy from surrounding systems, and re-emerge as "white holes" to project reformed energy back into the system.
* **Pyramid and Triangular Shapes:** The lines of force connecting these coordinate points form triangular or blue-colored energy configurations. These lines concentrate energy, which explains the energetic and psychic properties traditionally associated with pyramid structures.

## Historical Applications

* **Atlantis:** According to Seth, the citizens of Atlantis possessed conscious knowledge of these coordinates. They aligned their roads, buildings, and major structures precisely between various coordination points to achieve extreme physical stability and harness localized energy.
* **Closed Systems and Entropy:** Seth asserts that coordination points act as accelerators that constantly re-energize energy itself. This prevents the universe from winding down, refuting the second law of thermodynamics (entropy) by showing that there are no truly closed systems.
