## Concept Definition

The **Null Point** (also referred to as the **turning point** or **point of rest**) is a core physical-metaphysical mechanism in Itzhak Bentov's model. It represents the brief halt at the extreme endpoints of a pendulum's swing or an atomic particle's vibration, where the speed of the oscillator becomes zero relative to its direction of motion, and its potential energy reaches its maximum. 

Bentov asserts that at this exact moment of rest, the oscillator temporarily escapes physical three-dimensional reality, entering a state of infinite velocity and nonlocality (or "absolute space-time").

## Mechanics of the Null Point

* **Pendulum Analogy:** A swinging pendulum accelerates as it passes through the center of its swing and slows down as it reaches the endpoints. At the turning point, it halts for a fraction of a second before reversing direction. Bentov defines this point of rest as the null point.
* **Blinking Off and On:** Atoms, which are electromagnetic fields vibrating at approximately $10^{15}$ Hz, behave like microscopic pendulums. They accelerate, slow down, halt at the null point, and accelerate back. At the null point, the atom "blinks off" (disappears from physical reality) and enters the void or absolute space-time before "blinking on" again.
* **Infinite Velocity and Nonlocality:** At the null point, the distance to be traveled is zero, and the time taken is zero. Mathematically, velocity (distance divided by time) becomes infinite ($\frac{0}{0}$ or approaching infinity). In this state of infinite velocity, the particle is nonlocal—it is present everywhere in the universe simultaneously.
* **The Void:** The null point is the gateway to "the void"—the non-physical, absolute reservoir of intelligence and energy that underlies the physical universe.

## Altered States of Consciousness

Bentov applies the null point to human consciousness:
* During normal waking consciousness, the mind is locked into the physical body's vibrations.
* In altered states of consciousness (such as deep meditation, theta brainwave states, or out-of-body experiences), the mind's focus is quieted. This allows the observer (psyche) to align with the null points of atomic vibration, ejecting from the physical body's coordinates to travel nonlocally through absolute space-time.
