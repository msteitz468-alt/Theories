**Definition (from the source)**

The "**Babylonian Brotherhood**" is Icke's name in *The Biggest Secret* for the network of interbreeding reptilian-hybrid bloodlines — "a race within a race" — that he claims has covertly ruled humanity since the ancient Near East. The label fuses the **bloodline** (a genetic "tribe" of reptilian-Anunnaki descent) with the **organization** (the mystery schools and secret societies through which it operates). Icke also calls its master plan the "Great Work of Ages" the **Brotherhood Agenda**.

**Use in the source**

Icke's opening summary: "a race of interbreeding bloodlines, a race within a race in fact, were centred in the Middle and Near East in the ancient world and ... have expanded their power across the globe ... to create a network of mystery schools and secret societies to covertly introduce their Agenda." Crucially, "the structures of today's institutions in government, banking, business, military and the media have not been **infiltrated** by this force, they were **created** by them from the start."

Characteristics he assigns the Brotherhood:
- **Hereditary**: control "passes the baton across the generations, mostly sons following fathers," with chosen children indoctrinated from birth.
- **Named bloodlines**: the British House of Windsor, the Rothschilds, the Rockefellers, European royalty/aristocracy, and the US "Eastern Establishment," with the true controllers "outside the public domain."
- **Internally warring yet united** on the Agenda ("a gang of bank robbers who all agree on the job, but then argue over how the spoils will be divided").
- **Black Nobility**: Icke folds in the "Black Nobility" of Venice/Italy (Medici, Agnelli, Warburg-del Banco) as Brotherhood lines.
- Operating ultimately "from the lower fourth dimension" (see existing [[Claim: Icke asserts fourth-dimensional reptilian entities control Earth by possessing Illuminati bloodline bodies]]).
