## Definition and Characterization from Source

In [[Thomas Campbell]]'s *[[My Big TOE]]*, **PMR** (Physical Matter Reality) refers to any space-time-based virtual reality dimension within [[Concept: AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold)|AUM]]. Our physical universe is one specific PMR (often written PMRk, where k distinguishes it from other possible PMRs). PMR is not the only reality, nor is it the most fundamental — it exists as a constrained sub-dimension within the larger [[Concept: NPMR (Nonphysical Matter Reality)|NPMR]], which is itself contained within AUM.

Campbell defines PMR as:

> "A particular pattern of constraints applied to the energy transfers between individuated consciousnesses. Space-time is the construct within which our fleshy bodies appear to live and interact. It is a consciousness interface filter that defines the perception (experience) of Physical Matter Reality (PMR) to participating sentient conscious entities."

## Ontological Status: Virtual Reality

PMR is explicitly a **virtual reality** — not ultimately real in the primary sense. Physical space and matter are perceptual constructs:

> "Physical space, and your experience of it, is an illusion, a trick of your individuated mind, a virtual reality within the actual reality of your consciousness. I am developing the existence of a concept or idea of space within AUM's consciousness — an idea that will evolve to provide the experience of space to an individuated consciousness. Space as a mental construct, not physical space itself; there is no such thing as physical space!"

PMR's apparent solidity, three-dimensionality, and causal structure are features of the rule-set, not features of underlying reality.

## PMR as Design Solution

Campbell presents PMR as AUM's evolved solution to problems encountered in earlier NPMRn-based consciousness evolution experiments. Earlier experiments failed because:
- Individuated consciousnesses became confused about what was the "real" them
- No clear causal accountability linked intent to consequence
- Beings got stuck in "intellectual endless loops thinking about interactions instead of experiencing them"

AUM's design requirements for a better learning system included:
- Clear starting conditions without accumulated delusions (the physical birth mechanism)
- Accumulated knowledge and wisdom preserved (continuity of consciousness across lifetimes)
- Unambiguous causal relationships linking intent to consequence
- Immediate feedback making learning effective

Campbell states:

> "AUM solved these design issues by evolving a space-time rule-set that defines the physical experience or interactions of a set of hybrid beings that simultaneously have both physical and nonphysical components — beings that exist, grow, and evolve in two reality dimensions simultaneously. Mankind, or more generally PMRk-kind, is an exceptionally clever solution."

## Mechanics: The PMR Rule-Set

### Space-Time as Constraint
The PMR rule-set imposes a uniform set of constraints on all energy transfers between individuated consciousnesses within a given dimensional chunk (thought-space) of AUM. These constraints constitute what humans call "physics."

The rule-set has two components:
- **Structural component**: Defines three-dimensional isotropic space
- **Dynamic component**: Defines time and causal flow

### Reality Cells and Space-Time
Space-time is implemented via a matrix of "space-time reality cells" within AUM's consciousness. Each cell can flip state; sequential state changes propagate through the matrix. The upper speed limit of this propagation defines the speed of light:

> "The upper limit on propagating distortions in space-time is thus one space-time reality cell per quantum of space-time time. We define that upper speed limit as 'c.'"

The speed of light is therefore not a fundamental constant of nature but an evolved design parameter of the PMR rule-set.

### Quantized Time (DELTA-t)
PMR's smallest time increment (DELTA-t) is defined as an integer multiple n of AUM's fundamental time quantum. Campbell imagines n to be an "exceedingly large number" — perhaps 10^80 or similar — meaning:

- For every one tick of PMR time, AUM completes ~10^80 fundamental time quanta
- NPMR runs much faster than PMR relative to AUM
- PMR appears to move in extreme slow motion from AUM's perspective

This time hierarchy is why information propagation appears near-instantaneous in NPMR while bound by the speed of light in PMR.

### Deterministic Causality
Within PMR, the rule-set enforces local deterministic causality — events follow from prior causes in predictable ways. This structure is essential to the learning lab function:

> "A local deterministic causality reflecting the PMR rule-set is required to provide the overall structure within which our experience can be profitably defined and coherently generated."

## PMR as Consciousness Learning Lab

The central purpose of PMR, according to Campbell, is to serve as an "interactive multiplayer consciousness-quality development trainer for units of individuated consciousness." It provides:

1. **Accountability**: Actions have consequences that feed back to the actor
2. **Limited awareness**: Physical embodiment constrains consciousness, focusing learning on specific developmental challenges
3. **Immediate feedback**: The law of cause and effect creates clear lessons
4. **Fresh starts**: Physical birth enables restarting without accumulated delusions
5. **Continuity**: Consciousness quality (not memories) carries across physical lifetimes

Campbell compares PMR to "where the rubber meets the road — where your actions and feelings reflect the actual you, not what you think you are, or want to be."

## Plurality of PMRs

Campbell states explicitly that there are many PMRs — not just ours:

> "There are many, many versions of PMR humming along in parallel — and The Big Dude never breaks a sweat."

Other PMRk realities may follow different rule-sets (different physics), producing different physical experiences and different evolutionary pressures. Our PMR's specific rule-set has produced the form of evolution observed in our biological history.

## Our System (OS)

Campbell defines **OS** (Our System) as:

> "PMR (Physical Matter Reality — our physical universe) plus the subset of NPMRn [a specific part of Nonphysical Matter Reality] that is interactive with PMR."

OS is the combined system that governs human existence. It is smaller than all of NPMR and far smaller than AUM, but encompasses all of human physical and interactive non-physical experience.

## Humanity as Hybrid Beings

A central claim is that humans exist simultaneously in two dimensions:
- **PMR component**: The physical body operating within space-time causality
- **NPMRn component**: The consciousness aspect existing in the non-physical

This dual nature is the design feature that makes PMR effective as a learning environment. The physical component enforces accountability; the non-physical component retains the accumulated quality that carries forward.
