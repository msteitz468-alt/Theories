Coulthart's shorthand for the alleged, still-unproven secret that the US (and possibly Russia and China) has recovered non-human craft and is attempting to reverse-engineer them, concealed from Congress and presidents within contractor-held compartmented programs. The author repeatedly tests and qualifies the claim rather than asserting it as fact.
