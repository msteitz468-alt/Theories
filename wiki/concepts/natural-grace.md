## Concept Definition

**Natural Grace** (often referred to as a **state of grace**) is the inherent biological and spiritual baseline of all living beings. In this state, an individual's physical body, conscious mind, and inner self are in perfect alignment and balance, automatically producing health, growth, and a feeling of peace and belonging in the universe.

Seth asserts that grace is not a special favor bestowed from the outside, but is the natural, default condition of physical creaturehood.

## Key Attributes of the State of Grace

* **Biological Baseline:** Animals exist in a state of natural grace automatically, as they lack the self-conscious, limiting beliefs that humans generate. The human body is also designed to operate in this state, maintaining cellular health and self-regulation effortlessly.
* **The Role of Conscience:** Natural conscience is an inherent biological feedback loop that helps humans recognize their connection to the universe. Seth distinguishes this from "artificial conscience," which is built on cultural creeds, dogmatic guilt, and learned shame.
* **Disruption by Limiting Beliefs:** A state of grace is blocked when the conscious mind adopts beliefs of unworthiness, inherited sin, physical helplessness, or universal evil. These beliefs construct physical symptoms and block the natural flow of well-being.
* **Reconnection through Affirmation:** Re-entering a state of grace requires the conscious mind to affirm the self's validity, accept its feelings, and recognize its direct connection to All-That-Is, which automatically dissolves artificial blockages.
