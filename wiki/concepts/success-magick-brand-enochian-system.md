## Definition from Source

"Success Magick" is the umbrella system of *Success Magick*: a simplified, results-oriented adaptation
of [[Enochian Magick (Gallery of Magick approach)|Enochian magic]] aimed at producing ongoing
worldly success. Brand frames success not as a single goal achieved and abandoned but "a state of
unfolding growth and expansion," and his magic as "a way of manipulating reality to make your life
more successful from the moment you begin" — distinct, he insists, from mere positive thinking or
self-deception.

## Description from Source

The system consists of [[The forty-nine rituals of Success Magick|49 short rituals]], each themed
(Vision, Clarity, Confidence, Abundance, …) and performed by gazing at an Enochian sigil, reading a
"Foundation" idea, speaking a divine/angelic name or Aethyr name, and making
[[The offering to humanity (Gallery of Magick ritual method)|an offering to humanity]]. Its premises:
that [[Brand teaches Enochian magic can be simplified and democratized for ordinary people|Enochian can be democratized]],
that [[Brand teaches material success is a legitimate and spiritual use of magic|material success is itself spiritual]],
and that [[Brand teaches the magic works whether or not the practitioner feels anything|it works whether or not you feel anything]].
