## Definition from Source

Information field is a term used in Ch. VI as a synonym for the [[Alternatives Space]], emphasizing its nature as an energy matrix or template for all physical possibilities. Zeland writes: "Information Field: alternatives space = information field / energy matrix = template for possibilities." (Ch. VI)

The information field framing emphasizes the epistemic and energetic dimension of the alternatives space — it is not merely a set of possible scenarios but an active informational substrate that the soul (subconscious) can directly access.

## Access to the Field (from source)

- **Soul's direct access**: "Soul has access to information field and can see what lies ahead in unrealized sectors" (Ch. VI)
- **Subconscious as conduit**: the subconscious (heart/soul) connects directly to the information field; the conscious mind (reason) does not access it through analysis but through unity with the heart
- **Knowledge Out of Nowhere**: insights come from the subconscious accessing unrealized sectors of the field (Ch. VI) — the phenomenon of inspiration or sudden knowledge is explained as the soul retrieving information from the field
- **Brain as address store**: "The brain does not store information itself. It stores the address to where the information is located in the alternatives space." (Ch. VII)

## Individual Sectors (from source)

Every soul has its own individual sector in the information field corresponding to its unique qualities. "Every soul has its own individual 'star' sector in the information field." (Ch. IX) Copying another person's script or qualities attunes one to a foreign sector where one's heart potential cannot be fully realized.
