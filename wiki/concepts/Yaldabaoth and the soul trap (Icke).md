## Definition / Characterization from Source

In *The Reveal*, David Icke fuses Gnostic cosmology with the simulation idea: the perceived physical universe is a **holographic simulation** generated by a demonic, "Astral" false-creator — the Gnostic **Yaldabaoth** (the Demiurge / chief Archon) — expressly "to entrap" human consciousness, the "Divine Spark." Icke calls this the **"soul trap."** The body and brain "decode" the simulation much as a receiver decodes a Wi-Fi signal, so what seems solid is information rendered into apparent reality.

A core component is Icke's claim that **reincarnation** — the idea that souls return repeatedly to "learn lessons" — is itself part of the trap: a recycling mechanism that keeps the Divine Spark captive within the simulation rather than a path to growth. Escape comes not through the "lessons" cycle but through awakening to expanded awareness (see [[The Reveal is the awakening of expanded awareness through the heart]]).
