## Definition / Characterization from Source

"Illuminism" is Marrs's term for the doctrine — as opposed to the defunct Bavarian organization — that he says still "casts its shadow across the world." He defines it as a mindset: the belief that a self-enlightened few have "developed a personal enlightenment not accessible to the rest of humankind," coupled with "the desire to control others, to operate in secrecy, and adhere to the idea that the end justifies the means." He calls it "simply another 'ism,'… a philosophy, a mindset," practiced today by people who would not even recognize the word "Illuminati."
