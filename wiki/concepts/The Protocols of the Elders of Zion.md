## Definition / Characterization from Source

A notorious fabricated text purporting to lay out "procedures for usurping national governments and gaining world domination." Marrs treats it as a forgery — "this document may have wreaked more havoc than almost any other piece of literature in recent history" — and argues it likely began as an Illuminati-related document, was plagiarized into Maurice Joly's 1864 satire *Dialogue in Hell Between Machiavelli and Montesquieu*, then rewritten with antisemitic material by the Russian Ochrana and Sergei Nilus in the 1890s to portray revolutionaries as pawns of a "Jewish conspiracy." Marrs repeatedly rejects the antisemitic reading.
