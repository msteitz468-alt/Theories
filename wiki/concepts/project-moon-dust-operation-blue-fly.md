US Air Force programs for the covert worldwide recovery of 'descended foreign space vehicles' and unidentified objects, run via the 4602d Air Intelligence Service Squadron and tasked by the 1961 'Betz Memo'. Declassified files tie Moon Dust to sighting/recovery reports in Pakistan, Bolivia and Nepal. In 1987 the Air Force said the name 'no longer officially exists', having been replaced by a still-classified one.
