# The Western Esoteric Tradition (the Yoga of the West)

## Definition from Source

In *The Mystical Qabalah*, [[Dion Fortune (Violet Mary Firth, 1890–1946)]] argues that the Western nations possess their own initiatory and mystical system — a "Yoga of the West" — distinct from and better suited to the Western temperament than Eastern yogas. Its theoretical basis is the **Qabalah**, "the Wisdom of Israel," and its master-glyph is the [[The Tree of Life (Otz Chiim)]]. Fortune holds that the mysticism of Israel supplies the foundation of modern Western occultism and forms the theoretical basis upon which all ceremonial magic is developed, transmitted through initiatory schools by manuscript and oral tradition.
