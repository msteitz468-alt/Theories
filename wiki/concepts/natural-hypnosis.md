## Concept Definition

**Natural Hypnosis** is Seth's term for the normal, daily state of highly concentrated focus on specific ideas or beliefs. Rather than an artificial, sleep-like, or clinical trance state, hypnosis is a natural mental faculty that all humans utilize constantly.

Seth explains that all individuals are always in a state of hypnosis regarding their primary beliefs about themselves and the world. Whatever idea the conscious mind focuses on intensely acts as a hypnotic command, directing the subconscious to materialize it into physical experience.

## Mechanics of Natural Hypnosis

* **Concentration of Energy:** When the mind focuses on a single belief, excluding other stimuli, it concentrates emotional and electromagnetic energy. This concentrated focus acts like a command to the body's cells and the outer environment to align with that belief.
* **The "Trance" of Core Beliefs:** If an individual believes they are clumsy, unlucky, or biologically weak, they are in a self-induced "hypnotic trance" regarding that limitation. The body and circumstance execute this belief automatically.
* **Deliberate Application:** Seth provides a practical technique for using natural hypnosis deliberately to remove limitations:
  1. Sit comfortably and concentrate on a single positive statement or visualization (e.g., "I am healthy and valid").
  2. Suspend other thoughts and critical feedback, allowing the suggestion to enter the inner mind directly.
  3. Perform this for 5 to 10 minutes, using the current [[The Point of Power]] to seed the suggestion.
