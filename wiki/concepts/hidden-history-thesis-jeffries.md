## Definition from Source

The umbrella thesis of *Hidden History*: that the major events of modern American history are not a
series of accidents but a "deliberate systematic plan" — a sustained conspiracy of assassinations,
scandals, electoral fraud, and elite corruption, concealed or distorted by a complicit mainstream
media. Jeffries invokes Thomas Jefferson on "a series of oppressions… pursued unalterably… [proving]
a deliberate systematic plan," and FDR's "In politics, nothing happens by accident. If it happens,
you can bet it was planned that way."

## Description from Source

The book applies this lens decade by decade from 1963, treating each episode (assassinations, Waco,
Oklahoma City, TWA 800, 9/11, electoral "Votescam," the Franklin scandal, etc.) as a cover-up, and
identifying a recurring [[The political "body count" pattern (Jeffries)]] of inconvenient witnesses
and figures who die suspiciously. It culminates in chapters on the New World Order network (CFR,
Bilderberg, Bohemian Grove), the Federal Reserve, and the justice system. This is the source's
organizing frame; it overlaps the wiki's broader NWO-conspiracy material (e.g. [[Rule by Secrecy (Marrs)]]).
