**Concept Description**

In Cooper's framework, the "New World Order" (NWO) refers to a secret global government being assembled by interlocking elite networks — primarily the Council on Foreign Relations, the Trilateral Commission, the Bilderberg Group, the Knights of Malta, and Skull & Bones. Cooper argues this goal has been in preparation since at least the 1954 Bilderberg founding, with institutional control of media, government, and finance achieved through CFR membership saturation.

Cooper distinguishes the NWO from mere political ideology: he presents it as a operational plan with specific timetables, including detention camps for dissidents, a plan to replace the US Constitution with a "Newstates of America" constitution, and the use of AIDS as a deliberate population reduction mechanism.
