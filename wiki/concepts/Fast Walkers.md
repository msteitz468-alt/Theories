Term for objects detected passing through the field of view of Earth-observation sensors (notably Defense Support Program early-warning satellites feeding Pine Gap) that are suspected of being in orbit. The category includes ordinary debris, but Coulthart's source Bob Fish described a 1991 object that made a controlled 30-degree course correction before a controlled descent.
