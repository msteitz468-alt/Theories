## Definition from Source

In *The Secret Doctrine* (Vol. II), Blavatsky asserts that two now-sunken supercontinents served
as the homelands of the Third and Fourth Root-Races respectively: **Lemuria** (Third Race) and
**Atlantis** (Fourth Race). Both are presented not as myths but as geological and historical fact,
preserved in the "secret annals" of occult tradition and corroborated (she argues) by geological
ridge formations, ancient traditions, and the universal occurrence of "deluge" myths.

## Lemuria: Continent of the Third Root-Race

### Location and extent

Blavatsky teaches that Lemuria was a vast continent spanning the Indian and Pacific Oceans, extending
in a horse-shoe shape past Madagascar, around what is now southern Africa, through the Atlantic up to
Norway. She credits the name to zoologist P.L. Sclater's "Lemuria" hypothesis (proposed to explain
the distribution of lemurs), but assigns it a far older significance. The remnant ridge running
south from the British Isles and swinging toward South America is cited as geological evidence of
the submerged continent's extent.

### Character and inhabitants

Lemuria was the cradle of the Third Root-Race: the first physically incarnate humans. Early
Lemurians were:

- Androgynous (hermaphrodite) and of enormous stature — at the peak of the race, upward of 27 feet,
  according to Blavatsky's reading of the Bamian and Easter Island colossal statues as testimonials
- "Egg-born": reproducing via externally gestated egg-like structures before the separation of sexes
- Still possessed of a functional "third eye" (pineal gland as active sensory organ) granting
  direct spiritual perception
- Initially mindless, then endowed with intellect when the Manasaputras/Lhas incarnated in them

Blavatsky describes the later Lemurians, after the sexes separated, as having fallen into physical
sin — particularly the "mindless" sub-races who mated with animal-forms and produced semi-human
hybrids which she identifies as the ancestors of modern anthropoid apes.

### Destruction

Blavatsky teaches that Lemuria was destroyed primarily by volcanic fires and continental subsidence
("inner fires had destroyed the land of their fathers"), occurring in stages over millions of years.
Ceylon (Sri Lanka) is described as a northern highland remnant of the original Lemurian continent.
The destruction forced the surviving elect into migration, ultimately seeding the Atlantean race.

### The Lemurian legacy

Blavatsky asserts that Cyclopean megalithic structures worldwide — Stonehenge, Carnac, the Bamian
statues, Easter Island statues — are the physical remains of Lemurian and later Atlantean construction,
built by giant races with psychic mastery of matter (what she calls the "magic" of statics and
equilibrium). The "Sons of Will and Yoga" produced by the Third Race through Kriyasakti are described
as the spiritual forebears of all subsequent Arhats and Mahatmas.

## Atlantis: Continent of the Fourth Root-Race

### Location and extent

Blavatsky locates Atlantis in the mid-Atlantic, describing it as having developed from the Atlantic
prolongation of Lemuria — meaning Atlantis and Lemuria are geologically continuous, the former
growing from the latter's Atlantic arm. Atlantis at its height was a true continent, not merely a
large island; the Azores, Canary Islands, and mid-Atlantic ridge are cited as its remnants.

### Character and civilization

The Atlanteans were the Fourth Root-Race: fully physical, sexually reproducing, and of great stature
(giants by present standards, though smaller than Lemurians). Blavatsky attributes to them:

- **Advanced astronomy and science**: including the astronomical records of Asuramaya (a legendary
  Atlantean sage to whom Brahmanical chronological computations are traced)
- **Enormous cities** built of "rare earths and metals" and stone
- **Cyclopean architecture**: colossal statues nine yatis (27 feet) high
- **Psychic and occult powers**: especially the operation of the "third eye" in their earlier
  sub-races, which progressively atrophied as the race materialized
- **Divine Kingship**: the first sub-races were ruled by divine incarnate Dhyan-Chohans ("Divine
  Dynasties"), later displaced by human rulers as materialization advanced

### The Fall of Atlantis

Blavatsky teaches that the Atlantean fall was twofold — spiritual and physical. Spiritually, the
dominant Atlantean races turned their inherited occult powers toward sorcery and black magic,
worshipping idols (human bodies and phallic symbols), taking "wives from the mindless" (lower
sub-races), and breeding "wicked demons." Physically, great geological deluges destroyed the
continent in stages:

1. The first great "waters swallowed the seven great islands" — eliminating the oldest Atlantean
   sub-races millions of years ago
2. Further subsidences continuing over hundreds of thousands of years
3. A final major submersion Blavatsky places at approximately 850,000 years ago for the main continent
4. A last remnant — identified with Plato's Atlantis narrative — submerging around 12,000 years ago

"All holy saved, the unholy destroyed" — Blavatsky holds that the spiritually advanced Atlanteans who
had not succumbed to sorcery migrated to form the nucleus of the Fifth Race in Central Asia, while
the sorcerer-dominated masses perished.

### The Atlantean legacy

The surviving "Serpents of Wisdom" (initiated Adepts) from Atlantis are described as having taken
refuge in Central Asian mountain sanctuaries and underground sites (symbolized by pyramid-shaped
structures). Asuramaya's astronomical records were preserved in Brahmanical tradition. The legend of
the "Deluge" — from the Hindu (Vaivasvata Manu), Chaldean (Xisuthrus/Oannes), Greek (Deucalion),
and Hebrew (Noah) traditions — is identified by Blavatsky as a collective global memory of successive
Atlantean and Lemurian cataclysms.

## Geological arguments offered

Blavatsky cites the following as evidence:

- The mid-Atlantic ridge rising 9,000 feet, running from near Britain southward then toward Africa
- The "Wealden" fresh-water deposit in England as the bed of a former river draining Northern Lemuria
- The distribution of coral formations, lemurs, and similar fauna across separated continents
- The Challenger and Dolphin ocean-floor soundings showing submarine ridge structures
- The statues of Easter Island (original height up to 27 feet; "no reason to believe" they were
  built up by scaffolding, implying giant builders) and the Bamian colossi (173 feet)
