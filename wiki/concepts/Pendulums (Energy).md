## Definition from Source

An energy pendulum is an independent energy-information structure that arises when a group of people thinks and acts in the same way. Zeland writes that pendulums are created by the collective thought energy of their adherents and sustain themselves by continuing to attract and consume that energy.

"When groups of people direct their thought energy in a single direction a powerful energy-information structure is created — the energy pendulum."

## Key Properties (from source)

- **Independent**: once created, a pendulum exists as a structure independent of any individual adherent; it has its own survival imperative
- **All pendulums are inherently destructive**: "All pendulums are inherently destructive — they consume adherent energy" (Ch. II); even seemingly positive pendulums serve the structure's interests, not the individual's
- **Sustained by adherent uniformity**: the pendulum's origin and survival depend on the uniformity of its adherents' thinking (Ch. IX); individual expression threatens the pendulum
- **Frequency**: each pendulum has a characteristic oscillation frequency; it attracts people whose emotional frequency matches
- **Puppet strings**: the pendulum controls adherents through fear, guilt, duty, inferiority/warrior/truth-lover complexes, justice, pride, and ambition (Ch. II)

## Defeating a Pendulum (from source)

The source describes several techniques:
- **Refuse conflict**: do not fight or argue; opposition feeds the pendulum
- **Accept indifferently**: accept the pendulum's opening move without emotional engagement
- **Ignore** (not avoid): conscious indifference, not anxious avoidance
- **Gentle dodge**: accept the first blow diplomatically, then redirect (described as "aikido" principle)
- **Fighter role is also a trap**: the Fighter type who battles pendulums wastes energy fighting them and feeds them through opposition

## Pendulums and Importance

Pendulums exploit the mechanism of [[Excess Potential]] — they induce importance (fear, pride, guilt) which then creates excess potential and triggers [[Law of Balance]] against the individual's interests.

"The pendulums abhor streams in the alternatives flow" because such streams require minimal energy expenditure, leaving nothing for the pendulum to harvest.
