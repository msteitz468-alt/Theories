## Definition from Source

Chapter Seven is Kynes' catch-all "Miscellaneous" domain, covering what she identifies as the most
foundational axes of the correspondence system. Her introduction states: "Since ancient times, the
elements have been considered as the fundamental building blocks and organizing principles of the
universe. Likewise, numbers were seen as providing structure to the world as well as a means of
expression. Colors symbolize all energies and aspects of life, and can have an immediate effect on
our moods." She also notes that the seven-color spectrum maps onto the "magical number seven," and
uses **yin and yang** throughout the book rather than gendered polarity terms, linking them to cyclical
time and the waxing and waning moon.

The chapter covers: the four classical elements (Air, Earth, Fire, Water), the directions (North,
South, East, West, plus the four intercardinal directions), the colors (over 20 entries plus shades),
yin and yang energy, the seven chakras, numbers 1–13 (Kynes explicitly caps her number system at 13,
tied to the 13 lunar months), and the Major Arcana of the Tarot.

This domain resonates with the Hermetic doctrine of correspondence — the idea that elemental structure
pervades all planes of reality — as articulated by [[Hermes Trismegistus]] and systematized in
[[The Seven Hermetic Principles]] ("as above, so below"). The elemental correspondence system Kynes
describes is the Pagan/Wiccan practical application of that cosmological principle; see
[[The Principle of Correspondence (Kybalion)]].

## The Four Elements and Their Correspondences

Each element carries a full correspondence profile across all domains covered by the book.

| Element | Direction | Season | Time of Day | Energy | Principle | Ritual Tool | Sense |
|---------|-----------|--------|-------------|--------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Air | East | Spring | Dawn | Yang | To Know | Athame, Incense, Sword | Smell |
| Earth | North | Winter | Midnight | Yin | To Be Silent | Pentacle | Touch |
| Fire | South | Summer | Midday | Yang | To Will | Censer, Wand | Sight |
| Water | West | Autumn | Dusk | Yin | To Dare | Cauldron, Chalice, Cup | Taste |

**AIR** → Zodiac (Aquarius, Gemini, Libra); Jupiter, Mercury, Uranus; number 5; colors light blue, gray,
lavender, pink, red, silver, white, yellow; Tarot (Fool, Swords, Wands); elemental beings Sylphs,
Elves, Fairies; angels Michael and Raphael; eagle, falcon, hawk; intentions: communication,
creativity, divination, healing, inspiration, intelligence, justice, learning, the mind, protection,
psychic ability, travel, wisdom.

**EARTH** → Zodiac (Capricorn, Taurus, Virgo); Earth, Saturn, Venus; numbers 4, 6, 8; colors black,
brown, green, white; Tarot (Pentacles); elemental beings Brownies, Dryads, Elves, Fairies, Gnomes,
Pixies; angels Auriel and Gabriel; goddess Gaia, Demeter, Persephone; god Cernunnos, Pan, the Green
Man; intentions: abundance, agriculture, fertility, grounding, healing, nurture, prosperity, stability,
wealth.

**FIRE** → Zodiac (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius); Jupiter, Mars, Sun; numbers 1, 3, 9; colors crimson, gold,
orange, pink, red, white, yellow; Tarot (Judgement, Swords, Wands); elemental beings Salamanders,
Mermaids (also listed here); angel Michael; goddesses Aine, Amaterasu, Brigid, Sekhmet, Vesta; gods
Agni, Hephaestus, Prometheus, Vulcan; intentions: action, ambition, courage, creativity, defense,
healing, illumination, leadership, lust, magic (general), passion, purification, transformation.

**WATER** → Zodiac (Cancer, Pisces, Scorpio); Mercury, Moon, Neptune, Pluto, Saturn; numbers 2, 7;
colors aqua, black, blue, gray, green, indigo, lavender, purple, silver, turquoise, violet, white;
Tarot (Cups, Hanged Man, Moon); elemental beings Mermaids, Norns, Undines; angel Raphael; goddesses
Aphrodite, Isis, Tiamat; gods Aegir, Manannan, Osiris, Poseidon; intentions: adaptability,
compassion, dream work, emotions, fertility, healing, intuition, psychic ability, purification,
secrets, sensitivity, transformation, wisdom.

## The Directions

Kynes assigns eight directions, with the four cardinal directions corresponding to the classical
elements and seasons. The intercardinal directions (Northeast, Northwest, Southeast, Southwest) map
to transitional seasons and carry narrower intentional profiles.

- **East** → Air; Spring; Dawn; white, pink, yellow, green; Raphael; intentions: beginnings,
  communication, creativity, divination, illumination, intelligence, life, wisdom.
- **South** → Fire; Summer; Noon; gold and red; Michael; lion; intentions: ambition, courage, energy,
  faith, illumination, knowledge, light, passion, protection, purification, sexuality, strength,
  willpower.
- **West** → Water; Autumn; Dusk; blue, lavender, white; Gabriel; swan, eagle; intentions: afterlife,
  balance, compassion, death, dream work, emotions, endings, imagination, passion, psychic ability,
  travel, visions.
- **North** → Earth; Winter; Midnight; black, brown, green, white, yellow; bear, wolf, elk, tiger;
  intentions: abundance, clarity, fertility, healing, manifestation, power, purification, rebirth,
  self-work, strength, visions, wealth.
- **Northeast** (late winter) → blue; intentions: knowledge, self-work, wisdom.
- **Northwest** (late autumn) → gray; intentions: accomplishment, guidance, influence.
- **Southeast** (late spring) → purple; intentions: blessings, prosperity.
- **Southwest** (late summer) → pink; intentions: love, relationships, romance.

## Colors

Kynes treats color as a full-fledged axis of the correspondence web, not a supplementary coding
system. Each color links to zodiac signs, planets, elements, directions, chakras, deities, numbers,
Tarot, and a rich intentions list. Many colors have named shades with distinct profiles.

- **Black** → Earth and Water; North; New Moon; Samhain; numbers 8, 13; yin energy; goddesses Hecate,
  Kali, the Morrigan; intentions: acceptance, banish, bind, death, divination, grounding, hexes,
  karma, magic (black, crone), protection, reversal, secrets, transformation, wisdom.
- **Blue** → Water; Thursday/Friday; numbers 4, 6; Moon/Jupiter/Neptune; undines; Raphael;
  intentions: communication, dream work, emotions, fidelity, hope, intuition, protection, relationships,
  truth, visions, wisdom. (Dark blue: fertility, guidance, luck, occult power. Light blue: calm,
  devotion, peace, well-being. Royal blue: confidence, loyalty, psychic ability.)
- **Gold** → Fire; Sunday; South; Sun; numbers 1, 6; crown chakra; Raphael and Auriel; intentions:
  abundance, awareness, beauty, confidence, divination, happiness, healing, illumination, light,
  money, power, prosperity, wisdom.
- **Green** → Earth and Water; Thursday/Friday; Full Moon; heart chakra; numbers 2, 3, 7; Fairy magic;
  gnomes; Iris (goddess); intentions: abundance, agriculture, beauty, fertility, grounding, growth,
  healing, luck, love, money, nurture, prosperity, protection, rebirth, well-being, youth.
- **Red** → Air and Fire; Tuesday; South; Full Moon; root chakra; numbers 5, 9; Mars/Sun/Venus;
  Salamanders; Michael; goddesses the Morrigan, Maeve; intentions: action, battle, courage, desire,
  energy, fertility, justice, leadership, life, love, loyalty, lust, passion, protection, sexuality,
  strength, willpower.
- **Silver** → Air and Water; Monday/Wednesday; Moon and Mercury; numbers 5, 9; Tarot (High Priestess,
  Moon); goddesses Arianrhod, Hecate, Luna, Selene; intentions: astral realm, clairvoyance, dream
  work, healing, intuition, magic (moon), protection, psychic ability, purification.
- **White** → all four elements; Monday/Sunday; Moon, Earth, Venus; numbers 1, 2, 5, 7, 9; all four
  angels; intentions: balance, clarity, divination, enchantment, healing, innocence, peace, protection,
  purification, purity, truth, unity.
- **Yellow** → Air and Fire; Sunday/Wednesday; Mercury and Sun; numbers 1, 12; solar plexus chakra;
  fairies, sylphs; Raphael; intentions: clarity, communication, happiness, intelligence, knowledge,
  learning, light, the mind, psychic ability, willpower, wisdom.
- **Purple** → Water; Thursday/Wednesday; Southeast; numbers 7, 8; crown chakra; Jupiter/Mercury/
  Neptune; intentions: authority, clairvoyance, consciousness, divination, enlightenment, healing,
  influence, intuition, magic (crone), psychic ability, spirituality, transformation, wisdom.
- **Orange** → Fire; Sunday/Tuesday/Wednesday; Mars/Mercury/Moon/Sun; sacral chakra; numbers 1, 8;
  intentions: adaptability, ambition, attraction, communication, confidence, courage, energy, goals,
  healing, memory, money, stimulation, strength, success.

## Numbers (1–13)

Kynes caps her numeric correspondence system at **13**, tied explicitly to the 13 lunar months of the
year. Each number links to zodiac signs, planets, days, elements, colors, deities, angels, and a list
of intentions. The numbers are presented as genuine structural principles of the universe (a perspective
shared with Pythagorean and Hermetic numerology as systematized by [[The Seven Hermetic Principles]]).

| Number | Element | Energy | Key planets | Key intentions |
|--------|---------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Fire | Yang/Yin | Mercury, Sun | beginnings, ambition, independence, leadership, healing, optimism |
| 2 | Water | Yin | Mars, Moon | balance, challenges, dream work, empathy, insight, love, patience |
| 3 | Fire | Yang | Jupiter, Mars, Moon, Saturn | communication, creativity, cycles, friend/ship, harmony, luck, manifestation |
| 4 | Earth | Yin | Earth, Jupiter, Uranus | business, calm, compassion, justice, manifestation, order, stability |
| 5 | Air | Yang | Jupiter, Mars, Mercury, Venus | change, communication, freedom, passion, protection, senses, travel |
| 6 | Earth | Yin | Sun, Venus | affection, family, home, intelligence, love, loyalty, peace, relationships |
| 7 | Water | Yin/Yang | Neptune, Saturn, Venus | clairvoyance, enchantment, faith, healing, knowledge, love, wisdom |
| 8 | Earth | Yin | Mercury, Saturn | abundance, authority, discipline, fertility, justice, karma, money, power |
| 9 | Fire | Yang | Mars, Moon | compassion, courage, creativity, healing, independence, magic (moon), wisdom |
| 10 | (Yin) | — | — | accomplishment, cycles, death, fertility, life, marriage, order, unity |
| 11 | (Yang) | — | — | darkness, destiny, inspiration, intuition, personal power, spirituality |
| 12 | (Yin) | — | — | arcane knowledge, cycles, manifestation, the otherworld, prophecy, visions |
| 13 | Yang | — | Moon | astral realm, cycles, endings, manifestation, power, unity, wisdom, witchcraft |

Numbers 10–13 have lighter profiles in the text compared to 1–9; Kynes presents them more briefly.
Number 13 is associated with the Moon, the color black, the Oak (tree), and the gods Hades, Pluto,
and Zeus; its intentions include magic (black and moon), endings, manifestation, and unity.
