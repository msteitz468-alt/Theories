## Concept Definition

**The Point of Power** (specifically, **The Point of Power is in the Present**) is the core practical concept in Seth's metaphysical model. It states that the present moment is the only active junction where spirit and matter meet, where the soul projects itself into physical experience, and where the individual has the power to change their reality.

Seth emphasizes that assigning greater force to the past (such as childhood trauma or past-life karma) or to the future makes the individual feel ineffective and denies them access to their natural energy.

## Mechanics of the Point of Power

* **Simultaneous Intersection:** Because all reincarnational lifetimes and probable realities exist simultaneously, the present moment acts as a psychic touchstone. By changing beliefs and actions in the *current* present, an individual immediately sends psychological and electromagnetic ripples that alter their simultaneous pasts and futures.
* **Restructuring the Past:** The physical brain stores memories based on current beliefs. If an individual changes their beliefs in the present, they literally alter the biological and emotional influence of past events (what Seth calls "restructuring the past").
* **Selecting Probabilities:** Future events exist as a web of probabilities. The conscious choices made in the present act as selection mechanisms, determining which probable timeline will materialize into physical experience.
* **Unification of Spirit and Flesh:** The present is the focal point where the multi-dimensional entity dips its energy into the space-time continuum, creating the physical body and environment.

## Practical Application

Seth instructs individuals to sit quietly and recognize that their immediate physical surroundings and body are being projected by their thoughts in the present moment. By deliberately choosing new conscious beliefs and focusing attention on them in the present, symptom patterns, limitations, and circumstances are automatically redirected.
