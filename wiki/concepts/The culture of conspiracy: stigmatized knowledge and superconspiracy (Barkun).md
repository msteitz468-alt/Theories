## Definition from Source

The umbrella framework of *A Culture of Conspiracy*: Barkun maps the American conspiracist subculture
as a coherent worldview built from two key ideas — **superconspiracy** (the most extreme of his three
types of conspiracy belief, in which many conspiracies are nested under one all-encompassing evil
design) and **stigmatized knowledge** (claims that mainstream institutions reject, whose very
rejection believers treat as proof of their truth). He opens with the Bohemian Grove / Alex Jones
case and the "nonhuman species masquerading as human beings" motif as emblematic of the milieu.
