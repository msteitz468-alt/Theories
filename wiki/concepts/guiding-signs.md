## Definition from Source

Guiding signs are external events or signals that indicate a possible bend or turn in the [[Alternatives Flow]] — specifically, they appear when a shift to a different [[Life Lines|life line]] has been initiated. They are not general omens or messages from the universe but specific indicators of movement already in progress.

"Guiding signs: indicate possible bend/turn in alternatives flow; appear when shift to different life line initiated." (Ch. VI)

## Key Properties (from source)

- **Indicate movement already underway**: signs do not predict what to do; they confirm that a shift is occurring
- **Relate only to current transfer chain link**: signs relate only to the current link in the [[Transfer Chains|transfer chain]] and "only have a very tenuous connection to the final goal" (Ch. VIII) — they cannot reliably indicate what is happening several life lines away
- **Spontaneous phrases are among clearest signs**: "Spontaneous phrases (casual, unplanned) are among clearest guiding signs." (Ch. VI)
- **Negative inner voice takes precedence**: in Transurfing, "with the exception of a negative response from the inner voice, sign interpretation is considered to be the least reliable technique one can use" (Ch. VIII, Transfer Chains section) — the [[Rustle of the Morning Stars]] is more reliable than external sign interpretation

## Limitations (from source)

The source is notably cautious about sign interpretation:
- "It is not recommended that you attribute them a great deal of meaning" (Ch. VIII)
- Signs are the least reliable Transurfing technique, except for the negative inner voice
- If many transfer chains separate the current life line from the target, the signs perceived will not be indicators of the distant goal — they only map the current stage
