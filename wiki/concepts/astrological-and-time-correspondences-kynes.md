## Definition from Source

Chapter Six treats **astrological and time reckoning** as a primary dimension of the correspondence
system. Kynes' framing is cosmological: "Some form of the zodiac and astrology can be found in most
cultures and through all time, and it is the focus for the most important prehistoric ritual sites." She
situates the sun and moon as the two foundational poles — the sun symbolizing the highest God, the moon
the Goddess — and argues that cyclical (rather than linear) time is intrinsically meaningful because
the circle symbolizes unity, completeness, and continuity.

She also notes the structural importance of the number 13: there are 13 lunar months in a year, each
of 28 days, with a Celt-named "nameless day" left over. The Norse Runic fortnights were likewise
28-day lunar.

The chapter divides into seven subsections, each fully cross-indexed to plants, minerals, animals,
deities, and ritual intentions:

1. **The Zodiac** — 12 signs, each with date range, element, energy, day, season, planetary rulers,
   chakras, Tarot, runes, trees, herbs, minerals, deities/angels, animals, and powers.
2. **The Solar System** — Earth, Jupiter, Mars, Mercury, Moon, Neptune, Pluto, Saturn, Sun, Uranus,
   Venus — treated not just as astrological but as active correspondence nodes.
3. **The Moon Phases** — Full, Waning, New/Dark, and Waxing, each with distinct goddess, magical, and
   intentional profiles.
4. **The Full Moons** — all 13 named monthly full moons (January through December plus the 13th Blue
   Moon), each with folk names, colors, stones, and purposes.
5. **The Seasons** — Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter with elemental, directional, and deity
   associations.
6. **The Days of the Week** — Monday (Moon) through Sunday (Sun), with planetary rulers, stones,
   herbs, deities, and intentions.
7. **The Times of Day** — Dawn (Air/East/Venus), Midday (Fire/South/Sun), Dusk/Twilight (Water/West/
   Venus), and Midnight (Earth/North/Earth and Venus).
8. **Celebrations** — the eight sabbats plus other festivals (Neptunalia, Compitalia, etc.) and the
   Ogham/Celtic tree calendar (20+ entries).

## Representative Correspondences

**Zodiac signs**

- **Aries** (21 Mar–20 Apr) → Mars and Sun; Fire; Tuesday; Spring; number 1; red, pink, white, yellow;
  Tarot Emperor; garnets and bloodstone; goddesses Athena and Hecate; intentions: action, courage,
  beginnings, passion, leadership.
- **Taurus** (20/21 Apr–20/21 May) → Earth and Venus; Earth element; Friday; numbers 2 and 5; green,
  blue, pink; Tarot Empress and Hierophant; emerald, rose quartz, jade; goddesses Aphrodite, Hathor,
  Venus; intentions: sensuality, stability, love, money, patience.
- **Cancer** (22 Jun–22/23 Jul) → Moon, Pluto, Sun; Water; Monday; silver; moonstone, opal, ruby; crab
  and dog as animals; goddesses Artemis, Demeter, Isis; intentions: emotions, family, home, intuition,
  psychic ability, nurture.
- **Scorpio** (22/23 Oct–21/23 Nov) → Mars and Pluto; Water; Tuesday; black, crimson, maroon; obsidian,
  topaz, garnet; phoenix (mythical); intentions: transformation, sexuality, the otherworld, power,
  secrets, psychic ability.
- **Sagittarius** (22/23 Nov–20/22 Dec) → Jupiter; Fire; number 9; blue, gold, orange, purple, red;
  Tarot Temperance and Wheel of Fortune; turquoise, lapis lazuli; intentions: freedom, truth, travel,
  independence, spirituality, intuition.
- **Capricorn** (21/22 Dec–19/21 Jan) → Earth and Saturn; Earth; Thursday; black, indigo, dark green;
  Tarot Devil; garnet, jet, onyx; Auriel (angel); intentions: ambition, discipline, grounding,
  manifestation, stability.

**Planets**

- **Moon** → Water element; Monday; numbers 2, 3, 9, 13; silver; moonstone, selenite, aquamarine;
  goddesses Arianrhod, Artemis, Diana, Hecate, Isis, Luna, Selene; fairies and mermaids; intentions:
  fertility, divination, dream work, emotions, cycles, psychic ability, magic (moon), transformation.
- **Sun** → Fire element; Sunday; numbers 1, 6; gold; amber, citrine, diamond, tiger's eye; gods Apollo,
  Helios, Horus, Ra; intentions: healing, leadership, prosperity, solar energy, fame, clarity, strength.
- **Mercury** → Air and Water; Wednesday; numbers 1, 4, 5, 8; silver and aluminum; agate, opal, topaz;
  gods Hermes, Thoth, Odin; intentions: communication, business, travel, divination, crossroads,
  intelligence, memory.
- **Venus** → Earth element (planet node); Friday; numbers 5, 6, 7; copper; rose quartz, emerald, jade;
  goddesses Astarte, Ishtar, Venus; intentions: love, romance, beauty, sensuality, fertility,
  relationships, pleasure.
- **Saturn** → Earth and Water; Saturday; numbers 3, 7, 8; lead; jet, obsidian, onyx; goddesses
  Hecate, Kali, Demeter; intentions: banish, bind, discipline, karma, limitations, endurance,
  longevity.
- **Jupiter** → Air and Fire; Thursday; numbers 3, 4, 5; tin; amethyst, sapphire, turquoise; god Zeus
  and goddesses Hera, Nut; intentions: abundance, authority, luck, prosperity, wisdom, expansion.

**Moon Phases**

- **Full Moon** → green and red; silver; Arianrhod (goddess); Fates and Norns (magical beings);
  intentions: abundance, manifestation, psychic ability, fertility, goals, strength, healing.
- **Waning Moon** → Lughnasadh; crone goddesses (Cerridwen, Cailleach Bheur, the Morrigan, Rhiannon);
  intentions: banish, bind, release, endings, purification, protection, reversal.
- **New/Dark Moon** → Samhain; black; dragon (mythical); intentions: beginnings, introspection, psychic
  ability, inner illumination, self-work.
- **Waxing Moon** → Imbolc; intentions: growth, increase, attraction, love, luck, business, creativity,
  strength.

**Full Moons (selected)**

- **May** (Faery/Flower/Hare Moon) → pink; emerald, rose quartz; intentions: divination, enchantment,
  fertility, love, well-being.
- **October** (Blood/Hunter's Moon) → black and blue; aquamarine, opal; intentions: courage, healing,
  memory, stability.
- **13th Full Moon** (Blue/Hearth/Wine Moon, September or October) → intentions: goals, the home, truth.

**Days of the Week**

- **Monday** (Moon) → pale blue, gray, silver, white; moonstone, emerald, sapphire; Hecate and Selene;
  intentions: dream work, intuition, fertility, psychic ability, astral work.
- **Wednesday** (Mercury) → orange, purple, violet, yellow; number 3; Athena, Hermes, Mercury, Odin;
  intentions: communication, crossroads, divination, intelligence, travel, wisdom.
- **Friday** (Venus) → aqua, blue, green, pink; copper; Aphrodite, Freya, Lakshmi, Venus; intentions:
  beauty, love, romance, pleasure, fertility, friendship.
- **Sunday** (Sun) → gold, orange, yellow; number 1; amber, carnelian, tiger's eye; Brigid (goddess),
  Helios (god); intentions: healing, clarity, success, prosperity, fame, confidence.

**Sabbats (selected)**

- **Samhain** (31 Oct) → Moon; New Moon; black and orange; apple, yew, blackthorn; Hecate, Cailleach
  Bheur, Kali, the Morrigan; Arawn, Hades, Odin (gods); intentions: divination, ancestor honor,
  crossroads, the otherworld, release, death of the god.
- **Imbolc** (1–2 Feb) → Moon; Waxing Moon; fire element; green, pink, white, yellow; Brigid (chief
  goddess); intentions: healing, fertility, divination, purification, hope, prophecy.
- **Litha** (21–22 Jun, Summer Solstice) → Sun; Fire; blue, gold, green, red; oak (honored king of
  trees); Apollo, Helios, Ra (gods); intentions: manifestation, power, light, strength, success,
  fertility.
- **Yule** (21–22 Dec, Winter Solstice) → Sun; Earth element; gold, green, red, silver, white; holly,
  oak, fir, yew; Mithras, Odin, Ra (gods); Isis, Gaia (goddesses); intentions: rebirth, transformation,
  divination, light, purification.
