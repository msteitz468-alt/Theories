## Definition from Source

A catalog page for the 49 named rituals that make up the bulk of *Success Magick*. Each is short and
structurally identical (read the Foundation, gaze at the sigil, speak a divine/Aethyr name, make
[[The offering to humanity (Gallery of Magick ritual method)|the offering to humanity]]), performed
once a day for seven days. They are grouped in three sets.

## The rituals

**The Ritual of the Angels (1–19):** 1 Vision · 2 Clarity · 3 Pathway · 4 Confidence · 5 Desire ·
6 Development · 7 Becoming · 8 Undertaking · 9 Boundary · 10 Imagination · 11 Collective ·
12 Tolerance · 13 Conduct · 14 Endurance · 15 Progression · 16 Visibility · 17 Abundance ·
18 Assessment · 19 Actions.

**The Ritual of the First Aethyrs (20–38):** 20 Acceptance · 21 Insistence · 22 Eagerness ·
23 Freedom · 24 Intuition · 25 Creativity · 26 Contribution · 27 Adaptability · 28 Strategy ·
29 Rest · 30 Indication · 31 Disconnection · 32 Support · 33 Meaning · 34 Wordless · 35 Story ·
36 Time · 37 Bravery · 38 Completion.

**The Ritual of the Final Aethyrs (39–49):** 39 Belonging · 40 Satisfaction · 41 Release ·
42 Balance · 43 Bliss · 44 Forgiveness · 45 Learning · 46 Teaching · 47 Welcoming · 48 Silence ·
49 Light.
