> **Sensitivity note.** The Root-Race scheme assigns hierarchical attributes to races and was later
> misappropriated by race ideologues. The content below records Blavatsky's assertions descriptively,
> as a historical document of occult thought, without endorsement.

## Definition from Source

Blavatsky's scheme of human evolution in *The Secret Doctrine* (Vol. II, Anthropogenesis) describes
humanity as passing through seven sequential **Root-Races**, each occupying a distinct geological
epoch and continent, each with its own mode of reproduction, physical form, and spiritual character.
The scheme is drawn from the **Stanzas of Dzyan** and their commentaries, and is offered as the
hidden record of human history suppressed or fragmented in all world mythologies.

## Description from Source

**First Root-Race — the "Self-born" (Chhaya-born).** Blavatsky teaches that the first humans were
*astral shadows* extruded directly from the bodies of the "Pitris" (lunar progenitor-spirits), pure
ethereal beings without bones, flesh, or independent consciousness. They reproduced by a kind of
"budding off" of their astral doubles. They inhabited the original "Sacred Imperishable Land" at the
North Pole region, and were not truly physical; their forms have left no fossil record.

**Second Root-Race — the "Sweat-born."** The second race was produced when the first's astral
bodies exuded denser drops, which grew into separate beings — compared by Blavatsky to the budding
of cells or of sea-polyps. Still sexless and mostly a-sexual, this race was likewise semi-ethereal,
yellow-golden in the source symbolism, and inhabited what Blavatsky calls "the Hyperborean continent."

**Third Root-Race — the "Egg-born" and the hermaphrodites (Lemuria).** The third race marks the
first appearance of physical humanity on the continent of Lemuria. Early Third-Race beings were
androgynous (hermaphrodite) giants who reproduced by laying large egg-like structures in which
embryos developed over years. Mid-way through this race the sexes separated. Blavatsky assigns this
race to a vast Lemurian continent spanning the Indian and Pacific Oceans. The Third Race also
produced — through Kriyasakti (will-power) before the separation of sexes — "Sons of Will and Yoga,"
spiritually perfected forebears of later Adepts and Mahatmas. At the close of the Third Race,
some mindless men (those lacking the "spark" of Manas) mated with animal-forms, producing semi-human
hybrids that Blavatsky considers the actual ancestors of the anthropoid apes. Lemuria was eventually
destroyed by volcanic fires and submersion.

**Fourth Root-Race — the Atlanteans.** The fourth and most physically powerful race arose on
Atlantis, the continent that occupied the mid-Atlantic region. Blavatsky describes the Atlanteans
as fully physical, sexual, and of gigantic stature. They built enormous cities, developed advanced
civilizational arts including astronomy (attributed to the Atlantean astronomer Asuramaya), and
possessed psychic powers inherited from the Third Race. Their ruling classes fell progressively into
sorcery (Black Magic) and material power-worship, causing what Blavatsky calls the "Fall" of the
race. Great deluges — the "first great waters" swallowing the "seven great islands" — destroyed the
Atlantean continent in stages. The final major submergence is placed by Blavatsky at roughly 850,000
years ago, with a last remnant (Plato's Atlantis island) surviving until about 12,000 years ago.

**Fifth Root-Race — our current humanity.** The fifth race, descended from an elect stock of the
later Third and Fourth Races, is the present humanity. It is smaller in stature than the giants of
prior races but higher in intellectual development. Blavatsky teaches it arose in Central Asia, was
ruled initially by "Divine Kings" (incarnated higher Dhyan-Chohans), and is still incomplete, with
two Root-Races yet to come.

**Sixth and Seventh Root-Races.** Described only sketchily in the available text: the Sixth will
produce a still more spiritualized humanity; the Seventh will culminate the cycle of the current
"Round," after which spiritual rather than physical evolution predominates.

### Cross-cutting doctrinal features of the scheme

- Each Root-Race is born under the influence of one of the seven Planetary Spirits (Dhyan-Chohans).
- Races evolve through seven sub-races, each with its own character.
- The scheme runs on an arc of *spiritual descent into matter* and *re-ascent into spirit*; the nadir
  is the mid-Fourth Race Atlantean period.
- The pineal gland is described as the atrophied remnant of a working "third eye" possessed by the
  Third and early Fourth Races, enabling direct spiritual perception.
- "Man preceded the mammalia in this Round": Blavatsky explicitly inverts Darwinian sequence,
  asserting that apes are partly descended from mindless Third-Race humans, not the reverse.
