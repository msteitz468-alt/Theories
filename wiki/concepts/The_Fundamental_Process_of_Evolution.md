## Definition and Characterization from Source

In [[Thomas Campbell]]'s *[[My Big TOE]]*, the **Fundamental Process of Evolution** is one of only two foundational assumptions from which the entire theory is derived (the other being [[Concept: AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)|AUO]]). It is the universal mechanism by which consciousness evolves from high-entropy disorder to low-entropy organization and awareness.

Campbell defines it in functional terms:

> "The process is simple — maintain the winners, discard the losers — more or less random permutations and combinations are applied statistically to systems that have a significant array of potential outcomes. The winners continue to evolve while the losers fade away or maintain the status quo."

This is recognizably a generalization of Darwinian evolution, but applied universally to any self-organizing system with multiple possibilities — not just biological organisms.

## Core Principle

The Fundamental Process operates by:
1. Generating variation among states or configurations
2. Applying a profitability criterion (whichever states are more organized, more functional, lower in entropy are more "profitable")
3. Preserving the winning configurations and discarding losing ones
4. Iterating recursively

Campbell emphasizes recursion and self-reference:

> "Sometimes an evolving consciousness system may in time develop terrific complexity and seemingly endless potential as the Fundamental Process iterates upon itself repetitively, eventually evolving into something that begins to mimic its source."

This recursive self-application allows simple principles to generate immense complexity.

## Application to Consciousness

The Fundamental Process applied to [[Concept: AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)|AUO]] produces the history of all reality:

1. AUO exists as undifferentiated potential with random digital fluctuations
2. The Fundamental Process acts on these fluctuations, selecting states with more internal organization
3. Over vast evolutionary time, organization increases, entropy decreases
4. Structure, memory, awareness, and values emerge
5. AUO evolves into [[Concept: AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold)|AUM]]

Campbell describes this as analogous to — but vastly faster than — biological evolution:

> "With the aid of its specialized binary computing part, AUM can evolve at an incredibly fast pace compared to biological systems."

## Profitability as the Metric

A key concept is **profitability** — the Fundamental Process selects for states that are profitable in the evolutionary sense:

> "The Fundamental Process could not help but find time (an artifact of change) on the path toward greater profitability. Thus time evolves naturally within consciousness — it delivers the fundamental organizing technology that enables the decrease in entropy."

Profitability is defined by what enables further evolution and self-improvement, not by any external standard. For consciousness systems, profitability correlates with:
- Reduced entropy (higher organization)
- Greater awareness and functionality
- Increased capacity for purposeful action
- Movement toward lower entropy = toward love

## Irreversibility and Astable States

Campbell notes three possible conditions for an evolving system under the Fundamental Process:
1. **Profitable**: Making progress, entropy decreasing
2. **Unprofitable**: Regressing, entropy increasing — "say goodbye if that condition is not turned around in time"
3. **Astable**: Neither profitable nor unprofitable — "hanging around waiting to see which way it will go"

This framework applies to individual consciousness evolution as well as to larger systemic evolution.

## Application to Individual Humans

Campbell extends the Fundamental Process to individual human consciousness development:

> "Consciousness evolution does not progress through a process of successive epiphanies. Significant entropy reduction is accomplished through an iterative process that slowly accumulates a large number of nearly infinitesimal profitable choices to produce significant growth."

This means:
- Dramatic moments of insight are less important than consistent small choices
- Growth is cumulative across many interactions and events
- The Fundamental Process does not care about excuses: "Making it and getting it, within your given set of possible interactions, are the non-negotiable requirements of evolutionary progress"

### Entropy and Personal Growth
Personal growth = entropy reduction in one's consciousness. Campbell defines:

> "Right growth and right learning are technically defined as growth or learning that leads to a decrease in the entropy of your consciousness. Personal growth, growing the quality of your being, or growing wisdom — all increase your personal power as they decrease the entropy of your consciousness. I am talking about the growing-up and maturing of your being — a process that increases your capacity to love. I am also talking about spiritual growth, the evolution of your soul."

This directly equates spiritual growth, wisdom, maturity, and love with the physics concept of entropy reduction.

## Consciousness-Evolution Fractal

Campbell describes the output of the Fundamental Process acting on consciousness as a **consciousness-evolution fractal ecosystem**:

> "The dynamic interaction of a simple evolutionary process with the fundamental energy of a self-organizing digital consciousness spawns individuated sentient awareness (thinking beings) through a multi-leveled self-generating progression of recursive process that is reminiscent of how a simple geometric relationship generates an intricate fractal pattern. An interesting similarity: In both consciousness and fractal systems, each part of the pattern carries the blueprint of the whole."

This fractal nature means:
- Each individual consciousness mirrors the structure of AUM
- Evolution at any level reflects evolutionary dynamics at all levels
- The process is self-similar across scales

## Evolution of Values

A critical threshold in the Fundamental Process is when consciousness develops values:

> "By the time values appear in the evolutionary development of an individual consciousness energy system, entropy has been lowered sufficiently to allow the concepts of purposeful intention, premeditation, and planning to take root."

Before values emerge, evolution is blind. Once values appear, consciousness can direct its own evolution purposefully. This shift — from blind evolutionary drift to self-directed evolution — represents a major transition point in AUO's development into AUM and parallels human moral development.

## Negative vs. Positive Evolution

Campbell notes that entropy reduction can proceed in two directions:

> "AUM is dim consciousness evolved into brilliant love-consciousness — which is how positive consciousness naturally evolves. The negative intent consciousness critters and beings (evil) can evolve only toward power-control-force."

Positive evolution: consciousness → love, expansive, unlimited
Negative evolution: consciousness → power-control-force, limited, inherently capped

Campbell argues that negative evolution is "extremely limited and relatively worthless compared to the advantages gained by a consciousness evolving positively toward the expression of itself as love." He directly attributes PMR's dominance by power-control-force to the prevalence of "low quality consciousness, negativity, and evil running loose in our local reality."

## Relation to Physics

Campbell argues the Fundamental Process explains physical constants and structures:

> "How did AUM come up with the constant c? By occupying all the possibilities and building on the best results, by trial and error, by evolution. It was derived by applying the Fundamental Process on a scale and at a pace that is difficult for us to comprehend."

The speed of light, quantum granularity, and other physical parameters are thus evolved features of the PMR rule-set, not arbitrary brute facts.
