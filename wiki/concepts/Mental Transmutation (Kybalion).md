## Definition from Source

Mental Transmutation is, per the Kybalion, the true subject and practical art of Hermetic teaching.
The foundational maxim:

> "Mind (as well as metals and elements) may be transmuted, from state to state; degree to degree;
> condition to condition; pole to pole; vibration to vibration. True Hermetic Transmutation is a
> Mental Art."
> — THE KYBALION

The Three Initiates define it as "the art of changing and transforming mental states, forms, and
conditions, into others" — "the 'Art of Mental Chemistry,' if you like the term — a form of
practical Mystic Psychology."

Because [[The Principle of Mentalism (Kybalion)]] holds that "THE ALL is Mind; the Universe is
Mental," Mental Transmutation is not merely psychological self-help: it is literally "the art of
CHANGING THE CONDITIONS OF THE UNIVERSE, along the lines of Matter, Force and Mind." It is
described as "really the 'Magic' of which the ancient writers had so much to say in their mystical
works."

The Kybalion holds that what modern movements call "psychic phenomena," "mental influence," "mental
science," and "new-thought phenomena" all operate under this one principle, though their
practitioners "are quite ignorant compared to the ancient masters, for they lack the fundamental
knowledge upon which the work is based." The art operates through the Hermetic Principle of Polarity
— transmuting, for instance, Hate into Love, or Evil into Good — and through control of vibration.

Advanced Masters who have attained full mastery may, the text claims, control gross physical
conditions such as the elements of Nature, though they seek seclusion rather than public display.
