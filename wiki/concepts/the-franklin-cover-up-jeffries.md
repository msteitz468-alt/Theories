## Definition from Source

Per [[Hidden History (Jeffries)]], "The Franklin Cover-Up" refers to an alleged large-scale child sexual abuse network, centered on Republican Party operative Larry King and his management of the Franklin Credit Union in Omaha, Nebraska, alleged to have supplied underage victims to politicians, powerful business figures, and intelligence community figures across the country. The primary published source Jeffries draws on is attorney John DeCamp's book *The Franklin Cover-Up* (1992, updated 2005).

## Description from Source

Jeffries describes the scandal as extending well beyond Nebraska: victim Paul Bonacci, DeCamp's client, claimed he had been transported nationwide for sex, drug delivery, and snuff-film appearances at Larry King's behest. Bonacci also testified about Bohemian Grove ceremonies, describing them — without prior knowledge of the location — in detail later corroborated (per Jeffries) by Alex Jones's 2000 video of the "Cremation of Care" ritual; Bonacci alleged a real child was murdered there.

Key figures and institutional actors named in Jeffries's account:

- **Larry King** — Franklin Credit Union operator; Jeffries alleges King organized the abuse ring, had ties to Iran-Contra funding, and was connected to Omaha's social elite including Warren Buffett's wife Susie (who reportedly hosted a party for King).
- **Paul Bonacci** — primary victim-witness; testified to nationwide pedophile network involving military, CIA, political, and corporate powers.
- **John DeCamp** — former Nebraska state senator and attorney who wrote *The Franklin Cover-Up*; mentored by CIA director William Colby.
- **William Colby** — former CIA director who encouraged DeCamp's investigation; died in a mysterious boating accident on April 29, 1996 (see [[Jeffries alleges William Colby was murdered to silence his links to the Franklin Cover-Up]]).
- **Lt. Col. Michael Aquino** — Army officer who founded the Temple of Set in 1975; linked by multiple witnesses to a nationwide Satanic pedophile ring using military-base day care centers.
- **Gary Caradori** — Nebraska Senate Franklin Committee chief investigator; died in a suspicious plane crash on July 11, 1990 (per Jeffries, farmer witnesses said the plane exploded in midair).
- **Troy Boner** — witness who recanted under alleged FBI threats; died in a New Mexico hospital in late 2003 under unexplained circumstances after waving a copy of *The Franklin Cover-Up* and screaming "They're after me."
- **Alisha Owen** — victim who testified before a federal grand jury naming a Nebraska District Court judge and Larry King; sentenced to nine to twenty-seven years for perjury; her seventeen-year-old brother Aaron was found hanged in a correctional center on November 9, 1990.

Jeffries also covers the suppression of *Conspiracy of Silence*, a documentary produced by Yorkshire Television that was pulled from Discovery Channel airing (scheduled May 3, 1994, listed in TV Guide) under pressure from US politicians, with all copies ordered destroyed.

The scandal is treated as intersecting with:
- The McMartin Preschool case and the Finders (Ch. 12)
- Satanist/military connections via Aquino and the Presidio Child Development Center
- CIA "national security" suppression of the Finders investigation
- Penn State/Jerry Sandusky and charity "foster care" infrastructure
- The broader [[The New World Order network (Jeffries account)]]
