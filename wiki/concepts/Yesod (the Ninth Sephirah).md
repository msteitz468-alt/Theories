# Yesod (the Ninth Sephirah)

## Definition from Source
Yesod, the Foundation, is the ninth Sephirah, towards the base of the Pillar of Equilibrium. Fortune gives its attributions (Chapter XXIV) as: God-Name, Shaddai el Chai, "the Almighty Living God"; Archangel, Gabriel; Order of Angels, Kerubim ("the Strong"); Mundane Chakra, Levanah (the Moon); Magical Image, "a beautiful naked man, very strong"; Spiritual Experience, "Vision of the Machinery of the Universe"; Virtue, Independence; Vice, Idleness; correspondence in the microcosm, the reproductive organs; symbols, the Perfumes and sandals; Tarot cards, the four Nines (Great strength, Material happiness, Despair and cruelty, Material gain).

Yesod "purifies the Emanations," proving and correcting the "designing of their representations" formulated in Hod, before they reach Malkuth. It is the sphere of the Ether of the Wise (Akasha, Astral Light) — the "receptacle of the emanations" of all the other Sephiroth, and the only channel by which they reach Malkuth. Yesod is also the Sphere of the Moon, governing rhythmic etheric tides (the lunar cycle) that underlie sex, magnetism, and magic; it is described as the "Treasure House of Images," the sphere of Maya (Illusion), corresponding in the microcosm to the psychologists' Unconscious.
