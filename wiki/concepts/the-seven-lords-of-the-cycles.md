## Definition / Characterization from Source

Seven trans-temporal beings "from the Space-Times above," designated by the numbers **THREE–NINE**, who sit within the Flower in Amenti and direct human destiny, beneath the "Veiled Presence, Lord of Lords, the infinite Nine." Each holds a distinct office (Tablet 3): THREE — hidden magic / halls of the Dead; FOUR — Lord of Life; FIVE — Lord of all magic / the Word; SIX — Lord of Light / the hidden pathway; SEVEN — master of Space; EIGHT — orders and balances progress; NINE — the father, "forming… from out of the formless." The Logos issues from them.

Their secret names, invoked in the protective magic of Tablet 6, are: **UNTANAS, QUERTAS, CHIETAL, GOYANA, HUERTAL, SEMVETA, ARDAL**.
