US Defence Department security framework for compartmented classified projects. A SAP is a codenamed, Sensitive Compartmented Information project; a USAP (Unacknowledged SAP) may have its very existence denied; a WUSAP (Waived USAP) has normal oversight waived and is overseen only by the Congressional 'Gang of Eight'. Coulthart uses these definitions to frame claims that a UAP reverse-engineering program could be hidden as a WUSAP beyond ordinary oversight.
