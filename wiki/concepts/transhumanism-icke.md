## Definition and Characterisation (from the Source)

As presented in David Icke's *Everything You Need to Know But Have Never Been Told* (2018), "transhumanism" — which Icke says should be more accurately called "Nonhumanism" — refers to the planned merger of human biological and technological systems as the culminating phase of the Archontic control agenda. In mainstream discourse, transhumanism is framed as enhancement of human capability through technology. In Icke's framework, it is the final solution to the perennial problem that human consciousness has always retained the capacity to exceed and resist Archontic programming by connecting with Infinite Awareness. Once the human mind is hardwired to an AI hive mind through brain-cloud interface technology, this residual capacity would be permanently eliminated.

The process as described consists of graduated steps:
1. Mass adoption of smartphones and social media (addiction to the technology)
2. Internet of Things and smart devices (the physical infrastructure of the control grid)
3. 5G rollout (high-bandwidth network enabling the control grid to function)
4. Brain-cloud interface technology (Facebook Building 8, DARPA projects)
5. Full mind upload to the cloud / discarding of biological bodies

The "Singularity" (Ray Kurzweil's concept, adopted by Singularity University) refers to the moment when AI surpasses human intelligence and self-replicates autonomously — framed by Icke as the point of no return for human consciousness.

Key corporations and institutions named as primary vehicles: DARPA, Google/Alphabet, Facebook/Building 8, Singularity University, Apple (Siri via CALO project), Amazon.

## Primary Source Appearances

Transhumanism is the central subject of Chapter 16 (The Assimilation) and Chapter 17 (Synthetic Human). It is contextualised as the culmination of all prior control mechanisms described in the book.
