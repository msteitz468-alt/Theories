## Definition from Source

The "offering to humanity" is the distinctive closing gesture of every *Success Magick* ritual. After
raising a power (by reading the Foundation idea, gazing at the sigil, and speaking the divine/Aethyr
name), the practitioner imagines "how much better it would be if everybody could have this power,"
places one hand on the chest and moves it outward "in a gesture of offering," giving the raised power
"to all of humanity, seeking nothing for yourself."

## Description from Source

Brand teaches that this generous outward gesture is integral to the magic working, and that "stray
thoughts about what you want cannot damage this" — the focus is on giving the power away. It reflects
the book's broader thesis that personal success and service to the world are linked
("success comes when you discover your unique power and offer it to the world"). The full ritual
method (perform once daily for seven days, in private, with no special equipment or mood required) is
recorded in [[Brand teaches a standardized ritual method of sigil-gazing, divine names, and an offering to humanity]].
