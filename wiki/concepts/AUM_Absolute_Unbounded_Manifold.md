## Definition and Characterization from Source

In [[Thomas Campbell]]'s *[[My Big TOE]]*, **AUM** (Absolute Unbounded Manifold) is the name given to [[Concept: AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)|AUO]] after it has evolved sufficient complexity, organization, self-awareness, memory, value, and purpose to constitute a dramatically different type of entity. It is still the One Source, still made of the same fundamental consciousness substance as AUO, but now operates as an enormously complex, self-directing, low-entropy digital consciousness.

Campbell provides this characterization:

> "AUM is a more complex, lower entropy manifestation of AUO; no new substance or assumptions are added. At AUM's core is the same basic AUO consciousness energy sporting a more evolved level of awareness, lower entropy, and greater functionality."

## Core Properties

### Low Entropy
Unlike AUO's primordial disorder, AUM exhibits dramatically reduced entropy — high internal organization, awareness, purpose, and functional structure. This is the result of the [[Concept: The Fundamental Process of Evolution|Fundamental Process]] acting on AUO's digital substrate over evolutionary time.

### Self-Directing and Purposeful
AUM is characterized by:
- Self-awareness
- Memory
- Values
- Purpose and goals
- Active self-modification
- Ability to evolve its own organization

Campbell describes AUM as "an immense rational (has purpose and goals and works toward them) self-modifying dynamic system existing in a continual process of self-optimization."

### Digital Consciousness
AUM is fundamentally digital — constituted of digital reality cells, operating computationally in a "thought-space" or "mind-space" that has no physical extension. This digital nature allows AUM to:
- Evolve at dramatically accelerated pace
- Run multiple reality simulations in parallel
- Maintain hierarchical nested time structures
- Process events at rates far exceeding physical causality

Campbell offers an informal name: "digital-love-consciousness-being-thing."

### Love as Animating Principle
A central claim is that love — technically defined as the expression of low-entropy consciousness — is AUM's most fundamental animating property:

> "Love is the animating dynamic of AUM; it is AUM's most salient property. AUM is an evolving digital-love-consciousness-being-thing whose actions and intents are animated and motivated by unconditional love—which is the natural mode and expression of a low entropy consciousness."

### Not Perfect, Not Anthropomorphic
Campbell explicitly rejects idealizing AUM as omniscient, omnipotent, or perfect:

> "AUM is not perfect, AUM just is. It evolved to be how it is. Things evolve to be functional, not to meet some philosophical ideal of perfection."

AUM is not a man with a white beard. It is an evolved system, and its nature is a product of evolutionary pressures, not philosophical ideals.

## AUM's Organizational Hierarchy

### Internal Structure
AUM has evolved complex structural and dynamic components:
- **Objectives and values**
- **Dimensions** (specialized calculation spaces)
- **Memory** and content
- **Rule-sets** governing interactions within each dimension
- **Specialized computing functions** — including what Campbell calls [[Concept: TBC (The Big Computer)|The Big Computer]] (TBC)

### Dimensions within AUM
AUM contains nested dimensions of reality:
- **NPMR** ([[Concept: NPMR (Nonphysical Matter Reality)|Nonphysical Matter Reality]]) — less constrained reality within AUM
- **NPMRn** — specific interactive subset of NPMR, relative to our PMR
- **PMRk** ([[Concept: PMR (Physical Matter Reality)|Physical Matter Reality]]) — space-time reality dimensions; our physical universe is one PMR among many

Campbell visualizes this as: "a reality dimension (like PMR) within a larger reality dimension (like NPMR) within a larger reality dimension (like AUM)."

### The "Big Cheese" and Organizational Management
Campbell refers to a management entity — the "Big Cheese" — as "the division manager of 'N' division, dedicated to a specific subset of evolutionary development models containing many PMRs." This entity manages our system (OS) and its subset of PMRs, operating under AUM's broader direction.

> "AUM is thus chairman of the board and Chief Executive Officer of MOG Corp., as well as the entire evolutionary experiment itself."

Campbell uses this corporate analogy humorously but treats the organizational structure itself seriously.

## AUM's Relationship to Individuated Consciousness

### AUM Needs Us
A notable claim is the mutual dependency of AUM and individuated consciousness:

> "AUM needs us as part of its being for multiple purposes, just as you need your circulatory system for multiple purposes."

This creates a relationship of mutual benefit rather than mere top-down creation. Individual consciousness quality evolution directly contributes to AUM's own evolution.

### Sheet-and-Puppet Analogy
Campbell employs an extended analogy: AUM is a sheet, individuated consciousnesses are hand-puppets emerging from the sheet into the space-time dimension. The puppets are made of the sheet's substance but the sheet is not defined by the puppets:

> "The sheet and its protruding dimensions are engaged in a program of continuous quality improvement administered by the Fundamental Process of evolution acting upon a multi-leveled consciousness-evolution fractal ecosystem."

### No Direct Interference
Campbell states AUM generally does not directly intervene in PMR events. Those beings (like the "Big Cheese" and assistants) who manage OS operate under constraints. Direct interference in PMR by non-PMR forces is "generally forbidden" except under conditions regulated by the [[Concept: Psi Uncertainty Principle|psi uncertainty principle]].

## AUM's Evolutionary Experiments

AUM runs multiple experiments in consciousness evolution simultaneously — including multiple PMR "virtual reality" learning labs as well as NPMR experiments. Our physical universe is one such experiment. Campbell describes earlier, less successful NPMRn-based experiments:

> "Individuated units of consciousness using NPMR as their local reality became confused over what was the real them and what they thought was them. Learning was difficult."

The design of PMR's space-time rule-set was AUM's solution to these problems — creating immediate causal accountability that makes learning more efficient and productive.

## AUM's Knowledge of Consciousness

Campbell argues AUM's primary interest is:

> "AUM is interested in the science of the dynamics of consciousness evolution — the science of the creation of love-consciousness."

Just as humans study biology and physics, AUM studies consciousness — its own substance. This parallel is central to Campbell's framework: AUM's curiosity and ours are structurally analogous, differing in scale and domain.

## Time Hierarchy within AUM

AUM operates at the most fundamental time scale — the smallest possible time quantum. AUM's clock ticks eighty orders of magnitude faster than our physical seconds. This nested time hierarchy is essential:

- AUM has the smallest time increment (most fundamental)
- NPMR has a larger time increment (slower)
- PMR has the largest time increment (slowest — appears as physical reality)

This means AUM can observe PMR as if it were in extreme slow motion, enabling comprehensive observation and management of evolutionary processes.
