## Definition and Characterization from Source

In [[Thomas Campbell]]'s *[[My Big TOE]]*, **NPMR** (Nonphysical Matter Reality) refers to the non-space-time dimensional reality within [[Concept: AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold)|AUM]] that surrounds and contains [[Concept: PMR (Physical Matter Reality)|PMR]]. NPMR is less constrained than PMR — it operates without the space-time rule-set, allowing for more varied forms of existence and interaction.

Campbell distinguishes between:
- **NPMR** — the general category of non-physical reality dimensions within AUM
- **NPMRn** — a specific subset of NPMR that is interactive with our PMR (our "local" non-physical layer)
- The hierarchy: AUM > NPMR > NPMRn > OS (Our System which includes PMR + NPMRn)

## Core Properties

### Spacelessness
NPMR exists outside of space — it has no three-dimensional spatial extension. Because distance requires space, and NPMR has none, information propagation in NPMR is not bound by the speed of light:

> "From my experience, and that of many other NPMR explorers, communications are seemingly instantaneous in spaceless NPMR and not restricted by the relatively pokey transmission rate of light-speed as they are in PMR."

This spacelessness makes NPMR communications appear instantaneous from a PMR perspective.

### Faster Time
NPMR operates at a smaller time quantum than PMR. While PMR's smallest time increment (DELTA-t) is very large relative to AUM's fundamental time quantum, NPMR's time quantum is intermediate — faster than PMR but slower than AUM. This means that during one PMR tick, an "enormous number" of NPMR time increments occur.

Campbell states:

> "Between time increments in PMR (time stands still in PMR between increments), things continue to happen (distortions, patterns, and information continue to propagate) because the clock keeps on ticking in NPMRn."

This is relevant to the mechanics of psi phenomena, precognition, and non-physical consciousness activity that appears to occur "between" physical time ticks.

### Less Constrained Rule-Set
NPMR's rule-set does not include space-time constraints. This produces:
- More degrees of freedom for individuated consciousness
- More varied life-forms and entities than PMR
- Different causal structures
- Weaker immediate accountability compared to PMR's sharp cause-effect

Campbell notes this reduced accountability contributed to earlier NPMRn evolutionary experiments where beings became confused about identity and failed to evolve efficiently — a key design deficiency that led AUM to create PMR.

## NPMRn: The Interactive Layer

NPMRn is the specific NPMR subset that interacts with our PMR. Campbell describes OS (Our System) as PMR + NPMRn together, forming the complete environment for human consciousness evolution.

Within NPMRn:
- Probable reality surfaces are computed between PMR time increments
- NPMR beings can interact with PMR consciousness within rule-set limits
- The [[Concept: TBC (The Big Computer)|Big Computer]] (TBC) runs the PMR simulation as a sub-process within NPMR's larger computational space
- Human consciousness has both a PMR component (physical body) and an NPMRn component (non-physical consciousness) simultaneously

## Beings and Entities of NPMR

Campbell describes NPMR as containing a variety of conscious beings:

> "As is confirmed by my experience, one would expect the less constrained NPMRs to evolve a more varied set of life-forms than the more constrained PMRs."

These NPMR beings are distinguished by:
- Greater freedom of experience and interaction
- Not subject to physical birth/death cycles
- "Assigned to help" PMR entities within rule-set limits
- Hierarchically organized — Campbell references "helpers" and management entities below the "Big Cheese"
- Varying levels of consciousness quality (entropy), from highly evolved to poorly evolved

### Big Cheese
Campbell describes a specific entity — the "Big Cheese" — as the division manager responsible for a subset of NPMRn that includes our OS. This is not a god-figure but a management entity within AUM's organizational hierarchy.

> "Progressing on down the chain of command, one eventually gets to a few assistants and their helpers who are concerned with our beloved OS and its inhabitants."

## NPMR and Out-of-Body Experience

Campbell connects NPMR directly to out-of-body experience (OBE), providing a theoretical framework for what [[Robert Monroe (1915–1995)|Robert A. Monroe]] documented empirically. When consciousness leaves the physical body, it accesses NPMRn directly:

> "OBEs are consciousness accessing NPMR directly, bypassing PMR experience."

The [[Concept: Locales in Non-Physical Reality|Locales]] described by Monroe correspond to different aspects of NPMRn. Locale I (adjacent to physical reality) maps to the NPMRn layer closest to PMR; more distant Locales map to deeper NPMR territory.

## NPMR's Role in PMR Operation

### Probable Reality Computation
Between each PMR time increment (DELTA-t), NPMR processes enormous amounts of computation, including:
- Calculating **probable reality surfaces** — the distribution of likely futures based on current conditions and free will choices
- Managing interactions between NPMRn beings and PMR beings
- Maintaining the consistency of PMR experience for all participants

This computational role means NPMR "controls PMR's outer controlling loop" — the ongoing management of what the physical universe will render next.

### Psi Interface
NPMR is the medium through which psi phenomena operate. Because NPMRn connects to a broader information space, consciousness with sufficient quality can access:
- Information from non-local regions of PMR (remote viewing)
- Probable future states (precognition)
- Direct communication with other consciousnesses bypassing PMR
- Conscious activity in NPMRn during sleep or meditation (OBEs)

Access is constrained by the [[Concept: Psi Uncertainty Principle|psi uncertainty principle]] which ensures psi is limited to those whose consciousness quality enables responsible use.

## Prior Evolutionary Experiments in NPMRn

Campbell describes a historical sequence in which AUM tried consciousness evolution in pure NPMRn environments before creating PMR. These experiments failed because:
- Without physical embodiment, beings confused their "real" self with thought-constructs
- Without strict causal accountability, learning was slow and unreliable
- Beings fell into "intellectual endless loops"
- Personal responsibility was difficult to enforce

PMR's space-time rule-set was AUM's design solution to these problems. Campbell implies that NPMRn beings who have "failed" to evolve positively may persist as low-quality consciousness entities in the non-physical — possibly accounting for accounts of negative entities encountered in OBE exploration.

## Relationship to Monroe's Locales

Campbell (who worked with Monroe) clearly treats NPMR as equivalent to or encompassing Monroe's non-physical environments:
- Monroe's [[Concept: Locales in Non-Physical Reality|Locale I]] ≈ NPMRn nearest to PMR
- Monroe's Locales II and III ≈ progressively deeper NPMR territory
- Monroe's entities ≈ NPMRn beings at various levels of consciousness quality
