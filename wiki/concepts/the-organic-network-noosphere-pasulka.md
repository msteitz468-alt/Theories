## Definition from Source

A recurring theme in experiencer reports: an "invisible network that is not the internet" — an organic
field of information through which contact and knowledge seem to flow. Experiencers compare it to the
**noösphere** of Jesuit philosopher [[Pierre Teilhard de Chardin]] — "an Earth atmosphere of
information" — and to indigenous knowledge systems that tie it to being "in sync with land and place."

## Description from Source

Pasulka reports the organic network appearing independently across her witnesses — Jose the soldier,
Simone the venture capitalist, and the space psychologist Dr. Whiteley — and across indigenous
frameworks, as an inextricable aspect of [[Contact with nonhuman intelligence (Pasulka)|contact]]. It
is the imagined medium of [[The download (spontaneous cognition, Pasulka)|the download]] and connects
to the book's interest in "new forms of consciousness" arising from cosmic and virtual environments.
The same Teilhardian noösphere appears in the wiki via [[The Forbidden Universe (Picknett & Prince)]].
