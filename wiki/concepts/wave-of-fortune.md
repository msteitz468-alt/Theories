## Definition from Source

The wave of fortune is an accumulation of favourable [[Life Lines]] that each individual possesses within the [[Alternatives Space]]. Zeland describes it as a concentration of positive life lines available to a person that can be accessed and sustained through appropriate thought energy and attitude.

"A wave of fortune equals an accumulation of favourable life lines. Each individual has their own." (Ch. III)

## Key Properties (from source)

- **Individual**: each person has their own wave of fortune; it is not a general or universal flow
- **Cascade effect**: "A cascade of good luck follows if you embrace the joyful energy of the first stroke of luck" — responding with joy to an initial stroke of luck attunes the person's energy to the wave, sustaining access to further favourable life lines
- **Broken by wrong response**: failing to embrace the joy of early good luck — by attributing high importance, reacting with anxiety, or suppressing the response — breaks the cascade and shifts the person away from the favourable life lines
- **Transmission of positive energy** (Ch. VIII, Positive Slides section): "It is most advantageous to be open to all that is good and to ignore anything bad... if you accept it joyfully the positive will never leave you"

## Relationship to Other Concepts

The wave of fortune is closely related to the principle of unconditional love as described in Ch. III: "Unconditional love = admiration without worship or need to possess." Both concepts involve receiving positive energy without creating [[Excess Potential]] through excessive importance.

The wave of fortune can be disrupted by [[Pendulums (Energy)]] that capture the person's energy following an initial stroke of luck, redirecting it away from the favourable life lines.
