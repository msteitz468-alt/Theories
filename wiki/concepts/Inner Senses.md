## Concept Definition

**Inner Senses** are the fundamental faculties of perception belonging to the soul or inner self. They exist and operate independently of physical bodily organs, allowing consciousness to perceive multidimensional reality, interact with non-physical environments, and navigate states like sleep, dreams, and out-of-body experiences (OBEs).

Seth explains that the five physical senses are not primary; they are merely specialized, physical extensions of these inner senses, designed to translate non-physical reality into the three-dimensional "camouflage" of solid matter, linear time, and spatial coordinates.

## Role and Function

* **Bypassing Physical Focus:** Physical senses restrict focus to the material world. To perceive other dimensions or receive telepathic communications, consciousness must turn inward and utilize the inner senses.
* **Mediumship and Channeling:** Seth states that his dictation of *Seth Speaks* is directed through the inner senses of Jane Roberts while she is in trance. Bypassing her physical senses allows her inner mind to translate his high-frequency energetic thoughts into English speech.
* **True Spirituality:** The third personality of Christ (predicted to re-emerge around 2075) is described by Seth as a great psychic who will teach humanity to use these inner senses. Seth asserts that "true spirituality" is only possible when humans learn to perceive reality through these non-physical channels.
* **Out-of-Body Navigation:** During out-of-body experiences, the physical eyes and ears are left behind. The individual relies entirely on the inner senses to perceive and interact with the non-physical environment, where thought directly shapes form and movement.
