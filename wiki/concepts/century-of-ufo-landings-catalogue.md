The book's appendix: an indexed catalogue of UFO landing / close-encounter cases for 1868–1968, compiled from cross-indexed international sources (Aimé Michel, Guy Quincy, the *Flying Saucer Review* 'Humanoids' issue, ATIC files). About 35% of entries include occupant descriptions. Vallée includes a methodology and a warning that inclusion implies no claim of physical reality and that cases differ in reliability.
