Coulthart lays out competing on-record statements without adjudicating. He stresses that, even if authentic, the memo only records Davis's account and proves nothing on its own; and that the most credibility-straining element (a contractor volunteering program details to someone not on the 'Bigot list') is what his defence-intelligence sources flagged as least plausible.
