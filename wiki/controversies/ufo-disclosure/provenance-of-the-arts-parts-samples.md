The dispute turns on a sample whose only origin story is anonymous letters to Art Bell, set against TTSA's later investment and the US Army CRADA. Coulthart records both the sceptical objections and the unfulfilled claims of anomalous properties (e.g. terahertz-induced levitation) for which no public evidence exists.
