Vallée argues the ETH cannot be 'stronger than the Celtic faith in the elves' and that the historical continuity of the phenomenon makes a purely extraterrestrial, nuts-and-bolts reading insufficient — while explicitly refusing to assert the interdimensional alternative as fact.
